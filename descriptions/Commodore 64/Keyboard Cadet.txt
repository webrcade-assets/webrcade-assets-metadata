Keyboard Cadet is a typing game for multiple systems. The player is in control of a spaceship, that must do battle with letters and words. As letters appear on the screen, the player must quickly type the correct letter, or their spaceship takes damage. Later, words appear on the screen, and if the player fails to type them correctly, their ship is further damaged. If the player makes 5 mistakes during a lesson, their ship is destroyed. There are numerous different typing lessons available, with 3 speeds.
