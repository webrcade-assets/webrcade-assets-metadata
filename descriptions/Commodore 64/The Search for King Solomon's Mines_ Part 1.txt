The Search for King Solomon's Mines: Part 1 is an adventure game with graphics for the Commodore 64. Africa 1903: A dying adventurer staggers from the jungle raving about 7 foot tall natives, ice and snow, a searing volcano and... a cache of treasure fit for a king. At the start of the adventure, the player can pick 5 supplies from: a rifle, torch, compass, pick, binoculars, rations, knife, beads, medicines, canteen, matches, and rope. There are 3 levels of puzzle difficulty and danger. All commands are via VERB NOUN input, and locations are graphically displayed on the screen. The player must explore the jungle in search of King Solomon's treasure. Games can be saved in progress.
