Caverns of Doom is a text adventure for multiple systems. The player has found themselves lost in the Caverns of Doom with just a flashlight, and must escape to safety.  During their adventure, the player must build a raft, find and use a lamp, and ultimately contstruct a gun to defeat the bear that guards the cavern exit.  There is a maximum score of 80 points, with 10 points awarded for reaching specific locations.  All available directions are displayed on a compass at the bottom right of the screen. All commands are via VERB NOUN entry, and there is a save/restore feature.
