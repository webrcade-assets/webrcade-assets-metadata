Player can choose one or more of the four rulers. Ruler has the possibility to buy and sell provinces, sack the province, create an army, attack the province, make an alliance or participate in an archery tournament. The player has no effect on the battlefield. To ultimately defeat the enemy player must destroy the walls of his castle with a catapult. In addition the game features a variety of random events, such as loss of half of the gold. The game allows to play up to four players.
