This is a compilation of four games by Beyond. Both ZX Spectrum and Commodore 64 share two games and having two platform specific titles bundled in this compilation: Shadowfire Enigma Force On the ZX Spectrum this two games are accompanied by: Doomdark's Revenge (Spectrum) Sorderon's Shadow (Spectrum) While on the C64 the following games are completing the collection: PSI Warrior (Commodore 64) Quake Minus One (Commodore 64)
