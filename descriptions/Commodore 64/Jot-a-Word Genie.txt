Jot-a-Word Genie is a 1-player word game for the Commodore 64. The player and computer take turns trying to guess 5 letter words. Each turn, the player must guess a 5-letter word, and are told how many of the letter in their word are correct, and how many are in the correct position. Only real words are accepted, and the program understands approximately 5,000 words. The first player to guess correctly is the winner.
