ABC Caterpillar is an educational game for the Commodore 64. The player controls a small caterpillar which must spell words by eating the correct letters of the alphabet. The letters are displayed alphabetically in vertical columns on the right and left of the screen, while an image of the item to spell is displayed in the top center of the screen. Gameplay options include single letter recognition, as well as spelling 3 and 4 letter words. The game tells the player if they are spelling a word correctly or incorrectly. Contains an additional game called Butterfly Draw, where the player makes designs by moving a butterfly around the screen.
