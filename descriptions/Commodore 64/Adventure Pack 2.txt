Adventure Pack 2 is a series of 3 text adventure games for multiple systems Each game uses brief VERB NOUN entries. There is no save or restore feature, and the player has a limited number of turns to complete their tasks. African Escape: The player crashes their airplane in the desert, and must escape while avoiding cannibals, navigating a silver mine, and repairing a ship. Bomb Threat: Terrorists have left you prisoner at their hideout. You must escape and disarm their bomb before 4:00 o'clock. Hospital Adventure: Attempt to infiltrate a hospital, and finish off the bungled assassination attempt on the dictator of Gvench by poisoning him.
