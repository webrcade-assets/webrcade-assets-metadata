Trek is a 1-player strategy game for multiple systems Commanding the bridge of your starship, you must explore the galaxy, fending off the Klingon invasion. Gameplay is on an 8 x 8 grid of 64 quadrants displayed on the galactic map, and the player has limited fuel and years to complete their mission. Each quadrant contains its own 8 x 8 grid which contains stars, and may have enemy vessels. The player's vessel is armed with long range sensors to investigate adjacent quadrants, and is armed with a limited number of torpedoes and phasers. The ship has limited energy, which powers the shields and phasers. The player has access to a status report on the condition of their ship's equipment.
