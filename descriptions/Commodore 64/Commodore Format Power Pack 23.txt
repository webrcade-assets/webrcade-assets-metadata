Commodore Format Power Pack 23 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 23 (August 1992) contains:* Defenders (Full Game) Johnny Reb 2 (Full Game) Bug Bomber (Playable Demo) Biff (Playable Demo) Nobby (Playable Demo)
