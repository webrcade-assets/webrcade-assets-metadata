You are Mr Parker, a solicitor and one of your clients Mr Dickens has died and his last will and testament needs to be sorted out. Problem is that no one knows it's location, but rumours are that it is hidden in his house, outside of town. Starting in your office, you must get to the house before exploring it looking for the missing will. House Out of Town is a text adventure with text on the screen describing your location and you type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
