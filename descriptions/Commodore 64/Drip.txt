Drip is a side-scrolling platform game. The player's task is to collect all the coins that spilled out of the car carrying them to the bank. The game features 42 levels full of coins and hostile creatures. The creatures may be neutralized by throwing the ball, however contact with them takes one life. After collecting all the coins Drip must reach the end of the board before the time runs out. The remaining time is recalculated for extra points. The game consists two level passwords, acquired after finishing level 10 and 20.
