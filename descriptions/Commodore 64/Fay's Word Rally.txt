Fay's Word Rally is a language learning game for one or two players. The player controls a sports car which zooms around a maze. Interspersed in the maze are ten words. On the bottom of the screen, sentences or pictures are shown, and the player must drive to the correct answer. The player is rewarded a score based upon the speed and accuracy of their answering. There are a total of 80 questions sets included with the game, and the player can design their own word sets.
