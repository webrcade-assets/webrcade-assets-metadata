Softporn Adventure is a comedic, adult-oriented adventure game and became the inspiration for the animated Leisure Suit Larry series. Actions are controlled through a verb and noun combination, typed into the game. Ultimately, you must win over the heart of various ladies in creative ways so as to get laid. The player is unnamed but the plot of the game is very similar to Leisure Suit Larry in the Land of the Lounge Lizards. This is an unofficial port of the original version to the Commodore 64.
