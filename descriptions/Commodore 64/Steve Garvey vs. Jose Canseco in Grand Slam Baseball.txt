Steve Garvey vs. Jose Canseco in Grand Slam Baseball is a 1 - 2 player baseball game for the Commodore 64. Following basic Major League Baseball rules, there are a total of two teams, and the player can define the names and positions of their teammates. Individual players do not have unique stats, and all characters are identical. The game is player from the perspective of behind the behind the batter. The game relied upon the star power of Steve Garvey who retired the same year the game was published, and the at the time young star Jose Canseco for sales.
