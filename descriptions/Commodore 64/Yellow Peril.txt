In this sequel to Land of the Purple Sea, where the protagonist's experiments leave them stranded in a mysterious place wherein all environments are now yellow, evasion has been effected from the grasp of mauve - but at what cost? Now you find yourself on the shores of a copper sea, contemplating shelter from the golden sun above and flight into the amber hills to beigely plot your ecru escape. Yellow Peril is a text adventure where the screen describes each location and you type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
