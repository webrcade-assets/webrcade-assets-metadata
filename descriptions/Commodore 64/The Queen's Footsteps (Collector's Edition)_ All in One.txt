This collector's edition is a boxed mail order release of a text adventure. It contains:* The Queen's Footsteps Two Days to the Race (bonus game featuring the same protagonist) Cardboard box, shrinkwrapped Manual in DIN-A5 size in English (eight pages) Micro SD card with the disk images and bonus material Pocket mirror in a linen bag Poster (DIN-A3 size) Sticker All listed platforms are included with this release on two 3.5" disks, three 5.25" disks and one 3" disk.
