In the game made with SEUCK (Shoot 'Em Up Construction Kit) you control one or two cars (red Ferrari, blue Porsche) on the crazy track full of surprises. Cars should shoot missiles to eliminate other hostile cars, move in four directions, and avoid the attacks. On the track you'll findthe characters and landscapes of famous games such as Pac-Man, or Frogger, or meet the people hordes from Pirates. The two player game is cooperative and played on the same screen.
