SportSearch is a word search game with a sports theme. On a screen filled with 374 letters, the player's task is to locate twenty words and type them in. There are ten words vertically and ten words horizontally. The player has ten minutes to find all the words and will then get a score. Each found word is worth ten points and bonus points are given it there is any time left. The game contains three different variations with different words: Sport games Pro teams College team names
