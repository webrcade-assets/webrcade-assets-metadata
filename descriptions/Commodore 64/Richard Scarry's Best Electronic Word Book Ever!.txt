Contains 4 games based upon Richard Scarry's Busy People series. It contains 4 mini-games set on a farm, railroad, town, park, harbor, or construction site. Get the Picture: Drive around a picture, and interact with different characters. Scavenger Hunt: The player is given a list of items to find, and must search all 3 locations to find them. Stop, Look, and Listen: Search the setting, and find the picture displayed in the top left of the screen. Word Patrol: Look for the named item within the setting.
