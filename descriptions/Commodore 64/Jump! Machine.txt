Jump! Machine is a 1-player arcade game for the Commodore 64. Played from the side view, the player controls an obese character who moves laterally, climbs ladders, and jumps. Each level has two screens with warp points that allow movement between the two. The goal is to collect all the cherries between the two screens by jumping on them. There are numerous dangerous, including: spikes, various monsters, and falling too far. Levels also have slides, ladders, elevators, and other challenges. The player starts with 9 lives, and there's also an editing feature that allows the construction of custom levels.
