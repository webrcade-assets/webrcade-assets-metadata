While the screenshots on the back of the box may get players hopes up that they are getting a home port of Hat-Trick Hero, what they actually get may be a bit more humbling. European Football Champ is a soccer game viewed from a top down perspective with the pitch aligned horizontally. Players can choose the length of the game from 2 to 90 minutes. Unlike the Amiga / Atari ST game with the same title that only focused on European teams, this version of European Football Champ features 9 international teams:The 5 teams that were in both Football Champ / Hat-Trick Hero arcade games: Argentina, Brazil. England, , Germany, Italy The 2 Football Champ exclusive teams: France and Spain The 2 Hat Trick Hero exclusive teams: Japan and USA However this game lacks the Netherlands team which was in both of those Arcade games.
