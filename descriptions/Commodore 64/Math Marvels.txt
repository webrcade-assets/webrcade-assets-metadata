Math Marvels is an educational math game for multiple systems. Contains three 2-player math mini-games. Allows for a computer opponent: Decimal Hunt - The player and their opponent enters a 4-digit decimal, and the player is told how many digits are correct and whether the actual number is higher or lower. The first person to guess the correct number wins. Multi-Maze - The player must move through a maze by correctly answering decimal multiplication questions. Decimal Rally - 8 decimals are placed on the screen, and the player can add and subtract only these numbers. The player tries to reach all the data ranges on the screen with the minimal moves.
