Space journey is an educational math game for multiple systems. The player controls a spaceship, and must answer math questions in order to perform actions with their vessel. The player has 2 minutes to guide their spaceship back to Earth. The player must answer mathematical questions to move their spaceship, and they must intercept 10 meteors or land on 5 planets before returning to the Earth. The player will be lost in space permanently if they strike a meteor or do not return to the Earth within 2 minutes. Along the way, the player encounters dangers including force fields, meteor showers, refueling stations, and others which require answering math questions to escape. There are 3 levels of difficulty.
