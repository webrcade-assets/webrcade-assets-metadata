This compilation contains an assortment of sports games, taking in several different sports as well as arcade conversions, games originating from US companies, titles from New Generation and CDS, and licenses of professional athletes of the time: GBA Championship Basketball: Two-on-Two World Series Baseball Super Soccer Hyper Sports Ping Pong Daley Thompson's Super-Test Barry McGuigan World Championship Boxing Jonah Barrington's Squash Konami's Tennis CDS Software's Pool For the Commodore 64 and Amstrad CPC release of the game Konami's Tennis and CDS Software's Pool were replaced. In their place was: Match Point Visions' Snooker (Commodore 64 version only) Blue Ribbon's Pool (Amstrad CPC version only) Super Soccer appeared on Amstrad CPC and Commodore 64 only in this compilation (there was no separate release for those platforms).
