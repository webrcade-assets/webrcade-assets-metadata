Commodore Format Power Pack 28 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 28 (January 1993) contains:* First Strike (Full Game) Fifth Gear (Full Game) Reckless Rufus (Playable Demo) Locomotion (Playable Demo) Nick Faldo's Championship Golf (Playable Demo) Christmas Demo: A Public Domain demo (Demo)
