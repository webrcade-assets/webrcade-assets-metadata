Commodore Format Power Pack 21 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 21 (June 1992) contains:* Ancipital (Full Game) Southern Belle (Full Game) Euro Football Champ (Playable Demo) Arnie (Playable Demo) Addams Family (Playable Demo)
