Commodore Format Power Pack 32 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 32 (May 1993) contains:* Thrust (Full Game) Corya (Part 1) (Full Game) Steel (Full Game) FROST: A sprite creation program written especially for Commodore Format (Utility) Multihack Pokes: A program with listings and pokes already typed in for a collection of games to cheat at (Utility)
