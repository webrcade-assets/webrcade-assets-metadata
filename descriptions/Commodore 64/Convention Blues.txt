After reading about the annual Aventurer's Convention, you wish to go but feel that you are not qualified enough to attend, and feel as well that you will be mocked if you did. To prove to yourself that you can be confident that you belong there, you have decided to complete this adventure by exploring a house and it's grounds, finding the ten treasures hidden. Convention Blues is a text adventure where the screen has text describing your location and you type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
