Relaxator is a side-scrolling space shooter. As a spaceship pilot the player must patrol the area of the planet Cormos, invaded by Derm's. Depending on selected Joystick's port the player may pilot HRJ220V or HRJ200XP fighter, ships of different maneuverability and firepower. The ship can move in all directions, although the playing area moves constantly forward. The goal is to reach the end of each level without being killed by hostile units or contact with facilities.
