Commodore Format Power Pack 19 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 19 (April 1992) contains:* Attack of the Mutant Camels (Full Game) Sheep in Space (Full Game) Aquablasta (Full Game, an unreleased game from a CF reader, A. Smith) UDG System2 (Utility, a character set editor to change the character set on your C64) Light Disk64 (Demo, allows you to use the icon-based user system for the computer magazine Light Disk64)
