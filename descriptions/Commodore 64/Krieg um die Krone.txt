Krieg um die Krone is the predecessor of Heirs to the Throne a board game comparable with Risk. In a medieval fantasy scenario, which will be automatically created new with every game start, your aiming to eliminate your opponents and conquer the world by placing your armies and attacking neighbour territories. Tactical gameplay is required, because to win the game, you need to conquer those territories filled with treasure chests. In those treasure quests you will find crowns, orbs or sceptres. For each of them you receive an title of nobility which improves your ranking and will be rewarded with additional troops. You will win the game if you're able to get all three of them (crown, orb, sceptre). Towards most early games of this kind, you don't need an human opponent, you can play as single-player against the AI.
