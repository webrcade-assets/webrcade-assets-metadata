Vegetables Deluxe is a Bejeweled -style match-three game. In addition to traditional Casual and Classic modes, it offers two challenge modes: Shopping (make a particular number of matches of each type), and Countdown (make matches within allotted time). The game was released in cartridge and floppy disk formats for Commodore 64, with the digital release containing both disk and cartridge images. The Amiga version is only available as a disk image.
