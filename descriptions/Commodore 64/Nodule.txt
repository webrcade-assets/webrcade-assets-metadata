Nodule is an arcade game set in 2519 Year. Nodule, the guardian of the Galaxy, was on duty when an unknown strange power transported him to another time. While warping, his patrol ship was damaged and crashed on the unknown planet Alpha-X. Pilot must reach a mysterious magical place where there are olo runes that take him back to his reality. The way to the goal leads through a maze of obstacles that must be avoided, being careful of moving hostile creatures that can be shot.
