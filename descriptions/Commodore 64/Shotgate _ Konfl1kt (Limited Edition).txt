This is a limited edition of twenty cartridges of the games Shotgate and Konfl1kt, originally developed for an annual mini-game competition to make games within a certain file size. The game are 4 kilobytes and 1 kilobyte in size respectively. The cartridge consists of a PCB with 8 Kb EPROM and a black cartridge shell. The cart is labelled and the set also includes a plastic box with full color cover, an 8 page manual and a sticker with serial number and signature.
