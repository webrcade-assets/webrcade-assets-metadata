Race Car 'Rithmetic is an educational games where up to four players compete in a race by answering math questions. Players that answer questions correctly move one space ahead while those that answer incorrectly will have to make a pit stop. About fifteen questions have to be answered to complete a race. Each player can choose operation (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division or a variety), level of difficulty and whether to be timed or not. When reaching a score of 90% or more, the player is given the opportunity to play the Race Car Game. This is a simple top down racing game where the player has to pass as many cars as possible within the given time. When the time is up the player gets a score based on the distance covered.
