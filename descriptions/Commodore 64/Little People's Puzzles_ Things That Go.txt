Little People's Puzzles: Things That Go is a puzzle game for the Apple II. Contains several jigsaw puzzles based upon means of transportation, including: trains, cars, trucks, rockets, boats, and planes. Players can also import their own artwork to serve as the basis for puzzles. Pieces can be moved and rotated, and there are 4 levels of difficulty from 9 - 36 pieces missing to challenge players. On completion of the puzzle, the graphics become animated.
