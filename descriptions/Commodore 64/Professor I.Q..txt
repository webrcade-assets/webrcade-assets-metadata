Professor I.Q. is a 1 - 2 player educational trivia game for multiple systems. Players take turns playing a number of timed logic games. The player scores a select number of credits based upon their performance, which can be spent on the game board. After each round of play, players can take as many squares as they earned in that round. Players can take empty squares or squares owned by their opponent, and the first player to acquire all spaces on the board is the victor. As a player is more successful, they are presented with progressively more difficult logic challenges to help keep game play equal between players of different level. Players take turns spinning the Wizard Wheel, which chooses a multitude of logic games: Math Fun: the player answers mat h questions Word Scrambles: the player attempts to unscramble words Reverse It: the player reverses the orders of sections of numbers to try to put them all in numerical order Puzzle Time: sliding block puzzle where the player tries to put the alphabet in order Miss a Turn Gain 8 Credits
