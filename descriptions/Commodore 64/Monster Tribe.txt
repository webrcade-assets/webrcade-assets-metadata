Monster Tribe is a 1-player arcade game for the Commodore 64. The player controls a small spaceship that can move in all directions, and fires vertically forwards. The player finds 3 large alien creatures that move about the screen. When an alien is shot, they begin to return fire towards the player. The player receives points for hitting an enemy, but they cannot be destroyed. They player starts with 5 lives, and loses a life if hit.
