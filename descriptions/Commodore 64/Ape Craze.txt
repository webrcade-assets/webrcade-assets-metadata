Ape Craze is a 1-player arcade game for the Commodore 64. The player controls a small character who moves laterally, and can jump between numerous platforms. The goal is to collect all of the items, while avoiding the bombs thrown about the screen by the ape at the top left of the screen. If the player is hit by a bomb or falls more than 1 platform distance they lose a life. The player starts with 3 lives.
