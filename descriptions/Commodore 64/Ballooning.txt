Ballooning is an educational simulation game where the player from the ages of 12 to 16 years old can learn to fly a hot air balloon safely over an ever-changing landscape with the use of the instrument panel. The player can choose from different kinds of balloons and which of the three games they want to play. The first one is the Flying School where the player can learn how to fly a balloon with the controls. The second one is the Flying Test where the player has to complete three tasks to prove the ability to fly a balloon. The last one is the Live Mission. The player will receive points with each task fulfilled. However, if the player fails to land on the landing place to get the task or fails to respond to the signals the player will lose points.
