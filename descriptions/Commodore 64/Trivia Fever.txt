Trivia Fever is a 1 - 8 player trivia game. Players must verbally answer the questions given, and the pre-selected Master of the Game must decide if the answer was correct. A player must complete sufficient questions in 5 of the 7 topics, including: science & technology, sports, famous people, geography, history, film & entertainment, nature & animals. After the necessary number of points in each subject category has been obtained, a category completion question is generated. Once the category completion question has has been answered successfully for all 5 categories, a game winning question is generated, which if answered successfully, the game is won. Handicaps can be set up for more experienced quiz takers, to make their game more difficult. Three levels of question difficulty are available.
