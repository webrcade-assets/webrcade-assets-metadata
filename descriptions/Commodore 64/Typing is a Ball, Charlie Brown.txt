Typing is a Ball, Charlie Brown is a typing game for one or two players. Charlie Brown attempts to throw a basketball into a hoop or roll a bowling ball to knock down pins. As the ball is in motion, the player it given a short string of characters to type. If the player can type all of them before the ball reaches its target, the player scores a basket or a strike. Otherwise they fail. The game tracks how many strings the player has correctly typed in time. The player can set the speed of the ball and the type of characters.
