The Game Show is a 1 - 2 player trivia game for the Apple II. Played in a game show format, each player is assisted by their teammate Joe or May. The players choose from a variety of topics, and receive 12 questions on that topic. Joe or May starts by reading 10 clues for each answer, with the player able to guess the answer after each clue. The player is rewarded a maximum of 10 points with a correct answer on the first clue, and 1 point less with each clue thereafter. After 12 questions for each team, a winner with the highest points is announced.
