COMPUTE!'s First Book of Commodore 64 Games is an educational book with type-in games that were previously published in COMPUTE! magazine. Contains detailed descriptions on how to design and write computer games.  Also contains the following games: Blockhead Cyclon Zap Diamond Drop Goblin Laser Bounce Laser Gunner Minefield Mosaic Puzzle Munchmaze Mystery Spell Oil Tycoon Rats! Richthofen's Revenge Snake Escape States & Capitals Tutor The Hawkmen of Dindrin The Viper Zuider Zee
