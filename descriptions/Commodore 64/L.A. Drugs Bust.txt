L.A. Drugs Bust (or ' Los Angeles Police Department ') is a simple rail shooter like Operation Wolf. The player plays the role of a Los Angeles police officer, and shoots enemy gangsters. The camera moves automatically, the player only moves an UZI's viewfinder. The uzi has very limited ammunition, but ammo and body armor can be liberated from gang's members. The player's energy will be depleted, when he is shot, or when he injures innocent civilians are in crossfire.
