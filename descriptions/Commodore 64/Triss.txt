Triss is a platform game, where the player must stop Hephyr from taking over the world. The main task requires recovering 20 magical stones of Thefrog, scattered around Hephyr mansion. Triss will meet many unfriendly creatures and unpleasant obstacles, but he will also several useful items. Eight items can be carried simultaneously. Triss is weaponless, therefore the only way to avoid enemies is proper use of jumping skill.
