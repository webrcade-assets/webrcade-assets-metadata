Heroes & Cowards: The Pentagram of Power is a text adventure with graphics. You are watching TV when suddenly you are transported to the country of Dartenwood. All the inhabitants of Dartenwood were turned to stone by the magician Morlon. You must find and reconstruct the Pentagram of Power in order to break the spell. Instead of pure text input the available actions can be chosen as menu structures. This 2015 release is the finished version of Byteriders game originally created in the 90s. It was never completed and this release contains a finished version with bugfixes.
