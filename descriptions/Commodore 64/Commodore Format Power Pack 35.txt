Commodore Format Power Pack 35 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 35 (August 1993) contains:* Space Academy (Full Game) Jhotamia 6 (Full Game) Hallax: Game released on Public Domain (Full Game) Lemmings (Playable Demo) Real Writer: A program that makes anything you type move around the screen (Demo)
