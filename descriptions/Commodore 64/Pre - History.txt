You, Arkenia have returned from a hunting trip with your faithful man, Sarudan and found your village devastated and in ruins. To make matters worse, your Father and many relatives have been taken by Slave Traders. You have vowed revenge on these people and so you start your search in your village and surrounding area for equipment before tracking down the Slavers and bringing your family back. Pre - History is a text adventure where text on the screen describes your location and you type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
