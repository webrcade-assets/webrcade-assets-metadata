Commodore Format Power Pack 18 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 18 (March 1992) contains:* Firelord (Full Game) Sphinx Jinx (Full Game, an unreleased sequel to the Freescape game Total Eclipse) Fast (Full Game, unreleased game from CF reader S. Metcalfe, Leeds) Demon Blues (Playable Demo)
