Alioth is a sci-fi platform game set on the planet of the same name. After an unsuccessful scientific experiment, a robot & operators are sent in to destroy everything. The robot is equipped with a gun, but some of the mutants have created a protective system against the bullets. The amount of bullets is limited, but some of the killed mutants may leave an extra ammo. The player's task is to find out 10 parts of nuclear bomb and bring them to the start location in order to destroy the base.
