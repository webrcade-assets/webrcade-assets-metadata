In Aztec Tomb, the player has the task of finding the entrance to an Aztec tomb. Various tasks need to completed in the meantime, and these tasks include escaping from a house, avoiding an element, crossing a river, and exploring an island. The screen consists of a graphical representation of the room, a list of the objects in the room, and an area where the player can enter one- or two-word commands necessary to progress through the game. During the game, players can access their inventory and save or load a game in the process.
