Dinosaur Days is a series of educational games related to dinosaurs. All games require a special EasyKey keyboard overlay to play: Dino Dig: Guess the mystery dinosaur from the clues. Dino Discovery: See the dinosaurs and compare their facts. Dino Flash: Can you identify the dinosaurs? Try to guess as many as possible in 15 turns. Fact or Fable: Which dinosaurs lived at the same time? Try to match 5 pairs of dinosaurs. Who's the Biggest?: A game of dinosaurs and their weights. Choose the largest of 3 displayed dinosaurs.
