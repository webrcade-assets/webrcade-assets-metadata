The Mojon Twins: 3 Games in 1 is a compilation of three C64 games by the Spanish development group The Mojon Twins brought together on a single tape or a single disk. All games were previously released as homebrew downloads free of charge. The included games are: Nanako in Classic Japanese Monster Castle Sir Ababol Uwol: Quest for Money The game comes in a box that also contains a printed manual in colour and two stickers. The games are NTSC/PAL compatible.
