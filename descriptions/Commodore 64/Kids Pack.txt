Kids Pack is a compilation for children by Alternative Software. It is containing different games for the 8-bit and 16-bit platforms. 8-bit platforms (C-64, ZX Spectrum) Count Duckula Postman Pat Popeye 2 Sooty & Sweep SuperTed The Wombles 16-bit platforms (Amiga, Atari ST) Count Duckula Postman Pat The Munsters
