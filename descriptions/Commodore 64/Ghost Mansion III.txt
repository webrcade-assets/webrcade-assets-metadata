Ghost Mansion III is a 1-player arcade game for the Commodore 64. An incalculable amount of wealth in diamonds is contained in an old castle. It would seem like child's play to collect the treasure, but, the rooms are guarded by various traps and guards. The player controls a small character who can move in all directions, and tries to collect all of the gems on the screen, and then move to the exit at the right of the screen. There are a total of 5 levels, and dangers include conveyor belts, doppelgangers, and missiles. The player starts with 5 lives
