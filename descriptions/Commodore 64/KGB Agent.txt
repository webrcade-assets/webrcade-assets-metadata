KGB Agent is a horizontal scrolling arcade game for the Commodore 64. Ilya Ilyavanovik, the renowned agent of the KGB, has been tasked by his superior Farenov Gherkinski to track down their arch enemy supper yuppie Alexander Sharamski.  Sharamski has made off with millions of rubles worth of Eastern Block armaments, and plans to sell the to Mad Martinov, the Western arms dealer.  They plan to launch a budget range of tactical weapons, hence undercutting world profits and harming Soviet profits.  Ilya will be landed on the roof of their arms depot, and must descend through levels of dangerous guards and automated security.
