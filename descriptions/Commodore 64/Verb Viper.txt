Verb Viper is an educational game for multiple systems. The player controls a viper who attempts to eat the correct form of the verb "to be" A noun is displayed on the viper, while verbs scroll from the right. The player must try to eat the correct form of the verb, while ignoring the incorrect. As the player successfully eats words, the snake gets longer, which gives the player less time to react to an incoming verb. They receive a score at the end of the game.
