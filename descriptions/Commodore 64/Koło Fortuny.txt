Ko≈Ço fortuny is a game loosely based on the TV show Wheel of Fortune. The players take turns guessing letters to form a word or a saying. The game can be played with up to three human players. During their turn the players can spin the wheel, guess a consonant, buy a vowel or guess the password. The game is divided into 5 stages, passwords belong to one of nine categories. The game features built-in passwords editor.
