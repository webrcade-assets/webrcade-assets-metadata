Match Up! is a 1-player educational game for multiple systems. There are 3 modes of gameplay: Matching Game: Matching a pair of the same family within 2, 4, or 8 objects. Not Game: Choose which item does not look-alike, or is not in the same family. Sorting Game: Divide items into "Look-Alikes" and "Families". Objects must be placed in 3 separate columns depending on which group they belong.
