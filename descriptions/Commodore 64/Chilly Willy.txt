Chilly Willy is a 1 - 2 player arcade game for the Commodore 64. The player controls a small penguin that moves between blocks of ice while chased by enemy penguins. The player can destroy enemies by kicking ice blocks at them and trapping them between other blocks or the edge of the screen. There are several immobile blocks that can't be kicked or moved. After destroying several enemies, the player moves to a more challenging stage. The player starts with 3 lives.
