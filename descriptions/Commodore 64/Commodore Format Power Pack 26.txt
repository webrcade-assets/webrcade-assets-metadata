Commodore Format Power Pack 26 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 26 (November 1992) contains:* Twin Tigers: Unreleased shooter using SEUCK (Full Game) Bomber: Unreleased game (Full Game) Cosmic Causeway (Full Game) Stuntman Seymour (Playable Demo) Doc Croc (Playable Demo)
