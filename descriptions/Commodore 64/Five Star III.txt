Part of the Five Star Games series, this compilation contained five games plus two free ones: Uridium Plus The Way Of The Exploding Fist Trap Door Tau Ceti Aliens Firelord * Strike Force Harrier Uridium Plus is an enhanced version of Uridium with newly designed levels, additional challenge, and the ability to land at any time instead of waiting to be instructed. It was never released on its own and only appeared in compilations. On the Amstrad CPC version Uridium Plus is replaced with Tempest
