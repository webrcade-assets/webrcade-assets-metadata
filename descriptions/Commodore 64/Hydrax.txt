Hydrax is a 1-player side scrolling arcade/adventure game. You have entered a large cavern rumored to host an ancient dragon on the lowest levels. The dragon is believed to guard a great treasure, and will stop at nothing to protect it. The player controls a character who can walk, duck, jump, and swing a sword. The player explores numerous rooms, but can also type commands to move and control their character. The player can pick up and drop items, and has a limited inventory capacity. The player must solve puzzles, and also defeat enemies.
