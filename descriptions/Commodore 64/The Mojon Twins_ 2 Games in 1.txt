The Mojon Twins: 2 Games in 1 is a compilation of two C64 games by the Spanish development group The Mojon Twins brought together on a single cartridge. Both games were previously released as homebrew downloads free of charge. The included games are: Nanako in Classic Japanese Monster Castle Sir Ababol The cartridge has a transparent purple cartridge shell illuminated by an internal LED. It also comes with a printed manual in colour and a vinyl Mojon Twins sticker. There are two packaging types: either as a standard card carton box or a deluxe version that comes in a plastic universal game case with a cut foam insert to hold the cartridge. Upon ordering a digital version is also sent out.
