Luna Landa is a single screen arcade game. Moonbase 69 is under an alien attack. The task requires rescuing the stranded lunar scientists and return them to the Mothership before the fuel runs out. In order to complete the mission the player must land safely on the lander get back into orbit, avoiding numerous hostile ships moving horizontally from one side to another and performing vertical shoots. Using engine booster takes extra fuel, which can be supplemented only in the base. The lander can perform shots on the way back to the Mothership
