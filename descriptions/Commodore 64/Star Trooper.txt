Star Trooper sets you as a lone space soldier with a jet pack, with aliens to face. The first task is to successfully dock with your mother ship, in order to refuel. Then the battle beings, against 3 alien waves with different patterns and strategies, followed by a meteor storm, before successfully docking again. There are 6 levels, each with four new aliens to be faced. The action is viewed from the side, and the player fires in the direction they are facing.
