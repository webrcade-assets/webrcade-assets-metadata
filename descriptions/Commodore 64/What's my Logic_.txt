What's my Logic? is a 1-player educational game for multiple systems. A 7 x 7 grid is displayed on the screen containing different shapes of different colors. The player starts in the bottom left and must move to the top right of the screen. There are 20 total puzzles, and each one contains specific logic as to what moves are allowed or not allowed. If a move is not allowed, the player must restart in the bottom left. The goal is to solve the puzzle in as few attempts as possible.
