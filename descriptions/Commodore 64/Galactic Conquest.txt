Galactic Conquest is a 1 - 6 player strategy game for multiple systems. Each player takes turns colonizing and developing a game map of 30 planets. Each planet has an industrial capacity, and these can be used to construct more vessels. The player can send ships to nearby planets to conquer them. The player can scan nearby planets, and can evacuate planets in an emergency. The player specifies the number of years the game will last from 1 - 200, and there are 5 skill levels. Players can limit the number of moves allowed each turn. The player with the largest empire at the end of the game is the winner.
