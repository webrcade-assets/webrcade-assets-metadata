Soldier of Fortune is a 1-player arcade game for the Commodore 64. The player must explore a series of undergrounds caverns to kill the monsters within. The player's character can move horizontally, jump, and use the weapons they find throughout the labyrinth. Each individual monster requires a specific weapon or stronger to destroy, and stronger monsters require strong weapons. The player must travel through the catacombs and destroy as many monsters as possible in a limited time span. The player starts with 5 lives, and it's game over when all are lost.
