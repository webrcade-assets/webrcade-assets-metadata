Expeditions consists of three different educational games for the Commodore 64: Furs : You are leading a fur trading expedition as an Ottawa native american. Choose between three different routes to travel across North America in the mid- to late 1600's. Voyageur : Set between 1770 and 1830, you play as a fur trader who travels from Grand Portage into the fur trading area west of Lake Superior. Oregon : a port of The Oregon Trail This game set was offered as a software pack-in for Commodore 64s in 1984.
