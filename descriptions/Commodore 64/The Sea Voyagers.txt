The Sea Voyagers is an educational history game. It contains a series of three games related to early European ocean explorers and their expeditions: Explorer Match: a 1-2 player poker game using European explorers. The player is given 5 explorers, and has a single chance to discard 0 - 5 cards. They then receive a score including pair, two pairs, three of a kind, full house, etc. based upon the similarity between explorers. Typical criteria include nationality and location visited. Explorer Mix: three explorers and details of their voyage are mixed. The player must correctly match the explorer to their voyage. Who Goes There: the player receives progressively more clues about an explorer, including: their date of birth/death, the name of their ship, nationality, and their route. The player receives points for quickly naming the explorer.
