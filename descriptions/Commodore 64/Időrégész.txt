Time Archaeologist is a notable Hungarian text adventure game. In the year 2546, the Time Archeology Association was founded, and you are an agent of it. You are commissioned to acquire one from the fabulous griffon bird's eggs, so you are sent back to the medieval times. You are not helped by modern technology. The game features images about your current surroundings, a health meter, a progress indicator, and a compass showing the possible exits, plus some occasional musical cues. Save-load feature present. Hungarian characters set is supported by the parser.
