Commodore Format Power Pack 33 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 33 (June 1993) contains:* Water Polo (Full Game) Corya (Part 2) (Full Game) ATA: Game released on Public Domain (Full Game) Snack Man: Game released on Public Domain (Full Game) Sprite Demo: A game to be played as part of a feature found on p23 called 'Do The Sprite Thing' (Utility)
