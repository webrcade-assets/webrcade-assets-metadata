Interdictor Pilot is a 1-player arcade game for the TRS-80. The player pilots a spaceship from a 1st person perspective. The ship automatically warps through hyperspace, and arrives in combat with an enemy vessel. The player has a limited fuel supply, and must quickly destroy enemy vessels. As the player proceeds, they arrive at local starbases, which allow the player to save their progress and earn ranks. The goal is to reach the rank of Commodore.
