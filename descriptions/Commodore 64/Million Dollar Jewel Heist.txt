You awake tied to chair in a large room which looks part of a large mansion. You have a sore head and no memory. You know that you need to get out of this place. As you explore the mansion your memory slowly comes back to you and you realise you were involved in a robbery. The thieves for some reason have left the loot and hidden it about the house. You now want to find the treasure before making your escape. Million Dollar Jewel Heist is a text-only adventure where you type in your commands via the keyboard. Each screen describes your location and allows you to type in your commands.
