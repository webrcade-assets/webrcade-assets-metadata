Commodore Format Power Pack 30 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 30 (March 1993) contains:* Re-Bounder (Full Game) Slayer (Full Game) Daedalus: Unreleased shooter made with SEUCK from Andrew Smith (Full Game) Black Jack: Unreleased version of the card game Pontoon (Full Game) Music Maker 64: Allows you to play music on the keyboard (Utility)
