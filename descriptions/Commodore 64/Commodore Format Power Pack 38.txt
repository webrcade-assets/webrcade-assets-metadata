Commodore Format Power Pack 38 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 38 (November 1993) contains:* Freddy Hardest: Part One (full game) Life Force (full game) Lunar Jailbreak : unreleased game from readers Lee Taylor and Neil Raine (full game) Mayhem in Monsterland (demo) Font Editor 3: program that allows you to change the characters on your C64 (utility)
