Star Maze is a space-themed educational game teaching division. Thid lives on the planet Mathid. The object of the game is to navigate a maze, and getting as many points as possible, while getting home within a time limit. The maze contains a number of stars, and whenever the player moves on a star, they must answer a division-related math question. If the player answers the question incorrectly, they are able to move on. The maze contains Goodid stars which act to increase the time remaining, while Badid stars move the player further from their goal. Game has 3 difficulty levels with differing mathematics problems.
