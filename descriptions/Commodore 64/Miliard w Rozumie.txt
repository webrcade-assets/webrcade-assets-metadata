Miliard w Rozumie is a puzzle-board game for two, loosely based on the TV show. The task of the player requires answers to 15 questions, sorted in 12 possible categories, each containing 3 subcategories. Each question allows to select one of four answers. The correct answer is rewarded with 1 point, bad one gives a point to computer. The final screen shows the result of answers and points rewarded.
