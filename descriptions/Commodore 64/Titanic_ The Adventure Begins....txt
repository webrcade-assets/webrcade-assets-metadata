Titanic: The Adventure Begins ... is a single player text adventure with graphics. The game is in three parts. The first part involves the player in raising sponsorship money for the expedition and then equipping the expedition, the second is locating the wreck and the third is searching the wreck for lost gold. The game claims there are over four hundred rooms on the Titanic to be searched and that the location of the wreck and the room containing the gold changes each time the game is played.
