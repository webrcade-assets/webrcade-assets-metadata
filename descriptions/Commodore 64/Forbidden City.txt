Forbidden City is a text adventure for multiple systems. There is a long deserted alien city on a far away planet in which, legend has it, a vast treasure is hidden.  Many have attempted to find it, but none have returned.  The player must explore the city avoiding traps and other hidden dangers.  Numerous puzzles must be solved.  A help command is available at any time. All commands are via VERB NOUN entry.  There is a save/restore feature.
