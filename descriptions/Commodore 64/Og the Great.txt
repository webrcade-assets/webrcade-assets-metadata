You are Og, a noble adventurer who has fought many a battle and won with your determination, skill and some luck. You are known across the land with great wealth and fame.....then you wake up and it's all a dream. You have started out as an adventurer as an unknown with no money, so you have decided that this adventure will be huge. You are going to find and rob Death himself, seeing as he is able to put two coins on the eyes of every dead person he encounters. As you set off, you realise that a couple of things are needed.... Og the Great is a text adventure where the screen has text describing your location and you type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
