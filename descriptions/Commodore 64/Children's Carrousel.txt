Children's Carrousel is a series of nine games for early learners. Alphabet Alert: Type the missing letter in the sequence. Alphabet Song: Displays the letters of the English alphabet to music. Color Catch: Remember the color displayed on the screen. Counting Challenge: Type the missing letter in the blank. Letter Invaders: Type the letters on the screen to shoot them. Same or Different: Two objects are displayed, and the player selects same or different Shape Matching: Match shapes from a list. Shooting Stars: Stars appears as keyboard buttons are pressed. Typewriter: Type letters on the screen.
