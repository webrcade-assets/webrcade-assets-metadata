Wizard of Words contains a number of fantasy themed word games. There are 5 games in total with 8 levels of difficulty with each challenge: Castle Capers: a Hangman variant, where the player attempts to guess a word before a gate slams shut. Dragon's Spell: 1 - 2 player game where the players try to make as many smaller words as possible from the letters of a larger word. Herald's Hark: try to guess a 3 - 5 letter word. For each word, the player is told how many letters are correct. Jester's Jumble: unscramble the displayed word. There are 8 levels of difficulty. Word Spinning: players take turns filling words into a crossword, and are awarded one points for each correct letter.
