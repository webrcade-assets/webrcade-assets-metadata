Commodore Format Power Pack 34 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 34 (July 1993) contains:* Arac (Full Game) Corya (Part 3) (Full Game) Shellshock (Full Game) Invision: Unreleased game created with PCS (Full Game) 3 Sprite Tutorials: Demos that are part of the feature found on p19 called 'Do the Sprite Thing' (Utility)
