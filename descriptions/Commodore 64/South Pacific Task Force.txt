South Pacific Task Force is a 1-player wargame for the Commodore 64. You are in control of the White Fleet. Your job is to liberate a South Pacific island that has been captured by the Black Fleet. In order to win the encounter, the player must place a number of landing ships, depending on the difficulty level, onto the island. The player selects and issues orders for individual ships, and each vessel has a course, speed, damage rating. The player controls aircraft carriers, patrol aircraft, bomber aircraft, destroyers, battleships, patrol ships, and landing ships.
