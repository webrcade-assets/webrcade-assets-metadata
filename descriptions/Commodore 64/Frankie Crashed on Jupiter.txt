Frankie Crashed on Jupiter is a text adventure with graphics for the Commodore 64. Frankie has crashed his rocket on one of the moons of Jupiter. The player must gather supplies and build a homemade spacesuit to escape from their crashed ship, and then carefully navigate the moon's dangerous surface to read a nearby city All locations are graphically displayed. All commands are via VERB/NOUN entry, and there is no save/restore feature.
