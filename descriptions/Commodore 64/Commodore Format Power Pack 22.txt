Commodore Format Power Pack 22 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 22 (July 1992) contains:* Hover Bovver (Full Game) Agent Orange (Full Game) James Pond II: Codename Robocod (Playable Demo) DJ Puff's Volcanic Caper (Playable Demo)
