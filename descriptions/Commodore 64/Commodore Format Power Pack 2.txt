Commodore Format Power Pack 2 is a collection of games and demos found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. On Issue 2 (November 1990) it contained the following: Full game of Pig Tales (previously released as Oink!) A demo of the game Lords of Chaos with a playable level called Escape from Zol Full game of Empire Full game of The Blob, a Pac-Man clone
