Alphabet Circus is an animal-themed language learning game for the Apple II. Contains six mini-games designed to learn the alphabet: Meet the Circus - The player reviews the letters of the alphabet, and sees a graphic associated with each letter Alphabet Parade - The letters of the alphabet are displayed in order, and the player must input the next letter Lost Letter - The player must select the correct letter for the image displayed Secret Letter - The player selects a letter of the alphabet, and is told whether the hidden letter comes before or after this Juggler - The player must select the correct letter while a juggler juggles it, or else the letter is dropped Marquee Maker - The player can type what they wish
