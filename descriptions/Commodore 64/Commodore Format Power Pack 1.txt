Commodore Format Power Pack 1 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 1 (October 1990) contains:* Revolution (Full Game, a previously unreleased top view shooter by Simon Pick) Tau Ceti (Full Game) Rebel (Full Game) Ivan 'Ironman' Stewart's Super Off Road (Playable Demo) St. Dragon (Playable Demo)
