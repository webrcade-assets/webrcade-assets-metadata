Samochodowy Wojownik is an arcade racing game with shooter elements. You are the driver of the police combat car - Ferrari 1200 DRX, struggling with a gang of smugglers in the suburbs. During the patrol, you will encounter the vehicles of the criminals and the criminals themselves who will try to eliminate the threat. The car can move in any direction. A collision with a street border, another vehicle and a train, as well as an accurate shot of the opponent deprives the player of life. The shells in the vehicle are not limited.
