The World's Greatest Football Game is an American football strategy game. A 2-player competitive strategy game, players can design their own individual plays to compete against the opposing team. Plays can be saved on a strategy disk, and successful plays can be reused. Gameplay follows standard American football rules. During each play, the players watch the animated action on the screen from a top-down perspective, and can control 1 - 2 key players during the action phase. Includes an interactive timer and scoreboard, and the player can define the length of each quarter.
