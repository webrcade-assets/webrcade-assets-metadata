Commodore Format Power Pack 16 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 16 (January 1992) had two cassettes which contained: Tape One The Graphic Adventure Creator (Utility) Tape Two Mission Impossabubble (Full Game) Head the Ball (Full Game) First Samurai (Playable Demo) Creatures II (Playable Demo)
