Houdini Escape is a 1-player number guessing game for the Commodore 64. The player must help Houdini escape from a series of 3 death traps, with 2, 3, or 4 locks respectively. To solve a lock, the player must guess a number between 1 and 75. Each guess, the player is told whether the number is too high or too low. The player has 60 seconds to solve all the locks, or they succumb to their trap.
