Adventure Pack 1 is a series of 3 text adventure games for multiple systems Each game uses brief VERB NOUN entries. There is no save or restore feature, and the player has a limited number of turns to complete their tasks. Big Bad Wolf: The Big Bad Wolf is approaching. You must prepare yourself by locking the front door and getting a pot of boiling oil ready. Computer Adventure: The player has to find money, go to their locak computer store, buy a game, and start the game at home. Moonbase Alpha: Save Moonbase Alpha from an approaching comet by arming and firing a missile.
