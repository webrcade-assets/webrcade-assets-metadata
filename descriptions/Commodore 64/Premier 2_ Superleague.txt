Premier 2: Superleague is a standard football management game, in which you take charge of a team in the 16 team super-league and aim for league success. You can choose your team and substitutes for each match, with injuries and suspensions to factor in as the season goes on. You can use the substitutes at half-time or later in the matches (which last for 90 seconds of real -time). Between matches you can buy and sell players, each of which have ratings up to 9 for ability and form.
