Who Dun-It is a text adventure for the Commodore 64. You are a down on his luck detective, who just got called to investigate a robbery at the Carr estate. The lights went out during a party to show off the new necklace, and when they came back on, the necklace was gone. The player must explore the Carr estate, and look for clues as to who stole the necklace and where it was hidden. The player can talk to the party guests, and explore the different rooms of the estate. After determining the culprit, the player must return to the paddywagon and say "case closed". All commands are via VERB NOUN entry. There is a save/restore feature available.
