Commodore Format Power Pack 24 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 24 (September 1992) contains:* Famous Five (Full Game) Cool Croc Twins (Playable Demo) Match of the Day (Playable Demo) Ugh! (Playable Demo) Fuzzball: Unreleased game from System 3 (Playable Demo)
