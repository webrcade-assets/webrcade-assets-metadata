Stickybear Opposites is a learning game for young children. Based upon the Stickybear series of games for children, this time Stickybear is helping children learn opposites. Players are presented with graphical vignettes that feature concepts such as "near" and "far", "open" and "closed", or "tall" and "short". The player can switch between the opposites, and can also choose to progress to the next concept as they please.
