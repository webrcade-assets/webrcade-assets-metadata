Pharaoh's Curse is a text adventure for multiple systems. There is a long forgotten valley in Egypt, where legend has it, a vast treasure is hidden.  Many have attempted to find it, but none have returned.  The player explores the many rooms of the abandoned Egyptian tombs.  Numerous traps and dangers must be avoided.  There is a help command available. All commands are via VERB NOUN entry, and there is a save/restore feature.
