Supercup Football is a soccer game. It is action-oriented and shown from a top-down view. The player participates in a league with 10 teams overall. Regarding controls, the game offers the usual mix between kicking, running, and tackling. One specialty is that there is a special move to perform a curling cross. The player automatically takes control of the goalkeeper when the opponent enters the penalty area. During the match, the colour of the field grass can be changed on the fly.
