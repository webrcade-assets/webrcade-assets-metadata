Tink!Tonk!: Tinka's Mazes is an addition learning game. Tinka has woken up in the morning, and decided to go to school. There are lots of things she can see and do to help her learn how to add. Before going to school, she can explore several simple mazes, where she must answer addition questions to proceed. After arriving at school, there are 3 games available to her: Field Trip: the player can go on a fieldtrip to see a number of animals including: elephants, mice, bees, crabs, or fish. The player counts groups of animals. Quiz: answer math questions for the teacher Maze Fun: navigate a small maze for fun
