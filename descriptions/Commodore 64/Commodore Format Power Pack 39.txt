Commodore Format Power Pack 39 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 39 (December 1993) contains:* Freddy Hardest: Part Two (full game) Deep Strike (full game) Cops and Robbers (full game) The Curse of Volcan: Unreleased game from reader Richard Thompson (full game)
