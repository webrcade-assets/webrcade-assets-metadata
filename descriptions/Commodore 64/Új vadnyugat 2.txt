New Wild West 2 is a Hungarian specific text adventure game. The adventures of the first game continue. The newly populated Moon is much like the Wild West, and the Moon Mafia wants your property. Last time, you have escaped from them with via a space liferaft that crashed back into the Moon. The text descriptions about your surroundings are also visualized with simple images - plus there are some occasional musical cues. A save-load feature is present. The Hungarian character set is supported by the parser.
