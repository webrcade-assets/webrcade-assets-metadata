Percentage Panic is a 1-player educational math and arcade game for multiple systems. You are the admiral of Star Fleet #7. Your mission is to successfully get your fleet of 15 ships through to the mother ship Percentius. During the first stage of gameplay, the player answers 15 math questions. There are 3 levels of difficulty, and 9 types of math questions involving percentages. Questions can be timed or untimed. Every correct answer gives the player 1 more ship during the second stage of gameplay. The player then enters an arcade sequence, where they move a spaceship vertically between 4 lanes of space traffic while avoiding alien vessels. Ships must survive a length of time based upon difficulty to successfully reach Percentius. There are 3 levels of difficulty.
