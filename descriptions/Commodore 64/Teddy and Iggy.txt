Teddy and Iggy is a memory game for young children. It contains three mini-games:* Iggy's Boxes: Iggy the caterpillar opens 5 boxes to reveal prizes. The player must remember which order they were opened. Teddy's Bed: the player watches Teddy make his bed, and then must chose the order it was made. Teddy Shapes Up: Teddy stack three shapes, and the player must show which order they were stacked.
