Commodore Format Power Pack 36 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 36 (September 1993) contains:* Starray (Full Game) Squibbly Skwob's Big Day Out: An unreleased game from reader, Paul C Cardno (Full Game) Surburban Commando (Playable Demo) Breakthrough: Demo of an unreleased game (Playable Demo) Autorun & Flashload: Two tape loaders (Utility) Quick Start: A program that allows eight sprites on screen at the same time (Utility)
