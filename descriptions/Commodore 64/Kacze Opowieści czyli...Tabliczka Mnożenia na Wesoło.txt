Kacze Opowieści czyli...Tabliczka Mnożenia na Wesoło is an educational game based on the Duck Tales franchise for multiplication tablet teaching. The action takes place in the castle of cruel Count Mat, who imprisoned sister of Huey, Dewey, and Louie. Moving between the chambers requires solving mathematical tasks. A bad answer takes one life. At the end of each level the player will find an elevator that will transport him on the floor above. A white key activates the elevator. Ten collected items is awarded with an extra life.
