Bandit is a 1 player gambling game for multiple systems. Contains 2 slot machines: Kings Ransom and Silver Knight.  The player starts with $9 - $200.  The player can feed up to $3 into the Kings Ransom machine, and can only win on a single line.  Silver Knight allows the player to feed up to $5 into the machine, and the player can win on 5 separate lines.  The player must individually feed in coins and pull the handle on the machine.  The game is over when all money is used up.
