In this spoof of mystery stories, you play the role of Sammy Marlo, Private Eye. A room full of priceless paintings have been stolen from a local museum and it's up to you to find out who took them and track the thief down. Along the way you'll run into a crooked used electronics shop owner, a crazed bus driver and a yodeling cheese shop owner among other things. This game is a text adventure created with the Loadstar Quest Writer system.
