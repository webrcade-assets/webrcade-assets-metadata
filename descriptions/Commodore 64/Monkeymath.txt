Monkeymath is an educational math game for multiple systems. The player controls a monkey who works on an assembly line. They must punch the correct number fro 0 - 9 as it rapidly scrolls past to answer mathematical questions shown below. Allows for addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and counting questions of multiple difficulties. The monkey has a limited shift to work, and tries to maximize his score. Half way through the shift the monkey eats a banana for his lunch break.
