Tempo Typen is an educational program to learn ten finger blind typing. The course consists of seven lessons. The lessons are as follows: Lesson 1: The middle row of the keyboard Lesson 2: The top row keys Lesson 3: The bottom row keys Lesson 4: Numbers Lesson 5: Words Lesson 6: Sentences Lesson 7: Texts In the game, you can put the course into practice by typing the words you see on the screen as quickly as possible before it is destroyed by the laser beam. If they are hit by the laser beam, you lose energy until everything runs out and then you have to start all over again. There are nine levels. The MSX version of this game has eight levels.
