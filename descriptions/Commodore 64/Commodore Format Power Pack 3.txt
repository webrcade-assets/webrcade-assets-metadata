Commodore Format Power Pack 3 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 3 (December 1990) contains:* Gutz (full game) Split Personalities (full game) Badlands (playable demo) Midnight Resistance (playable demo) Spiderman (playable demo especially written for Commodore Format) Video art package Vidcom 64
