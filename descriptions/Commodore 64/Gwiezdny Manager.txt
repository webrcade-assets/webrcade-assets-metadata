Gwiezdny Manager is a turn based space strategy. The player takes on the character of the administrator of one of a planet. The task is to ensure the development of infrastructure, production, mining, agriculture and combat fleet, population growth and trade relations. Starting from one planet the player may discover and colonize or conquer the entire star system. The materials are divided into two groups - food and minerals. Minerals are required for production and buildings a weaponry. The player may trade via financial and commodity exchanges. The materials may be acquired by harvest, trade or facility's production. Population development requires the construction of hospitals, police, schools, cinemas, housing estates, steelworks, power plants, banks or military forces. Growing requires the purchase of grain and land, employment of workers, sowing and harvesting of crops. Each round ends with a summary of changes, information about needs and random events.
