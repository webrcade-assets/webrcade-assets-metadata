Commodore Format Power Pack 31 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 31 (April 1993) contains:* Subterranea (Full Game) Cauldron 2 (Full Game) Monster Mash: Unreleased game created with SUECK (Full Game) Arnie 2 (Playable Demo) Snare (Playable Demo)
