Tink!Tonk!: Tink's Subtraction Fair is a 1-player math game for multiple systems. Tink has decided to visit the subtraction fair. He has 22 tonkers that he can spend on the games there. Before he can play a game, he must tell the clerk how many tonkers he has, and how many he will have left after playing the game. If Tink is successful at the game, he can win bleepers. Bleepers can be spent at the arcade or moonwalk for an arcade challenge that allows the player to win a number of tonkers that can be spent on more games. There is diminishing returns, and eventually the player runs out of credits, and can return home. Subtraction games include: Ring The Bell: Answer subtraction questions to rink the bell and win bleepers. Witches' Brew: Answer subtraction questions to help a witch brew a potion. Magic Show: Answer subtraction questions to help monsters disappear. Dunk Gork: Answer subtraction questions to dunk the Gork and win bleepers. Bear Dare: The player compete removing 1 - 3 bears from a starting group of 11. The player who takes the last bear loses. There are two games to win tonkers: Moonwalk: Help Tink travel to the right of the screen screen while avoiding the chasing alien. Craters can help the player dodge their opponent. Arcade: Memorize the order that 5 toys fire, and correctly repeat it. At the end of the day, Tink can return home and watch fireworks.
