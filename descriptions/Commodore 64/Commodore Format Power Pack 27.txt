Commodore Format Power Pack 27 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 27 (December 1992) contains:* Deflektor (Full Game) Alternative World Games (Full Game) The Muncher (Full Game) Sceptre of Baghdad (Playable Demo) Loadmaster: A head alignment program for the Datassette (Utility)
