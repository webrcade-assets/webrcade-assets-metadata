You are Jason, leader of the Argonauts and your quest is to find the Golden Fleece so that you can remove Pelias from the throne after he stole it from your Father Aeson when he died. Setting sail you have encountered a fierce storm which has beached your ship on an island, but to make matters worse, killed the Argonauts. Setting off alone on the beach, you must encounter many creatures on the island as you try to find the fleece. Jason of the Argonauts is a text adventure based on the famous Greek mythological hero where the screen has text describing your location. You type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
