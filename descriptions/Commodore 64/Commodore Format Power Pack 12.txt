Commodore Format Power Pack 12 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 12 (September 1991) contains:* Spindizzy (Full Game) Speedball 2 (Playable Demo) Rodland (Playable Demo) 3D Construction Kit (Playable Demo) P. P. Hammer (Playable Demo) CF Show (Demo to demonstrate Public Domain software)
