Ucieczka ze Spejs-Szipu is an illustrated science fiction text adventure game. The game takes place on a spaceship prison. The player begins the adventure in a cell and his goal is to escape from the prison. The text in the game is supplemented by graphics. The gameplay is branching narratives - after reading a long descriptive texts the player is given two alternatives in the form of direct questions how to act in the current situation. The game contains some vulgar and sex offending language and pictures, therefore it is not advisable for young players.
