Factactics is a trivia board game. 1-player versus the computer or 2 players answer trivia questions in order to move their playing piece around a rectangular board. A question appears on the board, and each player has 10 seconds to ring in. The player must type in the answer, and If the player answers correctly, their score is based upon the number of seconds remaining when they rung in. If nobody answers in the first 10 seconds, an anagram of the correct answer is displaced, and an additional 10 second timer is added for players to answer correctly. Trivia topics include: movies, music potpouri, rock & roll, sports, television, Americana, or a mix of all topics. Players can choose to have additional tactical squares placed on the board.
