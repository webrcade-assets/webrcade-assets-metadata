One night Kate wakes up to find that all her toys have come alive.So she dons her bike helmet and off she goes to save them all! Bounce through 12 levels, flip the right switches, and help Kate rescue 6 of her cuddly friends Counterweight Kate is a platform puzzle game where the player takes on the role of Kate who has to rescue six of her toys using well timed dodging and jumping to the top of the full screen scrolling game area. Every level contains a puzzle that will need to solved to be able to reach Kate's toy.
