Burmistrz III is a management game, where the player as a city mayor must carry out activities resulting in a settlement of 10,000 residents. The game starts having $ 2.5 million, 100 people, 2 houses and a shop. One house can accommodate up to 50 residents. The game features following infrastructure: House, Shop, Bazaar, Factory, Glass, Stadium, Police, Circus, Zoo, Casino, Sex Shop. Each object attracts residents and generates revenues. At the end of the turn, the player receives information about the number of deaths, revenues, expenses and new citizens.
