Weather Tamers is a 1 - 4 player educational game for the Commodore 64. The player is given a map of the United States and Southern Canada, and current atmospheric weather conditions. The player can chose to start during any time of the year. The player has limited credits, which are used to manipulate atmospheric conditions, including the location of high and low pressure zones, as well as the temperature, humidity, and pressure in specific zones. Each day, the player receives calls from clients asking for specific weather conditions over the next few days. The player must manipulate the weather for the clients, and receives additional credits if they achieve the correct weather. There are 3 levels of difficulty, games can be 8, 16, or 30 days, and there can be 1 - 3 clients per rounds.
