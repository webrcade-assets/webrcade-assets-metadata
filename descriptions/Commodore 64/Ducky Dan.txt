In Ducky Dan, the player controls Ducky Dan flying in the bubble to the heavens to rescue his beloved female duck from the clutches of evil humanoids. Break through the five levels shooting enemies with bubbles, avoiding their attacks, while collecting other bubbles. During the perilous travel in scrolling mode, the enemy medusa, coyotes, and various kinds of birds and creatures will try to eliminate the player. Eliminate opponents and gain scores. At the end of each level the different kinds of bosses appear.
