Fortuna kołem się toczy is a game based on the TV show Wheel of Fortune. The players take turns guessing letters to form a word or a saying. The game can be played with up to three human players. During their turn the players can spin the wheel, guess a consonant, buy a vowel or guess the password. Each turn lasts 10 seconds. In the final, after drawing an envelope and providing additional letters, the player has 20 seconds to guess the password.
