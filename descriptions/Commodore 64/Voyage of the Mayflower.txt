Voyage of the Mayflower is a 1-player strategy game for the Commodore 64. The player must retrace the original route of The Pilgrims from Plymouth, England to the New World. At the start of he game, the player must choose their supplies for the journey. On easier levels, they only need to choose food and water, but on higher levels, they must choose food (preserved and fresh), water, spirits, animals, animal feed, and more. During the trip, the player must choose their ship heading, while navigating with winds, and on higher levels the prevailing currents. The player must set their supply levels, and can view their progress on a map of the Atlantic. Bad weather can slow the voyage. The player must have sufficient supplies to start a new colony upon their arrival.
