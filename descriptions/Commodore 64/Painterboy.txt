Painterboy is a game that is advertising Tikkurila paints. The main characters in the game are the Father and the Son, who is known from the old TV ad which the company showed on television in the 80's. The player must first choose a paint that is suitable for the house which is being painting. Once a suitable paint has been selected, the player has to drive to the house and begin to paint it. On the way to the house the player needs to be careful not to get in a car accident. When the house is painted, the player can go back home and to select a new task.
