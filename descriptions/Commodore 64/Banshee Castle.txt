Banshee Castle is a text adventure for multiple systems. The player has been captured by banshees within a castle. The player must find and free the princess hidden within the castle, and escape to safety. The player has limited health, and can be injured by banshee attacks and roaming jesters. A wandering monk can heal the player. The player must gather items and solve inventory puzzles. All commands are via VERB NOUN entry. There is a save/restore feature.
