Commodore Format Power Pack 40 is a collection of games, demos and utilities found attached to the cover of the monthly Commodore 64 magazine Commodore Format in Cassette form. Issue 40 (January 1994) contains:* Jocky Wilson's Darts (full game) IO (full game) Monster Mash Editor: A program that allows you to create sprites (utility) InterWord: A word processor (utility)
