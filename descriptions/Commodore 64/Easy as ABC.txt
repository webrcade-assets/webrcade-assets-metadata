Easy as ABC is a series of learning games for young children. It contains five games: Dot to Dot: the player chooses 6 letters in alphabetical order from a scrambled list, and place them in space. A rocket ship leaves the Earth, follows the letters, and lands on the moon. Honey Hunt: the player must move a honey bee to the flower that has the matching capital and small letter. Leapfrog: three frogs sit on a lily-pad, with letters on the frogs on the left and right. The player must choose a letter from a list to complete the letter series. Lunar Letters: the player must select the mixed letters in space in alphabetical order Match Letters: the player must choose scrambled letters to spell the word on the screen. As a reward, they receive a picture of word.
