The ultimate collection of a various official (and some not so official) releases of one of the most popular games in history. Featuring eight different game types (Pac-Man, Puck-Man, Ultra Pac-Man, Random Mazes Pac-Man, Hangly Man, Ms. Pac-Man, Ms. Pac-Attack, Ms. Random Mazes Pac-Man) and two modes (Plus and Fast) that add increased difficultly, speed, maze variation and bonuses.
