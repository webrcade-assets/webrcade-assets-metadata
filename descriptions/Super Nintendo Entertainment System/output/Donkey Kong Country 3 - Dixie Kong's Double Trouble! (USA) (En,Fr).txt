Donkey Kong Country 3 was released for the SNES in 1996 as the 3rd installment in the Donkey Kong Country Series. 
Dixie Kong and her cousin Kiddy Kong have to find Donkey Kong & Diddy Kong, who went missing on a fishing trip. Meanwhile, the Kremling Krew returns under the leadership of the mysterious robot KAOS, which is secretly controlled by the notorious villian King K. Rool aka K. Roolenstein. Once more various loyal animal friends and Kong family members stand by your side to find the missing Kongs - check out dusty mills, deciduous forests and snowy mountains, explore high cliffs, waterfalls & beautiful riverside areas!
