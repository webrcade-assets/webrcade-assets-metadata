Obsessed with the desire for wealth, King Magridd cut a dastardly deal with the ruler of evil: one gold piece for each soul in the kingdom. Now the Freil Empire is in shambles and you are sent by the Master to save it. Free the kingdom's captive souls. Teach the King a thing or two about integrity. Rescue a beautiful maiden. Liberate an imprisoned inventor.
