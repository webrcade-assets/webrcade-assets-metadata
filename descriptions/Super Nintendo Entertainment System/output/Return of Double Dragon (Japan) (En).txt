Return of Double Dragon (?), is a side-scrolling beat-'em-up released for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System in 1992. It was published by Techns Japan in Japan and by Tradewest in North America and the PAL region. Return of Double Dragon is the fourth console game in the Double Dragon series developed by Techns Japan, following previous titles for the NES, although unlike the NES games, Return of Double Dragon is an original title and not based on any preceding arcade version.
