Hokuto no Ken 7 : Seiken Retsuden Denshsha e no Michi (  ?, Fist of the North Star 7: History of the Sacred Fist - The Way of the Successor) is the third and final Hokuto no Ken video game for the Super Famicom in Japan, released on December 24, 1993. It is a fighting game similar to the previous installment, Hokuto no Ken 6, which changes to the game mechanics and character roster.
