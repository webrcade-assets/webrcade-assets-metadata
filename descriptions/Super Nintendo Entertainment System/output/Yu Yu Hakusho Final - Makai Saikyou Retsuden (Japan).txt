The fourth game to be released for the SNES, however in Japan only.
This game is an anime figher game with 13 Characters are available (Yusuke, Kurama, Kuwabara, Hiei, Sensui, Super Sensui, Shigure, Shura, Demon Yusuke, Demon Fox Kurama, Raizen, Yomi, Mukuro) and one extra hidden character Yakumo (The main antagonist in "Yu Yu Hakusho the Movie: Poltergeist Report") can be unlocked by entering a secret code. 
Special moves are used with the special moves bar, to charge it, hold one of the 4 action buttons.
