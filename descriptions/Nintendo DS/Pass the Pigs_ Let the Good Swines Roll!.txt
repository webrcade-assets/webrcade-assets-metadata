A Big Pig Adventure! Visit pig-tastic landmarks and silly landings around the globe on your quest to Pass the Pigs before you Pig Out and lose all your points. Rack up 100 points by Pig Rolling using the stylus. Increase your chances of throwing a Snouter or Trotter by upgrading your pig with an Iron Snout or a Pig In Boots. Earn fame and fortune in the World Tour mode as your travel around the world
