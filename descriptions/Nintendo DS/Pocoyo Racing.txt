Pocoyó Racing is a child-oriented racing game with characters from the Spanish animation series Pocoyó. The main characters include Pocoyó (a curious child who loves to learn new things), Pato (a duck who is Pocoyo's best friend), Elly (a pink female elephant), Lula (Pocoyo's pet dog) and Pajaroto (a sleepyhead bird).

The game follows the typical kart racing games mechanics like those of the Mario Kart series, where you race with different characters around a circuit and collect items with special effects in order to win the race.
