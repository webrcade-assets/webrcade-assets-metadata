Power Pro Kun Pocket 8 (パワプロクン ポケット 8 Pawa Puro Kun Pocket 8?) is a sports game developed and published by Konami for the Nintendo DS handheld system. It is the eighth installment in the Power Pro Kun Pocket series, which in turn is a spinoff of the Power Pros series. The game was released on December 1, 2005, exclusively in Japan.
