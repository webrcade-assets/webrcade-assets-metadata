World Snooker Championship: Season 2007-08 brings snooker to the Nintendo DS. In a Championship mode you can play 9 different tournaments against 96 professional players such as Davis, Higgins and Mark Selby. Challenge mode allows you to play challenges that you can unlock in the Championship mode and Quick Match allows you to play a single game against a random opponent. Finally Friendly mode allows a single game against an opponent of your choice.

Multiplayer mode is provided in hot-seat, but not through wireless play.
