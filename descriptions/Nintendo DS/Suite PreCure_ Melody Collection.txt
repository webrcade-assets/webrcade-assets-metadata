Suite PreCure: Melody Collection is a rhythm-based game set in the Suite PreCure anime universe. Players take on the roles of characters from the series, participating in musical mini-games that involve timing-based button presses. The game features various songs from the anime, with players required to hit notes in sync with the music to achieve high scores.

The gameplay consists of multiple stages, each with its own song and difficulty level. Players can choose their favorite characters and unlock new content as they progress through the game. The objective is to complete songs with the highest accuracy possible, earning points and unlocking additional tracks and costumes for the characters.
