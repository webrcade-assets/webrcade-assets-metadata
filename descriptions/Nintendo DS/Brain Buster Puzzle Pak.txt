Brain Buster Puzzle Pack features hundreds of puzzles ranging from the hugely popular Sudoku to the latest craze in Japan, Kakuro. Also included are the uniquely challenging and innovative games Light Up, Nurikabe, and Slitherlink. These puzzles have all been created in conjunction with Nikoli, the leading puzzle creators in Japan.

Along with the straightforward puzzles in Brain Buster Puzzle Pack, there will be unlockable bonuses. For example, various monsters and asteroids distract you as you work on puzzle, but by removing them you earn special hints, adding to the fun factor. You can also unlock background graphics.

• Hundreds of different puzzles to play through! Five puzzle games—Sudoku, Kakuro, Light Up, Nurikabe, Slitherlink
• Randomly generated puzzles in the training center creates infinite playing time
• High score leaderboard so players can try to beat their best times.
• There is a board puzzle that rewards wallpaper graphics to change the look
• Special color therapy mode that features bonus fortune-telling
