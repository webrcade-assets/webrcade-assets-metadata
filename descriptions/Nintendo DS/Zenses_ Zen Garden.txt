Zenses: Zen Garden was released on October 31, 2009. It was developed by Shin'en and published by Game Factory for NDS.
Escape the world around you. Zenses is active relaxation and the perfect "me time" companion, allowing the player to transcend the stresses of everyday life, unplug and experience an "active escape." Zenses – Zen Garden is the third title in a series of relaxing puzzle games for women that enjoy a little spa-like escapism when they’re at home or on the go.
Building on popular features of previous titles, Zenses Ocean and Zenses Rainforest, Zenses -- Zen Garden combines the best in game-play from puzzle and brain-training genres with a clean, fresh and stylish design. The new game, featuring beautiful Zen garden-inspired aesthetics such as stones, sand and gravel includes six puzzle games, two activity games and a relaxation area. A soothing, ambient soundtrack also compliments the game’s calm and relaxing atmosphere.
