Gamebook DS: Aquarian Age: Perpetual Period focuses on a story set in the Aquarian Age universe, a world from a Japanese trading card game by Broccoli. It follows Kokona Suzushiro, a high school girl who gets caught up in mysterious fires and a murder case tied to supernatural powers. The setting mixes modern Japan with secret factions fighting for control, a core theme of the Aquarian Age franchise.

Gaming Mechanism:

Players read through a branching story and make choices at key points to shape the outcome. The game uses text, character art, and voice acting by well-known Japanese actors to tell the story. Different paths lead to multiple endings, based on player decisions. There’s no action or puzzles—just reading and picking options to move the plot forward.
