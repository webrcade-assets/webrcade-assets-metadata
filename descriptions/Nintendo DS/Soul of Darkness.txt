Enter a fascinating gothic world as Kale, a mysterious, powerful man. Try to save your beloved's life and soul and get her out of an evil vampire's clutches. Explore dark woods and caves, huge castles and cathedrals under the full moon's light. Master and upgrade your sword and spear, and learn how to control your powers: use the Fire and the Ice, or transform into your enemies. Face and defeat werewolves, gargoyles, zombies, and more, with a customized hero thanks to the exclusive camera features. Good or evil - be the sole master of your destiny!
