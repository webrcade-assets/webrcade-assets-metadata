The most popular talking elephant in the world lives in the Neustädter Zoo. For over 30 years, Benjamin Blümchen has been inspiring children with his loveable stories and adventures. This time, the fans can accompany Benjamin and Otto on their morning tour in the zoo and not only discover the animals but also actively support the two when help is needed. So they help for example as when repairing the igloos of penguins or animate the kangaroo for morning exercise. In addition, even warden Karl has birthday, for urgent need to prepare a few more gifts ...
