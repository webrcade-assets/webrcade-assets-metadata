It’s all in the beat.

The Diva Girls are heading to the bright lights of Fiesta City ready to bust some moves and enter the greatest dance competition in the world.

It’s not going to be easy though and along the way they must face-off against B-boys, bullies, princesses and showgirls.

If they make it through then the girls must take on the hardest challenge of all and compete against each other –will their friendship survive?
