For the second time, Lillifee's magical world comes to the Nintendo DSTM. There are many new surprises and games to discover! Come with me to Lillifee's castle garden and discover magical secrets and fun games with her. Lillifee can take a cloud ride with the unicorn Rosalie or make and record music with a small band. In the forest clinic, she examines and treats her little friends or has an exciting race - under water!

Features:
• Nintendo DS-exclusive touchscreen and microphone controls
• High-quality voice output, so no reading skills required
• 10 different games
• With fairy secrets to collect and trade.
