"Winnie the Pooh: Kuma no Puu-san: 100 Acre no Mori no Cooking Book" is a Japanese exclusive for the Nintendo DS. The game takes place in the Hundred Acre Wood, where players join Winnie the Pooh and friends in various cooking activities. The story revolves around collecting ingredients and following recipes to prepare different dishes.

The game features cooking-based mini-games where players use the stylus to perform tasks such as chopping, stirring, and mixing ingredients. Players interact with beloved characters and complete recipes, enhancing their cooking skills through engaging and educational gameplay.
