Kimi ni Todoke: Tsutaeru Kimochi 「君に届け ～伝えるキモチ～ meaning "communicating feelings"」 is the second game for Kimi ni Todoke. The story is a continuation of the previous game's Kazehaya route, and follows Sawako in her second year of high school.[1] The story changes depending on the "heart" the player chooses. This game also features more characters.[3]
