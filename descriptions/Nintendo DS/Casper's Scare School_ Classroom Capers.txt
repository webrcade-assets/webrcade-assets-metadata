The clock strikes midnight, the school bell rings and a new term begins at the creepiest school you've ever seen - Scare School! And today there's a new student in class - Casper the Friendly Ghost! Can you help Casper and his friends Ra and Mantha graduate with flying colors?
Take part in Science, History and Gym classes in 24 levels of spook-tacular fun!
Dodge getting caught by the terrifying teachers and the vampire bully, Thatch!
Use your Nintendo DS Stylus to control Casper, collect special items and even scare teachers!
