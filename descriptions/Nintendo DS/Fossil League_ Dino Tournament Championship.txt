BATTLE OF THE BEASTS



Jump into a time traveling adventure to help Taiga recover his famous fossil from the sneaky Syndicate X! Explore prehistoric eras, battle and capture over 100 dinosaurs to compete in the legendary fossil league finals! Use the touch screen to battle, collect and use items, and add dinosaurs to your team!

Features

•Choose your attack from 5 elements like fire!•Dig up fossils using the touch screen!•Look up dinosaurs using the 3D dinopedia
