Make a difference at your very own Paws & Claws Marine Rescue as you diagnose, treat and care for injured animals. Release the healthy animals back into the wild to swim and roam freely!

Features

• Welcome and treat 7 marine animals including penguins, dolphins, seals and baby polar bears at your clinic.
• Search the sea for injured animals aboard your very own Marine Rescue boat.
• Use the tools the experts use including an x-ray machine, microscope, stethoscope and check oxygen levels.
• Care for injured animals and nurse them back to health, paying attention to each of their very special needs.
