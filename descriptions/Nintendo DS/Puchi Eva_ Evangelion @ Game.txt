Puchi Eva: Evangelion @ Game ( ぷ ち え う ゛ ぁ , Puchi Eva Evangerion atto geimu ? ) Is a strategy video game for the Nintendo DS system released in Japan on March 20, 2008 by Bandai Namco Games. The game is inspired by Petit Eva: Evangelion @ School. 

It is a game of strategy and minigames. In this peculiar universe, the entire cast of the original series are high school students in Tokyo-3. Even Evangelion Unit 01 is one of the classmates, being a human-sized robot and fulfilling the role of the classic school bully.
