Sesame Street: Cookie's Counting Carnival is a Sesame Street video game released on October 19, 2010 from Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment for the Microsoft Windows, the Wii, and Nintendo DS.

The Wii version came packaged with a Cookie Monster "gameplay helper", a soft plush cover for the Wiimote that hides unnecessary buttons. The Nintendo DS version comes with a special stylus.

There are 14 minigames in the Wii, Nintendo DS and PC versions, and 12 games in all the versions.
