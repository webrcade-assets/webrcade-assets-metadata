Furries are cute beings that enjoy rolling around in the sun all day long. But stone-cold Squaries - hating everything that is round and fluffy - attack! Now it is up to you to create chain reactions that will pop the invaders. Prove your skill in four different game modes: CLASSIC, CHALLENGE, TIME TRIAL and ENDLESS. Use the Squaries' special abilities like gravity, air blast and split to plan your blasts for maximum efficiency!
