Crayon Shin-Chan: Obaka Dainin Den: Susume! Kasukabe Ninja Tai! is an action adventure game where players follow the antics of Shinnosuke Nohara (Shin-chan) and his friends as they form the Kasukabe Ninja Squad. 

The game involves platforming, combat, and puzzle-solving, with each character having unique abilities to navigate through various levels and overcome obstacles.
