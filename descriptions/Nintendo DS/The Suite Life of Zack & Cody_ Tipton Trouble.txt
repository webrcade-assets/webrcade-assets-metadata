The Suite Life of Zack and Cody: Tipton Trouble is a 3d action platforming game featuring puzzles based on the popular Disney show. You control both twins separately, helping each other as you deal with aliens & ghosts inside the hotel. Cody shoots water balloons, bubble gum and cream pies from his vacuum. He can also hover over pools. Zack races through the corridors with his skateboard. You can also dress both characters in a variety of outfits. The game features unlockable mini-games (skateboard & sail boat racing) & two-player wireless action.
