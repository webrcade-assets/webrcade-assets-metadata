Oshare Majo Love and Berry ~DS Collection~, also known as Love and Berry: Dress Up and Dance! ~DS Collection~, is the Nintendo DS port of Oshare Majo Love and Berry. It was released on November 22, 2006.

The game was packaged with a white card reader, stickers, and 10 cards.  Sega also released a limited version of the game packaged with a pink Love and Berry DS Lite, Oshare Majo Love and Berry ~DS Song Collection~ CD, a pink card reader, stickers, and 60 cards. Some features exclusive to this release are the story mode and the ability to create your own card.
