Become a Fire-Fighting Hero!



Play as Jake, helping him respond to emergencies and complete his dangerous missions. Fight fires, drive your own fire truck, and rescue people in danger to become a city hero.

Demonstrate your fire-fighting abilities using the Nintendo DS stylus and microphone to complete fun mini-games.
Unwind hoses, extinguish fierce fires, and respond to emergency calls quickly and safely.
Make your fire truck bigger and better by adding new wheels, engines, sirens, and more.
