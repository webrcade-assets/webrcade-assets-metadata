Live the Fabulous Lifestyle of the Bratz Girlz!

Bratz Girlz Really Rock! The video game with serious style.

The Bratz are out of Stilesville and attending Camp Starshine, a summer camp for the most talented students of the performing arts! They meet Anna, a lonely ballerina who has dedicated so much of her life to dance that she has missed out on fun and friendship. When Anna has a crisis of confidence, her new friends are there to rally around her, helping her create a performance for the end of camp showcase that no one will forget!

• Rock Shows: Play the guitar, rock out on the keyboard, or show off your dance moves to a live audience! Play by yourself or with friends and show the crowd how the Bratz Girlz Really Rock!
• Recording Studio: Head to the Camp Starshine Recording Studio to learn and practice a range of rockin’ songs on Lead Guitar or Keyboard.
• New Mini-Games: Experience some killer Skydiving and fierce Snowboarding. Play by yourself or with friends in this new rockin’ game!
• Fashion Designer: Use the new Fashion Design Suite in the Bratz room to create a wider range of custom fashions than ever before. Upgrade the editor throughout the game by collecting, buying and winning a range of exciting accessories.
• Petz: Play with your pet and teach him some cool tricks using your voice commands. Race your new best friend against other animals in this totally cool mini-game!
