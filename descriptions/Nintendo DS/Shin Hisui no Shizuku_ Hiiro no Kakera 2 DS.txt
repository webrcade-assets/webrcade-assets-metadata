Hisui no Shizuku: Hiiro no Kakera 2 DS(翡翠の雫　緋色の欠片2 DS) is an otome game created by Idea Factory. It is the second installment of Hiiro no Kakera series of video games. It tell the adventure of Takachiho Suzu who was summoned back to her village when Mao, her sister figure, disappeared, to succeed the role of Tamayorihime, the miko who'd sacrifice herself to keep the land safe from the wrath of the sea lord.
