Finally! Wendy is allowed to do an internship at the horse hospital. And we with her. The dream of all young girls. What will she experience?

 Wendy_Das_Pferdehospital9Vier mini-games to master. Two of them deal with the topic of horse-hospital, the other two with the topic riding in general. First, go to the Horse Hospital, where you can pick one of four horses to examine. To do this, you use the stylus to remove the sick animal until three symptoms have been automatically detected. It is important to remember and select from several others. Once you have done this, the disease must be detected. For this, the symptoms are replaced by symbols.
