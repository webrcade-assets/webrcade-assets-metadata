Zoids Dash is an action title released for the Nintendo DS on the 15th of June, 2006. It is a mission-based platform game that allows the player to control a team of three Zoids (once unlocked, five are available to choose from: Liger Zero DASH, Salamander, Red Horn, Iron Kong and Geno Saurer). As is standard for Zoids games, parts are earned to upgrade the Zoids.
