Asterix – These Romans are Crazy!” is going to test your reflexes. This “party game”, as the Gauls now call them, will lead you through a series of adventures via a sort of snakes and ladders game comprised of 20 mini-games that take you through four different universes:

the Gaulish village

the Roman camp

the forest

the seashore

Everything it takes to thrill fans of all ages and enjoy fun with your family! The game offers a 2-player mode, playing by turns on the same Nintendo DS.
