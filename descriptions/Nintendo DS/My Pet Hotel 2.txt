Owners of fish, budgies, guinea pigs, rabbits, cats, dogs, horses and their young will leave their animals with you and you will, of course, return them in excellent condition. The more success, the more opportunities! Take the best care you can of your four-legged and feathered guests: feed them, keep their enclosures clean, play with them, get new toys for them, and ride the horses in the countryside. You will build and expand your pet hotel based on your ideas in exciting scenarios of a realistic 3D world with different levels of difficulty, in order to provide a good and protected place to stay for pets.
