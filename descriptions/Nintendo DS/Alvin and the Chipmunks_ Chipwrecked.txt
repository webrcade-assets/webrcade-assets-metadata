Dance along with Alvin and the Chipmunks in this new adventure for Nintendo DS that lets you perform for the crowd as one of your favorite Chipmunks or sassy Chipettes.

Dance to classic Chipmunk hits as you play in environments right from the big screen. Unleash your ‘Munk Power’ to perform ultra-cool dance moves while following an exciting story based on the movie. Get the party started in the exciting musical adventure that is Alvin and the Chipmunks: Chipwrecked!
