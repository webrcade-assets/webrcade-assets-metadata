Hello Kitty no Paku Paku & Logic (ハローキティのパクパク&ロジック) is a puzzle game, developed and published by Dorart, which was released in Japan in 2009. The game features two puzzles: Paku Paku, which is a puzzle in the vein of Dr. Mario and Puyo Puyo, and nonograms. Solving the puzzles unlocks Hello Kitty-themed stickers, which are sorted in a memoir.
