Metcha! Taiko no Tatsujin DS: 7-tsu no Shima no Daibouken is a rhythm game where players use the DS's touch screen to play the taiko drum along to various songs. The game features a story mode where players embark on an adventure across seven islands. Each island has its own set of challenges and songs to play, gradually revealing more of the game's narrative as players progress.

Gameplay involves tapping, swiping, and holding the stylus on the touch screen in time with the music. Players must match the rhythm and follow on-screen prompts to successfully complete songs and challenges. The game includes a variety of musical genres and difficulty levels, providing both casual and more experienced players with an enjoyable drumming experience.
