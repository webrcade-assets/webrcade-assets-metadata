Welcome to Hugsville!



Welcome to Hugsville! Build-A-Bear Workshop mascots Bearemy and Pawlette Coufur guide you throughout this open-ended adventure. Explore the world, decorate your home, play games, or chat with friends!

Features

• 13 playable furry friends to choose from and customize• Go on quests, build and create, play local wireless mini-games with your furry friends, and more!• 5 different "Fun Zones" featuring mini-games, including the Beach, Forest, Jungle, Desert and Icecap.• Collect items to unlock new gameplay and rewards.
