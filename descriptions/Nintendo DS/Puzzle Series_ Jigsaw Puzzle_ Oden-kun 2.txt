This game is the follow up of Puzzle Series: Jigsaw Puzzle - Oden-kun. In addition to the story mode and Oden-kun Puzzle mode from the previous game, there is now a Fun Puzzle mode where the player can unlock new weekly puzzle to play. Solving puzzles can reward the player with mini-games to play. Also, Instead of having only just the traditional jigsaw pieces, there are now triangular, square, and even animated.pieces.
