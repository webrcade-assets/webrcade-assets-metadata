The bewitched game with witch Lilli for the Nintendo DS. Not so easy living together with a voracious kite like Hector. Witch Lilli has her hands full, but also a lot of fun! Take care of the dragon Hector together with Lilli. In order to provide him with everything a dragon needs to be happy, you have to complete tasks and adventures with Lilli. For this he gives you many gifts that you can collect in your friendship book and send to friends with a personal message.
