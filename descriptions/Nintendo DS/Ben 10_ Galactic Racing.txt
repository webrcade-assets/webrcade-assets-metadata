The first kart racing game set in the popular Ben 10 universe features Ben and his alien friends competing in a galactic grand prix through a variety of tracks!

Fast-paced racing action set in the BEN 10 universe and based on the hit Cartoon Network TV show.

• Race in over 30 different karts in 25 exhilarating and challenging grand prix tracks spread across 5 environmental worlds from the BEN 10 universe.
• 12 characters for players to race as, including heroes like Ben, Kevin and 10 of Ben’s most popular alien forms.
• A fierce knock-aside or superb drift energizes your Sub-Energy bar for a jolt of speed
• DS exclusive! Play as the alien hero Diamondhead.
• Each character has a special power that is a unique to the charcter's abilties.
