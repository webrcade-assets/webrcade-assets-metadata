"Cocoro no Cocoron" is a lifesimulation game where players assume the role of Mirumiru, a young girl who is teleported to the magical Cocoron World. 

The main objective is to explore this whimsical planet, interact with its unique inhabitants, and build friendships. Starting in a small area, players gradually expand their reach, meet various creatures, and uncover the secrets of Cocoron World through their interactions and adventures.
