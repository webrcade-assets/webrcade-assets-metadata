"Ore-Sama Kingdom: Koi mo Manga mo Debut o Mezase! Doki Doki Love Lesson is a simulation game where players step into the role of a high school student aspiring to become a manga artist. The game combines elements of romance and creative pursuit, tasking players with balancing their school life, social interactions, and manga creation.

In terms of gameplay, players must manage their time effectively to improve their drawing skills, complete manga projects, and build relationships with various characters. The game features decision-making moments that influence the storyline and character interactions. Players can also participate in mini-games and events that add variety to the gameplay experience.
