Follow the tracks of Hercules and recreate the glorious ancient world at the foot of the acropolis. Construct the Pergamon Alter, the famous Theater of Dionysus and countless other legendary buildings!

In this one of a kind Match 3 Adventure, swap adjacent tiles so that rows of three or more of the same symbols emerge. By doing this, you will collect food, building resources, jewels and gold, as well as magical artifacts for your mission. Will the Goddess of Wisdom be merciful to you?

Features

• Experience 100 new levels! • Construct 20 monumental buildings! • Pit yourself against Greek myths in 15 Bonus Levels! • New Bonus Tools and Bonus Game Modes!
