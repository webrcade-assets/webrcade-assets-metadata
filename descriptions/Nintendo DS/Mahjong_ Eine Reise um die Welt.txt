Mahjong: Eine Reise um die Welt is a sequel to the Nintendo DS game Mahjong by the same development studio. This time players participate in a tournament where the worlds' best players from twelve different countries need to be defeated. In certain games both players' boards are shown and he who clears it first is the winner.

New to this edition is a multiplayer mode to play against a friend and level editor to create custom levels. There are 127 game levels with a progressive difficulty, as a well as a zoom mode to get a better view.
