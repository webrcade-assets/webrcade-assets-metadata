Test your knowledge of geography and beyond! Almost everyone knows the Diercke World Atlas from school lessons. The Diercke Geography Junior Quiz is now available for the Nintendo DS. Through varied game variants, your knowledge is queried in many different ways. You have to find the objects you are looking for on the map, match country flags, solve puzzles and much more! Features: - Exciting geography quiz: Find a region or place you are looking for on maps of Germany, Europe and the world - Many other games such as where does it come from, city, country, river, country flags and more! - Pass the Dierke passport test with many interesting questions! - Extensive reference work on the countries of the world
