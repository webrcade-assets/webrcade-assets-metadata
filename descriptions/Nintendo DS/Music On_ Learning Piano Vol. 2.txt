Bravissimo! The entire orchestra follows your performance of 15 great classics.

By playing each day, your ability may increase. Earn the bronze, silver and gold medals for each song, completing all different levels. A simplified music score is shown on the upper screen and a full piano keyboard on the touch screen, guiding beginners note by note on their first steps.

Songs included:

• Frühlingslied Op. 62 (F. Mendelssohn)
• Gymnopédie No. 1 (E. Satie)
• La Campanella (F. Liszt)
• Gnossienne No. 1 (E. Satie)
• Pavane for a dead Princess (M. Ravel)
• Rêverie (C. Debussy)
• Piano Concerto No. 1 (P. I. Tchaikovsky)
• Etude Op 25 No. 1 (F. Chopin)
• Of foreign lands and peoples Op 15 (R. Schumann)
• Impromptus Op 142 D. 935/2 (F. Schubert)
• Impromptus Op 90 D. 899/3 (F. Schubert)
• The Seasons Op 37 June (P. I. Tchaikovsky)
