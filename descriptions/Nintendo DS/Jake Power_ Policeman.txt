Become A Crime-Fighting Hero!



Play as Jake, helping him fight crime and complete his missions. Patrol the streets, chase criminals, and save innocent people to become a city hero.

Uncover burglaries, and blow the whistle on criminals in fun mini-games that test your crime-fighting skills.
Investigate crime scenes, follow footprints, and take part in thrilling car chases to clear the streets of criminals.
Make your police car bigger and better by adding new wheels, engines, and sirens.
