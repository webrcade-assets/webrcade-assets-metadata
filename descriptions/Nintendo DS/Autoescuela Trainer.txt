Driving School Trainer is a coach created to get a complete preparation in order to pass the class B driving test, and has the advantage that you can exercise wherever you are because it is as mobile as your Nintendo DS! It includes a section with more than 4,000 practical exam questions, adapted to the new guidelines of the DGT. As for the theoretical section, it incorporates numerous schematic tables and a complete signaling guide, as well as 12 puzzles set in real driving situations.
