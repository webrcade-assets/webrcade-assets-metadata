Gintama DS: Yorozuya Daisoudou is a simulation game based on the popular Gintama anime and manga series. The game allows players to control the main characters of the manga as they manage their odd-jobs business. The storyline revolves around taking on various tasks to help different characters, reflecting the humor and chaos of the series.

The gameplay involves completing a series of mini-games and missions to earn money and keep the business afloat. Players engage in a variety of activities, from fighting off enemies to solving puzzles, each designed to capture the essence of the Gintama universe.
