Shōnen Sunday & Shōnen Magazine White Comic is a RPG video game released by Konami for Nintendo DS on October 15, 2009 in Japan. The game is a crossover featuring several characters from manga published in Weekly Shōnen Sunday and Weekly Shōnen Magazine, including Ghost Sweeper Mikami, Zettai Karen Children, Urusei Yatsura, Ranma ½, InuYasha, History's Strongest Disciple Ken'ichi, Hayate the Combat Butler, Touch, Detective Conan, Kindaichi Case Files, GodHand Teru, Cyborg 009, Love Hina, Rave Master, Fairy Tail, Code:Breaker and other manga series.

Ghost Sweeper Mikami characters in the game include Reiko Mikami, Tadao Yokoshima, Okinu, Doctor Chaos, Maria, Meiko Rokudou (with the Shikigami) and Yakuchin (in Yakuchindou, the first shop available in the game).
