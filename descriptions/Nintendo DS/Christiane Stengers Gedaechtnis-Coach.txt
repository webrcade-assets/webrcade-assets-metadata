With Christiane Stenger's memory coach, the namesake conveys her learning methods to the DS owners and trains their gray matter. In addition, learning methods are shown that should make everyday life easier.

Christiane Stenger's memory coach offers seven different disciplines, in which several ways of visualization are presented and trained: For example, pictures can be better memorized and reproduced in the correct order if - like in the history technique in the memory coach - a story is made from them spins. In another discipline, the numerical memory system, numerals are replaced by images and remain in the memory.
