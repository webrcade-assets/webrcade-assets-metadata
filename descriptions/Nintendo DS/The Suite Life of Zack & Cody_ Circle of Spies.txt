The Suite Life of Zack & Cody: Circle of Spies is a video game for the Nintendo DS developed by Behaviour Interactive (known then as Artificial Mind and Movement) and Engine Software and published by Disney Interactive Studios. It was released on October 9, 2007. The title spoofs the famous Disney song "Circle of Life" from The Lion King.

Gameplay
Players take the role of Zack and Cody Martin, the principal characters in the show, and begin an adventure with the "new Arwin's toys", as the first mission is called "New Toys". The players also get a chance to play as Maddie and London in the Minigames.
