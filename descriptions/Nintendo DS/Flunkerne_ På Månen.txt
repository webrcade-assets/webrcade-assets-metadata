The flunkers on the moon are full of speed and excitement and that's something the flunkers love! Aliens from all over the universe have taken to the moon to participate in the intergalactic moon race. But there is a bit of an owl in the moon bog, because all the while the moon race is in full swing, someone is in the process of destroying the moon. And of course the flunkies are mischievous and cheeky, but they won't be involved in destroying the moon! Help President Lobl find the culprits and put an end to the destruction.
