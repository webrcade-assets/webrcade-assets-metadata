Help Hello Kitty find the perfect birthday presents!

Collect stamps and stickers for Hello Kitty's birthday book by playing mini-games, helping friends, and finding special objects. By winning the mini-games you will get hints for birthday gifts. Attend special holiday parties throughout the year. Deliver birthday presents to your favorite Sanrio Characters.

Features

• Spend time visiting friends, hanging out, and shopping for gifts in 5 different locations: Central Square, Forest, Grassy Hill, Main Street, and Downtown
• Includes many Sario Characters
• Over 100 mini-game styles: varies for each character or goal
• Fully realized Hello Kitty world
• Blow out the birthday candles using the DS microphone
