GAME DETAILS
Grab Your Board! Carve It Up! It's SpongeBob's most awesome roadtrip ever!

Hit the beach with SpongeBob and his best pal, Patrick, for some super seaworthy boarding escapades! Ride mondo waves and winding waterways, skate gnarly seaside streets and neighborhoods and enjoy endless hours of LOL excitement while performing your awesome boarding skills!

Features

• Surf and skate across more than 24 maps
• 2-player Single and Multi-card gaming
• 7 play modes including Score, Combo Mayhem
• Customize your boards with colors, wheels, fins, decks
