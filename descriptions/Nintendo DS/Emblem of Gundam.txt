Emblem of Gundam is a strategy role-playing game that features a turn-based combat system where players command Mobile Suits and characters from the Gundam universe in tactical battles.

The storyline spans various series within the Gundam franchise, allowing players to relive iconic battles and events. Players can recruit and command a variety of Mobile Suits and pilots, each with unique abilities and attributes. As they progress, they can upgrade their units and unlock new Mobile Suits to enhance their team's strength.

The game also includes a mission mode with various challenges and side missions, as well as a multiplayer mode for strategic battles against other players.
