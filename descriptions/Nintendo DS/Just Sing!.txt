From the small time to the big time, Just SING! Will take you on a musical adventure from the stages of local clubs to the limelight and massive crowds of superstardom.

    Perform today's hits and yesterday's favorites with an array of music from Lady Gaga, Maroon 5, Amy Winehouse, Gloria Gaynor, The Supremes and many others.
    Create your custom singer and rock your first open mic night! With your amazing talent -- good friends, better times and the best music will be yours to enjoy!
    Embark on a solo career in the game's story mode or just hang with friends and sing the night away. Either way, there is a ton of fun to be had when you Just SING!.
