MAJOR DS: Dream Baseball (メジャーDS ドリームベースボール) is a Nintendo 3DS game developed by Amedio and released by Takara Tomy on July 31, 2008. The game is based on the anime's 3rd and 4th seasons, where the protagonist (name of player's choice) enrolls into Seishu High School until he lands in America to win the League and defeating Joe Gibson Jr.
