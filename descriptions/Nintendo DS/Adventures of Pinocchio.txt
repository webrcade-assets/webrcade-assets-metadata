The puppet maker Gepetto carves a wooden puppet boy as he has no son of his own. The puppet comes to life and Gepetto names him Pinocchio. Gepetto sends him off to school - will Pinocchio be a good boy, or will his mischief get the better of him?

Pinocchio is a game based on the popular fairy tale of the same name. It features a short cartoon which tells the tale of Pinocchio and his many adventures. The mini-games are based on scenes from the cartoon.
