BRINGING THE FAMILY TOGETHER LIKE NEVER BEFORE! READING STORIES AND PLAYING GAMES WAS NEVER QUITE LIKE THIS! 

Full of gaming goodness and positive fairy tales, this title immerses both the parent and the child into the world of skill building, learning, reading and having fun! Bringing different generations together like never before, Interactive Storybook DS aims to bring loved ones together to share, to grow and to prosper. 

Create your very own masterpieces with the drawing tool that allows your imagination to come to life! Help develop your memory skills by putting together a puzzle formed from the scenes of the stories within. Develop and record your own melodies on the piano. Choose your favorite image and paint them up! Listen, read and play along with four fairy tales!
