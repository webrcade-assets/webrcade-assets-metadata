A sorcerer has turned the villagers of Bruna Island into stone and has taken over the palace. The player has to enter the palace and help our members of the royal court and then defeat the sorcerer in order to reverse the gentrification curse.

Naraba World: El Palacio Misterioso is an educational game that focuses on math. It is aimed at children between the ages of 5 and 9. The game responds to the players entered age and difficulty level by giving the player more or less assistance and also effecting the length of the twelve challenges the player partakes in.
