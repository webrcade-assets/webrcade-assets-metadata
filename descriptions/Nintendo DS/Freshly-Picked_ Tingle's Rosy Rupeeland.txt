Still single at 35, Tingle sets off on a search for happiness! One day, our hero hears a mysterious story... It says that only those who save their rupees and throw them into the Western Pool are sure to be led to the dreamy Rupeeland.
Mesmerized by the tale and tired of his dull everyday existence, our hero becomes Tingle and sets out on a journey to collect all the rupees he can.
Tingle finds himself traveling through a harsh world where no one is to be trusted, and whose inhabitants have an insatiable desire for rupees. He must learn to hold his nerve in high-stakes negotiations and enter dangerous dungeons on the road to Rupeeland!
