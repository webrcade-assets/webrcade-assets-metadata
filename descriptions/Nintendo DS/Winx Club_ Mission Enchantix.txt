The Winx Club must defend the peaceful land of Magix when Lord Baltor, an evil mage, threatens havoc. Bloom and her friends must each uncover her Enchantix transformation to defeat him, but the knowledge comes at a high price...

Features

• Use spells and smarts to advance the action-packed story
• Design and trade outfits for each of your Winx Club members
• Listen to music tracks and watch clips from the TV show
• Enjoy six exciting mini-games, from puzzle to dancing
