With Dreamer: Shop Owner, your dreams can come true running your own shop: a florist, grocery store and bakery!

Water and cut the flowers as a florist and take care of the special needs of your customers by preparing exquisite bouquets for them. As a grocer you have to get your store organized; put fruits and vegetables in the right order and place yummy treats in front of the cashier.

Finally, the baker has to ensure all recipes are followed to bake the best cookies in town. Sign your famous cakes and make sure your customers leave the shop with a smile!

Features

• Learn the job of one’s dreams: florist, grocer or baker
• 15 mini-games: 5 for each profession
• Keep your products in perfect order and take care of your customers
