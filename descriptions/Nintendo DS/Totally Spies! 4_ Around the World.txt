Based on the astronomically successful series "Totally Spies!", created and produced by Marathon Media, "Totally Spies! 4 – Around the world!" is the fourth opus of Totally Spies! for Nintendo DS. In this adventure game, the player takes on the role of a fourth Spy. With her friends, she’ll travel the 5 continents, following a pulsating investigation filled with discovery after discovery. An art thief is pillaging the planet’s museums. Sent by the MM0 (Multinational Museum Organization) , the 4 Spies head to the scenes of the thefts to question the witnesses and collect clues in an attempt to capture the mysterious thief…
And so they’re off to: Paris, London, Madrid, Malibu, Caracas, Beijing…!
