Bibi Blocksberg - Der verhexte Schloss-Schatz is an action game, developed and published by Kiddinx, which was released in Europe in 2012. Sensational exhibition in Klunkerburg Castle: The castle treasure is only shown to the public in the large throne room every ten years! Bibi's school class is amazed. But then it happens: Bibi accidentally bewitches the treasure, which then spreads throughout the building. Fly, run and climb through the castle with Bibi and help her find the treasure!
