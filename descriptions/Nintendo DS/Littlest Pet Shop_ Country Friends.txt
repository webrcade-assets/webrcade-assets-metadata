Littlest Pet Shop: Country Friends features 2-player multiplayer wireless support via a Nintendo Wi-Fi connection.
Collect all-new pets packed full of personality! Meet the Great Dane, the Peacock, the Collie, the Ladybug, and many more of your favorite pets.
Now each pet has a best friend that helps them prepare for the party. Plus enhanced multiplayer features allow you and your real-life best friend to enjoy the fun together.
An all-new story guides you along your journey as you play mini-games and accomplish tasks to get closer to the celebration.
Style Out Your Pets - For the first time, you can design your own accessories and style out your pets in your own unique way.
