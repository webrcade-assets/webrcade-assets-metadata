Enjoy two of the most popular iOS games together in the fabulous To-Fu Collection. Combining together iOS hits To-Fu: The Trials of Chi and To-Fu 2, you can now experience the same challenge and fun that has engrossed over a million customers.

Stretch and ping To-Fu as he tries to become the illustrious ‘Master of Chi’. Each new trial comes equipped with a multitude of dangers, from slippery walls to deadly circular saws, all designed to stop you reaching the Fortune Kitty exit.

With over 200 trials to pass, can you reach each exit unscathed and become the Master of Chi?
