Now you can be a part of a winning soccer team with Olympic gold-medalist Mia Hamm as your coach! Interact with teammates on and off the field to build camaraderie and become a better team. Run, kick, pass and dribble as your team competes in local tournaments and work your way up from beginner to pro levels!

Features

• Play against a variety of teams in 5-on-5 soccer!
- Use your stylus to navigate your players around the field, running, passing, and dribbling around your competition.
• Practice teamwork to improve your mood and the mood of your team, which affects how well you play.
- Learn new tips and tricks from Coach Mia Hamm!
• Lead the team as Captain –
- Design the team’s colors, uniforms and logos, as well as assign positions.
• Play through 5 exciting seasons, each with its own team adventures both on and off the field. Star with your team in a reality TV show! Help save your team from being sold! And much more!
• Customize your avatar’s features including height, hair, eye color and body shape.
• Local wirless support for head-to-head play.
