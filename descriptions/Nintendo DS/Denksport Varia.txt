The ultimate puzzles compilation from the official puzzle market leader is finally available in pocket size. With Denksport for the Nintendo DS it is now possible to play the best Denksport puzzles with 1 game. Have hours of fun with crossword puzzles, Swedish puzzles, word search, loopers, sudoku puzzles, jigsaw puzzles and so much more. Now you can puzzle anywhere. On the train, on the beach or on the couch, you always have your favorite puzzles at hand.
