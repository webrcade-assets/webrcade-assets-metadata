Join in on the adventure, traveling through ancient lands, as you match three same-colored magical runes to supply your workers with the building stones needed to create the 7 Wonders of the Ancient World.

Time is short and the challenge is great. Collect valuable power-ups and keep a watchful eye out for secret bonuses. See if you have what it takes to conquer this action-adventure puzzler as you experience the wonders of the Great Pyramid of Giza, the Hanging Gardens of Babylon, the Colossus of Rhodes and many more Wonders of the Ancient World.

- Three challenging modes of play: Story, Free Play and Rune Quest
- Exciting power-ups: Lightning Ball, Fire Ball and Extra Bonus
- Educational history facts about each of the magnificent Wonders
