The only club for fun, fashion and charms!
It’s all about friendship, fun and collecting charms in CHARM GIRLS CLUB. Girls are immersed into the exciting world of the Charm Girls where they complete challenges that are fresh, fun and relevant to their world, collecting sparkly virtual charms along the way.

Customize Your Charm Girl – Create endless trendy and stylish looks and customize a Charm Girl to reflect your unique style. Choose everything from hair, make-up, wardrobe, shoes and accessories!
Meet Your New BFFs – The Charm Girls – Anna, Sara, Olivia and Emilie – are the BFFs every girl dreams of. The Charm Girls will guide you on adventures, encouraging you along the way, like all best friends do.
Run the Mall Your Way – Tackle wacky mall mishaps everywhere from the food court to the nail salon in CHARM GIRLS CLUB My Fashion Mall.
