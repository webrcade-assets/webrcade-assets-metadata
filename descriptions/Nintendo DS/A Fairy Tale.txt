When a giant menace threatens their village, the young faeries Delfbert, Wilf and Angelica set out on a fantastic journey to save their home!  Join them on their whimsical quest through 100 puzzling levels with stunning graphics and magical music that will fill you with wonder.

Featuring an exciting new type of puzzle gameplay and a humourous, light-hearted storyline that is sure to make you smile, A Fairy Tale is an amazing journey of enchantment!  Begin your unforgettable adventure to save the forest!
