Take care of the animal inhabitants in your very own wildlife preserve! Interact with wild animals like lions, elephants, and koalas! Explore the grounds and take inventory of the different species, care for injured animals, and protect the preserve from poachers and pollution.

Features

• Run your own Wildlife Reserve – Build your own animal preserve while you care for its inhabitants and lead visitors on a fun tour of the park.
• Exploration and Knowledge – Explore your preserve and inventory the different species as you learn what their individual needs are. Photograph them to document the growth of baby animals.
• Rescue – Rescue injured animals and nurse them back to health at the preserve headquarters.
• Preserve the Territory – Prevent poachers from entering your preserve and learn to fight pollution to keep your park safe for all animals.
