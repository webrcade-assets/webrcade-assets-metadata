In this game, players take on the role of a teacher of 8 different students. Player can choose to be either a male or female teacher. As the teacher, players have to schedule two classes a day for four days a week, with the school subjects of things like math, history, and gym classes for the students to attend. The classes play out as mini-games. For example, the math class makes the player draw over writing on a chalk board and judges the player on the cleanliness of their steadiness with the chalk. Another mini-game will have players navigating their way through a food fight in the cafeteria. The goal of the game is to have the teacher keep both their teaching skills as well as their popularity levels among the students high.
