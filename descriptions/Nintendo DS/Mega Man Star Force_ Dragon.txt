When aliens from planet FM plot to invade Earth, alien Omega-Xis escapes to warn a courageous boy, Geo Stelar. Together, they must face the aliens on the electromagnetic system known as the Wave Road, and combine to become the ultimate champion for Earth: Mega Man!
