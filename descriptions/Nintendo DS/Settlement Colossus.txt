Build an empire out of its grass hut roots! Take the role of a 300-year old emperor cursed to live without his true love until he appeases his god for a grievous offense. Gather resources, research new tools and build bigger and better accommodations as you care for your people and attract new citizens to your fold. There are no boundaries as you search the surrounding wilderness, attract sea trade and eventually build a colossal statue to the god Helios. Find magic artifacts, play dozens of exclusive mini-games and follow a captivating story as you enjoy hour upon hour of revolutionary gameplay. Featuring outstanding graphics and a breathtaking score, Settlement: Colossus is as epic as it is thrilling!
