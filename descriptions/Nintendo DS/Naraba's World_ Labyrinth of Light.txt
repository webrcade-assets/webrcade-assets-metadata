The mysterious labyrinth that produces energy for the people of Bruna Island has suddenly stopped working. Now the player has to enter the labyrinth and try to figure out why the power has stopped.

Naraba World: Le Labyrinthe de Lumiere is an educational game that focuses on math. It is aimed at children between the ages of 5 and 9. The game responds to the players entered age and difficulty level by giving the player more or less assistance and also effecting the length of the twelve challenges the player partakes in.
