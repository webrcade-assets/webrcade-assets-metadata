Power Rangers:  Go-Busters is a video game based on the Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters series. The game incorporates voice acting and features a storyline derived from the Tokumei Sentai Go-Busters show. It highlights both the strengths and weaknesses of the five main characters and showcases interactions with their respective partner Buddy Roids.

The gameplay is primarily a 2D platform beat 'em up where players can switch between the Go-Busters. Players collect ammo for their blasters, which they can use to fire at enemies. The stages are interconnected and culminate in boss battles where the Go-Busters deploy their Zords and Megazords. These battles are controlled through Quick Time Events.
