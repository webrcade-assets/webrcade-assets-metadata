Unlock the riches of the legendary Crystal Caverns of Amon-Ra by capturing the crystal spheres before they reach the Pit of Fire! Blast through over 1000 action-packed levels in 10 dazzling worlds filled with twists and turns, winding caverns and crystal sphere teleports. Catch magic hieroglyphs sent by Amon-Ra to help you in your quest, including Laser, Bomb, Paint, Reverse and more. Test your strategy and skills with 3 game modes and tons of explosive action!

Crystal Caverns features classic gameplay enhanced by easy-to-use controls and lots of surprises. With three different game modes, 10 amazing worlds to discover and 14 special power-ups, it's the perfect combination of action and puzzle fun!
