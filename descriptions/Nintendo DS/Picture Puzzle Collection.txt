Picture Puzzle Collection is the ultimate collection of picture puzzle games. Whether you want a fun challenge or a relaxing moment after stressful day, these puzzlers provide something for everyone. In the game you are a young art conservator who works in a museum and is tasked with fixing paintings by famous Dutch artists. Picture Puzzle Collection contains 25 games with varying gameplay, such as Ring puzzle, Block rotate, Pixelation, Sliding puzzle, Jigsaw puzzle, Memory jigsaw, Spot the difference and Shaken tiles.
