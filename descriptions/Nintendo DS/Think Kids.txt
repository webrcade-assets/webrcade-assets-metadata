Think Kids for is a logical thinking trainer developed for kids. It helps to playfully increase skills in language comprehension, maths, memory, logical in a way that is suitable for kids aged eight years or up. The kids are guided through the different levels by a character who will comment on the their results and give good advice. The character can be selected when the game is started (boy or girl). In the multiplayer mode (with support for a single cart) the kids get the chance to match with friends and family members. There are eighteen games divided over six categories.
