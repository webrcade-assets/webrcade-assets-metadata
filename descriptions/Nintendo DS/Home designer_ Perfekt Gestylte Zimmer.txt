Experience the dream job of an interior designer up close. In the creative game "Home Designer - Perfectly Styled Rooms" you can decorate and furnish numerous rooms. Design rooms for different customers - each client has their own ideas and wishes. With 500 pieces of furniture and accessories, there really is something for every taste. Furnish children's rooms, bedrooms or living rooms, design work rooms and studios. Let your creativity run free! With the Nintendo DSi's camera, you can personalize the rooms: take a picture and hang it on the wall in a picture frame!

Your customers have a variety of wishesFeatures:

Three different game modes: career, move-in and creative
Great 3D graphics with trendy designs and cool tracking shots through the designed rooms
You can use your own photo to decorate the room via the Nintendo DSi
Share your creations with friends via Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection
