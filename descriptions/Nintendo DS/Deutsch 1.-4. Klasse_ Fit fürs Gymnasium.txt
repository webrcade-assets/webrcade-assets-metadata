"Grundschule 1.-4. Klasse: Fit fuers Gymnasium 2013" is an educational title focusing on a virtual learning environment designed to prepare German elementary school students (grades 1 through 4) for the transition to Gymnasium, a type of secondary school in Germany.

Players engage with over 3,000 tasks across core subjects: German, mathematics, geography, biology etc. The game includes a test mode intended to help students prepare for exams, alongside a two-player mode for simultaneous play.
