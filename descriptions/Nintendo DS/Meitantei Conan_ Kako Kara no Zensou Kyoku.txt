Meitantei Conan: Kako kara no Prelude tells the story of two detectives, Shin'ichi Kudou, a high-school student who is always helping the police solve the cases, and Conan Edogawa, the version of Shin'ichi after he was drugged by the secret organization and woke up with his body shrunk down to the age of elementary school student. The story let you play as both Shin'ichi in the past, and Conan in the present, both stories revolving around different incidents that share a connection. While searching for the truth, you will question suspects, examine and search for clues, and use your deductive skill to pinpoint the criminals.
