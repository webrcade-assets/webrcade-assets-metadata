Jewel Link: Galactic Quest
Help Captain Smith of the Starship Victory on an intergalactic adventure to save the planets! A massive explosion has rocked the entire cosmos - a gigantic star has gone supernova, use your match-3 skills to search galactic locations and help destroy the space debris!
- Search 12 galactic locations
- Solve over 100 match-3 levels
- Complete challenging mini-games
- Includes 3 game modes - Swap/Chains/Group
- Collect powerful extras to use in your quest to save the planets!

Jewel Link: Atlantic Quest
A tanker has capsized leaking tons of oil into the ocean!
Join four marine friends in this exciting puzzle adventure to save the reef and it's underwater inhabitants.
Use your match-3 skills to clear the contaminated water before it's too late!
- Includes 3 game modes - Swap / Chains / Group
- Solve over 120 match-3 levels
- Search 12 underwater locations
- Collect power extras to help rescue the ocean!
