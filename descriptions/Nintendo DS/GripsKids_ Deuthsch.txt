The "GripsKids" series meets the goal of uniting the interests of parents and children. The focus is on the connection between playing and learning: children learn without being asked to do so because playing is so much fun. Funny environments and two likeable trainers make learning fun with explanations and many examples, and thus motivate you to keep going. Corresponds to the syllabuses of grades 2 - 4
"GripsKids Deutsch" deepens all relevant content of elementary school in a funny detective setting. Spelling, parts of speech, grammar, tenses and comprehension of the text are taught in a child-friendly way, packed into exciting tasks such as “opening a safe” or “fingerprint game”.
