Gunpey is a new kind of musical puzzle game where you flip lines and flip the music. In this one-of-a-kind puzzle experience your objective is to flip the lines located in squares to connect them over five columns. Connecting linesfrom left to right remove the connected row. If an incomplete line reaches the top of the screen the game is over. Gunpey will test your reflexes, stretch your mind, and dazzle your senses. With 40 varied backgrounds, stylized music, and two-player multiplayer over wireless ad-hoc connectivity this puzzle title is the game to pick up.
