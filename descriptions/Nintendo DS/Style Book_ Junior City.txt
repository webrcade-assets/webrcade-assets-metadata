Style Book: Junior City is a part of the Style Book series. It serves as an electronic organizer designed primarily for children, integrating various management tools in a fun and engaging way. The interface is adorned with colorful and playful graphics to make organizing tasks and schedules enjoyable for younger users.

Players can use the software to manage their daily activities, set reminders, and maintain a digital diary. The game features a system where users can collect and use points to customize their organizer with different themes and stickers. The emphasis is on creating a user-friendly experience that combines practicality with entertainment, encouraging children to develop organizational skills through interactive and appealing features.
