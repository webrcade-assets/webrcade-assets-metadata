Emma Shock Nurse is a Simulation / Puzzle game developed by Tivola and published by Anuman Interactive. The game offers a dozen operations over four chapters which consists of several mini-games achievable with the stylus. Thus it will be necessary to carry out actions as varied as disinfecting wounds, making stitches, taking the tension, bandaging and plastering etc. There is also a multiple choice conversation system to discuss with the patient beforehand as well as multiple choice questions which consists of being able to locate bones, muscles and organs on different patterns.
