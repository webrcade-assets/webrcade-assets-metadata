In Stone Age, experience and discover the Stone Age with all its tricks and pitfalls!
Imagine you are awakening on a strange beach of a foreign island - with the feeling of being destined for something higher.
This is where the enigmatic story of Nhali, the primeval girl and her tribe, begins. Will the tribe take you?
Can you end the reign of Rafraz and become a good tribal leader? Which way will you go and are you prepared for all adventures?
Immerse yourself in a bygone world and meet the people of the Stone Age. Find friends and watch out for enemies. Trade in tools, raw materials and goods.
