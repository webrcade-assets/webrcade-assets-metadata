In the fight against darkness, three magical siblings encounter frightening figures and develop extraordinary powers. It's up to you to defeat the dark vampire before one more innocent life is lost

Special Features

• Travel to 8 eerie locations on a quest to uncover the 6 powerful amulets.
• Cast fire, wield water and destroy with ice as you wage war against powerful powers.
• Use the Nintendo DSi to cast spells to defeat Gorazul's reign of terror in Ashbury.
