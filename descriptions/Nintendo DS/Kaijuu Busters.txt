Kaijuu is a Japanese term that means "strange beast" and is commonly used to refer to giant monsters in Japanese popular culture. In Kaijuu Busters, players take on the role of members of the Science Patrol, tasked with defending Japan from waves of monsters.

The gameplay involves a mix of action and strategy. Single-player missions usually have the player either battling or capturing different monsters. Players utilize a variety of weapons and tactics to defeat the kaiju, navigating through diverse environments.
