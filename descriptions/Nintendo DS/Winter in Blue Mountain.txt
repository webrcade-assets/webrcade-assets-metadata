Developed by acclaimed Ravn Studio, the game is based on Gudny Ingebjg Hagen's fantastic Nisse universe, as made famous by the Christmas calendar on NRK, books and CD's. With original music by Geir Bren and Bent Aaserud.

Winter in Blue Mountain is an exciting platform game with challenging exploring and jumping, combined with fast racing where you go with sledge down the snowy slopes. The game contains challenges both in and outside of Blue Mountain.
