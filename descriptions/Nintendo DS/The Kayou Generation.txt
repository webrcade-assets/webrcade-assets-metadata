The Kayou Generation is a music and rhythm game set around the theme of classic Japanese pop songs, known as "kayoukyoku." Players participate in rhythm-based gameplay where they must hit buttons in time with the music, matching on-screen prompts that appear in sync with the beat. The game features a collection of popular kayoukyoku tracks from the 1960s to the 1980s.

The difficulty varies across tracks, and players can choose different modes and settings to adjust the challenge. The game also includes a multiplayer mode, where players can compete against others. Additionally, The Kayou Generation includes various visual themes and backgrounds that align with the era and style of the featured songs.
