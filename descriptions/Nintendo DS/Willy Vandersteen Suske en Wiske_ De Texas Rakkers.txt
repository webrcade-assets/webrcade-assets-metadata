Suske en Wiske: De Texas Rakkers – also know as Spike and Suzy: The Texas Rangers – is based on one of the comic books created by famous Belgium writer Willy Vandersteen. As of 2008, more than 300 comic books have been published in this series. In this game Suske and Wiske leave their beloved Belgium for Texas to stop Jim Parsiet from shrinking the world. The player is able to choose between the different abilities of Suske and Wiske to succesfully finish this 3D action platformer.
