A ROM hack of Pokémon Fire Red by Kingdomhearts2luv3r created in 2008 with new Pokémon, trainers, storyline, items, types and moves. It's a whole new adventure! The story begins in the Tiren Region, which, over the past few months, has beenn falling victim to numerous naturak disasters. Not only that, but a gang of mysterious people have been stealing many things, from antennas to crystals. Also, some strange creatures have been discovered roaming in the dark. News of it has been ringing through towns, and the old and young both have thought of only one reason this could be happening-Apocalypse. You wake up, and walk downstairs. Your mom and Professor Spruce are sitting down, talking. Your mom walks up to you and says the professor would like to speak to you. So you go talk to him. He says you are ready to become a Pokemon Trainer-your dream. He gives you a Pokemon, and asks you as a favor, to look for the reason of all these disasters. Little do you know, as soon as you leave, you are immediately trapped in this conspiracy. Watch out for the evil team, and the Creatures of the Night.
