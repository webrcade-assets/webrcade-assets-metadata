Hugo: The Lost Giant

Hugo has just started at the Academy of Witchcraft and Wizardry when he is called to a meeting with the head of the Academy of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Giles the Wise. Something terrible has happened - the huge storm troll has disappeared! Giles asks Hugo to leave to find the storm troll. But will he find the missing giant and what has happened? Join Hugo on adventure. But be careful - there are many dangers lurking out there!
Play and learn with Hugo, defeat enemies and dangerous situations Learn magic arts and transform Hugo into fire, air and stone.
