The Scrabble board game is one of the most popular board games. You need a mixture of good vocabulary knowledge, tactic and chance to be successful. This game aims at recreating this experience on PC and DS while giving the player the tools he would need to progress (exercises, famous game examples, hints on how to play and what are the best possible moves or adaptive AI level).

On PC the game can be played with up to 200 players on the net and on DS you can play with friends with only one cartridge.
