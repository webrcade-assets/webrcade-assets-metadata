When the cross gate broke down, the worlds merged together to form the new Endless Frontier. After all the war and strife, people are learning to get along with each other and live in this new world. However, previously unknown tribes and disappeared species started cropping up. What conflicts will they cause?

Choose your character, play either as a Shura tribe youth or as a princess from the previously thought to be extincted fairy race. Characters from the first Frontier came back along with their seiyuus and graphics in the animation sequences are updated, characters look even more powerful, dainty or sexy.

Battle commands and combos are simple, anyone could enjoy the fun of blowing enemies away.
