My Virtual Tutor was created to help children learn and master early reading via the incredibly popular Nintendo DS. Featuring individualized learning exercises, eight interactive books; four play modes and staged learning all aimed at keeping readers motivated and encouraging literacy. Backed by six years of research and development from the University of Colorado and using proprietary Foundations to Literacy software, My Virtual Tutor makes reading fun, affordable and portable.

8 Interactive Books
4 Different Play Modes
Staged Learning - Through self-correcting practice, the different codes of interacting with the exercises and stories will advance along with player
5 Reading Areas - Phonemic Awareness, Phonics, Comprehension
Proprietary software in My Virtual Tutor has been tested and proven effective with over 1200 students
