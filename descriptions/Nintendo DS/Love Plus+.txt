While Love Plus spans the autumn and winter term, Love Plus+ will pick up at the summer term. Go on vacations, mountain climbing and all sorts of summer activities with the three girls. This game is around 2 times as big as the last game, so there would be more events and dates.One of the greatest draw of this second installment is that the girls will learn to call your name even when you change your name from the default. Those who have beat the last game, import your data into this game and enjoy the bonus events.
