The path to the treasure seems unhindered, but suddenly a wall slides across and your plan ends up in a cul-de-sac. Is there really no escape? 

Accompany Alok on an exciting campaign around the world and travel Egypt, Mexico, or Antartica. Help him find the necessary treasures to rescue his dad from the clutches of the evil Mr. Hoppert. 

Move the patchways using the Nintendo DS stylus and find all treasures in the maze. But be careful - you're not alone! Your computer adversaries or your friends are also searching and will try to put paid to your plans.
