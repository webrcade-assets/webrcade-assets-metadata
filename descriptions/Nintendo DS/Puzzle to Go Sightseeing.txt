Puzzle to Go Sightseeing is the jigsaw puzzle for on the move. Puzzle together the most beautiful motifs wherever and whenever you like. Collect stars for each completed jigsaw puzzle and unlock new images. Each motif is available in three different degrees of difficulty.Would you rather decide yourself on the images you wish to puzzle together? No problem! Puzzle to Go also allows you to puzzle together all the images from the photo album of your Nintendo DSi.

Features:

-10 wonderful sightseeing motifs
-3 different degrees of difficulty: 68, 88 or 130 pieces
-Play to obtain stars and release new motifs
-Puzzle together all images stored in your album
