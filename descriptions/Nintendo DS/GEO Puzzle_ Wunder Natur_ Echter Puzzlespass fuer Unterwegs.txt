With the Puzzle series, Tivola has brought a real novelty to the Nintendo DS. Now follows with "Puzzle GEO - Wunder Natur" a collection of impressive nature motifs, selected in cooperation with the magazine GEO. There are 25 motifs (including volcanic eruptions, hummingbirds and icebergs) to choose from. Features:

Mobile gaming fun without limits: puzzle anywhere and anytime
In cooperation with the magazine GEO (Publisher: Gruner + Jahr)
Impressive animal and nature pictures
25 different motifs
Three levels of difficulty
If you are looking for a challenge, try the time game mode
Never lose puzzle pieces again
