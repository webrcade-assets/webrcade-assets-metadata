Happily Ever After Volume One introduces you to two popular fairy tales, Little Red Riding Hood and Puss N’ Boots.

This interactive storybook includes many puzzles and educational games, and enhances your reading experience with the ability to navigate through the storyline at your own pace with the Touch Screen. Each interaction is rewarded with an animation, and a sound effect or a voiceover.

Features

• 2 classic stories, Little Red Riding Hood and Pus N’ Boots.
• 8 different game modes.
• Studio recorded voiceovers with professional actors.
• Over 200 animated objects or characters
