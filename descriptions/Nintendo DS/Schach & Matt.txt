Learn and train chess in a playful way! The game is clearly structured in: rules/practice, tactics/strategy, training and competition. All areas are accessible from the start. Thus, the game is also suitable for players with previous knowledge. If you already know the rules, you can start training right away, deal with tactics or fight against the "opponent". The story: Giselmund, the chess-mad ghost was robbed by pirates! Ironically, they swiped his favorite chess game! Drago, the fierce pirate, is only willing to give up his chess pieces if he is beaten in a chess game on his pirate ship... - Clearly structured game structure - Fast successes - Motivating story - Multiplayer mode possible
