Follow Pat on his delivery route, discovering 15 exciting games, puzzles and brain-teasing activities along the way!

With his new job as Head of the Special Delivery Service, Postman Pat and his faithful companion, Jess the Cat, can now deliver any parcel, any time, anywhere! With his very own fleet of vehicles and a pilot's licence, Postman Pat proves no job is too big!

Includes fun word, picture, number, puzzle and memory games
3 levels - easy, medium and hard to suit all ages
Postal Service medal reward system to keep young children motivated
Encourages the development of memory, logic, problem-solving and creative skills
