The world has become a cold and distant place, but the Oshiri Kajiri bugs (butt biting bugs) are here to remedy that. They are fairies interested only in making people happy, and once bitten by them, you are invited to sing and dance until you are prepared to live happily ever after.

Follow the cues on screen and tap your stylus accordingly. There are numerous of instruments to choose from, from the tambourine to the piano. Aside from rhythm lessons, take vocal lessons by talking into the microphone of the DS, and learn how to conduct. For those who are fond of classical music, this software contains a hundred full length piano pieces for your enjoyment. It is a game for the whole family, while the children learn to tap their stylus according to the rhythm, the parents lose themselves in the classical music.
