Explore the amazing world of Puppyville. Experience 20 fun filled mini-games where you get to train, play with, and groom your puppies. With simple stylus controls you'll be raising show-worthy puppies in no time. Raising puppies has never been so fun and easy! 

- 20 Amazing Adventures: Bathe and groom your puppies, play hide and seek, and more! 
- Touch and Train: Use the stylus to flick biscuits, play fetch and toss flying discs!
