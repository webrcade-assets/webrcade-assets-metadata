Can you help restore the colors that are disappearing from the world? Color through the adventure...

Something terrible has happened! Somebody has stolen the three Color Crystals and is making colors disappear all around the world! It is up to you to use your Crayola crayons to color and draw your way through different puzzles and pictures to find the thief and bring color back to the world!

• Draw your way through various exotic locations
• Play connect the dots, speed-coloring and assemble jig saw puzzles
• Transform your stylus into 120 authentic digital Crayola Crayons
