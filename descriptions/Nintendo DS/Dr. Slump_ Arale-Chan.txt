Dr. Slump: Arale-Chan is a video game for the Nintendo DS based on the Dr. Slump franchise. It was released on October 30, 2008 in Japan.

Controlling Arale Norimaki, the player has to complete several missions and mini-games in Penguin Village. Goku appears in the game, in addition to several Dr. Slump characters such as Akane Kimidori, Gatchan, Kinoko Sarada, Donbe, Sourman, Daigoro Kurigashira, Senbei Norimaki, and Midori Norimaki.
