Engage in an aristocratic battle of wits in PictureBook Games: The Royal Bluff, a classic-style game of strategy and deception. Win points by adding or removing coloured coins from the table, and trying to guess which colour belongs to your opponent. Call an opponent’s bluff for a chance to earn bonus points, or risk giving them an extra point if you’re wrong! Add Trick Cards into the mix for a cunning combination of tactics and trickery. Compete against a variety of computer opponents in Tournament and Free Play Modes, or challenge up to three friends in Multiplayer Mode using DS Wireless Communications (only one PictureBook Games: The Royal Bluff is required for DS Download Play).
