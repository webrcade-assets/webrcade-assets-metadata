Our favorite crime-fighting cheerleader is once again forced to deal with her nemesis, Dr. Drakken, who this time has kidnapped our favorite soda-swilling geek genius, Wade. Can you help Kim (and Rufus, our favorite naked mole rat) go undercover to disable the bad doctor's latest Doomsday Device and rescue Wade?

• Join Kim as she busts out backflips, double jumps, and roundhouse kicks - all in brilliant 3D
• Crank up your Kimmunicator (using the stylus and microphone) to employ stealth, engage in combat, construct gadgets and unscramble maps in 15 hair-raising levels
• Make like a naked mole rat in a series of cool mini-games
