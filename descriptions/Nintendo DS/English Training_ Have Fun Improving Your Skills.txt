English Training is an educational game in the wake of the success of the Brain Age games. It is a recreational way for speakers of foreign languages to learn or review the basics of English.

The core of the game is based on numerous dictations of different difficulty levels. The gamer has to use the stylus to reproduce the sentences heard from the digital voice or can repeat the phrases aloud. The player can follow his progression on a slot account with graphics, scores and level attributed; he can also unlock several mini-challenge games and features like crosswords, pronounciation exercises (requires a microphone), timed dictation, new congratulation messages, ...

English Training was rated for difficulty under the TOEIC testing system (Test of English for International Communication) and was developed for speakers of different languages, such as Japanese, Korean, French, ... Owners of the game can share a demo version through Wi-Fi.
