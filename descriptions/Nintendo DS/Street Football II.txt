The Celestini are the reigning champions, but the qualification for the next world championships is still in the balance, especially after the departure of the Finezza twins. Fortunately, two new players have taken their place, their names are Jeremy and Samira. If they want to defend the title, the Celestines will have to start training again and show everyone that solidarity, friendship and respect are the foundations of Street Football. Take part in the adventures of the second season, with 7 new minigames and 2 new characters. As in the television series, each character has their own particular playing techniques. Travel the world and face opponents of all nationalities.
