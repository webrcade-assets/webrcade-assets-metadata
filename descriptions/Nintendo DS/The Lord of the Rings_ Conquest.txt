The Lord of the Rings: Conquest is a Battlefront-inspired game in which you play as a soldier (archer, warrior, or mage) from either side (Good or Evil) in the most memorable battles from The Lord of the Rings movies. There's 10 maps, five maps for good and five maps for evil. You walk around these maps killing enemies and taking over control points (flags). There are six game play modes, three for wireless game play and three for single player game play: Campaign Mode, Instant Action and Conquest modes. You can increase your attack power and speed by collecting orbs from dead enemies. You can also play as Aragorn, Ringwraiths, and other heroes and giants. The game features a single player mode and a two player multiplayer modes.
