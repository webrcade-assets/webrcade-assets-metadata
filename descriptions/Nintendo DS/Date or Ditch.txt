Think you’re a real Casanova? Let’s see how you act in real life situations when you face a whole gamut of dates, from romantic to wild! Date or Ditch is an absorbing dating adventure for both guys and girls, testing you through an evolving scenario. Discover unique gameplay mixing social simulation, hilarious face to face conversations, and fun quizzes. Have dates, but a surprise visit from your ex may spice up your love story a bit. Love has always been a game. Date or Ditch just makes it more fun.
