Young witch Schubia Wanzhaar turns Neustadt upside down!

From Aunt Mania's witch book, she tried out a very special witchcraft that was forbidden and caused a huge chaos in the whole city. Great fun for Schubia, a nightmare for the citizens of Neustadt. And everyone thinks Bibi Blocksberg is the culprit.

Now it's up to you: slip into the role of Bibi and uncover the error! To do this, you must try to undo the whole mess. 10 exciting and varied games! For little and big witches from 6 years.
