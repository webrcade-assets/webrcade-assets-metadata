The residents of Het Huis Anubis need your help!

Welcome to the house Osiris. The house Osiris is a boarding school where you have recently become a resident. The landlord has grounded you. If necessary, you had to hang out again ... If only you had not sprayed it wet with the garden hose ... You are now stuck in your room but there is not really time to get bored. Adventure is in the air!

House of Anubis - The Secret of Osiris takes you to an abandoned inn, a beautiful garden full of ancient artifacts and even distant Egypt. You make a wonderful journey through time and you end up in the world of the original inhabitants of the house Anubis; the couple Winsbruggen-van Hennegouwen and their daughter. Can you solve the riddles, find the treasures and solve the Secret of Osiris?
