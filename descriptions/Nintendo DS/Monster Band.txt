Do you want to travel the world? Do you want millions of people to know your name and to sing your songs? Do you want to become a pop star? Make your dreams come true!

You are the leader of the hottest new band around. But, before you can make the charts you have to get creative. Compose your own music on 3 different instruments, learn to play famous songs and have tons of fun playing together with your friends. Make your wish come true – the stage is yours!

Features

• Learn and play famous songs and styles
• Play 3 different instruments and write your own songs (single or multi-player function)
