Kaijuu is a Japanese term that means "strange beast" and is commonly used to refer to giant monsters in Japanese popular culture. Kaijuu Busters Powered is a continuation of the original Kaijuu Busters game, where players are part of the Science Patrol tasked with defending Japan from giant monsters. The game expands on the original by introducing new kaiju and missions, enhancing the storyline of protecting humanity from these colossal threats.

The gameplay retains its action and strategy mix, featuring missions where players hunt or capture monsters using various weapons and tactics. The game allows for character upgrades and cooperative multiplayer options, emphasizing strategic monster hunting in diverse environments.
