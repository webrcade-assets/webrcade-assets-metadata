The year is 1953. Somewhere in the Caribbean, Virginia and her father discover an ancient galleon on a deserted beach. The ship is laden with mysterious artifacts that appear to have once belonged to Christopher Columbus. And yet, everything on the ship is in pristine condition, exactly how it would have been 500 years ago. Now it is up to you to solve this baffling enigma and unearth the curse! With over 1000 hidden objects to be found, more than 50 levels to complete, 42 exotic locations to explore, and 12 exciting mini-games, Mystery Quest: Curse of the Ancient Spirits will put your detective skills to the ultimate test!
