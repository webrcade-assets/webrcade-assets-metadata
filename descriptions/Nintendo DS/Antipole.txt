Arm yourself with the ability to reverse gravity in this action-packed side-scrolling adventure!

With the power to flip the force of gravity, you can run on the ceiling, leap incredible distances, and even use gravity to vanquish your enemies!

Try your skills at multiple difficulty levels and unlockable Challenges. Channel your inner speed-runner and find the fastest way through each level to beat their target times. Also features a special bonus mode for owners of Cosmos X2!
