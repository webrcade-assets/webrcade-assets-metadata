This game is a software for English word training that is based on the book "Kikutan Advanced 6000". The game is a continuation of Kikutan DS Basic and is meant for players who already have somewhat conversational level of English. The game strives to prepare the player for TOEIC (Test of English for International Communication) test with score of 700 points and more.

The game is designed for daily training, after creating your profile, you are presented with 16 new words every day (they are written and pronounced in both English and Japanese). The word games then vary from translating the word, properly reading the word, multiple-answers selection, or entering the correct word that is missing within the sentence.
