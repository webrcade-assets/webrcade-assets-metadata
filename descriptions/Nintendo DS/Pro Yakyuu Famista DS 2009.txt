Pro Yakyuu Famista DS 2009 is a sports game that is part of the long-running Famista series, also known as Family Stadium, which features arcade-style baseball gameplay. Players can participate in various modes, including single-player and multiplayer, focusing on fast-paced baseball action.

The gameplay involves traditional baseball mechanics where players can select teams, manage rosters, and compete in matches. The game includes updated rosters and new teams, reflecting the latest season's players, some based on real Japanese baseball teams and athletes.
