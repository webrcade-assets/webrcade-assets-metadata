Take control of a U.S. naval fleet to wage war against an unknown alien force. Secure the seas before it’s too late with real-time strategy. This is your fleet. These are your forces. This is our fate.

• Defend and Protect: As Danny Hunter, you must guard your Battleships out at sea!
• Call for Support: Call on your fleet to assist your fight against the superior alien force!
• Close Combat at Sea: Play in DUEL! Mode to fire on your enemies in close ship-to-ship combat.
• An Arsenal of Ships: Select from U.S. Navy ships like the Carrier, Destroyer and Submarine, as you move and fight to protect the Hawaiian archipelago!
