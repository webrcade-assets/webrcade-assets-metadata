This game features a collection of jigsaw puzzles that focuses on putting back together pieces of photographs of puppies/ The photographs are from Comin's baby animal calendars. The puzzles themselves range in three difficulty levels by the number of pieces they are broken into, from 20 to 56 pieces. When a puzzle is solved, the picture is displayed on the upper screen of the game title screen, and it can be used as decoration of an in game calendar. The game also has a unique feature where new puzzles will appear on certain days over time.
