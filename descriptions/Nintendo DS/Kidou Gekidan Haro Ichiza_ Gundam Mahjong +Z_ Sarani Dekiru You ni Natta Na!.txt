"Kidou Gekidan Haro Ichiza: Gundam Mahjong +Z: Sarani Dekiru You ni Natta Na!" is a mahjong game that features a variety of Gundam characters, each bringing their own style and dialogue to the mahjong table.

The gameplay focuses on traditional mahjong mechanics, where players must form winning hands through strategic tile selection and discarding. As they progress, players encounter different Gundam characters as opponents, each with unique abilities and strategies. The game features both single-player modes and competitive multiplayer options.
