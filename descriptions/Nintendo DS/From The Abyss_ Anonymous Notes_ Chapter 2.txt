This is a full-on action RPG, packed with breathtaking challenges, that takes place inside a neverending dungeon.

Get ready to face the monsters crawling about the various dungeons, and try your hand at defeating the terrifying boss monster lurking in the darkest depths.

Using the attack known as "Soul Capture", the main character can steal monsters' magic and other skills, as well as pick up weapons and shields to power up along the way...

You'll get an exhilarating rush from strengthening the character through combinations of different equipment and skills, before crushing the monsters with a powerful sweep of your weapon.
