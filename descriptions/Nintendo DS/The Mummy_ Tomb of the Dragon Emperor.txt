The Mummy: Tomb of the Dragon Emperor is a third-person action-adventure video game adaption of film of the same name. The PlayStation 2 and Wii versions of the game were developed by Eurocom, while the Nintendo DS version was developed by Artificial Mind and Movement (or A2M). The game was published by Sierra Entertainment, a subsidiary of Vivendi Games. It was released in the United States on July 22, 2008, in Europe on August 8, 2008, and in Australia on August 28, 2008.
