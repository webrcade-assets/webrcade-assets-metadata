Imagine Teacher: Class Trip gives you the chance to lead a classroom full of students, this time taking them out to explore the thrilling outdoors on an exciting field trip!

Features

• Proven Classroom Gameplay -
- Create and teach morning lessons in a variety of subjects, including Math, Science and Nature, and Geography
- Grade your students’ work and keep them attentive during class.
• Break Out of the Classroom -
- Take your class on an exciting outdoor treasure hunt – Search for clues and souvenirs to be the first class to complete the hunt!
- Encourage teamwork and bonding to boost your scores.
- Discover and foil traps set up by other competitors.
• Explore – Identify and collect rare plants on your outing and enjoy a variety of fun mini-games set outside from the forest to the lake shore!
