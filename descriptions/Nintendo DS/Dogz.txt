In Dogz, you adopt an adorable puppy from different breeds, raise and train him just like in real life.

• Adopt a cute puppy, take care of him, bond with him, and make him happy.
• Choose your favorite from 18 breeds of adorable puppies!
• Teach your clever pooch all kinds of tricks such as sit and shake. He'll do anything to make you happy.
• Dress up your puppy in all kinds of adorable outfits
• Take up to 5 pictures of your puppy each day and write a diary that you can exchange with friends online.
