Following the success of Dawn of Sorrow and Portrait of Ruin, the legendary Castlevania series is back on the DS.

Play as a member of Ecclesia, an organization that has sworn to defeat the evil forces of Dracula. Featuring intense, side-scrolling action as you collect elements to help in your quest against Dracula. Use the brand new Glyph attack system with more than 100 combos to battle Dracula and his minions throughout 20 explorable areas.
