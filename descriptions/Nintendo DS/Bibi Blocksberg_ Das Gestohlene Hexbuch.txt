Bibi Blocksberg: The Stolen Witch Book is a witch-powered adventure on Nintendo DS. In the game, Bibi Blocksberg has the responsible task of bringing her mother's "all-knowing herbal witch book" to her friend Mania. But Bibi is once again too curious and takes a break in the Dark Forest to take a look at the secret writing. Little does she know that she is being watched by the evil sorceress Malicia, who is using cunning and cunning to steal the witch book.
