Eun Hye DS is a homebrew jigsaw puzzle game for the NDS, the 6th homebrew by Beodeulpiri. The heroine of this game is Eun-Hye Yoon who is an actress from Coffee Prince 1st, a Korean TV drama.

Put all the puzzle pieces to the correct place to recover the image. If the position of the puzzle piece is correct, it is fixed with a sound and does not move.

There are 6 puzzles in total. When a puzzle is completed, a melody will be played randomly among 3 background music songs (Lalala, Go Gochan, Sea Travel).
