Best Friends Tonight was released on February 9, 2010. It was developed by Longtail Studios and published by Ubisoft for NDS.
Newly recruited as the star of Teen Planet's TV show, you must make the cast become best friends -- otherwise the TV show will be cancelled! Be charismatic as the anchor of the Teen Planet TV Show, guide the cast to success as you build a friendly atmosphere on the TV set to keep the Teen Planet's TV show on air.
