The Disney Club Penguins from the wildly popular snow-covered Disney Club Penguin online world waddle onto the Nintendo DS for the first time. Disney Club Penguin: Elite Penguin Force introduces kids to all new adventures in the Disney Club Penguin world, giving players a chance to complete secret agent missions, solve mysteries and connect with friends and the Disney Club Penguin Web site in new ways. Kids can earn coins while playing on the Disney Club Penguin Nintendo DS, which will seamlessly transfer into their online accounts via a wireless connection. Players will embark on missions utilizing familiar and all-new gadgets, accessories, disguises, vehicles and locations to investigate mysterious events in the Disney Club Penguin world.

• Take on the role of a Disney Club Penguin Elite Penguin Forces agent, completing top secret missions only available on the Disney Club Penguin Nintendo DS
• Play brand new exclusive DS-only mini-games including Snowboarding and Dance Challenge.
• Play favorite Disney Club Penguin mini-games such as Cart Surfer, Jet Pack Adventure and Ice Fishing in a whole new way, using the DS stylus and the unique dual-screen view.
• Earn coins while playing on the Nintendo DS to use or transfer wirelessly to online accounts.
• Download new missions and weekly newsletters.
