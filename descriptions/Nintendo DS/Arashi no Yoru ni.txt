Arashi no Yoru ni (One Stormy Night) is based on the hit anime film of the same name. The game is about a Wolf named Gabu, and a Goat named Mei and their adventures. It came out at the same time when the film was released. It follows the plot and features visual novel elements plus multiple minigames. There's multiple puzzle challenges, a platformer,, a race, a small RPG and finally an action level.
