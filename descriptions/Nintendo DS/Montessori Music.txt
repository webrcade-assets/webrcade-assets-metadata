Learn all about music in a playful way! For the first time it is possible to read and play notes and music in a playful way. In this game the same learning principle as with Dr. med. Kawashima's brain jogging applied.

You learn everything you should know about music. The knowledge that you acquire in this game can also be used in normal life: reading notes, holding the right rhythm, composing, playing the piano and much more. The conductor leads you through the lessons to musical success and the lexicon explains terms related to the topic of music simple and understandable. Music for everyone!

features:

- Six different game modes
- 18 lessons to learn music in a fun way
- 65 classical music songs to choose from
- Compose your own songs
- 300 questions and answers with different degrees of difficulty
