Digimon Story: Super Xros Wars Red is a role-playing game based on the Digimon Xros Wars anime series. The game follows Taiki Kudo and his Team Xros Heart as they navigate the Digital World, encountering and befriending various Digimon. Players engage in turn-based battles, using a unique DigiXros system to fuse Digimon and create powerful new forms. The game features many Digimon from the anime, offering an extensive roster for players to collect and train.

The primary difference between Digimon Story: Super Xros Wars Red and its counterpart Digimon Story: Super Xros Wars Blue  lies in the exclusive Digimon available in each version. For instance, Blue features Greymon and MailBirdramon, while Red includes SkullKnightmon and DeadlyAxemon. This distinction encourages players to choose their preferred version based on the Digimon they wish to encounter and fuse.
