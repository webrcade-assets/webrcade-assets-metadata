A Servant of Two Masters is a video game for the Nintendo DS. It was released on 26 October 2012. It is based on the series 4 episode, A Servant of Two Masters. Although various elements in the game will not have appeared in the episode, such as Lancelot and the Saxons.

Exciting hidden-object adventure game based on the TV series four episode.
Features Merlin and all the popular cast members on the show.
Hundreds of hidden-objects in over 30 beautifully detailed scenes
Over 15 spell-binding puzzles and mini-games.
