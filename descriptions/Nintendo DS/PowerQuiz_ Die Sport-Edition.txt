PowerQuiz: Die Sport-Edition - D:SF is a quiz game for all who love sport and want to test their knowledge. As the player progresses through the questions, he or she will improve knowledge in several sport related topics such as: soccer (Federal League and international), basketball and European handball, Formula 1, action sports, trend sports, Olympics and sports history.

PowerQuiz: Die Sport-Edition - D:SF utilizes thirteen different ways to present the questions and allows the player to unlock different backgrounds and music. Additionally it features a test mode for exam-like tests and a quick play mode to jump right into the action.

The game features an official license of the DS:F (Deutschen Sportfernsehen).
