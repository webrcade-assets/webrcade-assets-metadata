Join the Winx Club in their newest adventure Winx Club: Saving Alfea! In order to throw the perfect party, the girls must run, jump and fly their way through their home worlds to gather all of the finishing touches for the schools anniversary. But their return is anything but welcoming. Thanks to her new Siphonix Talisman, Selina has unleashed hordes of creatures from within the Legendarium. Its up to the Winx to battle Selinas creatures and discover how to destroy her all-powerful Talisman, all while making sure the schools celebration isnt ruined!
