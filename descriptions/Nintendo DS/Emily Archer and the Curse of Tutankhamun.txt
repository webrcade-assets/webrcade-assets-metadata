Emily Archer and the Curse of Tutankhamun is a mix of hidden object and detective adventure game. Accompany Emily on her travels to Egypt. Once you arrive, she learns that her friend, Lord Carnarvon has been killed. Your task is to solve the murder and find the missing mask of Tutanchamun. You have to find clues and proof in more than 30 hidden object locations and solve more than 30 mini puzzles to find the murderer.
