Joe, Beth and Frannie take their cousin Rick on an adventure he’ll never forget to the magic Faraway Tree, where he meets Moon-Face, Silky the fairy and Saucepan Man. They visit all the different lands at the top of the Faraway Tree, like the Land of Spells, the crazy Land of Topsy-Turvy, and the Land of Do-As-You-Please, where the children ride a runaway train!
