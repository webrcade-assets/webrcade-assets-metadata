"Futari wa PreCure Max Heart: Danzen! DS de PreCure: Chikara o Awasete Dai-battle!!" is a beat 'em up  inspired by the anime series "Pretty Cure Max Heart." Players control Pretty Cure characters, battling enemies and using special abilities to progress through the game's storyline, which closely follows the anime.

The gameplay involves single-player mode where players can perform combos and special moves. The game also includes various mini-games and additional modes to provide variety in the playing experience.
