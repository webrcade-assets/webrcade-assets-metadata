Gripskids: Mathematik is a game based on schooling exercises in order to learn while having fun. It consists of 15 mini games in the training mode and twelve exercises with three degrees of difficulty in the exercise mode All mini games are set in an over-all context: The player visits a fun park and meets two coaches – a girl and a boy – who belong to the carnival staff. They function as tutors who lead the player through the different mini games and exercises, give advice and explain the gameplay as well as the basic idea of the tasks. The aim of the game is to motivate kids to learn even without their parents telling them to.

The game consists of the following categories:
General Mathematics
Geometry
Addition and Subtraction
Multiplication and Division
