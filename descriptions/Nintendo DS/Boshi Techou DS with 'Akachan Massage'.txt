"Boshi Techou DS with 'Akachan Massage' is a Nintendo DS program designed to assist new parents by offering various tools and information to help track a baby's development, such as health records, vaccination schedules, and growth charts. It provides parents with access to resources commonly found in traditional maternity health handbooks.

The "Akachan Massage" feature within the program provides guidance on baby massage techniques, aimed at promoting relaxation and bonding between parent and child. This feature includes step-by-step instructions and demonstrations using the Nintendo DS's touchscreen and stylus.
