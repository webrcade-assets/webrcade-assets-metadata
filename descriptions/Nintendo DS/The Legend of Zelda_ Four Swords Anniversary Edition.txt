The Legend of Zelda: Four Swords Anniversary Edition is a special commemorative version of the Four Swords section of the 2003 GBA release "A Link to the Past & Four Swords". In it, you'll embark on an exciting multiplayer adventure filled with action and puzzles as you work to save Princess Zelda from the evil Vaati. You can play with up to four friends at once, as long as each player has a copy of the game installed on their Nintendo DSi or 3DS system.

This game was originally made available for free download for DSi and 3DS owners between September 28th, 2011 and February 20th, 2012, and later for 3DS owners between January 30, 2014, and February 2, 2014.

It is currently unavailable for purchase via DSiWare or the 3DS eShop.
