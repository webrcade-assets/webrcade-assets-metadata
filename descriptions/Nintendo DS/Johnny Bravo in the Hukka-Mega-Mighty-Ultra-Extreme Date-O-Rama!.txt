Johnny Bravo: Date-o-Rama! is a video game released in 2009 on Nintendo DS . This is a video game adaptation of cartoon Johnny Bravo , broadcast on Cartoon Network . The game is composed of various events, and the ultimate goal of the game is to get an appointment with the presenter of the fictional game show Hukka-Mega-Mighty-Ultra-Extreme Date-O-Rama . It is possible to unlock clips from the cartoon.
