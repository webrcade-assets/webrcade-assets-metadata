Laura and the magical pony Startail have to face their first adventure on the Nintendo DS: A hiking trail is to be built right through the secret clearing and the secret of the unicorns is about to be discovered. The players slip into Laura's role and search for a way to prevent the construction of the hiking trail.

Take good care of Startail, because you can only protect the clearing with his magical powers. But Laura's friends and family also need your help.

The rousing adventure offers 15 quests, lots of exciting tasks, tricky riddles and tricky puzzles.


Features:
- Exciting adventure with 15 varied levels
- With many tricky riddles and puzzles
- Easy to use with intuitive point-and-click controls
- Recommended age: from 7 years
- Reading skills required
