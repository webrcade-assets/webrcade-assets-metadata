Chickens have invaded a movie studio, starting a hilariously messy rampage around the film sets and properties! It's time to take position in the director's seat, reload the shotgun and aim with precision at these winged intruders!

Shoot down the right number of chickens and stage props in order to progress to the next take and solve tons of brainteasing riddles for bonus points. Movie fans and action lovers will have a blast playing this game.
