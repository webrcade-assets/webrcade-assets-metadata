Roogoo Attack is a puzzle platformer for the Nintendo DS, published by SouthPeak Games and released on June 16, 2009. The game is based on Roogoo, an Xbox 360 puzzle game available for download through the Xbox Live Arcade that was later made available for Windows. The game's release will coincide with the release of Roogoo Twisted Towers for the Wii. The two games offer unlockable stages when connected to each other wirelessly.
