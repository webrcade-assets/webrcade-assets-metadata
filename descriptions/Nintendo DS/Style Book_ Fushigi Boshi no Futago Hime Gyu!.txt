"Style Book: Fushigi Boshi no Futago Hime Gyu!" is a part of the Style Book series by Bandai. This game centers around the characters from the anime "Fushigi Boshi no Futago Hime," also known as "Twin Princesses of the Wonder Planet." The game functions primarily as an electronic organizer and diary, allowing users to manage their schedules and set reminders.

Players can use this software to organize their daily activities, set alarms, and keep track of appointments. The game also includes features that allow players to earn points and unlock various customization options, such as decorative stickers and colored pencils, to personalize their digital diaries.
