As a genre, it will be a side-view type puzzle game.
Press the colorful blocks that disappear when you stick three of the same color together, and clear the stage by erasing all the blocks on the screen. It's an easy-to-attach work that anyone can quickly learn the rules, as if adding the characteristics of Sokoban and Puyo Puyo.

The most distinctive feature is the world view and direction. This game consists of one story with several stages of game parts, the first episode is a cake maker, the second episode is the South Pole where penguins live, and the theme scenes change, but each The background, the clothes of the main character, and the music will change in line with the story.
