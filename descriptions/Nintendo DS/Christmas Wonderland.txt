Christmas Wonderland is a hidden object game featuring superbly detailed screens with lots of inter-level games and puzzles.

Santa and his team are working non-stop to get everything ready - but time is running out. Travel around a Wonderland crammed full of objects, with beautiful graphics and mini-puzzles for all the family to enjoy.

Can you help Santa find all the toys scattered around Wonderland and deliver the presents in time for Christmas?

Objects change with every play-through, increasing replayability and making this a game not to be missed.
