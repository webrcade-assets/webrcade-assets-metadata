A world where tribes battle for supreme control

Enter the realm of Chaotic and assume the role of one of its Creatures in this turn-based arena combat fighting game. Engage in explosive animated onslaughts that combine turn-based strategy with vicious ranged assaults. Collect formidable battle gear, cast devastating magic spells, and boost your creatures powers as you unleash high energy attacks in your quest to master the merciless.
