In Animal Life: Dinosaurs you start a fantastic voyage into the past. You’ll get into the world of dinosaurs, solve tricky quests and experience exciting games. The more you know about dinosaurs, the more puzzles and dinosaurs you’ll get to see. Collect all stars to become a real pro in the field of dinosaur research.
This product can take advantage of the following functions when used with a Nintendo DSi system: Camera, Parental Controls.
Game is in English, Deutsch, French, Spanish and Italian. Manual is only printed in English and Deutsch.
