Atlantis - Did it ever really exist?

For a milennia, adventurers have sought the sunken kingdom and its countless treasures - but who created the mystic city in the first place?

Discover the secret, create magnificent buildings and restore Atlantis to its former glory. Collect ancient artefacts and secret treasures to help you along the way. Test your Jewel-matching skills over a series of challenging levels, in this captivating, underwater puzzle game!
