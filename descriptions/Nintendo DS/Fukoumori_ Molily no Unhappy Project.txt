In Fukoumori: Molily no Unhappy Project, players follow Molily, a princess from the land of misfortune. Molily, along with her misfortune bats, helps characters in her village overcome bad luck. The narrative unfolds as Molily interacts with the villagers and discovers the causes of their misfortunes.

The game features diagnostic tests and mini-games that players use to identify and dispel misfortune. Activities such as fortune-telling, room decoration, and other mini-games provide a mix of gameplay styles aimed at turning misfortune into happiness.
