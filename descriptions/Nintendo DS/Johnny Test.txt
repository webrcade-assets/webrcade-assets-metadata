Based on the hit TV series, Johnny Test chronicles the adventures of a fearless 11 year old boy, his genetically engineered super talking dog Dukey and his 13 year old super genius scientist twin sisters, Susan and Mary, who use Johnny as their guinea pig for their out-of-this-world scientific experiments.

Johnny’s sisters dressed up their beloved Gil as a princess, but now he’s been captured by the jealous Bling-Bling Boy. What’s worse, Brain Freezer and Mr. Whacko have managed to get involved. As usual, it’s up to Johnny and Dukey to save the day!
