Minna de Taikan Dokusho DS: Cho-Kowai! Gakkou no Kaidan (みんなで体感読書DS チョーこわ~い! 学校の怪談) (lit. Scary Experiences with Everyone! Ghost Stories at School) is a game of the Gakkou no Kaidan game series. It was released only in Japan for the Nintendo DS. The game follows the same objective of the previous game: exorcise ghosts in a school.
