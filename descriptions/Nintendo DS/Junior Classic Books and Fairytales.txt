Mobile reading devices are becoming more and more popular, and now there is a way kids can get in on the action. Junior Classic Books for Nintendo DS gives kids the chance to learn how rewarding reading can be! Holding a large book in your hands can be daunting, especially for children. Junior Classic Books takes the pressure off young readers by making reading fun and easy! Turn pages by sliding your finger on the screen, choose between font styles and sizes, and select from a library of greats! Never lose your page again! Junior Classic Books will pick up from where you left off!
