This combo pack contains the full versions of both Madagascar 3: The Video Game and The Croods: Prehistoric Party. It is loaded with action and adventure featuring your favorite DreamWorks Animation characters and is sure to provide hours of fun for kids of all ages. In Madagascar 3: The Video Game, adventure across Europe as Alex the Lion, Marty the Zebra, Gloria the Hippo and Melman the Giraffe while you find a way home under the perfect cover: a traveling circus. The Croods: Prehistoric Party. combines the ultimate fun of a family party game with Croods style action as you journey through 30 unique and addictive mini games.
