Based on the upcoming Disney Channel Original Movie of the same name, Phineas and Ferb: Across the Second Dimension for Nintendo DS takes players on an exciting journey through all new environments in an all new fun-filled game. Play as Phineas, Ferb or Agent P and master combat techniques to battle and overcome enemy drones using gadgets and combat maneuvers while leveling up through over 30 stages to defeat the evil Dr. D and his robot army and save the Tri-State area.

Features

• Play as Phineas, Ferb or Agent P and unlock a special bonus-a new secret Agent
• Jump, dodge and battle through 5 outrageous worlds with over 30 wacky levels
• Wield power-packed gadgets such as a baseball launcher, electricity gadget and a grapple gadget and earn upgrades by solving environmental puzzles
• Enjoy Platypus Day and Halloween themed environments
• Face off with boss enemies including Goozim, Platyborg and Evil Doof
