Without you everything is stupid! Apart from Mäh Jongg DS, of course! For the first time, the well-known and unspeakably cute sheep will enchant you on your Nintendo DST! he goal of MähJongg DS sounds very simple at first: Clear the playing field by finding two matching game pieces! - Now it has to be admitted that this ancient Chinese game packs quite a punch! Some stones are hidden under others and can therefore only be seen when the upper stones have been cleared away. However, a healthy mixture of brains and luck always helps to find a solution! All Mäh Jongg stones are of course decorated with cute sheep, flowers, crawling beetles and other cute motifs from sheepworld.
