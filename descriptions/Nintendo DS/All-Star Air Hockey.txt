All-Star Air Hockey captures all the thrill, fun and realism of Air Hockey on the Nintendo DSi!

Remember the days of hanging around the local arcade playing air hockey until all hours? Then grab your paddle and head over for some classic arcade action with All-Star Air Hockey from Skyworks, creators of some of the best quality and most fun sports and arcade games on the Nintendo DSi system!

In Practice Mode, select one of eight computer opponents to play against, while in Tournament Mode see how good you are against all of them! All-Star Air Hockey features state-of-the-art graphics and local high scoreboards.
