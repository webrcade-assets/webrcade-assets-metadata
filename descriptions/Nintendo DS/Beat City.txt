Start a Rhythm Revolution

Can you feel the beat? Life color and happiness are being drained from the people of Beat City. Use the power of the beat to prove that rhythm is life and revitalize the city!

• Join the Beat: Join a crazy cast of characters as they start a rhythm revolution in Beat City.
• Tons of Mini-games: Grab your stylus and Tap, Swipe and Hold along to the rhythm in 20 mini-games.
• Hit the Beat: Restore music, color, life and love with the power of the beat. See the world evolve in front of your eyes!
• Hours of Fun: Easy to complete, with enough depth and secrets for endless playability.
