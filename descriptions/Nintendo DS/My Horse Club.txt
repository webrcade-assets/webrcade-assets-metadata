On this mission driven adventure you discover the Appaloosas in the Wild West of America. In addition, there are many missions and 25 horse breeds. The locations where you can drive are free and open. No beaten paths or a straight-line story. Use the DS stylus during dressage, jumping and lunging.

- Ride horse in beautiful areas around the world. 
- The American West, Canada, the lakes of Ireland, Spain and the Moroccan desert. 
- 25 horse breeds.
