Pocket Pack: Words & Numbers is a collection of popular word and number games. Choose to play any of: Anagrams, Sudoku, Kakuro, Word Cubes, Word Guess. Win the game trophies which are shared across all of the "Pocket Pack" series. Play with friends through wireless play, or via DS Download Play. Pocket Pack: Words & Numbers is one game in the "Pocket Pack" series available on Nintendo DSiWare.
