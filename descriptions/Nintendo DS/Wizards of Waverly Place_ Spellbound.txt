Take a magical adventure with Wizards of Waverly Place: Spellbound, the new Nintendo DS game that lets fans play as their favorite wizards-in-training, Alex, Justin and Max Russo. Players will embark on a journey through a series of hilarious mishaps as a dress designed by Alex Russo goes missing just before the big fashion show. Fans will be able to take their wizard skills to the next level with cool spells, new outfits and more magic and mayhem than ever before!
