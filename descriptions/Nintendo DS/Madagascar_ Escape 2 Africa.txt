Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa DS is a third-person action-platformer based on the movie of the same name.

All of the Madagascar characters are available for play, including Alex, Gloria, Melman, and the giant Moto-Moto, each with their own unique moves. Players explore the African savanna, climbing, jumping, swimming, confusing tourists, finding monkeys, and collecting items along the way.

The game also includes various minigames, into Mort's Pinball and a humorous sidescrolling, teamwork game featuring the four plotting penguins from the film.
