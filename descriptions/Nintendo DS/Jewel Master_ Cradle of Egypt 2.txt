Cradle of Egypt 2 allows players to journey through 100 levels of challenging match-three fun, all while recreating the millennia-old civilization of Ancient Egypt. Players advance from hard-working farmers to Pharaohs, building the Pyramids, Sphinx and 18 other famous monuments. As part of the best-selling Jewel Master series, Cradle of Egypt 2 builds on the previous games' formula that has made the series a phenomenal success
