A group of curious children venture into their school during the dead of night to investigate rumors of ghosts haunting the premises. Their exploration leads them to unexpected dangers, but they find solace in the presence of Hanako, a friendly ghost girl who comes to their aid. 

Set within a 3D environment, players navigate through various rooms and areas, solving puzzles, collecting items, and engaging in mini-games. 

Based on the anime series of the same name.
