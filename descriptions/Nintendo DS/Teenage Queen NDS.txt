Teenage Queen was a port of a card game which was published in 1988 by ERE Informatique Atari ST, this game is also on the Amiga, Amstrad and PC. Programmed by Eric Doireau graphics and Jocelyn Valais. Player is being challenged to a 32-card game of strip poker (no cards lower than 7).

The graphics are mostly of the Amiga, the Atari a few sprites and by Foxy.

It was ranked 5th in the programming contest NeoCompo 2005.
