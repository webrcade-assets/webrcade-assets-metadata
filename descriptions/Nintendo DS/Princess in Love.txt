Princess in Love is a game where the player assumes the role of Queen-to-be Isabella and lives out one year of her life. The player has exactly one year (in game) from July to June to achieve the goals and has to interact with the royal family, her friends, her staff and family guests to grow as a person and become the next queen.

To do so, this game is a combination of interactive storytelling, exploration, and a collection of 14 different mini games (General Knowledge: a quiz mini game; Arranging Seating plans; Create a menu; Dancing; Dress up; Draw Hearts "Sleep"; Counting Sheep "Sleep"; Write Diary; Press Flowers; Trick Security Officers; Cog game "Fix Cars 1"; Oil flow game "Fix Cars 2"; Dry Tears; Wash Clothes) that the player has to overcome to succeed.
