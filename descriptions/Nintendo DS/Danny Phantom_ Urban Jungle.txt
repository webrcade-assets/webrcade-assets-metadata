Danny Phantom: Urban Jungle is a video game for the Game Boy Advance and Nintendo DS consoles. It is based on the episode "Urban Jungle" from the television series "Danny Phantom" and is the second video game for the series (The first is Danny Phantom: The Ultimate Enemy for the Game Boy Advance). Players play the role of Danny Phantom in his adventure to save Amity Park from various ghosts from the show. It's console versions were all cancelled for unknown reasons. The console version was suppose to be an Action-adventure game.
