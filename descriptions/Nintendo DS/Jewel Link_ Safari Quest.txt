Having graduated top of her Biology class, Jane is sent off to Africa at the request of her University Director, to locate and study the very rare White Lion. Accompanied by four animal guides, the quest turns out to be more challenging than originally thought.

Use your match-3 and puzzle-solving skills to help Jane and friends track down the highly elusive King of the Jungle. With 3 game modes to choose from, Jewel Link Safari Quest is the ultimate African-themed match-3 adventure!
