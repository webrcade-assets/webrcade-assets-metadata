With "Learning math while playing", children are playfully prepared for the requirements of pre-school and 1st grade. 18 mini-games introduce prospective school children to their first arithmetic tasks. They build on each other and are divided into the areas of writing and recognizing numbers, counting and arithmetic.
Writing and recognizing numbers: Practice writing and recognizing numbers
Counting: Matching a specific number of objects to the corresponding number
Arithmetic: First addition and subtraction in the number range from 0-9
Learning without reading: "Learn math while playing" does not require any written texts. All exercises can be completed easily and without reading skills. Each exercise is explained using a tutorial with music, so that no questions remain unanswered.
