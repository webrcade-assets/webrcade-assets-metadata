Hi Hi Puffy AmiYumi: The Genie & the Amp is a 2006 action game for the Nintendo DS handheld video game console based on the Cartoon Network animated television series Hi Hi Puffy AmiYumi. 

The player must navigate various levels by using the d-pad to move. Throughout the levels, the player will come across a variety of enemies trying to stop them. To attack, the player can either use the Nintendo DS's buttons or use the guitar strings that appear on the touchscreen. The game also features a two-player cooperative mode.
