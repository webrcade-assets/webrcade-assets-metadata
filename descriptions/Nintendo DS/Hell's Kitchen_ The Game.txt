Based on the FOX network television show, chef Gordon Ramsay hosts a cooking competition for aspiring chefs.

Play through several time management tests in the kitchen and dining room such as taking orders, cooking, serving meals, and cleaning up to the standards of Chef Ramsay or risk his scorn. In the kitchen section, the player has to put different ingredients into the right pots and pans timed so all dishes at one table will be ready around the same time. The player is rated on a five star scale and can work up from a dishwasher to Senior Chef at a 5-star dining establishment.

Unlock 35 recipes from the US TV show, try them at home, or share them with friends.
