In Deviens Miss France players take on the role of young woman who aspires to become the next Miss France. To do this, she must first compete at and win several lower pageants before attempting to achieve her ultimate goal.

Players can create their character by choosing a hairstyle, the color of her eyes, and skin tone, The game consists of different activities related to the beauty pageants. Activities include properly doing your make-up, performing a fitness regimen, and preparing how to answer questions.
