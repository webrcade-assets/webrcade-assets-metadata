Another game in the Ubisoft's Imagine series of games that show female children and early teenagers the basics of some of the professions culturally assigned to women.

This time the player learns to compete in the world of artistic skating. You start by choosing one of the three characters available and train her until she becomes a champion. You can choose the hairstyle and clothes for your character and music and moves for your performances.
