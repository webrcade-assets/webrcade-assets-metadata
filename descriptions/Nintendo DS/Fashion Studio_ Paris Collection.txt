GAME DETAILS
Design your own line of high-end fashion in the heart of Paris!

Open your own studio in the heart of Paris, the capitol of high fashion. From couture gowns to beachwear and handbags to scarves, now YOU can create complete high-end fashion designs and display them in your very own boutique!
• Create your own fashion house in the heart of Paris – From skirts and coats to shoes and bags, you create today’s high-end fashion.
• A powerful creation engine – Craft one-of-a-kind fabric designs by combining colors, textures and patterns. Each article of clothing has up to three different zones allowing for intricate designs.
• Participate in fashion challenges – Further your design career and build your clientele as you design pieces to meet the needs of trendsetters. Unlock new design options, like new colors and photo shoot backgrounds, as you succeed in different tasks.
• Increase your FashionScale – As you progress, your skill is measured by the FashionScale. Achieving certain levels of the scale will allow you to unlock and explore all the creative studios of your multi-floor fashion house: a makeup and hairdressers’ studio, a handbag studio, and much more!
