Players are challenged to solve different types of puzzles by flicking, dragging, and tapping the screen using the stylus. These puzzles require quick reactions and pattern matching. Players can play through the story mode, where they try to stop the alien attack by playing these puzzles against various bosses. They can also play with a friend using Wireless DS Multi-card play. Endless mode allows a player to pick a specific puzzle game mode and try to survive as long as they can.

Features

• 14 different puzzle types
• Humorous story mode with 9 different monster bosses and various bonus stages
• Up to 2 players can compete head to head using Wireless DS Multi-card play.
• All controls use the Nintendo DS stylus
