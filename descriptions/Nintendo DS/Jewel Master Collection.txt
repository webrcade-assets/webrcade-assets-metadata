Follow the tracks of Hercules, Pyramids and the Sphinx, to the cradle of civilization! Construct the Pergamon Alter, the famous Theater of Dionysus and countless other legendary buildings!In this one of a kind Match 3 Adventure, swap adjacent tiles so that rows of three or more of the same symbols emerge. By doing this, you will collect food, building resources, jewels and gold, as well as magical artifacts for your mission.
