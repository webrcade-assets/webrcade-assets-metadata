You like music? You like platforms? You like the jungle? Then you'll love "Maestro! Green Groove"!

So jump in and enjoy this wonderful feeling of being a virtuoso using your heart, stylus and voice! Everything you do is music, EVERYTHING! Hit the spiders to play some drums, shoot the fruit to bring out the melody, pinch the strings to play the tune...

Challenge the mischievous Staccato the Spider in a battle in which the most talented one will win!
