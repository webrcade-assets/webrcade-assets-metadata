For today's generation of parents of school-age children, one thing counts above all: They want their children to reach their learning goals quickly and directly and to achieve noticeable learning success. With these NDS titles, Cornelsen is now combining the successful console with highly efficient educational software. All tasks are based on the framework plans and the Cornelsen textbooks that are used in German schools. FEATURES: - Suitable for high school and junior high school - 8 types of exercise with over 1,000 language tasks - Story mode: 14 learning situations in one story - Training mode: grammar and vocabulary exercises that can be repeated as often and as required - Evaluation of personal learning progress - Numerous customizing Individual elements - Design of the interface - Exciting mini-games - Dual-player mode - English tutorial for grades 7 and 8
