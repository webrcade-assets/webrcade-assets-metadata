Anonymous Notes 1: From The Abyss" is an action RPG set in a mysterious and dark dungeon known as the Abyss. Players take on the role of an adventurer who explores the Abyss to uncover its secrets and find powerful artifacts.

Gameplay involves real-time combat where players use various weapons and magical abilities to defeat enemies. The dungeon is randomly generated, offering different layouts and challenges in each playthrough. Players can collect items, upgrade their equipment, and improve their skills as they progress deeper into the Abyss.

The game focuses on exploration and combat, with minimal narrative elements. The player's objective is to delve as far as possible into the dungeon, battling increasingly difficult enemies and discovering rare treasures. The roguelike elements ensure a variety of experiences with each playthrough.
