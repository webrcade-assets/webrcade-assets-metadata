This game is a 3rd person adventure mystery game involving Raven and her friends Chelsea and Eddie. Raven receives a vision that her favorite theater is closing down and she enlists Chelsea and Eddie to get to the bottom of it. The game has an assortment of puzzles and mini-games which the 3 characters use to help get them closer to solving the mystery of the theatre.

Some features include a driving mini-minigame, fashion matching, sewing minigame, cooking minigame, and an order match minigame.
