In a dusty attic, the player finds the mysterious journal of the Egyptian researcher August Rasmussen. This gives up a variety of puzzles that guide the player to Egypt. So, on to the land of the pyramids! Once there, it takes the player to the remote part of a pyramid. Objective: Solve the puzzles the explorer left in the pyramid and defeat the curse that holds him captive.In several chapters, there are various types of hidden object puzzles to solve. The player can also choose other logic puzzles to complete one stop of the itinerary. In various game modes he solves tricky puzzles in spectacular locations and experiences a unique adventure.
