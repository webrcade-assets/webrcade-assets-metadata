SD Gundam Sangoku Den: Brave Battle Warriors: Shin Militia Taisen is an action game with some light RPG elements set in the Gundam universe. The game's plot follows the events of the anime, blending elements from the Romance of the Three Kingdoms with SD Gundam characters.

The gameplay involves hack-and-slash mechanics similar to the Dynasty Warriors series. Players control Gundam units in various battles, upgrading characters and unlocking new abilities as they progress through the story.
