Get ready for an adventure at your very own Paws & Claws Pet Vet clinic down under! It’s your job to nurture and love all of the wild animals down under and bring them back to health.

Features

• Work as a doctor and care for 6 fun loving animals such as koala bears, wombats, dingoes and kangaroos!
• Be an entrepreneur! Use the profits from your Pet Vet clinic to expand your business and increase your clinic’s capabilities.
• Hop in your very own vehicle and use your map to locate all the sick animals in the eucalyptus forest, the vast plains or the mountains.
