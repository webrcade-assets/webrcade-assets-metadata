Accompany Moorhuhn on a fantastic puzzling adventure from his lovely castle in the Scottish Highlands to sunny Egypt, where treasures, magic artifacts and cursed mummies are waiting on every corner!

To get there and back again you not only have to solve countless archeological puzzles, but also master tons of riddles and numerous other challenges to test your skills. If you strive to become a great adventurer, you will need to have the right solution for every possible situation you encounter - as bizarre as it might be.
