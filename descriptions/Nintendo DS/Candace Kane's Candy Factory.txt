The Most Fun Factory in the World!

The zany world of Syrupton comes to the DS in Candace Kane’s Candy Factory, a fast-paced, addictive puzzle game that puts players in the role of Candace Kane as she attempts to earn enough to make her way to the International Candy Cup

Championships. Go it alone or play with a friend in the colorful world of Syrupton, only in Candace Kane’s Candy Factory!
