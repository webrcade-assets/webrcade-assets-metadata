Maintain and grow your very own garden from a lonely patch of grass to a lush environment teeming with activities in this simulation game for the Nintendo DS. Breathe life into your garden through multiple seasons by simply enjoying yourself in a series of fun and fast paced minigames. Along the way you’ll expand your knowledge and learn fun facts about plants, crops and gardening. So, pick up your shovel and put on your gardening boots, it’s time to put your green thumb to the test and have some fun!
