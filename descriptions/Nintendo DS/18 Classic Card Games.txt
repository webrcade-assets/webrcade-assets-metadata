Play Texas Hold 'Em, Blackjack, Rummy, Hearts and many more classic card games on your Nintendo DS. Challenge up to 5 computer-controlled opponents -- bid your deck to max and take all the tricks in Skat, lay your cards down for a game of Solitaire, or make the bluff of your life in a hand of Poker. Step-by-step instructions are included for those who need to brush up on the rules.

18 Classic Card Games includes the following playing card games: Texas Hold 'Em • Omaha Hold 'Em • Casino Blackjack • Spanish Blackjack • Blackjack • Pontoon • Dutch Eenentwintigen • Rummy • Canasta • German Mau Mau • Dutch Pesten • Bridge • Dutch Klaverjassen • French Belote • German Skat • Freecell • Hearts • Klondike.
