Set in the Might and Magic universe, Might and Magic Clash of Heroes is the first-ever instalment developed for the Nintendo DS incorporating role-playing elements, adventure and an innovative and dynamic combat system based on puzzle mechanics. Might and Magic Clash of Heroes takes place 40 years before the Heroes V saga. Scattered across five different regions of Ashan, 5 special heroes must travel their own dangerous paths to grow in strength, unravel a demonic plot, and ultimately save the world from Chaos.
