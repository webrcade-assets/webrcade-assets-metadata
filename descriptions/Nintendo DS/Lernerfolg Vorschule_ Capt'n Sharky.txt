Even little pirates have to go to school at some point! Luckily Captain Sharky helps with the preparation. In this game, the player learns how to deal with letters and numbers, trains his memory, practices dealing with money and plays small logic games. As a reward for successful learning, the player unlocks more and more levels and bonus games.

Features:

Playfully prepare for elementary school with Captain Sharky
No reading skills required, as the game uses continuous voice output
Children learn their first letters and numbers
Memory and logical thinking are trained
For all preschoolers from 4 years
Based on the award-winning PC edutainment series
