My Little Baby is, well, a baby simulator. Have you always dreamed of having a cute little baby to lovingly care for? Well, now is your chance!

My Boyfriend is a teenage romance simulation. The object is to win the heart of your "dream" boyfriend. Create your boyfriend in the editor and then get him to notice you with the right clothes, hair style and make-up. Earn money to buy new clothes with some part-time jobs like working in a parlour, helping out in a pizzeria, walking the dog or washing cars.
