DoraBase 2: Nettou Ultra Stadium is a baseball game based on the Dorabase manga series, a spin-off of the popular Doraemon franchise. The story mode allows players to train their characters and engage in various matches, introducing new characters along the way.

The gameplay includes improved graphics, AI, and music compared to its predecessor. Players participate in baseball matches with mechanics that include pitching, batting, and fielding. The game offers various modes such as exhibition matches and tournaments, and includes training sessions and character customization.
