You'll be the Hollywood "it" stylist in no time!



In Dream Salon you play as Jenny, a recent graduate of the New York Beauty Academy. Help her build her career as she styles model after model, creating the ultimate fashionistas! Every aspect of hair, makeup, and fashion is unique and customizable, so create the total superstar package! Whip up a cutting edge hairdo, complete with color and highlights. Use the latest cosmetics and trendiest colors so your models put their best face forward. Design fashion-forward outfits for runway shows that will put your models on the cover of magazines worldwide.

Features

• Get your models ready for the runway in 10 different locations worldwide• Choose from 20 categories of hair styles and customize colors• Over 100 interchangeable cosmetic items including eyeshadows, lipsticks, blush, and more• Swank up outfits with hats, sunglasses, and dozens more fashion accessories• Lacquer up fingernails with custom nail colors and decals• Save your favorite looks by adding snapshots to the gallery
