Mind Body Soul - Mindstorm 2 is the sequel to the best-selling Nintendo DS game of twisted mental puzzles that utilise all four corners of the brain, from logic puzzles to analytical conundrums.

Multiple puzzle types across a various of genres, with ever increasing difficulty levels to give fans of Brain Training games more to think about.

Features:

- Four genres of mind games incorporating Logic, Analytics, Memory and mental endurance
- Three puzzle types per genre (12 games in total)
- Five levels of difficulty per game
- Five to seven puzzles are automated i.e. infinite number of games
- Collection of bonus unlocks to change your mentor avatar
