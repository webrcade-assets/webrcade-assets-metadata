Quickly and skillfully form rows of three of the same diamonds to shatter them on both screens. This is how all golden plates disappear until the level is won. Unlock powerful extras, use the hammer to smash the diamonds in difficult situations, or create a magical storm by blowing into the microphone! - 4 Elements: In this puzzle game "4 Elements" you have to prevent the destruction of the earth. In order to learn the necessary magic, you first have to make four old magic books readable again and go on a journey that is made more difficult or easier by many different puzzles. Only those who solve all the riddles can unravel the mystery and save the empire from collapse.
