"Chajara! Gaeguri Jungsa Keroro: Teullingeurimchatgi Daejakjeon!" is a hidden object puzzle game. 

Based on the "Sergeant Keroro" anime and manga series, players are tasked with finding differences between two similar images. The game features multiple levels and difficulty settings, including single-player and competitive modes. Successfully completing levels unlocks new opponents and stages, enhancing the overall gameplay experience.
