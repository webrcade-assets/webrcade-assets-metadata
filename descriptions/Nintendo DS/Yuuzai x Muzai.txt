Yuuzai x Muzai is an adventure game revolving around the Japanese legal system, allowing players to participate in courtroom scenarios and solve mysteries. The game aims to simulate the experience of a lay judge (saiban-in) in Japan's judicial process, where players must gather evidence, question witnesses, and make decisions on guilt or innocence.

The gameplay features a mix of investigation and courtroom drama, with players exploring crime scenes, collecting clues, and presenting their findings in court. The game also incorporates various puzzles and challenges to test the player's deductive skills. The storyline is designed to be immersive, providing a detailed look into the workings of the legal system and the moral dilemmas faced by judges.
