Go “Trick or Treating” with Mike and Sally in this fun-packed, all-action Halloween game. Collect lots of treats and even get tricked along the way. Bump into friendly ghosts, wicked witches, scary skulls & flapping bats! Explore the Haunted House of a nutty professor. 

Get trapped in, then escape from, a witch’s house in the Pumpkin Forest. Play the game over and over to get a higher score than your family and friends - but be careful you don’t get tricked too often... Ha ha ha haaaaaah!
