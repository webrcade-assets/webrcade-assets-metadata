Pro Yakyuu Famista DS is baseball game in the long-running Famista series, also known as Family Stadium. 

The gameplay involves traditional baseball mechanics where players can select teams, manage rosters, and compete in matches. The game includes a variety of teams and players, some of which are based on real Japanese baseball teams and athletes. The controls are designed to be accessible, allowing players to pitch, bat, and field with ease using the DS's controls. The game also features mini-games and challenges.
