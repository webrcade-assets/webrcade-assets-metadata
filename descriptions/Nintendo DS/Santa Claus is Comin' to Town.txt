Since 1970, children and adults have shared in the magic of Santa Claus is Comin’ to Town, the tale of how Santa Claus and many traditions came to be. This timeless classic brings families together year after year.

Santa Claus is Comin’ to Town provides a fun and interactive way of presenting this Christmas tale known by both children and their parents across the globe. Each interactive adventure can be read aloud by the parent or child. In addition, every scene of each adventure is presented as a fun and colorful illustration with many animated elements and interactive areas that will play enjoyable sounds and animations when touched.In addition to the wonderful adventures, there are a bunch of activities available to provide many hours of fun games for the child to play.
