Zenonia features Anime-style characters and graphics with numerous quests following an epic storyline which offers playing time of over 40 hours to conquer the quests. The player can choose from three different classes: warrior, paladin and assassin, and customise attributes. The game provides hundreds of different weapons and skill trees. The game also provides items that can be collected from five different classes (normal, magic, rare, unique and epic) to enchant or combine them for upgrades.
