Doodle Jump Journey was released on November 15, 2013. It was developed by Smoking Gun Interactive and published by Game Mill Entertainment for NDS.
Lima Sky's perennially best-selling "jump 'em up" comes to the Nintendo DS! Doodle Jump Journey features the franchise's three original themes (Classic, Jungle, and Space) in Endless Mode. And of course, it showcases the same endearing characters and art style that made the original game a hit.
