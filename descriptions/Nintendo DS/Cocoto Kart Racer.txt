Colorful cartoon characters keenly compete kart-to-kart.

Play as Cocoto or one of his 11 rivals across the Cocoto universe. Play alone or with a friend through Championship, Single Race, Time Trial, and Battle modes across and five distinct worlds.

• Four-player wireless multiplayer fun in Championship, Single Race, Time Trial, and Battle modes
• Race through three cups on five worlds: Abyss, Volcano, Atlantis, Jungle, and Heaven
• Play as one of 12 characters and unlock new cars for each one as you play! Ensure your survival through the use of deadly power-ups such as the homing missile
• Three difficulty levels
