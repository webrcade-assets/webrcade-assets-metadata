Let Lucille Werner take you to the world of Lingo. This Nintendo DS version of the popular game offers you as a player unprecedented possibilities to experience Lingo! Play the career mode and win several Lingo seasons to become the best Lingo player of all time. Features: - Test your knowledge of different dialects with the unique Lingo Dialects version - Dialects including Frisian, Gronings, Twents, Limburgs, Achterhoeks and Straattaal. - Very educational for children who learn to read and write. - A game suitable for the whole family. - 3 in 1: Lingo Original, Lingo for children and Lingo Dialect.
