The game is about surviving on an uncharted island. You control a character that has been washed ashore and help them to survive by building shelter, finding food and creating useful objects. Over the course of the game you meet other castaways and form friendships with them.

The game includes many mini games such as bug finding, fire starting and spear fishing. The game lets you craft furniture, create unique clothes, paint pictures and look for treasure. Unravel the ancient mysteries of the island and find a way back to civilization.
