Let's Play: Mums is another game of the Let's Play series. The series give the possibility to girls between 6 and 12 years old to discover the basics of some dream jobs for girls.

In this game, the player acts a young nanny and will have to take care of kids. Every morning, the schedule of the day is given to the player. The game's environment is a house in 3D, which the player can navigate using a point and click movement system. 

The game is composed of 11 minis games that are played with the stylus. To participate in a mini game, the player must go to the room of the house which is shown on the game map, displayed on the top screen of the DS. 

During the mini games, the player can feed the kids, change nappies, wash and dress the kids but also play with them.
