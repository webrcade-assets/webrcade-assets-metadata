Fight off hordes of the undead as they seek out the living in Zombiez Seeker. Struggle for survival as infected, snarling zombies set to turn you into a mindless decaying corpse with a hunger for human flesh. Armed with deadly weapons, you must fight your way through the living dead or find yourself walking among them.

Features

• Choose from 3 fatal weapons to annihilate zombies• Single player mode or play co-op with a friend• 5 levels of intense zombie combat in a graveyard, church and more!
