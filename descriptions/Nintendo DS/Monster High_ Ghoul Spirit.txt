Now you can join the student bodies with Monster High Ghoul Spirit, the Video Game. Mix and mingle with the Monster High guys and ghouls, perform creeparific activities, build a freaky-fab reputation and find out if you have what it takes to be crowned Scream Queen. Be the New Ghoul in School!

Features

- Start by creating a ghoul version of you, using tons of monsterific options. Choose your ghoul type, pick a scary-cute outfit and killer hairstyle, customize your skin color and select your very own freaky flaw.
- If you have a DSi camera, you can snap a picture and ghoulify yourself.
- Hang out with your new Monster High friends, perform scaretastic assignments (like a hair trim for Clawdeen or leading the Spirit Cheer) and go to classes to become an ogre achiever by earning ghastly grades.
- Just like normie high school, your goal is knock 'em dead with your sinister style, gain a spirited rep and complete for the scream queen crown at The Terror Under the Sea Dance.
