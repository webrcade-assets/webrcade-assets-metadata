Super Mario 64 is a Jump & Run which was developed and published by Nintendo in 1996 as one of the realease titles for the N64. Set in Princess Peach's Castle, Mario discovers that Bowser has invaded the castle and imprisoned the princess as well as her servants within it, using the power of the castle's 120 Power Stars. Mario searches the castle and discovers paintings which are portals to other worlds where Bowser & his minions guard the stars - encouraged to save Princess Peach, Mario embarks on a journey to recover all the Power Stars & defeat Bowser in his lair!
