Be sure to press your plaid slacks, because you're invited to rub elbows with some international swingers in the first-ever game to feature the PGA European license.

PGA European tour is a hard-core golf sim featuring four authentic European courses. Over 60 pros appear in the game, including Colin Montgomerie and Jose Maria Olazabal of recent Ryder Cup infamy. A complete list of available golfers is displayed on the right.
