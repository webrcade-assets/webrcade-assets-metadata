Wonder Project J2: Corlo no Mori no Josette ( J2 ?, lit. "Wonder Project J2: Josette of the Corlo Forest") is a Japan-exclusive Bishjo and raising simulation game developed by Givro and published by Enix (now Square Enix) for the Nintendo 64 in 1996. It is the sequel to Wonder Project J: Kikai no Shnen Pno, and was packaged with a game themed Controller Pak.
