NBA Live 99 is the 1999 installment of the NBA Live video games series. The cover features Antoine Walker of the Boston Celtics. Don Poier is the play-by-play announcer.
The game featured outdated rosters due to the 199899 NBA lockout. After the end of the lockout, a patch was issued for the PC version to update the rosters and add the 1998 NBA draftees to the game.
