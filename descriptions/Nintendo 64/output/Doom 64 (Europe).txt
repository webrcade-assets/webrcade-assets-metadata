Doom 64 is a First Person Shooter developed and released by Midway for the N64 in 1997. After the demonic invasion on Deimos & Phobos was averted, a single, mysterious entity, possessing the ability to resurrect any dead demon it came across, survived the bombardment by the United Aerospace Corporation and recreated the entire demonic horde - stronger and more vicious than ever before, they invade Earth to spread chaos and death! As the solely surviving marine of your troop you only got one mission left - exterminate all the demons and send them back to hell!
