Plundered Hearts is a text adventure by Infocom. A young woman in the 17th century travels to the West Indies island of St. Sinistra to visit her dying father who has contracted 'wasting tropical disease'. However, shortly upon setting off, her ship is attacked by pirates and she is kidnapped. She finds herself caught between two men striving for her affections, the dashing pirate Nicholas Jamison, and the conniving pirate Jean Lafond.
