Stationfall is a text adventure by Infocom that is the sequel to Planetfall. The ensign has now been promoted to Lieutenant First Class, but finds the Stellar Patrol to be less satisfying than before after being saddled with paperwork. This changes after being assigned to transport a spacetruck to a space station, as, upon arrival, it's discovered that the space station has mysteriously been deserted, with only an ostrich and an Arcturian balloon creature left behind.
