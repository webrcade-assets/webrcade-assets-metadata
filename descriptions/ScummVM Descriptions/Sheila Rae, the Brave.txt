Sheila Rae, the Brave was an interactive storybook for children in the Living Books series, created with the Mohawk Living Books player. The book is an interactive version of the Kevin Henkes children's book of the same name. Shiela Rae thinks she's not afraid of anything and makes fun of her sister when she gets scared. However, her braveness doesn't help her when she gets lost. As the story is read, the objects in the background are clickable.
