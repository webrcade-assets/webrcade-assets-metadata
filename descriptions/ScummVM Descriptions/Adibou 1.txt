Adibou 1 is an educational game destined to 4-7 years old by Coktel Vision that uses the Gob engine. The game is divided between an 'Environment' playground, including a series of mini-games (planting flowers, sliding puzzle, breakout game...) and four educational applications ('Read 4-5 years', 'Read 6-7 years', 'Count 4-5 years', 'Count 6-7 years') containing amusing math and reading exercises.
