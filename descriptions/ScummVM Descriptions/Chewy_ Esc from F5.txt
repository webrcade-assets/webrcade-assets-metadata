Chewy: Esc from F5, was a point and click adventure game that followed a pink alien named Chewy his partner Clint.  Their mission was to break into F5, the Borkian high security zone, and steal the powerful Red Glum to prevent the Borks from using it for evil. Clint stole the Red Glum and escaped the pursuing Borkian starships.  However, his ship got pulled into a wormhole, and crashed on the planet Earth.
