Quest for Glory III: Wages of War is the third game in the Quest for Glory series.  In this adventure/RPG hybrid, Rakeesh the Paladin sends the hero of Shapeir to his jungle homeland of Tarna, where war is about to erupt between the tribe of the Simbani and the Leopardmen.  Both people have stolen items from the other, and refuse to return them.  It is up to the hero to prevent a war and to ultimately stop a demon wizard.
