Simon the Sorcerer 2: The Lion, the Wizard, and the Wardrobe is the second game in the Simon the Sorcerer series, and the fifth game to use Adventure Soft's AGOS Engine. In the game, the evil sorcerer Sordid has returned to get his revenge on Simon.  Sordid sends a magical wardrobe to retrieve Simon, but due to an error the wardrobe ends up at Calypso's house.   Simon must once again defeat the evil Sordid and find a way back home.
