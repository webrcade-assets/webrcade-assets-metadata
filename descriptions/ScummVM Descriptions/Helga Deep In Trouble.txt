Helga Deep in Trouble is an adventure game that follows a woman named Helga, who used to be a manager of an indie game studio.  As that studio struggles to survive in a harsh economic climate, Helga is forced to leave her position and is replaced by an incompetent former secretary named Magda.  Helga decides to start her own studio, and hire her former co-workers, however not everything goes as smoothly as she had planned and she finds herself having to deal with many unexpected problems.
