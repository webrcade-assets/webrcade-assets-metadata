Crazy Nick's Software Picks: Roger Wilco's Spaced Out Game Pack was part of Sierra's Crazy Nick's Software Picks line of budget games.  The Crazy Nick's Software Picks games usually bundled the arcade game minigames from their game releases.  This bundle contains three minigames from Space Quest IV: Ms. Astro Chicken, the burger making minigame Monolith Burgers, and a minigame where you try to avoid rocks in your Sand Skimmer.
