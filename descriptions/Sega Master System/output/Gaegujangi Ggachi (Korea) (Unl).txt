Gaegujangi Ggachi is a platformer released in Korea in 1993 from the company HI-COM. The name of the game has been translated to mean "Ggachi, a naughty boy." It has also been translated as Mischevious Magpie. While the word Gachi means magpie (a bird) in Korea, in this case  is used for the character's name. It is a popular character from the comic books of Hyeon-se Lee. Kkachi is famous for his first TV animation "Tteodori Kkachi - Kkachi the wanderer" 1987, based on the comic book of same name.
