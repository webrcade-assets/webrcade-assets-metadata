Crossed Swords II is a sequel to Crossed Swords released exclusively for the Neo Geo CD. Nausizz makes a comeback with his castle emerging from the underworld right in the middle of Belkana. Three brave warriors then join forces to finally put an end to Nausizz's reign of terror. The game expands on the original's gameplay by adding a a new button for jumping and dashing, giving the player extra abilities and allowing the player to dodge incoming attacks. The game offers two game modes: "Nausizz Counterattack" which is the story mode and "Karividu Arena" which is a survival mode.
