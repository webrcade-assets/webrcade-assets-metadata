Ryuuko no Ken (龍虎の拳) is a fighting game by SNK originally released for the Neo Geo MVS arcade and AES home console in 1992. It was ported to, among other consoles, Arcade CD-ROM² hardware in 1994. In the West, it is known as Art of Fighting. This is the second fighting game to be developed by SNK, following Fatal Fury (which co-exists in the same fictional universe).
