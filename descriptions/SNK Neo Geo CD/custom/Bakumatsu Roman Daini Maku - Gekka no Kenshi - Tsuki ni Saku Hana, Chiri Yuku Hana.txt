Bakumatsu Roman Daini Maku - Gekka no Kenshi - Tsuki ni Saku Hana, Chiri Yuku Hana is a video game developed and released by SNK in 1998. Like its predecessor, Bakumatsu Roman - Gekka no Kenshi, it is a weapons-based versus fighting game originally released to arcades via the Neo Geo MVS arcade system, although it has since been released for various other platforms
