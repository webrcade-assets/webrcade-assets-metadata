Super Sidekicks 3: The Next Glory in Japan called Tokuten Ou 3: Eikoue no Michi (得点王3 栄光への挑戦?) is the third game in the Super Sidekicks series developed and published by SNK, for the Neo Geo platform on March 6, 1995. Super Sidekicks 3 was re-released a few years later as part of SNK Arcade Classics Vol. 1 for PlayStation 2, PlayStation Portable and Nintendo Wii.
