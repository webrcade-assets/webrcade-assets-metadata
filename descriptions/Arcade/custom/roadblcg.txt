Road Blasters &copy; 1987 Atari Games.

Road Blasters is a futuristic driving game in which the player must shoot rival racers and collect fuel globes to top up his ever-diminishing fuel supply. Power-up weapons are dropped from above and and must be &#039;caught&#039; by the player. Collision with enemy vehicles will result in the destruction of the player&#039;s own vehicle. Digitized speech, effects and music play throughout the game.
