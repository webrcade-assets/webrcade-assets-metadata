Shadaloo - The world's most dangerous crime-infested city located in Southeast Asia. When UN sent a team here to rescue the refugees, 63 of them were taken hostage by General Bison, the dictator of Shadaloo who owns a high-tech weapon army. Bison demanded a ransom of $20 billion for their release. Now the UN has decided to send an emergency force to Shadaloo under the command of Colonel Guile! As the entire world anxiously watches the situation unfold, the intentions, the fighting spirits, and the ambitions of the world warriors are ready to explode in Shadaloo!
