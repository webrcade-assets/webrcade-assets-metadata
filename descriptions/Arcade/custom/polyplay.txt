This game features 10 different games: Hase und Wolf [Rabbit and Wolf] (like "Pac-Man"), Hirschjagd [Deer hunt] (like "Robotron 2084"), Schmetterlinge [Butterflies], Abfahrtslauf [Downhill], Schiessbude [Shooting Gallery] (like "Carnival"), Autorennen [Auto Racing], Merkspiel [Memory], Wasserrohrbruch [Water Pipe Burst], Hagelnde Wolken [Hailing Clouds] (like "Space Invaders"), Der Taucher [The Diver].
