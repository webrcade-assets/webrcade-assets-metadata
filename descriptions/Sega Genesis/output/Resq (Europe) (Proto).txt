Ariadne, a peaceful mining colony on the fringes of the Condor system. It's people prospered in thriving cities. Until... Interplanetary corporate raiders from the Wellmax system planned a very hostile takeover. They trained their formidable Boetsky Fusion Brokers on the defenceless cities of Ariadne. A civilization that had taken hundreds of years to build..... ...was destroyed by the Fusion Brokers in nano seconds. A handful of surviving miners took refuge in the network of mines beneath the scorched earth. The Wellmax raiders despatched a crack team of mercenaries to carry out a mopping up operation. Under fire and under siege, the survivors' one remaining hope is a daring interplanetary rescue mission. But time is running out. The corporate raiders are rampaging across the planet. The rest is up to you!
