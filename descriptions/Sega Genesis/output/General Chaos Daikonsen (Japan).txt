Command the armies of General Chaos against General Havoc's troops in this multi-player action game of brute strength and awesome firepower! 1-4 player head-to-head and co-op play. Over 50 different battlefields. Four squads to choose from: the Assault Team, the Commandos, the Demolition Squad and the Brute Force Troop. Brutal hand-to-hand combat. Five unique weapon types: flame throwers, machine guns, bazookas, grenades, and dynamite.
