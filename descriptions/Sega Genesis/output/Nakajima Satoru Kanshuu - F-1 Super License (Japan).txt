Nakajima Satoru Kanshuu F1 Super License ( F-1 ) is a 1992 racing game by Varie for the Sega Mega Drive and the third Mega Drive game in their Nakajima Satoru Kanshuu games, following F1 Hero MD.

This game is a 2D racer, like F1 Grand Prix and unlike F1 Hero MD. B accelerates. This game adds a save system, allowing you to save your progress over the course of the prix.
