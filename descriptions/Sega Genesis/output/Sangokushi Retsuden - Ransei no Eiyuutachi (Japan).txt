Sangokushi Retsuden: Ransei no Eiyuutachi ( ) is a 1991 simulation game by Sega released in Japan  and China, where it was called San Guo Zhi Lie Zhuan: Luan Shi Qun Ying. Whether or not the game has any connections to Koei, who makes the most popular games based around China's Three Kingdoms time periods, is unknown.
