Shura no Mon is a 1992 strategic fighting game by Sega for the Sega Mega Drive based around Kodansha's Shura no Mon manga.

The game is a menu-driven fighting game. You can move around the field while a meter on the left side of the screen rises  this shows your attack charge level. Once your attack is powerful enough, hit C to open a menu of attacks, which you navigate to make an attack. A and B do something; exactly what is unknown.
