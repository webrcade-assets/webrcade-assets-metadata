Can Earth be doomed! The aliens have returned to waste the planet and no-one can stop them. Surely no human can defy this marauding evil from the other side of the galaxy. No human maybe. but there's one last chance. The new PROBOTECTOR, the result of Earth technology's finest scientists, is the planet's only hope. Now you must carry the challenge to the heart of the alien threat. Take control of the Probotector and banish the unearthly horde from our world forever.
