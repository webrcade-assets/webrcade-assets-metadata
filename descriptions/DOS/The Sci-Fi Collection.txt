The Sci-Fi Collection in Infocom's series combines five interactive text-based adventure games on CD-ROM. The featured titles are:

The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy
A Mind Forever Voyaging
Starcross
Stationfall
Suspended

Bonus games:

Beyond Zork
Planetfall

The games are compatible with Microsoft Windows or can be run directly in DOS. They are also compatible with Apple Macintosh computers.
