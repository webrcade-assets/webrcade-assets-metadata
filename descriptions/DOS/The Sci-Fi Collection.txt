The Sci-Fi Collection in Infocom's series combines five interactive text-based adventure games on CD-ROM. The featured titles are:The Hitchhiker's Guide to the GalaxyA Mind Forever VoyagingStarcrossStationfallSuspendedBonus games:Beyond ZorkPlanetfallThe games are compatible with Microsoft Windows or can be run directly in DOS. They are also compatible with Apple Macintosh computers.
