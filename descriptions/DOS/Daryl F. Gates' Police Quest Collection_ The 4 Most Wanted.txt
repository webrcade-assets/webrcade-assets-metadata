This entry in Sierra's Collectors Series bundled the three original Jim Walls -designed Police Quest adventure games in one package along with Daryl F. Gates' first addition to the series:

Police Quest: In Pursuit of the Death Angel (both the original EGA version and the VGA remake)
Police Quest 2: The Vengeance
Police Quest 3: The Kindred
Daryl F. Gates' Police Quest: Open Season

The package also includes a book which contains all the original manuals and hint materials, as well as an abridged version of the Los Angeles Police Department Manual and an interview with the new PQ designer Daryl F. Gates.
