Jungle is an individual release by B&N of the Jungle pinball table from Epic MegaGames ' Epic Pinball. The big selling point of B&N's games is they were run or installed directly from the floppy disc by just typing GO.
This table features Serpent Path, Death Blade Hole, and Frog Hole bonuses, in addition to Beehive Millions and an original soundtrack. As part of the Epic Pinball series of games it also has the regular Epic Pinball innovations such as a scrolling table and a dot-matrix look-alike display, as well as music.
If other games from the Epic Pinball series are installed in the game directory they are automatically detected and included in the game's menu system. The game is entirely keyboard controlled.
