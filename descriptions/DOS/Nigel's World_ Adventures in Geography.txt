Nigel's World: Adventures in Geography is an edutainment game where the main character, Nigel the Scotsman, needs to travel the world and take photographs of various subjects in an attempt to win first place in a photography contest. Nigel has a limited amount of time in each mission to take as many relevant pictures as possible and turn them in before the deadline. Part of the gameplay is figuring out where to go based on the clues and directions provided.
