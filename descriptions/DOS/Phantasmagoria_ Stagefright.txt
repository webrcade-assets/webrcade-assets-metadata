The Phantasmagoria Stagefright box contains 12 discs:Roberta Williams' Phantasmagoria (7 discs)Phantasmagoria: A Puzzle of Flesh (5 discs)Phantasmagoria is for Windows 3.1 and/or DOS 5.0 or higher, and Phantasmagoria: A Puzzle of Flesh is for Windows 95 and/or DOS 5.0 or higher. P1 needs 8MB of RAM and 2 times speed CD-ROM, and P2 requires 12MB of RAM and 4 times speed CD-ROM.
