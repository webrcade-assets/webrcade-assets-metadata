Match 'Em is a shareware, one or two player Concentration style game.It is played on an 8x8 grid and players take turns to reveal two pictures at a time. If they match the player gets another turn. The winner of the game is the player who matches the most pairs.The game is played entirely with the mouse, has optional sound effects, and three levels of difficulty. Each game can consist of either sixteen sets of four pictures or thirty-two sets of two pictures.
