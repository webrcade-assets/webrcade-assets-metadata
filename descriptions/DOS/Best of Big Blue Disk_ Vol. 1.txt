Best of Big Blue Disk: Vol. 1 is a compilation that covers the first year of Big Blue Disk publication and includes some of the editors' favorite games, applications and utilities they had published in that year. Games include Calc-Man, the editor's own Pac-Man -meets-Lotus-1-2-3 mashup, and Superdots, a digital interpretation of the classic graph-paper-based game Dots, played by bored middle-schoolers everywhere. It also includes other applications.
