This is the January/February 1998 issue (Volume 4) of the Best of Sierra series. This issue's title games are:

Space Quest VI: Roger Wilco in the Spinal Frontier
Lode Runner: The Legend Returns

In addition to these two full version games, the following demos are also included: 3-D Ultra Pinball: The Lost Continent and Print Artist 3.
The magazine's special features from this issue are the following:
A résumé of the story from Space Quest 1 to Space Quest 5 , a 8 pages long walkthrough for Space Quest VI: Roger Wilco in the Spinal Frontier, a 11 pages long walkthrough for selected levels from Lode Runner: The Legend Returns, and articles about the following 5 newest Sierra games: Diablo: Hellfire, Lords of Magic, Red Baron II, Birthright: The Gorgon's Alliance, and Betrayal in Antara.
