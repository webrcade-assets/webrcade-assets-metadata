Help! Charity Compilation is a British game compilation with all profits going towards child charities including ChildLine and The Prince's Trust. It contains eleven games with a mixture of adventure, platform, simulation and strategy games:
Adventure:

Jack in the Dark
The Lost Files of Sherlock Holmes: The Case of the Serrated Scalpel

Platform:

Prince of Persia
Pushover
The Humans

Simulation:

F1
Pinball Dreams
Pro Tennis Tour
Super-VGA Harrier
World Class Leader Board

Strategy:

Dune II: The Building of a Dynasty
