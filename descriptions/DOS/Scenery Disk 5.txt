Scenery Disk 5 is a scenery add-on for subLOGIC 's Flight Simulator II, Flight Assignment: Airline Transport Pilot, UFO, Jet, Thunderchopper, Stealth Mission, it can also be used with Microsoft Flight Simulator 2.x.
It covers the areas of Salt Lake City (some of the airports provided: Boise, Logan, Fairfield), Cheyenne (airports: Rapid City, Denver, Douglas), and Denver (airports: Aspen, Colorado Springs, Pueblo), adding airports, landmarks, visual detail and navigational aids.
There are 12 total Scenery Disks that cover the continental US, plus 4 special ones covering Western Europe, Japan, Hawaii and San Francisco. They are derived from National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration Sectional Aeronautical Charts and Airport/Facility Directories.
