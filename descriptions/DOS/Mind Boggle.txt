Mind Boggle is a collection of games that includes the following:

Hi-Que : a variant of the classic Hi-Q board game
Flipper
Reflector Detector : a game where atoms are hidden in an 8x8 square, and the player has to discover their position by seeing how they interact with rays that the player projects from the sides of the box
Master Memory : a Mastermind variant
Dr. Frank - a variant of Hangman
