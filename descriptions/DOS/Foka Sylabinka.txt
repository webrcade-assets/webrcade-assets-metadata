Foka Sylabinka is an educational game designed to teach basic reading. The game has 3 game modes: maze, one word, two syllables.In the maze mode, the player has to reach two parts of the word, located in different parts of the maze.In one word mode, the player has to match the word to the picture out of three available.In two-syllable mode, the player must match the two syllables that make up a word by choosing from the four syllables available on the screen.The game has a built-in word and picture editor.
