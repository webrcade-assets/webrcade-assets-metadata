This box contains the first seven installments of King's Quest adventure series and a sneak preview of King's Quest: Mask of Eternity. In addition to that, the collection features three more games:

Roberta Williams' King's Quest I: Quest for the Crown
King's Quest II: Romancing the Throne
King's Quest III: To Heir is Human
King's Quest IV: The Perils of Rosella
King's Quest V: Absence Makes the Heart Go Yonder!
King's Quest VI: Heir Today, Gone Tomorrow
Roberta Williams' King's Quest VII: The Princeless Bride
The Colonel's Bequest
The Dagger of Amon Ra
Mixed-Up Mother Goose Deluxe
