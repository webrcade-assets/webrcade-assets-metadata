This unofficial scenery disk for the Microsoft Flight Simulator 5 brings you the full spectrum of San Francisco's area. Soar from San Jose to Marin County and from Napa Valley to the Golden Gate.Along your flight, enjoy cities like Oakland, Livermoore and Pleasanton. Take off from a variety of different airports including San Jose, Oakland, Alameda NAS, Half Moon Bay, Travis Air Force Base, all of them complete with runways, taxiways, main buildings, lighting and NAVAIDS.
