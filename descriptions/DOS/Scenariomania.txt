Scenariomania is an unofficial Add On CD containing about 650 maps for 5 different games. Furthermore, it also includes Level-, Savegame-Editors, and Trainers. But none of these were particularly designed for this CD, just collected from different sources and bundled together.The 5 games in detail:Command & Conquer (89 maps)Descent (62 maps)SimCity 2000 (34 cities)Star Wars: Dark Forces (5 maps)WarCraft II: Tides of Darkness (354 maps)
