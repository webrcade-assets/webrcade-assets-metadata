Vardit VeHaShokolada BeMasluley HaOlympiada was released as a part of a marketing campaign of Vered Hagalil, an Israeli dairy product manufacturer, around the 1996 Summer Olympics.The game is one out of three games which together form the Vardit Adventures (the other two were released separately on a different floppy) .In this game, Vardit has to run and complete an Olympic course while avoiding various related obstacles and balls, and collect a set amount of chocolate packets in the process.
