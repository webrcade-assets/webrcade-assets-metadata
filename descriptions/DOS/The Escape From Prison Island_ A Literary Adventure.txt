The Escape From Prison Island: A Literary Adventure is a single player, freeware, keyboard controlled text adventure.
In this adventure the player is tried and found guilty of one of the worst crime that has ever been recorded. The sentence is that you are to be transported to Prison Island for the rest of your life. However there is a get-out clause, there is way to escape and to do that you must find your favourite book.
As the title implies this adventure includes references to many books as the player must not only find and use their favourite book they must find the favourite book for other prisoners as well.
