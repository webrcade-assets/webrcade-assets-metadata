The demo version of The Settlers II: Veni, Vidi, Vici features a unique bonus scenario which is not found in the retail game. The scenario is called Initial Contact, similar to the second chapter from the full version's campaign, however the map is completely different. There are two computer opponents: a Viking tribe and a Nubian tribe.The demo version also contains a tutorial scenario, which is identical to the first chapter of the retail campaign. Additionally, the demo version includes a non-interactive preview that illustrates the features of the game.
