Lazarus is a platform game with some adventure elements. The action takes place in a not specified future. The story starts when the player buys a new game called Lazarus. The game loading process transfer the player to the virtual world. The aim of the game is to find the exit, traversing through several worlds inhabited by hostile creatures and full of deadly traps. Getting access to the next realms requires execution of various tasks, finding appropriate items and reaching the teleport. The player may use five types of weapons (shotgun, rocket launcher, UZI, basalt launcher, plasma launcher) and a jet pack. Ammo and fuel may be found in different places of the realms or purchased from the merchant and in the supply coin-op machine for gained coins.
