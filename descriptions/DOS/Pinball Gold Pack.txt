The Pinball Gold Pack collects all 20 tables from the first five pinball compilations by 21st Century Entertainment onto one CD. Included are:The Dreams Tables : Beat Box, Ignition, Nightmare, and Steel Wheel.The Fantasies Tables : Billion Dollar Game Show, Party Land, Speed Devils, and Stones 'n Bones.The Illusions Tables : Babewatch, Extreme Sports, Law 'n Justice, and The Vikings.The Mania Tables : Jackpot, Jailbreak, Kick Off, and Tarantula.The Dreams II Tables : Neptune, Robot Warrior, Safari, and Stall Turn.
