Gamblers And Money Grubbers is a collection of three shareware games from Hyperware, collectively they are referred to as 'G&G Games'. 
The games in this collection consist of:

Gamblers And Money Grubbers - Craps later released by Villa Crespo under the name Casino Craps
Gamblers And Money Grubbers - Friday Night Poker
Gamblers And Money Grubbers - Blackjack

The games could be purchased individually or as a package.
