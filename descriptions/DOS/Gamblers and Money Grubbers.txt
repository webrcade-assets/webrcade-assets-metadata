Gamblers And Money Grubbers is a collection of three shareware games from Hyperware, collectively they are referred to as 'G&G Games'. The games in this collection consist of:Gamblers And Money Grubbers - Craps later released by Villa Crespo under the name Casino CrapsGamblers And Money Grubbers - Friday Night PokerGamblers And Money Grubbers - BlackjackThe games could be purchased individually or as a package.
