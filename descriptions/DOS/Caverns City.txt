Caverns City is a text adventure in the Battune series.This adventure sees the player and Battune their friendly alien guide exploring the eponymous Caverns City. The game starts when the player and their guide Battune of Amaphore arrive in the city. The first challenge is to make a reservation at the hotel. Once in the hotel they must find a way to the caverns, through the caverns and beyond.This is a text adventure, there are no graphics, and all commands must be entered in upper case. It is possible to die in this game
