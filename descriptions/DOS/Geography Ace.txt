Geography Ace single player, shareware, keyboard controlled, educational quiz game covering both world geography and U.S. geography.The game has two levels of difficulty. Questions are shown one at a time and they take the form "Germany is on which continent?"; "What is the state capital of Montana?", "What is the capital of Saudi Arabia?". These are not multiple choice questions - all answers questions have to be typed in. The questions are not timed.After five turns the game asks the player if they wish to continue and this repeats until the player decides to quit at which point their final score is given.
