Vietnam is a historical combat flight simulation that was created with Domark Software's Flight Sim Toolkit. The game allows to pilot either the F-4 Phantom or the A-6 Intruder for the US/South Vietnam side in various missions set during the Vietnam War.The simulator was distributed as shareware. The unregistered version contained a couple of missions, with more scenarios available in the full game, including escort, rescue and other types of objectives. Registered users would also receive programme updates and a printed manual.
