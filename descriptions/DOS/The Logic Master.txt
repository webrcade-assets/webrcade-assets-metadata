The Logic Master is a series of educational games for multiple systems.The player challenges a number of educational logic games:Analogies: Find the 2 similar words from a list.Number Series: Complete a number series by filling in the last 2 numbers.The Quest: Your quest is to locate the Dragon's Lair, which is hidden deep within the labyrinth. You must move through a series of tunnels, and fight or avoid the monsters that patrol the labyrinth. You can fight the labyrinth monsters by using your hero's ranged weapons. These weapons are found within chests that can be opened by correctly answering questions. Magical objects can be found in the labyrinth to help, including the magic amulet, health potion, and magic globe. The goal is to find the dragon, successfully answer its questions, and receive the treasure. There are 3 levels of difficulty.
