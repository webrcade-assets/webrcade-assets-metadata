Video Vegas is a gambling video game for multiple systems.Players start with an initial $1000 on their visit to the Las Vegas casinos. The player can participate in several gambling options, including: Lucky 7 Slots, Draw Poker, Keno, and Blackjack. In Lucky 7 Slots, the player can inset $1 - 3 for a chance to win big. A number of combinations can turn up lucky for the player and lead to big cash. In Draw Poker, The player bets $1 - 5 and then receives 5 cards. The player can hold or draw new cards once, and payout is based upon the player's hand. In Keno, the player bets $1 - 5, and can select 1 - 15 numbers from a total of 80. 20 numbers are drawn, and a player is rewarded based upon how many they pick correctly. Finally, there's Blackjack, were the player and computer draw cards trying to get as close to 21 without going over.
