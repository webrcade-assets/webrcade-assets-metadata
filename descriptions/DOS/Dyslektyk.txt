Dyslektyk is a set of games that allows oppose dyslexia, through the exercise of vision, memory, hearing, concentration and orthography. The set features following mini games:Two exercises of spelling, where the child must provide the missing letters;Image arrangement from the cut parts;Two exercises of combining images in pairs;Two exercises of sounds repeating.Each exercise can be configured in terms of resources and level of difficulty. The game allows to create several child data profiles.
