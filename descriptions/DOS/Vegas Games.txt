Vegas Games is a series of casino games for multiple systems.
Contains graphical versions of draw poker, blackjack, craps, keno, spin-to-win, horse racing, and slot machine.  Each gambling program has individual instructions, and the player can modify starting funds and value of bets. Spin-to-win is a 2-player phrase-guessing game similar to Wheel of Fortune.  Horse racing allows the player to bet on animated horses as they run across the screen.
