MER Adventure Climbing is an advertisement game where the player controls a free climber and the aim is to reach the top of Mt. Guano. Climbing is done by the player by clicking one one of the climbers four extremities, hold in the mouse button and move selected extremities to a suitable crack in the rock wall. At the bottom of the screen two meters can be found: one for energy and one for fatigue. Fatigue is best keeping low by always keep both feet and hands in cracks not too far apart. Energy can be replenished by drinking MER that can be found on cliff shelves here and there.
