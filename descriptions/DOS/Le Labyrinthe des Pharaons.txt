"Le Labyrinthe des Pharaons" is a French educational game in 'Le Labyrinthe' series intended for school pupils of 10 to 12 years of age.
As a messenger of Anubis who has to restore the balance of Justice that was violated by the evil wizard named Khian, we will travel in space and time witnessing some important events in the history of Ancient Egypt, meeting prominent personalities - and making some key decisions.
Of course, the historical information is quite vast, and the game features several quizzes based on the events witnessed and things learnt.
