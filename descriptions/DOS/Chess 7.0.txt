Chess 7.0 is a chess playing tool for multiple systems.
Allows for 2-player chess play, or 1-player versus the computer with multiple available difficulty levels. Allows for graphical representation of the chess board, and follows standard chess rules. Unique gameplay options include the ability to flip the board, as well as allowing blind play were the rank of different pieces is hidden. The computer can optionally list best moves for players, and games in process can be saved to disk.
