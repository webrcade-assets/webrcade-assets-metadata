Apollo Collection 1: Airbus Family is a set of sixteen aircraft in an add-on package, requiring the 3rd-party application BAO FlightShop, to use with Microsoft Flight Simulator v5.x.
The included aircraft are:

Airbus A340-300
Airbus A330-300
Airbus A321-100
Airbus A320-200
Airbus A310-200
Airbus A300-600
DHC-2 Beaver
DHC-3 Otter
DHC-5 Buffalo
DHC-6 Twin Otter
DHC-7 Dash 7
Boeing B-17 Flying Fortress 'Tiger Bird'
Boeing B-24 Liberator
Boeing B-29 Superfortress 'Enola Gay'
Lockheed C-130 Hercules
Avro Lancaster 'Miss-Fit'
