Tommy's Gorilla Ball Pak is a shareware compilation featuring ten DOS games. The games included in this compilation are: Tommy's Gorilla Balls Tommy's Gorilla Ball Pythons Tommy's Gorilla Ball Ramparts Tommy's Packrat Tommy's Gorilla Ball Gauntlet Tommy's Gorilla Ball Babies Tommy's Gorilla Ball Train Tommy's Gorilla Ball Gotcha Tommy's Gorilla Ball Tag Tommy's Jupiter Jet
The player could play these games for twenty-one days after which a registration fee was required.
