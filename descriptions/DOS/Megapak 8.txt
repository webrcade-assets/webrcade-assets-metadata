Megapak 8 is the eighth installment in Megamedia's Megapak -Compilation series. This compilation includes 9 games and 1 other compilation (with 15 games) from many different genres.
These 9 games are included:

Circle of Blood
iM1A2 Abrams
Jack Nicklaus 4
Jagged Alliance: Deadly Games
Master of Orion II: Battle at Antares
MechWarrior 2: 31st Century Combat
Return to Zork
Screamer 2
SimCity 2000 Network Edition

German versions of this compilation replaced Screamer 2 with Rallye Racing 97.
The included compilation is Activision's Atari 2600 Action Pack, which contains an Atari 2600 emulator and Atari 2600 ROMs of the following 15 games:

Boxing
Chopper Command
Cosmic Commuter
Crackpots
Fishing Derby
Freeway
Frostbite
Grand Prix
H.E.R.O.
Kaboom!
Pitfall!
River Raid
Seaquest
Sky Jinks
Spider Fighter
