This part of Crazy Nick's Software Picks line of budget games introduces Roger Wilco from Space Quest IV: Roger Wilco and the Time Rippers and Space Quest I: Roger Wilco in the Sarien Encounter. The player tries to help him in three mini-games:Ms. Astro Chicken, where the player needs to fly as a chicken on side scrolling screen, avoiding the foxes flying in the opposite direction and laying eggs over dogs with hunters shooting from the ground;Monolith Burger, where the player needs to compose a proper burger, adding all necessary ingredients while it is rolling to its package;Sand Skimmer, where the player needs to reach the Ulence Flats riding the skimmer in a desert of planet Kerona and avoiding the obstacles on his way.
