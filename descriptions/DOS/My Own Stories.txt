My Own Stories is an adaptation of Storybook Weaver - the educational game/application that allows children of 8 to 14 years of age to write their own stories. Now those stories are based not on fairy tales, but on everyday contemporary situations.The objects present as possible illustrations for stories depict real life experiences: city, town, school, gym, sports, beach, backyard etc. Change sizes and colors of the objects put on pages is also possible, as well as adding music and sound effects and printing all the results.
