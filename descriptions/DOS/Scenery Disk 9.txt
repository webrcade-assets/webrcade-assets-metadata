Scenery Disk 9 is a scenery add-on for subLOGIC's Flight Simulator II, Flight Assignment: Airline Transport Pilot, UFO, Jet, Thunderchopper, and Stealth Mission. It can also be used with Microsoft Flight Simulator 2.x. It covers the areas of Chicago, St. Louis and Cincinnati, adding airports, landmarks, visual detail and navigational aids.
There are 12 Scenery Disks in total that cover the continental US, plus 4 special ones covering Western Europe, Japan, Hawaii and San Francisco. They are derived from National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration Sectional Aeronautical Charts and Airport/Facility Directories.
