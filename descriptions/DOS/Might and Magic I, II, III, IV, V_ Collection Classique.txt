Might and Magic Collection Classique is a compilation on a single CD of the following games:

Might and Magic: Secret Of The Inner Sanctum - English version
Might and Magic II: Gates to Another World - English version
Might and Magic III: Isles of Terra - English and French versions
Might and Magic IV: Clouds of Xeen - English and French versions
Might and Magic V: Darkside of Xeen - English and French versions

The manuals for all of these games (on English and French languages) are on the CD.
