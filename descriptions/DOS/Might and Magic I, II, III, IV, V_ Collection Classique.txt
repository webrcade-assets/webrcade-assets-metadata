Might and Magic Collection Classique is a compilation on a single CD of the following games:Might and Magic: Secret Of The Inner Sanctum - English versionMight and Magic II: Gates to Another World - English versionMight and Magic III: Isles of Terra - English and French versionsMight and Magic IV: Clouds of Xeen - English and French versionsMight and Magic V: Darkside of Xeen - English and French versionsThe manuals for all of these games (on English and French languages) are on the CD.
