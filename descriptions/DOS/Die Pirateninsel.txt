Have you ever played Boulder Dash? If so, Die Pirateninsel is an very similar game to Boulder Dash.
In this puzzle-solving arcade game for 1 or 2 players (against each other or as team), you have to master 100+ levels in 5 different areas (a temple, a pirate's cove, the dschungle) to reach your aim: Winning an huge pirate's treasure.
Collect items while avoiding tricky traps, fallen stones and enemies and squares that redirect or push you in several destinations. Collect all items in a level and then find the exit to the next one.
