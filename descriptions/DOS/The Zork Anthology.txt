Released just after the 1994 Return to Zork, this is a compilation of all previous Zork games, including:Zork: The Great Underground EmpireZork II: The Wizard of FrobozzZork III: The Dungeon MasterBeyond Zork: The Coconut of QuendorZork Zero: The Revenge of MegabozPlanetfall (bonus)Several versions of The Zork Anthology were also shrinkwrapped along with Return to Zork and sold together.
