Released just after the 1994 Return to Zork, this is a compilation of all previous Zork games, including:

Zork: The Great Underground Empire
Zork II: The Wizard of Frobozz
Zork III: The Dungeon Master
Beyond Zork: The Coconut of Quendor
Zork Zero: The Revenge of Megaboz
Planetfall (bonus)

Several versions of The Zork Anthology were also shrinkwrapped along with Return to Zork and sold together.
