White Wolf Software: Series XVI is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label.
This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: Alien Carnage

B: Cosmo's Cosmic Adventure
C: Night Raid
D: Zone 66 episode one only
E: Moraff's Super Entrap
F: Dork's Dreams
G: Tommy's Backgammon
H: Rapid Response
I: Organize Your Photographs: a self explanatory, non-gaming title from H. C. P. Services
J: Star Goose

The software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
