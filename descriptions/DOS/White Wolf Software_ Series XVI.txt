White Wolf Software: Series XVI is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label.This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: Alien CarnageB: Cosmo's Cosmic AdventureC: Night RaidD: Zone 66 episode one onlyE: Moraff's Super EntrapF: Dork's DreamsG: Tommy's BackgammonH: Rapid ResponseI: Organize Your Photographs: a self explanatory, non-gaming title from H. C. P. ServicesJ: Star GooseThe software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
