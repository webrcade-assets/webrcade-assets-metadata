Ultimate Flight Pack offers the player five flight simulations, from three different categories. Two games are military airplane simulations, two games are military helicopter simulations, and the last one is a civil airplane simulation.
The two airplane flight simulations are:

iF-16
Tornado including the Operation Desert Storm add-on

The two helicopter flight simulations are:

Apache Longbow
Hind: The Russian Combat Helicopter Simulation

And the civil airplane flight simulation is:

Flight Unlimited
