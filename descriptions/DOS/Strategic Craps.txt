Strategic Craps is a betting simulator and craps game.Designed to teach players the game of craps, and help them improve their technique. Numerous betting strategies and tutorials are embedded within the game, and the player can also play individual games of craps to test their technique. The craps board is graphically displayed on the screen during gameplay.The player can also create a craps strategy, where they bet specific ways for different rolls and situations. The player can then simulate a number of rounds to see how their strategy performs, and their expected gambling returns.
