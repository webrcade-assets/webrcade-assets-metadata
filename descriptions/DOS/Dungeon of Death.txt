The kingdom needs a new king, and first person to descend into the dungeon, retrieve the crown, and escape will become that new king.Dungeon of Death is a first-person RPG with a single character. Before entering the dungeon, the player is given the ability to buy various supplies, such as food, healing potions, or crossbow bolts. Then they enter the dungeon, to either emerge as the new king, or die trying. Their only weapon is a crossbow, with a limited number of bolts, and their attacks are manually aimed; they set the horizontal and vertical of the bolt, then let it fly.Dungeon of Death was distributed as shareware, but without any missing features.
