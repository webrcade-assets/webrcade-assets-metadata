Paiteo: Yeong-ung-eul Gidalimyeo, rendered in English as Fighter: Waiting for Hero, is a one-on-one versus fighting game with hand-drawn characters and backgrounds, similar to Street Fighter II. According to the official description, the story takes place in 1999 when a certain "dark organization" attempts to conquer the world, but this plan may be thwarted by an outcome of a fighting tournament. There are eight playable characters. The game supports human versus human play over serial cable.
