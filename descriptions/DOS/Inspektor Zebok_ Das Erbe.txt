You are playing as Inspector Zebok who has a new case to solve: the death of a notary - natural death or murder?The gameplay consists basically of three actions: going to certain destinations and search them, questioning witnesses and giving orders to your staff. When searching a place you have to click on interesting items in the video window and watch a video of Zebok's actions. It is possible to miss clues and make the game impossible to beat.At the start of the game you have to choose your staff of six people out of about 20. You can't use them anytime, just at certain places.
