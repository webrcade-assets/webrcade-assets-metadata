Poker Challenge: 7 Card Stud Version is one of a series of poker games byThe WorkWare Co.. This,Poker Challenge: Texas Hold'em Version and Vegas Pro Video Poker were released as shareware, if the player registered any one game they were sent discs containing all three.In this game, the player starts with $500 and plays against up to seven other computer opponents in a game of 7 Card Stud. The game is entirely keyboard-controlled. It has basic sound effects, beeps and so-on, and a help option. When a game is restarted, the player can choose whether they wish to start with their pot from the previous game or start afresh.
