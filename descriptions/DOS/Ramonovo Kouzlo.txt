Ramonovo Kouzlo is a point-and-click adventure game. The game's graphics are composed entirely from photographic backgrounds, with no animation. The game features descriptions of the items and characters, puzzles and very simple dialogue options for conversations.
The game is set in Nové Město nad Metují, where the evil wizard Ramon settled and enslaved good elves. The role of your nameless hero is to drive Ramon out of town and to save the elves from the curse.
