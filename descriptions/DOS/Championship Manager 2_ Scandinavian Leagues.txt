This is Championship Manager 2 featuring the Danish, Norwegian, and Swedish leagues.The core of the game is its database containing more than 15,000 players from all over the world. Your job is to find and buy new talents, find new staff members, choose the right tactics, make substitutions and a lot of other decisions related to being the manager of a football club. It is possible to take control of the teams from the three best divisions in each of these countries.Which players to buy can be evaluated based on the player's rating in approximately 20 different categories (e.g. an attacker needs to have a high value in finishing, whereas a defender needs a high value in tackling).As with the other games in the series, the presentation is based on a huge amount of numbers. Graphics and sounds are kept to a minimum.
