Vegas Video Poker is a 1-player simulation of video poker for multiple systems.The player starts with $100, and chooses their bet at the beginning of each hand. The player starts with 5 cards, and can discard 0 - 5 cards depending upon their wishes. The player is rewarded monetarily depending upon their hand, with the player able to choose from two different reward schemes. The game is over when the player runs out of money or cashes out.
