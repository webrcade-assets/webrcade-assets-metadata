The first add-on to Earth 2140 features two new campaigns with 25 missions for each faction and a new soundtrack.It also adds three difficulty-levels (Easy, Normal and Hard) to the main game as well as a statistical overview of the battle which is now shown after each match. In addition there are 20 new multiplayer-maps and many smaller improvements (including better multiplayer-performance) to the main game.Inside the retail box there's also an Earth 2140-Mousepad and a music-video on the CD.
