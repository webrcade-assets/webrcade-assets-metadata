Operation: Fighting Tiger is the third add-on for Falcon 3.0. Three new scenarios are included:Japan vs Russia in a fight over the KurilsA second Korea WarAn all-out conflict involving Pakistan and IndiaAdditional planes introduced in these scenarios:MiG-31 Foxhound, Mirage 2000, F-5E Tiger II, J-7 Airguard, Q-5 Fantan, Jaguar, Mitsubishi F-1, Unmanned fighter (Russian experimental fighter), FSX (Japanese modified F-16). As there are a lot of naval missions, Harpoon anti-ship missile has been added to the arsenal.Unlike previous add-ons for Falcon 3.0, Operation: Fighting Tiger integrates itself into the base game instead of being launched separately.
