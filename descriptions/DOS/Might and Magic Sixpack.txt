The Might and Magic Sixpack contains the first six games in the Might and Magic series. Those are:

Might and Magic: Book I
Might and Magic II: Gates to Another World
Might and Magic III: Isles of Terra
Might and Magic 4: Clouds of Xeen
Might and Magic 5: Darkside of Xeen
Might and Magic VI: The Mandate of Heaven

Depending on the release, additionally included titles are:

Might and Magic: World of Xeen (merged new game of Clouds of Xeen and Darkside of Xeen)
Might and Magic: Swords of Xeen (a fan-made modification of World of Xeen)
