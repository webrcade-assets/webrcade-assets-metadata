The Might and Magic Sixpack contains the first six games in the Might and Magic series. Those are:Might and Magic: Book IMight and Magic II: Gates to Another WorldMight and Magic III: Isles of TerraMight and Magic 4: Clouds of XeenMight and Magic 5: Darkside of XeenMight and Magic VI: The Mandate of HeavenDepending on the release, additionally included titles are:Might and Magic: World of Xeen (merged new game of Clouds of Xeen and Darkside of Xeen)Might and Magic: Swords of Xeen (a fan-made modification of World of Xeen)
