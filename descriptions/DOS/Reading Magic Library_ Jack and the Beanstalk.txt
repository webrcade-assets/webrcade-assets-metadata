Reading Magic Library: Jack and the Beanstalk is a multiple-choice interactive story for the Apple II.
The player takes on the role of Jack, in a science fiction retelling of the story of Jack and the Beanstalk. Help Jack rescue the young goose that has been kidnapped by a giant living in the clouds. During the adventure, the player has multiple branch points which can effect the story, but ultimately the story always has the same end point.
