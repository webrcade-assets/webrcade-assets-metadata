BARS is a match three puzzle game made in Iceland. Four bars made of flags and other symbols are growing bigger from the bottom of the screen. Your task is to order the stones by color and to destroy enough stones before one of the bars touches the ceiling. The gameplay is very fast and requires quick thinking.There are 150 levels in three gameplay modes with limited time or where you have to destroy a given number of stones or stones of specific colors. Every mode can be played with normal or crazy rules with a lot of bonus stones and crazy things happening like the screen going upside down. The registered version includes an editor to create own stones.
