Hopper is a Frogger clone for multiple systems.
The player controls a young frog, who must cross several lanes of busy traffic, and then hop across logs and lilypads in a raging river to to try to get to their home at the top of the screen. There are a total of 5 homes at the top of the screen, and the player advances to a more difficult level if they manage to get a frog into each of the five spots. The player has limited time units to get a frog into each spot, or they lose a frog. The player has a total of 5 lives, and it's game over when all are lost.
