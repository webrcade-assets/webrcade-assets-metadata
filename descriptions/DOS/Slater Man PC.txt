Slater Man PC is a side-scrolling platform game. The goal of the game is to gather all the fuel canisters knocked down during the titular hero's careless ride across the galaxy. The game features 15 levels with 3 different graphic scenery's, where the player must collect a specified number of fuel canisters and reach the end of the level to advance to the next planet before the time runs out. The task is complicated by numerous animals and hunters. Contact with them means loss of energy. The hostile creatures may be killed with various limited ammo guns.
