Bicycle Poker is a single player, mouse controlled game in which the player takes on seven AI opponents, everyone starts with a stake of $500.
The game plays either Draw Poker or Stud Poker and has many options to customise. For example in Anything Opens and Jackpots variations to the main game, customisable betting and raising limits, a choice of wild cards or no wild cards and so forth. The game also maintains statistics and a limited history for each player.
