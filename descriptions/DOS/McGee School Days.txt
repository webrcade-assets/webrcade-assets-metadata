McGee School Days is an edutainment title for young children, aged two to six, with the aim of preparing them for school. It contains mini games of hop-scotch and I-Spy and teaches basic shape and colour recognition, counting to ten. The screens contain 'hot-spots' that trigger little animations to"... encourage a sense of exploration."
The CD also includes the software to set up a Compuserve account and Compuserve's WOW! for Windows 95.
