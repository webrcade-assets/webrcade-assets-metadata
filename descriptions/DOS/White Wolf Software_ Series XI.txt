White Wolf Software: Series XI is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label.This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: Hocus PocusB: Major StrykerC: FacetrexD: Bolo BallE: Hoosier CityF: Moraff's PinballG: Isle WarsH: Organize Books & Mags: a self explanatory, non-gaming title from H. C. P. ServicesI: Gamebuilder LiteJ: In Search of Dr. RiptideThe software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
