White Wolf Software: Series XI is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label.
This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: Hocus Pocus

B: Major Stryker
C: Facetrex
D: Bolo Ball
E: Hoosier City
F: Moraff's Pinball
G: Isle Wars
H: Organize Books & Mags: a self explanatory, non-gaming title from H. C. P. Services
I: Gamebuilder Lite
J: In Search of Dr. Riptide

The software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
