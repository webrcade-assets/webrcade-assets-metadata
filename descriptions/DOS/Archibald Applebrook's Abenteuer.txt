You control Archibald Applebrook, a Scottish man who works in a library and is bored of his job. All day he dreams of exciting adventures, what drives his boss crazy. Through luck you get a book in your hands which describes intriguing happenings on an expedition. This diary is the starting point of your adventure, which sends you all around the world.Archibald Applebrook's Abenteuer is a comic adventure with a very simple interface. If you click with the right mouse button on a hotspot you will open a small menu that lets you choose between various actions. In some situations, you can switch between different characters.
