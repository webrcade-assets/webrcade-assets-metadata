Black Jack '2000 is a computer version of the popular card game of the same name. The game features three gameplay modes: single, tournament, league. Single mode allows to play up to two players on a same screen. Tournament and league mode allows to play up to five players. The tournament is played by a classical tree system with sixteen players. League introduces standard table with games, wins and points. Each match consist of eleven deals. The game allows editing cards score and computer player's name. The game has no music or sound effects.
