This official scenery disk for Microsoft Flight Simulator (at least Version 5.0 is required) adds the Japanese Scenario and flight paths for your MS Flight Simulator.
From Okinawa to Hokkaido, soar over majestic Mount Fuji and the Fuji lakes. Navigate your aircraft over the hundreds of beautiful islands forming the Pacific Rim of Japan, all based on real satellite data. Access to every Japanese airport including Tokyo and New Tokyo International (Haneda and Narita), Osaka International and Chitose (Sapporo). See the vivid details of Tokyo and Kyoto.
