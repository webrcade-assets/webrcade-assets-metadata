IndyCar Circuits Expansion Pack is an track pack for Papyrus' IndyCar Racing (required) adding the following 7 circuits, each a replica of the actual circuit, to the game:

Burke Lakefront Airport (Cleveland, Ohio)
Phoenix International Raceway (Phoenix, Arizona)
Surfers Paradise (Queensland, Australia)
The Raceway on Belle Isle Park (Detroit, Michigan)
Road America (Elkhart Lake, Wisconsin)
Mid-Ohio Sports Car Course (Lexington, Ohio)
Pacific Place (Vancouver, British Columbia)

It includes also the latest improvements for the original game: Quick-Motion cam / Improved Modem functions / Editor for Replays / Print options for car setups / Playable season with the new tracks.
