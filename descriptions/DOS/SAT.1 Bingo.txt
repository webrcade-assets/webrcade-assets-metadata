This is the official game to the German SAT.1 TV Show "Bingo". To win the game you have to answer questions of several kinds: Nature, Sports, TV, Music, Geography etc.You have a board with 25 squares (5x5 fields). Each question has a number between 1 and 25. For every correct answer you get a mark on your square field.  To win the game you must get 5 marks in a row (horizontal, vertical or diagonal).
