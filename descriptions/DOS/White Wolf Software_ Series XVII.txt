White Wolf Software: Series XVII is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label. This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: God of ThunderB: FintrisC: World QuestD: BrainstormE: Skunny KartF: Operation Just BecauseG: Organize Your Comic Books: a self explanatory, non-gaming titleH: TriboloI: Women of the World TriviaJ: Organize Your Comic Books: another self explanatory, non-gaming titleThe software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
