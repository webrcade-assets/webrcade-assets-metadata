White Wolf Software: Series XVII is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label. This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: God of Thunder

B: Fintris
C: World Quest
D: Brainstorm
E: Skunny Kart
F: Operation Just Because
G: Organize Your Comic Books: a self explanatory, non-gaming title
H: Tribolo
I: Women of the World Trivia
J: Organize Your Comic Books: another self explanatory, non-gaming title

The software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
