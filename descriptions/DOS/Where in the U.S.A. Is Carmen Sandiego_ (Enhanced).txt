This time you continue to try to catch Carmen Sandiego and her American gang in the United States. Breaking out of jail somewhere in Europe, she comes to America to steal its treasures. As an ACME detective the player must travel from town to town on the tail of suspects, using clues and collecting evidence, which allow them to put the suspect in jail. The words of witnesses provide clues for those with basic geographical and historical knowledge of the United States.This version is enhanced with VGA graphics, network support, mouse and joystick support, and detailed descriptions of suspects.
