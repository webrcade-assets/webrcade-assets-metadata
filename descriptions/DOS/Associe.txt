Associe is an educative game for children (3-6 years). The goal of the game is quite simple, associate an object proposed by the computer with one of the objects in the lower grid. These objects must have a nexus (for example: wood with saw, carrot with rabbit.....). For this, the player must select, with the help of the cursors, the object of the grid that thinks is more suitable.Before starting the game, the computer will ask the player's age (3-6) to adequate the difficulty level to the children capacities.
