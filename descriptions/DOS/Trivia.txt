Avner Arbel's Trivia is a trivia game in Hebrew which contains about 1,200 questions in 30 different topics. Additional questions and topics can be manually added with a word processor.
The gameplay is identical to the earlier trivia games by Amir Rosenblum and Aryeh Segal.
The game supports up to 4 players. Each player starts the game with ₪1,000. The goal of is to reach the amount of ₪1,000,000 first, by answering the trivia questions correctly.
On each turn one player has to type in an amount to wager, select one of four topics and answer the displayed question before the predefined time limit expires. The reward for a correct answer depends on the player's wager and the question's difficulty.
