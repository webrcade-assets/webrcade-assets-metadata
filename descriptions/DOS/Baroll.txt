Baroll This is a single player, freeware puzzle game. Barrels roll out of the factory but the track between the factory and the destination is incomplete. The player has to arrange the existing pieces of track to route them to their destination. Pieces of track are selected by clicking on them, they them appear on screen within a hexagon, a green hexagon means the track can be moved while a red one means it cannot. Points are scored when a barrel reaches its destination and are lost if a barrel gets smashed.
This game comes with seventy-two levels and a level editor.
