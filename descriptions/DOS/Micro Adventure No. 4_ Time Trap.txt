Micro Adventure No. 4: Time Trap is a novel and series of type-in BASIC adventures for multiple systems.
Secret agent Orion, computer whiz on ACT (Adventure Connection Team) is about to take a trip through time.  The mad scientist Bartholemew Bacle has gone back in time carrying a nuclear bomb.  Orion must follow him into the past before he destroys the future.
With the help of ACT agents, the player travels to the age of the dinosaurs and American colonial times to stop the mad scientist.
Though the story, there are numerous interactive type-in programs that offer numerous outcomes. An appendix is included describing how to modify the BASIC program listings for greatest compatibility with many of the most popular home microcomputer BASICs, as well as describing what each program does and how it works.
