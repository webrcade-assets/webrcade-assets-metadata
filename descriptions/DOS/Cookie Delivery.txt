In Cookie Delivery, the player assumes the role of Cookie, a delivery girl who is tasked to deliver a package to a haunted house. She isn't afraid to face ghosts because she is an expert ghost-smasher.The game's goal is to go through all of its levels while smashing ghosts hiding in the furniture. It's a simple 2D platformer where the player can only move left and right, and travel between floors by the use of elevators.
