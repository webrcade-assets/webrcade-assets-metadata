Megapak 9 is an installment in Megamedia's Megapak -series. This time, there are seven games in the Compilation, with a total of 10 CDs.
The following games are included:

Destruction Derby 2
Discworld II
iF-22
Interstate '76
Jack Nicklaus 5
Pro Pinball: Timeshock!
Sid Meier's Civilization II
Some editions also come with Admiral: Sea Battles
