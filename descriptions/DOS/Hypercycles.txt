Hypercycles is a single-player, shareware, first-person action game with a plot that is reminiscent of the film Tron. The evil Dr. Devaro controls a place known as 'The Grid', he lures users into this realm to play his malicious games. In these games the player must ride their hypercycle and pit themselves against the doctor's legions of riders. In each maze-like arena there are rings that must be collected, these are the keys that will open the door to the next level. There are also missiles and other power-ups. The game has music, sound effects and is entirely keyboard controlled. The full game has thirty levels.
