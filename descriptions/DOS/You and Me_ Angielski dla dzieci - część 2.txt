You and Me: Angielski dla dzieci - część 2 is an educational program for kids, allowing to learn English through play. It allows to compete with second player - human or computer, although single mode is also available. There are eight play modes included. Before selecting a game, the player may choose one of three difficulty levels and one of seven (or all together) sets of words. 800 words are implemented. Games include various forms of grammar learning, such as choosing the right form of words, arranging style in a sentence, choosing a reclaiming word describing the picture, or playing on the wheel of fortune. Each game has its best scoreboard.
