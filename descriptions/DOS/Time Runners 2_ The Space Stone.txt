Time Runners 2: The Space Stone is a game in the Time Runners series of games about an alien race called the Skunks who are trying to conquer the universe, and a man named Max who's trying to stop them. The gameplay is primarily platforming action, with some point-and-click adventure elements.
In the second episode, Max tries to escape from the jail that the Skunks threw him into in prehistoric times after he tried to steal the Space Stone from them.
