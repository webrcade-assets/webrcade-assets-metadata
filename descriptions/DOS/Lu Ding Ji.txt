One day, the Qing soldiers suddenly came to Lichun Courtyard to search for Chen Jinnan, the chief of Tiandi Society.When Chen Jinnan heard the cries of the Qing soldiers, he rushed out and began to scuffle with them.Wei Xiaobao saved Chen Jinnan's life in danger.The two escaped in a hurry.Wei Xiaobao admired Chen Jinnan for a long time, so he decided to follow him.Chen Jinnan asked him to find four medicines, Qinglongcao, Zhuqisan, Baihudan and Xuanwu pills,In order to improve his ability.So Wei Xiaobao embarked on an adventure journey.
