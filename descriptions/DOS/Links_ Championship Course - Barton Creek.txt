Links: Championship Course: Barton Creek is a golf course add-on compatible with the following games:

Links: The Challenge of Golf Links 386 Pro Microsoft Golf

The featured 18 hole course is the Fazio Course located in Barton Creek, designed by architect Tom Fazio. The course is one of the four championship courses located at the Barton Creek Country Club in Austin, Texas. Also included is a photo tour of the Barton Creek course and facilities.
