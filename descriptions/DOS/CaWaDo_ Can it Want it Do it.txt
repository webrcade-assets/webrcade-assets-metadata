CaWaDo: Can it Want it Do it is an educational adventure title commissioned to teach children safety and risk prevention. It uses a German parser with an interface very similar to the classic Sierra adventures. The title is combination of Can + Want + Do. You control a young man or a young woman through a day of their everyday lives, to school, to work, to go out in the evening ... always observing labor & traffic safety and accident prevention. Through different actions up to 33 points can be earned similar to Sierra adventures.
