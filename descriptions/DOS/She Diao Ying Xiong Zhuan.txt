She Diao Ying Xiong Zhuan is a point-and-click adventure title. It is played as if reading a martial arts novel. During the game the cursor on the screen has three colors. Red is the place that cannot be visited, yellow is the place that can be reached, and green is the goal. At the beginning of the game, Guo Jing appears in a snack bar. He can ask someone to go around and get familiar with the operation. There is an inventory and different types of actions can be selected near the bottom of the screen.
