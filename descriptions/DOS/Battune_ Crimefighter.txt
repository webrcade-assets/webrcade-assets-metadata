Battune - Crimefighter is a text adventure in the Battune series.
In this adventure the player is a recent graduate from the Police Academy. Starting as a patrolman and aided by Battune their friendly alien guide they are assigned to a crime ridden district. The object of the game is to talk to people, gather clues, solve crimes and make an arrest. With every successful arrest the player will advance in rank until they become the commissioner. With every incorrect arrest they will be killed in the line of duty.
This is a text adventure, there are no graphics, and all commands must be entered in upper case. It is possible to die in this game
