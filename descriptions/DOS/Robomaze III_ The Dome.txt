You are a freedom fighter, a member of the resistance, fighting against the tyrannical dictator of your country and his henchman, the mad scientist.In the Robomaze II, you traveled to the mad scientist's tower and battled him and his robots. When you won, you took off in your helicopter in search of the dictator and his dome. Thanks to the help of the resistance, you quickly located his dome. Unfortunately, you forgot your weapons at the tower.You are completely defenseless, but you vow to conquer the dictator.  You must find weapons, keys and equipment, search underground hideouts and battle your enemies.This game consists of two episodes: The Dome and The Final Journey.
