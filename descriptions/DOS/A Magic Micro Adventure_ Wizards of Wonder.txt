A Magic Micro Adventure: Wizards of Wonder is a novel and series of type-in BASIC adventures for multiple systems.
Using their Magic Micro, the player travels to the enchanted Kingdom of Karn to meet an evil wizard and the good king he has bewitched.  The player must break the wizard's spell and save Karn from war.  The player will use their computer to decode the message on a magic sword, break the wizard's spell, and stop the armies that are marching towards Karn.
Though the story, there are numerous interactive type-in programs that offer numerous outcomes. An appendix is included describing how to modify the BASIC program listings for greatest compatibility with many of the most popular home microcomputer BASICs, as well as describing what each program does and how it works.
