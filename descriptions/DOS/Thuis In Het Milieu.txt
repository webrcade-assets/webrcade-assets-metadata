Thuis In Het Milieu is a Dutch educative software title. In the game you play a man and have to perform tasks in and around the house. Everything has to do with the environment. You have to control the radiators, clean the house, throw out the trash, take a shower, go to the bathroom, do the laundry.At the end of the week you are evaluated on how well you've conserved energy and saved the environment. During each task you perform you are prompted with a question that has to be answered correctly. These questions again have an environmental nature.The game is in 2D and shows each room of the house in a typical Point & Click Adventure style. The main character is animated for each of the tasks you can perform.
