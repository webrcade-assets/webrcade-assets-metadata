Micro Adventure No. 5: Mindbenders is a novel and series of type-in BASIC adventures for multiple systems.Secret agent Orion, computer whiz on ACT (Adventure Connection Team) has just discovered a vicious mind-control plot that could cause World War III.  The world's leaders are being turned into mindless slaves by the evil forces of BRUTE (the Bureau of Random Unlawful Terror and Evil).Though the story, there are numerous interactive type-in programs that offer numerous outcomes. An appendix is included describing how to modify the BASIC program listings for greatest compatibility with many of the most popular home microcomputer BASICs, as well as describing what each program does and how it works.
