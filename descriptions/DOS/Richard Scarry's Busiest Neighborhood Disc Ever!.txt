Richard Scarry's Busiest Neighborhood Disc Ever! is an edutainment title for young children that is based on a book by Richard Scarry. In the game players can explore Busytown's shops and offices. All around town they will encounter familiar characters created by Scarry. There are various games to be played, such as a visual memory game in which you have to recreate an ice cream recipe. The player also learns what he or she can expect to see and find in various locations such as a doctor's office as well as more fundamental skills such as recognizing the weather, patterns and sounds and matching up shapes.
