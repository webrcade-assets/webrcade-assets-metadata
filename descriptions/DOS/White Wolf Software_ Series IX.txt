White Wolf Software: Series IX is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label. This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: Epic Pinball : Just the Android Table from Epic Pinball Pack 1

B: TeleCards
C: Pyramid a solitaire game from Eduardo Martins
D: Pirada 1
E: Cheat: a selection of game codes and cheats
F: Gemstones III
G: Adventure Math
H: Breakfree
I: Trugg
J: Micropede v1.40

The software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
