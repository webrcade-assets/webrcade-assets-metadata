Two Bit Poker is a video poker game. After betting, you will be dealt five cards. You can hold on to some of the cards, and then replace the others. You will then get a payout based on your hand. It includes Canadian, Reno, and Deluxe variations. Game can be set to easy, medium, and hard. Auto-hold can be enabled, which will automatically select reasonable cards to hold.
The game was released as Shareware. The registered version just removes the shareware reminder screen.
