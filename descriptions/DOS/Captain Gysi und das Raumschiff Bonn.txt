Captain Gysi und das Raumschiff Bonn is a promotional game for the German political party PDS (Party of Democratic Socialism). You are playing as Captain Gysi, in real life the chairman of the PDS, who has to rescue the spaceship Bonn, the German seat of government. Why rescue it? Because the whole crew is frozen and incapable of acting.
The controls of the humorous comic adventure are simple: right click to examine an item, left to use or take it. You solve inventory-based puzzles with average difficulty.
