Kosynka is a single-player, mouse controlled card game. This is a Russian solitaire game, "Kosynka" means "Big Scarf". It is played with two decks and is very similar to the popular solitaire game Klondike. Cards are built up in suits on their foundations starting with the Ace and ending with the King. In the main game area the player moves cards around, and adds to them from the stack, arranging them in order highest to lowest in sequential order, alternating between the red and black suits so, for example, a red ten would be played on a black jack. This game has save, load, repeat game and undo functions.
