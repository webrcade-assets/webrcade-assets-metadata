From Corner To Corner is a four-players or two-players turn-based board game. The goal for each player is to move all balls from player's corner to the opposite corner of the playfield faster than others. The ball can move a one space and also jump over a single nearest ball for unlimited times in any direction - vertical, horizontal, diagonal. Each turn the player selects one ball and points out the destination of the ball's route. The game has an option to select human or AI opponents as well as to set a one of four difficulty levels.
