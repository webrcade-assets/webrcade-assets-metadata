Muzeum Mrtvol (Museum of Corpses) is a Czech text adventure,
The player's character starts in a dark, musty smelling room. It is very cold and they are short of breath. The strong smell of death comes from the north. On the ground beside them is a piece of yellow paper which has a drawing of a skull with ribs and blood on it, they can travel north or west.
In this game the player does not have to type in commands such as GO NORTH and so forth, the game is played via a set of icons at the top of the screen below which the text appears. The player navigates between these icons using the arrow keys. The game has save/load function and an inventory but no sound or pictures. The game has over fifty rooms to explore some of which lead to instant death.
