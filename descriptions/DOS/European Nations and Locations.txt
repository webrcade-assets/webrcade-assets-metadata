European nations and Locations is an educational geography game for multiple systems.The player receives trivia or geography questions regarding a specific European nation, and must use the cursor to select the correct nation from a marked or unmarked map. The player also has the option to place geographic outlines of nations on the European map. Questions are timed, and a player receives more points by answering questions quickly. Topics include: nation names, capitals, historic facts, current facts, neighbors, and landforms. The player can also create their own facts to play with.
