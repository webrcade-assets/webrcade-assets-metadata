Orientation To The Van Pelt Library Is a single player, text adventure that aims to teach students how to use the Van Pelt library services at the University of Pennsylvania.The game was played by students who were given printed maps and other materials to aid them, the objective being to locate a book about atomic energy, Lawrence Scheinman's"The International Atomic Energy Agency and World Nuclear Order".The game has seventy four locations and a perfect score would net the player one hundred points.
