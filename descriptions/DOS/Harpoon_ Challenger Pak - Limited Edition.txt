This compilation includes floppy diskettes for the Harpoon Master Game, a renowned computer conversion of a tabletop naval strategic game published by the Game Designer's Workshop, as well as for its Battleset #2: North Atlantic Convoys and the Harpoon Scenario Editor, on top of which it also includes manuals for the game and its extensions as well as a 300-page "Official Strategies & Tactics Guide" or "Battlebook".
