Spellakazam is a 1 - 2 player n educational vocabulary game for multiple systems.
The player competes against a magician in a maze filled with the letters of the alphabet. The player must try to move through the maze and spell the word shown on the bottom of the screen before the magician reaches the magic hat. In an alternative game mode, both the player and magician have to spell the word before either can go for the hat. There are 3 levels of difficulty.
