While traveling to a village festival with your friends, you are lured to the ruins of an ancient castle.Here you will learn about pagan activities that took place here many centuries ago.These rituals open the gate to hell. Will the ritual be successful at this year's festival?Pass all the tests in this action adventure and prevent the occult rituals from being carried out.Eight old ruins and many kilometers of dirt roads between them are reproduced.Video recordings and 3D graphics merge to create fully-moving views.
