The third installment in the unique multi-user dungeon series by The Sierra Network. Ruins of Cawdor uses the same game engine as The Shadow of Yserbius and its sequel Fates of Twinion but has a completely new plot based on Macbeth. In the beginning of the game the player creates a character of different guilds: thief, barbarian, cleric, knight, wizard, ranger. Exploring six floors of castle Cawdor the adventurer faces numerous battles, solves puzzles, and finds secret doors and magic items.
