Piotrków 1939 is a tactical/operational level turn- and tile-based strategy game set during the first days of World War 2, when the Battle of Piotrków Trybunalski took place.The player takes command of either Polish or German forces, and may issue move, fire support or supply orders for infantry, armor, artillery and air units.The game evolved from the earlier developer's title, Ardeny - it features icon-based interface and sound effects.
