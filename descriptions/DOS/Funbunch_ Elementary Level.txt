Funbunch: Elementary Level is a series of educational games for multiple systems.
Contains three educational mini-games:

Computer Doodle: A program for drawing geometric shapes.
Speed Read: Students type the phrase that flashes on the screen. Users can choose the word level and how long it is displayed. The player receives points for correct answers.
Word Flash: Students type the word that flashes on the screen. Users can choose the word level and how long it is displayed. The player receives points for correct answers.
