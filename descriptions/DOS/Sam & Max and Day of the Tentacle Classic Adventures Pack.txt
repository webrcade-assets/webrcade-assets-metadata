This compilation contains the Sam & Max: Hit the Road and Day of the Tentacle speech versions, as well as a Grim Fandango demo movie, all on a single disc. Also included is a reference card on how to properly run the games with sound. There are no manuals, neither on the CD nor printed.
This is the first release of both games for the Amiga platform. It consists of the original PC CD (LucasArts Classic release) and an additional Amiga CD with the installer and documentation. The games are played with the bundled emulator ScummVM.
