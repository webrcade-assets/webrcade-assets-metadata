Vardit BeHarpatka Hadasha BeAlifut HaGlisha was released as a part of a marketing campaign of Vered Hagalil, an Israeli dairy product manufacturer. The floppy contains two out of three games which together form the Vardit Adventures (the remaining game being Vardit VeHaShokolada BeMasluley HaOlympiada).The first game takes place on a ski course, Vardit the cow has to ski and reach the end of the course while avoiding snowmen, penguins and other obstacles, and collect a set amount of chocolate packets, based on selected the difficulty level.The second game takes place next to the beach and shares a very similar gameplay to the first. Vardit has to surf while avoiding obstacles such as dolphins, submarines and others, and once again collect a certain amount chocolate packets.
