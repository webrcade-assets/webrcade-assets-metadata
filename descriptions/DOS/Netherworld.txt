In this game you are trapped in Netherworld. With your spaceship you have to make your way through different levels with a hostile environment. Under a time limit you have to collect diamonds to get to the next level. The game is filled with dragons, moving mines, acid spitting creatures and lots more.
Your space ship is able to shoot, furthermore you can collect several power-ups, such as sandglasses to ease the time limit, shield restore or items that give you invulnerability.
