Komebumb is a platform game with cartoonish characters.In this game the player takes the role of a little hairy creature, with a great jumping skill, that has one mission: eat all the bombs in each level before the time limit reaches zero (there is a limited time to eat each bomb). The creature must avoid a lot of traps and enemies in all the levels. To kill the enemies the starring must jump on their heads. To increase the amount of points the player can kill enemies or collect coins in each level.
