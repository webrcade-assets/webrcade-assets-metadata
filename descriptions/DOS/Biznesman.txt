Biznesman is a simple trade game, which consists of buying and selling all kinds of goods in cities around the world. Players can buy and sell goods, borrow money, or invest the money in the accounts or actions of companies. During the course of commercial activities various events may occur, such as inheriting the estate of a relative, losing some goods, or the bankruptcy of the bank where the player had all their savings. The game has no sound and simple visuals that only present statistics and values. The game is played in a text mode.
