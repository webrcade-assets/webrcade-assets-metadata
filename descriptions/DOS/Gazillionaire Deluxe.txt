An update of the previous Gazillionaire space-trading game. Gazillionaire Deluxe includes the existing gameplay of that title and offers the following changes and updates:Ability to customize the solar system from a set of pre-existing planetsThere are twice as many spaceships to choose fromDouble the number of "random event" travel encountersA new Stock Markets system that lets players invest on a particular planetAdjustable intelligence for the computer playersPlay-by-email functionality for multiplayer games
