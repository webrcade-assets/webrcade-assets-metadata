Figury-Rytmy-Litery is a set of 3 educational games.
Literki is an arcade game, where the player must shoot down the correct number or letter before it falls to the bottom of the well. The game allows to choose the level of difficulty and the speed of falling blocks.
Rytmy requires repeating the rhythms shown on the top of the screen. The game allows to choose the number of musical accents and the speed of performing mascot.
Figurki-Cyferki-Kierunki requires dropping the appropriate letter, number or symbol from the helicopter onto the corresponding platform.The game allows to choose the type of image being transported and the speed of the helicopter flight.
