Stone Mist 2: Legend of the Overfiend is a single-character RPG with extensive character creation options and a large world to explore.The great beast is soon to awaken, and the goddess Kybele has given the player the task of stopping the destruction this will cause.Stone Mist 2: Legend of the Overfiend was distributed in two parts, as shareware. The shareware version included a cut-down version of the first part, and both parts could be ordered separately.
