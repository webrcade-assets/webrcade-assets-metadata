Grand Fleet is a World War I Tactical Naval Combat Wargame/Simulation. The time frame covers from the beginning of the dreadnought era in 1906 thru the end of World War I and extending through 1920. It covers the major clashes between the Germans and British and includes a scenario generator. The player represents the senior naval commander for his side. When the game ends the player is given an after action report composed of the final victory point totals and a ship status report that will display the closing situation for each vessel involved in play.
