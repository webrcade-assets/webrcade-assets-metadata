The Incredible Adventure of Mad Mac is a 2D platformer in German with ten stages that consist of single screens linked together using the flip-screen mechanic. The treasures of the kingdom Wakajamistan were stolen by the terrible ghost Tsu Bai, who is now hiding in his fortress Saka Tschi. Only Mad Mac is brave enough to stand up to Tsu Bai and to recover the treasures. Mad Mac needs to evade enemies, collect keys and find his way through mazes to reach the final boss.
