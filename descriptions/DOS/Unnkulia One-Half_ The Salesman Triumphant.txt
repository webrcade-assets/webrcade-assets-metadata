Unnkulia One-Half: The Salesman Triumphant is a simple freeware text adventure dungeon crawler, developed with TADS - Text Adventure Development System. The game was written for advertising purposes of the shareware game Unnkulia Zero: The Search for Amanda. As the Acme salesman forced to work out of the Golden Dragon Inn, located next to Dread Unnkulia, the player must collect enough treasure in order to buy a local inn. The game is fully keyboard controlled by using dozen types of commands available.
