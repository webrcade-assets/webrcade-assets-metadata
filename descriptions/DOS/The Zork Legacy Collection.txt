The Zork Legacy Collection includes all seven DOS/Windows Zork games made up to that point and one bonus game in one package. The games ranging from classic Interactive Fiction to modern graphical Zork adventures.
Each game has their individual printed manual, and in addition, all five Zork Interactive Fiction games have a detailed printed map.
The Zork Special Edition contains these three game items:

Return to Zork
Zork Nemesis: The Forbidden Lands
The Zork Anthology, itself a compilation of:
Zork: The Great Underground Empire
Zork II: The Wizard of Frobozz
Zork III: The Dungeon Master
Beyond Zork: The Coconut of Quendor
Zork Zero: The Revenge of Megaboz
Planetfall
