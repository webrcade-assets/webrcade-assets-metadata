The Zork Legacy Collection includes all seven DOS/Windows Zork games made up to that point and one bonus game in one package. The games ranging from classic Interactive Fiction to modern graphical Zork adventures.Each game has their individual printed manual, and in addition, all five Zork Interactive Fiction games have a detailed printed map.The Zork Special Edition contains these three game items:Return to ZorkZork Nemesis: The Forbidden LandsThe Zork Anthology, itself a compilation of:Zork: The Great Underground EmpireZork II: The Wizard of FrobozzZork III: The Dungeon MasterBeyond Zork: The Coconut of QuendorZork Zero: The Revenge of MegabozPlanetfall
