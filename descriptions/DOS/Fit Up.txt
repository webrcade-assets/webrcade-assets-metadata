Fit Up is a variant of Tetris.Play against the computer or up to three human players, sharing the same computer screen. Fit Up features 40 levels, and has a built-in editor. There's even a story : during the 5th millennium, planet Earth is overpopulated and the "Fit Up Test" was chosen by Darwin (sic), the almighty computer, to handle this problem. If you pass the "Fit Up Test", you survive, otherwise ...
