Word Attack French Plus! is a vocabulary learning game for multiple systems.Contains 9 levels of vocabulary for teaching French, and the player can chose to learn adjectives, verbs, or nouns. Players can review words and their meaning, and there is a multiple choice quiz, word match, and fill in the blank options.Finally, there is a game component called Word Attack Plus!. In this game, the player controls a small gnome who moves horizontally along the bottom of the screen. A definition appears, and the player must shoot the appropriate definition with their hat before the timer on the right runs out. As the player answers questions, the timer begins to drain quicker. Every 5 correct answers in a row, the player advances a level, and the player receives more points for each correct answer. After all time has expired, the player receives a final score.
