This compilation includes two of Digital Integration's helicopter-simulators:

Apache Longbow
Hind: The Russian Combat Helicopter Simulation

There is a certain amount of cross-game features available through the two titles. Players can fly with/against each other using either helicopter in the Virtual Battlefield, in two-player head-to-head, leader/wingman, or pilot/gunner configurations, 
or with up to 16 players in Apache vs Hind deathmatch battles.
The package also includes the multimedia disc MIG Russian Aircraft.
