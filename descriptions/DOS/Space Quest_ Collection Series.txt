This compilation includes:

Space Quest: Chapter I - The Sarien Encounter (VGA version)
Space Quest II: Chapter II - Vohaul's Revenge
Space Quest III: The Pirates of Pestulon
Space Quest IV: Roger Wilco and the Time Rippers
Space Quest V: The Next Mutation
Space Quest 6: Roger Wilco in the Spinal Frontier

As a bonus, besides the six Space Quest games, there is a preview of Space Quest VII: Return to Roman Numerals, but this game was never completed.
