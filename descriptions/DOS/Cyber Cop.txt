"Cyber Cop" is an arcade action game set in a generic sci-fi world where we play as a robot-like galactic law enforcer. The mission is to seek out and destroy some rebels and their robots on an alien planet.The planet is divided into three separate spheres, each with its own lanscapes and enemies: the Air World lies in clouds, The Geosphere is on land, and The Aquatic Realm is underwater. Besides moving and shooting, the protagonist has the ability to transform itself into a special machine unique to each of the realms: Cyber Copter, Cyber Tank and Cyber Sub.
