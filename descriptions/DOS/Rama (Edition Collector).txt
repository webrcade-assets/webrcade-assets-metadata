Rama (Edition Collector) is a collector's edition of Rama exclusive to the French market. It was created through a partnership between Sierra France (Coktel Vision) and the French book publisher J'Ai Lu. It includes:

Rama
All 4 Rama novels by Arthur C. Clarke and Gentry Lee

J'ai Lu also re-released the novels in France in October 1996 with new covers inspired by the game's artworks.
