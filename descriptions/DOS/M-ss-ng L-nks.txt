M-ss-ng L-nks is a 1 - 2 player educational game for multiple systems.Players are given a short excerpt from a famous piece of literature with letters missing. The level of difficulty determines how many and how the letters are missing. The player's goal is to type in the missing letters. The player has 5 attempts per letter to complete each section, and if they fail, the letter is automatically filled. The player received a score of 100% if they fill in every letter correct the first try, with the score progressively decreasing from there.
