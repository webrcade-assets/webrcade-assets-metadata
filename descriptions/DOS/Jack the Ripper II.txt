Jack the Ripper II is the eighth of Softside Magazine's Adventures of the Month, and the sequel to Jack the Ripper Adventure , and was published for multiple systems.
It has been 100 years since your grandfather caught Jack the Ripper, only to have the killer plunge to his presumed death from London Bridge. Now, a new murderer has appeared, claiming to be seeking revenge for your grandfather's deeds. A dead woman has been found, and the mayor's pregnant daughter has disappeared. Can you solve the murder before all is lost?
The player must explore the streets of modern London, as well as the dark sewers beneath the streets. A day and night cycle allows different events to occur through the day.
All commands are via 2-word VERB NOUN entries, and game play contains several object puzzles. There is no save and restore feature.
