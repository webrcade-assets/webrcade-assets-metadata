Biprolex+ is an action puzzle game that is a mix between Tetris and Arkanoid. One player controls the Tetris falling block puzzle part in the top part of the game field, while the other player controls the Arkanoid part at the bottom.
The game has a complex strategy, so the player needs to keep an eye on a variety of parameters and a variety of game situations. More than eighty game levels can be played with different tactics.
