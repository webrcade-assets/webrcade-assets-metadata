Yatzy is a keyboard controlled implementation of Yahtzee for up to nine players. The game is in Danish and there in no option to play in another language.Action keys are used to roll the dice, hold selected dice and roll again. There are two modes of play. Forced where the game decides to put the player's score into a specific category and Optional where the player is asked at the end of each turn where the want to assign the current dice.The game uses the standard Yahtzee scoring system, scoring for the numbers one to six with a bonus if appropriate, one pair, two pairs, three of a kind, four of a kind, little straight, big straight, full house, Yahtzee and a Chance - but all are in Danish. It has optional sound effects - occasional whistles and high score tables for each game mode.
