Deluxe Edition of Roberta Williams' Phantasmagoria. Packaged in square box covered with black velvet and silver-colored printing. The seven CD's are separately packaged in a cardboard sleeve and bundled by an intricately folded sliding box. The manual contains full-color illustrations and a short story by Gieneke Spannenburg.This packaging was used in the Benelux only, it was designed by Rangeela BV and distributed by HomeSoft Benelux.
