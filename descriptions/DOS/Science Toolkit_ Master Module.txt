Science Toolkit: Master Module is an educational program that allows users to perform experiments to understand the answers to questions such as why do people perspire, do heavier objects fall faster than lighter objects, and what happens to star light as it travels to Earth.The package comes with the game disk, combination manual and experiment guide, and an interface box that connects to the computer. A light probe and a temperature probe are also included. They connect to the computer via the interface box and are used for the experiments.
