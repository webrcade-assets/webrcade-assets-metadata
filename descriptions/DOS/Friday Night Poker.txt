Friday Night Poker is a simulation of poker. It features 13 different variations of poker (the dealer chooses the variety), and only has a one-player mode (with five computer players). It tracks high scores. Varieties are 5 Card Stud, 5 Card Draw, Jacks or Better Trips to Win, 7 Card Stud, 7 Card Stud High Chicago, 7 Card Stud Follow the Queen, 7 Card Stud Command W/High Chicago, 7 Card No Peek, 7/27, Acey Ducey, 3 Toad Pete, 31 Card Gut, 4 Card Gut (Spit).The game was distributed unrestricted, but with an address for donations.
