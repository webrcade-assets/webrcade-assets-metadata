The Classic Flight Simulations compilation is issue #9 of the Bestseller Games Special magazine and contains the following games:

Battlehawks 1942
Their Finest Hour: The Battle of Britain (including mission pack)
Secret Weapons of the Luftwaffe (CD-ROM release, including all four add-ons)

The magazine contains detailed instructions for each of the included games, additionally the full original manuals are also available on the game disc as scanned PDF documents.
