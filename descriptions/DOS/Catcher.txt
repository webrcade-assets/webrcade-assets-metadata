Catcher (transliterated as Кэтчер in Russian) is an arcade puzzle game. On each level the goal is to catch all the bugs riding on the rectangular 5x5 grid. The quantity of bugs is increased with each level, and it is at least one item greater than a quantity of traps, which is also limited to be not greater than five. The player tries to catch the bugs with as less quantity of traps as possible for a given time. The highest scores are stored in top ten table.
