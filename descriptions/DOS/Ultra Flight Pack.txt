Ultra Flight Pack is a compilation which contains eight flight simulators, mostly military, but there is also a civil flight simulator included.
The one civilian flight simulator:

Flight Unlimited II (1 CD version with reduced features)

The seven military flight simulators are from four different eras in time:
World War I:

Flying Corps: Gold
WarBirds: Dawn of Aces

World War II:

WarBirds

Present:

iF-22
F/A-18 Korea
F/A-18 Hornet 3.0

(Near) Future:

Ultra Fighters
