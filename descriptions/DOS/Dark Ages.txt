Dark Ages is a shareware series consisting of three episodes:Dark Ages: Volume 1 - Prince of DestinyDark Ages: Volume 2 - The Undead KingdomDark Ages: Volume 2 - Dungeons of DoomWhile the episodes were originally distributed separately, there were later released as bundled retail/freeware versions. The Macintosh and Windows versions are emulated from DOS and the first releases on the platform.
