Robix is a 1 - 2 player puzzle game for DOS.Played with a field of red, blue, and green blocks, the player's goal is to make their colored blocks fall out the bottom of the screen.  Each turn, the player selects a row, and chooses whether to push all the blocks 1 column to the left or right.  Green blocks never fall, but all red or blue blocks that are free will fall until they hit the next blocks.  There are 3 game lengths, and players score 1 point for each of their blocks that fall off the bottom of the screen.
