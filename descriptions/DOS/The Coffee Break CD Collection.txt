This is a bundle of 'casual' games of different types published by Villa Crespo Software in popular 'Coffee Break' series, presented here on one single CD.
Included are 21 games: arcade action ('Ninja Rabbits', '3D Pinball', 'Space Vegetables' etc.); cards and tabletop games ('Casino Craps', 'Ruckus Roulette', 'Dr.Thorp's Mini Blackjack' etc.), and some strategy / trivia / puzzle games ('Armada', 'OnTarget', Think Cross' etc.).
