Feng Chen San Xia Zhi Jin Jian Shi Zhe is a role-playing Chinese swordsman game. The story is about the three disciples who went down the mountain and met the Golden Arrow messenger. In order to prevent him from practising magic, three treasures were found and finally the golden arrow messenger was defeated. Chen Ling, Rose and Gao Qiu, the three disciples of Fengshan (three Heroes of the Wind and Dust), have been practising on the mighty peak for eight years. On this day, they were ordered to go down the hill. Many treasures can be found and many enemies can be encountered on the way down.
