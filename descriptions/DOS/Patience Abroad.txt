Patience Abroad is a pack of nine solitaire games, that can be controlled with either the mouse or keyboard. It has a high scores table.Games included are:Beleaguered CastleFlorentineThe Great WallFour KingdomsMonte CarloRussian PatienceSir TommyTower of HanoyWeddings in ParisPatience Abroad was distributed as shareware. The registered version allowed modification of colors and modification of other options.
