Patience Abroad is a pack of nine solitaire games, that can be controlled with either the mouse or keyboard. It has a high scores table.
Games included are:

Beleaguered Castle
Florentine
The Great Wall
Four Kingdoms
Monte Carlo
Russian Patience
Sir Tommy
Tower of Hanoy
Weddings in Paris

Patience Abroad was distributed as shareware. The registered version allowed modification of colors and modification of other options.
