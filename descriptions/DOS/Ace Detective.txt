Ace Detective is an educational game for multiple systems.The player takes on the role of a detective, and must solve a crime by investigating the event. The player starts in their office, where they receive report of the crime. The player must then visit the scene of the crime, where they can interview witnesses to the event. Next, the player can call up suspects, and review mugshots. The player must then use their notepad to explain the motive, means, and opportunity of the suspect to commit the crime. After the player has correctly gathered these, they can take the information on the judge to obtain an arrest warrant. The player has a limited time frame to crack the case.
