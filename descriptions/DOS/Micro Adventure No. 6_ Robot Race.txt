Micro Adventure No. 6: Robot Race is a novel and series of type-in BASIC adventures for multiple systems.Secret agent Orion, computer whiz of ACT (Adventure Connection Team) is face to face with the most advanced form of artificial intelligence ever created.  Too bad, it's out to kill Orion.  Half-human, half-computer and all evil, the vicious Brutus is leading a robot army against the world.During the adventure, the player must beat a supercomputer at its own game, break through the enemy's unbreakable failsafe security system, and reprogram the robots to attack each other.Though the story, there are numerous interactive type-in programs that offer numerous outcomes. An appendix is included describing how to modify the BASIC program listings for greatest compatibility with many of the most popular home microcomputer BASICs, as well as describing what each program does and how it works.
