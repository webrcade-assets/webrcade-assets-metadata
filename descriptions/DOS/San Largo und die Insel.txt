San Largo und die Insel is a strategy game developed by the prolific German developer Motelsoft. The player commands the forces of San Largo on the quest to explore the hostile island of prince Khalid and return the magic disc he stole. The gameplay consists of exploring the island, trading with settlements to get money, and training soldiers to fight against Khalid. During the game, the queen of San Largo will occasionally make a request of the player which has to be fulfilled.
