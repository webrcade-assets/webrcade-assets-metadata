Animated Money is an shareware edutainment title designed to teach children coin recognition. It used American and Canadian coinage and can be controlled either by the keyboard or by the mouse.
There are three games in this title:* Game 1 where the pupil matches a picture of a coin to it's denomination

Game 2 has the child match a group of coins to their value
Game 3 is the most advanced game, here the pupil matches one group of coins to another group of the same value
