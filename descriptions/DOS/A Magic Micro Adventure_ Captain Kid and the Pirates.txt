A Magic Micro Adventure: Captain Kid and the Pirates is a novel and series of type-in BASIC adventures for multiple systems.Using their Magic Micro, the player becomes the leader of a pirate crew.  Together, they must sail to Parrot Island, and find the buried treasure.  Along the way, the player must use their computer to defeat angry pirates, giant snapping turtles, and sea monsters.Though the story, there are numerous interactive type-in programs that offer numerous outcomes. An appendix is included describing how to modify the BASIC program listings for greatest compatibility with many of the most popular home microcomputer BASICs, as well as describing what each program does and how it works.
