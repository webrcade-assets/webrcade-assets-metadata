The sequel to La luz del druida serves as a closure to the Leyendas de Lhodrye short series (only two games).This time the action takes place in some far away lands in the Elidon kingdom. Our hero must find "The Ancient One" so he can find a solution to the dark prophecies that threaten his land.The gameplay is the same as the first game. You control your character through your keyboard in a platform-like environment solving puzzles.
