Sumo wrestling is a popular sport in Japan, but you can rarely find a game for it in the US.  In Super Sumo Wrestling 2002 you can punch, kick, bite, or whatevver else you want in order to work your way to becoming the yokozuna.     10 characters, four tournaments, multiplayer mode, timed attack mode, and a slew of other goodies await you.  Super Sumo Wrestling 2002 has the ability to import players, which means you can create your own player from scratch or download a new one off the net!  Do you have what it takes to become the yokozuna?
