A Magic Micro Adventure: Superworld is a novel and series of type-in BASIC adventures for multiple systems.Using their Magic Micro, the player joins a powerful group of super heroes when they arrive in Superworld.  The player must defeat the evil Zargo by leading the heroes past Zargo's zap traps, destroying a robot army, and surviving the dangers within Zargo's castle.Though the story, there are numerous interactive type-in programs that offer numerous outcomes. An appendix is included describing how to modify the BASIC program listings for greatest compatibility with many of the most popular home microcomputer BASICs, as well as describing what each program does and how it works.
