Kto spotka UFO is a game for two players. The action takes place in 1958, when alien creatures arrived to Klucze village. The game is divided into two stages. In the first one the players arrange theirs pawns in the city. In the second one the goal requires reaching the vaults. The pawn may be shifted vertically, horizontally and diagonally by only one field or two, when using the road. Winning the game requires hiding all the pawnes in the vaults. Each vault may accommodate only one pawn.
