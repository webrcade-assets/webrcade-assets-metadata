1995Card+ is a single-player, shareware version of the Japanese game Hanafuda, literally 'Flower Card'. This game is known by other names such as Koi-koi, Sakura, Higobana, Hwatu and is played throughout Korea and Micronesia. This is a card game in which the cards display different pictures and the object is to collect 'families' of cards to score points. It is played with the mouse. There are in-game help screens, optional sound effects, and four levels of computer opponent.
