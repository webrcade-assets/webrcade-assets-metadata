Beyond Destruction is an unofficial add-on for three strategy games:WarCraft II : 700 new maps.Command & Conquer: Red Alert : 250 singleplayer levels, 200 multiplayer levels.Command & Conquer : 120 new singleplayer levels, 100 new multiplayer levels, strategy guides and walkthroughs.The levels were not designed specifically for this add-on, but merely collected from the internet, a common practice in the early modem days.
