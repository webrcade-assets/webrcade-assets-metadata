Not just a BBS door with a handsome terminal program, Slot-Tris answers the eternal question: what do you get when you combine the classic gameplay of Tetris with the bonuses of a One-Armed Bandit?Not only does this additional dynamic (each falling block studded with little slot machine symbols to match during line-clearance for big bonuses) influence the final score, but the "money" earned can go toward every Tetris player's greatest dream -- buying power to influence the shape of the next block up on deck.Local play allows users to enjoy this stop at the Russian casino crossroads without needing to dial in.
