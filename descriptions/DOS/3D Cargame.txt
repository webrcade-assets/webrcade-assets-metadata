3D Cargame is a car game where the player can drive either a car or a tank. There are three game modes: Practice, Chase game and Cube game. In practice mode the player just drives around the town without any specific goal. In chase game the player has to chase another vehicle until the time runs out (approx. 3 min). In Cube game the player has to collect cubes (there are 39 cubes spread out across the town) every cube collected will add 5 seconds to the timer and when the time runs out it's game over. The player may also chose if he/she wants to drive during the day or night.The game was created using djgpp and Allegro.
