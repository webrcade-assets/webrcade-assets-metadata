Bastion is a strategy game with the plot modeled on Lord of the Rings. A ring forged by the dwarfs stole the soul of its creator and made him build an army of evil. Many times he was the cause of wars, until one day the ring disappeared along with its owner. After many years the ring was found by Hobr and representatives of all races gathered to resist the rule. The game plays like a strategy board game with the military figures moving around the map. Units can be given simple commands. The fight proceeds by displaying messages. Besides the traditional units the player can use magic. There are 12 scenarios in total.
