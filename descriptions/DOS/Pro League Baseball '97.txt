Pro League Baseball '97 is a baseball simulation. You can play games at 32 ballparks, both past and present, each with multi-screen views. Players can create their own roster, including drafting and trading, or use a team from the past 92 years. There are complete batting, pitching and fielding stats for each player.The game features both a simulation and an arcade mode. In simulation mode, you are playing as the coach and manager of your team, or teams, and have a variety of strategy options during a game. In arcade mode you can actually choose the direction to throw or hit the ball as well and even can do so from a first person perspective.It uses rotoscoped animated graphics.
