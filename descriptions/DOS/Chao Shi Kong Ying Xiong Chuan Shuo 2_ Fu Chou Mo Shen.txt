Chao Shi Kong Ying Xiong Chuan Shuo 2: Fu Chou Mo Shen is the sequel to Chao Shi Kong Ying Xiong Chuan Shuo. It is an RPG with tactical battles. After ten years of a peaceful life, the world is once again plunged into war. After accepting the task of the queen, Yan Qingfeng and his friend Bai Zhichao jointly seek out the heroes of the previous game in order to restore peace to the world.
