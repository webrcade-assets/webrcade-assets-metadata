After the success of Silmarils' Eye of the Beholder -influenced RPG trilogy, they released this collection of all 3 games:Ishar - Legend of the FortressIshar 2 - Messengers of DoomIshar 3 - The Seven Gates of InfinityCharacters from earlier games can be imported into the later ones, ensuring that you get advantage of successful experience as your party improves through the sequence of adventures they face.
