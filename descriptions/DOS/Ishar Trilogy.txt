After the success of Silmarils' Eye of the Beholder -influenced RPG trilogy, they released this collection of all 3 games:

Ishar - Legend of the Fortress
Ishar 2 - Messengers of Doom
Ishar 3 - The Seven Gates of Infinity

Characters from earlier games can be imported into the later ones, ensuring that you get advantage of successful experience as your party improves through the sequence of adventures they face.
