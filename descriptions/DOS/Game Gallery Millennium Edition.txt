Game Gallery Millennium Edition is Volume 2 in Swing 's Game Gallery series. In this compilation, there are 12 games spread over 13 discs. Like the predecessor, or most of the other Compilations around, the complete manuals are only stored on the discs in PDF format.
Included games:

Air Warrior III
F1 Racing Simulation
Gex 3D: Enter The Gecko
Machines
Re-Volt
Seven Kingdoms: Ancient Adversaries
Shadow Man
Snow Wave: Avalanche
Spearhead
Test Drive: Off-Road 2
The Settlers II: Veni, Vidi, Vici
Turok 2: Seeds of Evil
