Compared to Richman 2, Richman 3 has many new ways of making money. Apart from the options of the predecessor, players can buy stocks of major companies, build large office buildings, hotels or playgrounds in some places.
In addition to that, besides cards, the props can also be used to buy motorcycles or cars, missiles, machine dolls, remote control dice and other things. This makes the game more interesting and difficult.
Stocks have no exaggerated ups and downs, it is not easy to rely on stocks to increase their power, which makes real estate continue to be the key to success in the game.
