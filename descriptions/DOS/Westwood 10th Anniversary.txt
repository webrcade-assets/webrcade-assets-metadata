Westwood 10th Anniversary is a compilation of the following games:

Dune II: The Building of a Dynasty
Lands of Lore: The Throne of Chaos
The Legend of Kyrandia
The Legend of Kyrandia: Hand of Fate
The Legend of Kyrandia: Malcolm's Revenge

It also features a CD with .avi videos which show the growing of Westwood Studios, their games and behind the scenes footage.
