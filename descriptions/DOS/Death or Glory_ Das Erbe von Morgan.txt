Death or Glory: Das Erbe von Morgan (The Heritage of Morgan) is an interactive, multi-optional, turn-based strategy game with role playing elements.
There are 16 ingame-levels, 4096 square area with different landscapes, use of fight abilities or optional magic power, five different difficulty levels, an option for semi-automatic moves, and more than a dozen magical spells. Gather experience by winning fights to build up your character in AD&D-style.
