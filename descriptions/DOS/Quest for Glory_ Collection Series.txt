A compilation of:

Hero's Quest: So You Want to Be a Hero
Quest for Glory II: Trial by Fire
Quest for Glory III: Wages of War
Quest For Glory: Shadows of Darkness
Quest for Glory I: So You Want to Be a Hero (VGA remake of the first game)

This is almost the same as the Quest for Glory Anthology. However, it also includes a demo of Quest for Glory V: Dragon Fire and a soundtrack CD.
