Lucky Streak is a single-player shareware set of gambling games comprising:Horseracing: The player is given a list of ten horses and their odds of winning. They select a horse, place a bet, and the game tells them whether they have won or lostBlackjack: A basic implementation of the game that does not allow the player to 'split' when they are dealt a pairFruit Machine: A simple single line, three reel game where the stake is fixed at Â£1 per spin. The game randomly generates three symbols in a little window and the game pays out if the generated symbols contain two or three alikeThe game is entirely keyboard-controlled, has sound effects and uses character graphics.
