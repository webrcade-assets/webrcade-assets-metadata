A two player battle to the death. Choose from some of the toughest, meanest, quick-drawin'est gunslingers to ever put on a set of spurs, like: Frosty the snow man, Jeffrey Dahmer, a stereotypical police officer, and... Bill Clinton. Who will be left standing after the dust settles? You and a friend will decide.Outlaw 1997 is a clone of the Atari game Outlaw, with 256 color graphics and the addition of Mortal Kombat style "fatality" finishing moves.
