The Great Sage: Heaven's Equal is a 2D game very similar to Prince of Persia in presentation and gameplay. This Taiwanese video game for DOS uses Mandarin Chinese language in its intro and outro cutscenes, and was not released in the Western world. It shipped on six 5¼–inch floppy disks. Its story is based on characters and events from the Journey to the West novel. Each stage uses a unique tileset and a variety of traps and enemies.
