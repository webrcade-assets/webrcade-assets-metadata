Bust Hillary 3D is a first-person shooter game, created with the ACKNEX virtual game toolkit. The player takes on the role of Col. Lance Boyle, who enters an underground compound full of Secret Service agents in order to find ten folders containing evidence against Hillary Clinton. Two weapons are available, a pistol and a machine gun. There are three kinds of pickups: the secret folders, ammo clips, and medikits which replenish health depleted by enemy shots. The game has a time limit of 15 minutes.
