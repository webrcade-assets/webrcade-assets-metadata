Ladder Man II is a the second Ladder Man in Soleau Software 's Mindscape series.In terms of game play it is essentially the same as Ladder Man I. It holds scores for up to five people, though only one can play at a time, and it has thirty rooms or puzzles to complete.Each screen depicts a room around which are scattered objects which Ladder Man must collect. Once he has done this then the player must guide Ladder Man to the exit which leads to the next room.Ladder Man's ladder has a finite length and usually is not enough to reach the objects on its own. To assist him there are other artifacts in the room. The puzzle is to use these artifacts correctly enabling Ladder Man to collect all the objects before leaving.The game tracks the number of moves made so when playing with / against other members of the family the player who completed the puzzle with the lowest number of moves would win.
