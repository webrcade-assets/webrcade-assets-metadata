Humanity is in an intergalactic war with hostile aliens.  You command a ship that, after a space battle, boards the alien mothership.  Inside, you can fly or walk through the MadSpace where the laws of physics at times don't apply.  There are some pre-rendered interactive sections and cutscenes viewable in Stereo3D.  There are  25 levels plus secret areas, with 16 enemy types and 11 extraterrestrial weapons.  Up to 16 players can play over a network or through a modem.  It also uses speech recognition in gameplay.
