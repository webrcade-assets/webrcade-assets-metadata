Bake & Taste is an educational game for multiple systems designed to teach children how to bake.The player must cook a recipe for a group of people who either like or dislike a specific food. The player has limited time to complete all aspects of the cooking process. First, the player must choose a recipe, and then correctly gather the ingredients from the fridge, faucet, or larder. After which, the player must correctly measure each item, and set the correct amount of mixing. The player then chooses the correct pan, temperature, and cooking time. After which, the items are taste tested, and the score is based upon how closely the player adhered to the recipe. The player can review whatever mistakes they may have made.
