Das Haus is an economical simulation in which the player owns an apartment building with 30 flats. The goal is to earn as much money as possible. The player has various opportunities to influence the earnings that respectively keep the house running, e.g. buying fuel oil, changing the rent, renovating the house or investing in land or industry. There are various unforeseen occurrences to spice things up a bit, e.g. house damage, moving tenants or a lottery winning.
