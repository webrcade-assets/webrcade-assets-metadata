ranTrainer 2 is an economic simulation were the player coaches a soccer team from the Bundesliga, the highest German soccer league. The main menu is a static screen which represents the player's office. By clicking on certain items he can switch to other menu screens. There are several settings the player can use to influence the athletic success of his team, e.g. training, player market or tactical settings.The matches itself are shown in real time isometric view. During the match the player can react to the proceedings on the field, e.g. by changing his tactics. The eponymous German soccer show "ran" is represented in the game with videos which are shown in the half-time and the end of the matches were host Johannes B. Kerner and commentator Werner Hansch analyze the results.In comparison to its predecessor the game features a more detailed graphics during the match simulation, more tactical options and more videos.
