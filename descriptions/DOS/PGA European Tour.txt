This game lets players compete on the PGA European Tour. It features the K Club, Wentworth, and Gut Kaden golf courses. It also features real professional golfers and commentary by Peter Allen. Full motion video detailing the golfers and the golf courses is also included.Players can choose to either practice a round or compete in a stroke, tournament, or skins match or have a shoot-out. The game also has options like difficulty level, caddie, tap-ins, mulligans, and tee color. Golfing is done by selecting where to aim the golf ball and timing the clicks on a swing meter which determines how hard the ball is hit.
