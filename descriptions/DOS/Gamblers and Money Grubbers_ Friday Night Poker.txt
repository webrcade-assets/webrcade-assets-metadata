Gamblers And Money Grubbers - Friday Night Poker Is a single player, shareware poker game that is part of Hyperware's Gamblers And Money Grubbers series, collectively they are referred to as 'G&G Games'.This game is a poker game which offers five poker variants, 5 Card Stud, 7 Card Stud, Hi Chicago, Hold 'Em, and Random Play. The game is set up so that the player always occupies chair number four, i.e. to the right of the dealer.There are options to change the AI players - there are eight in total each with their own biography, speak the cards, change the deck, and the game speed.
