This is a compilation of ten classic Electronic Arts titles, which include:

Chuck Yeager's Air Combat
PGA Tour Golf
Wing Commander II: Vengeance of the Kilrathi - Deluxe Edition
Ultrabots
Kasparov's Gambit
Ultima VII: The Black Gate
Seal Team
Indianapolis 500: The Simulation
Grand Slam Bridge II

Also included is Financial Workshop, a personal finance program.
