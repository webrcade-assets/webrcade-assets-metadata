Definitive Wargame Collection 2 is a compilation of wargames that comes on four CDs. The games in this compilation are:* Panzer General

Steel Panthers
Tanks!
Clash Of Steel
Command H.Q.
High Command Europe 1939-'45
Genghis Khan II
V for Victory: Utah Beach
V for Victory: Market-Garden
Harpoon II with Harpoon II Battle Set 2 and Harpoon II Battle Set 3
Operation Europe: Path To Victory
Romance Of The Three Kingdoms III

The games and some demos are on two CDs, the manuals are on a separate CD and some more demo games are on the fourth disc.
