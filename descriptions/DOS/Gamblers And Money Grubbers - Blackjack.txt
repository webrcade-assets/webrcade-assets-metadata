Gamblers And Money Grubbers - Blackjack Is a single player, shareware Blackjack game that is part of Hyperware's Gamblers And Money Grubbers series, collectively they are referred to as 'G&G Games'.This game is a Blackjack game which offers the following features:Play with up to 4 other players.Use up to six decksChangeable the game rulesGame statisticsBats can be systematically increased as the count sweetens.In game card counting, plus a customised card counting optionCustomisable strategies plus the option to have the game verify their effectiveness by playing up to 30000 hands quicklyThe player can 'change their seat' at the table at any time.Statistics on other payersA replay feature for the last gameAl AI players have their own biographies and styles
