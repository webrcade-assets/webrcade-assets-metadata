Gamblers And Money Grubbers - Blackjack Is a single player, shareware Blackjack game that is part of Hyperware's Gamblers And Money Grubbers series, collectively they are referred to as 'G&G Games'.
This game is a Blackjack game which offers the following features:

Play with up to 4 other players.
Use up to six decks
Changeable the game rules
Game statistics
Bats can be systematically increased as the count sweetens.
In game card counting, plus a customised card counting option
Customisable strategies plus the option to have the game verify their effectiveness by playing up to 30000 hands quickly
The player can 'change their seat' at the table at any time.
Statistics on other payers
A replay feature for the last game
Al AI players have their own biographies and styles
