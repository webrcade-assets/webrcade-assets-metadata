Richman 2 is a strategy title designed as a board game. It is played in three regions: Taiwan, Hong Kong and Monopoly City. Players take turns rolling dice to move around. The number shown by the dice is the number of steps taken by the player's character. The goal is to accumulate wealth by traveling around, investing in land, gambling in casinos, understanding the stock market, buying cards in the black market, falsely accusing opponents in the courts and setting mines for opponents, etc. to defeat opponents and become a millionaire.
