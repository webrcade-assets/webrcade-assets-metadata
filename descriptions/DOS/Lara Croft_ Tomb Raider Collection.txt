This compilation includes the first six games in the series stored on seven discs in three keep cases held together by a cardboard box. For the older versions, installers have been included to help configure graphics and sound. Next to the base games, gold versions and add-ons are thrown in as well, along with patches and PDF manuals.The games included are:Tomb Raider GoldTomb Raider II GoldTomb Raider III: Adventures of Lara CroftTomb Raider: The Last RevelationTomb Raider: ChroniclesLara Croft Tomb Raider: The Angel of Darkness
