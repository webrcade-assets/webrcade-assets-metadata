This compilation is the counterpart to Big 10 Strategie-Sport-Adventure, but this time only Action and Simulation games are included. And like the game title says, there are 10 games in the compilation spread over 6 CD's. Full (but not original) manuals are on disc only, while the printed manual has only setup instructions.The following games are included:Absolute ZeroCyclemaniaEF2000Flight UnlimitedFX FighterJack Nicklaus Golf & Course Design: Signature EditionJammitJetfighter II: Advanced Tactical FighterSilent Steel SESuper Karts
