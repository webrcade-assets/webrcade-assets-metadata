Inspired by Winnie the Pooh, Happy Birthday! is an educational adventure that tells the story about a birthday of a teddy bear Finnie, who lives in the woods with his friends: piglet, donkey, owl, and rabbit. The friends decided to make a present for him, but the player should help Finnie to invite the friends and to collect the proper fruits for the feast. Walking around the forest, Finnie should also overcome certain obstacles, such as mazes or falling cones, giving either proper answers to the questions or allowing the player to show his action skills. The game has introductory story told in Russian, but filled with English nouns written and pronounced to be memorized and used by the player.
