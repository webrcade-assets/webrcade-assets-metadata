Nukem is a Missile Command -style game, where you defend cities from incoming missiles by firing anti-missiles at them. This particular game features jets, splitting missiles, and even more features. It has the option for two-base mode and three-base mode. In two-base mode, the base you fire missiles from is selected using the left and right mouse buttons. In three-base mode, you use S, D, and F to fire from each of the three bases. It also features both keyboard and mouse-based aiming.
Points are given for remaining cities and remaining missiles after each round.
