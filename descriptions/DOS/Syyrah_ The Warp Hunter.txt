Syyrah: The Warp Hunter is a Bust-A-Move variant.You have to team up with Syyrah in 120 levels, when strategy and reflexes mix up in action-puzzle game. You have to put grenade in necessary places to eliminate dangerous obstacles. The game now features 2D and 3D graphics.You can play the game by 1 or 2 players (including head-to-head challenge in a split-screen mode).
