Bowling Champ!! is a simple bowling game for one to four players with text graphics.After entering the number of players, their name(s), and setting a difficulty level the game begins. It plays left to right across the screen. At the far left the ball oscillates rapidly at the end of the lane, by pressing the space bar the player releases the ball which travels across the screen and hits the pins at the far right. Scoring is as in a normal game of bowls.This game is adware. On exit the player is shown a message advertising "The Techstaff Amazing Preview Disk" a menu driven catalogue of IBM Shareware and Public Domain programs.
