Ants: The Exodus is a single player strategy game with 25 levels and a level editor. You control an ant, and need to guide all four princess ants to the exit safely. You do this by collecting and placing various objects, such as direction arrows and blocks. There is a time limit for each level.Items include arrows (that force travel along a given path), bridges (to cross gaps), dynamite (to destroy blocks and enemies), mines (to destroy enemies), keys and locked doors, webs (that will trap you), and holes (that you can fall in).
