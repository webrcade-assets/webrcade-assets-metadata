This compilation contains Raven Software's fantasy shooters based on id Software 's Doom-based graphics engine:

Heretic: Shadow of the Serpent Riders
HeXen: Beyond Heretic
HeXen: Deathkings of the Dark Citadel

The compilation is the first release of those titles for the Acorn platform. The PC release comes with both Windows 95 and DOS versions of Hexen: Beyond Heretic.
