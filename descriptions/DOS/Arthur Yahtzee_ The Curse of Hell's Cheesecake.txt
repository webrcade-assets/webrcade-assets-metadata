Arthur Yahtzee: The Curse of Hell's Cheesecake is a interaction fiction spin-off from the Arthur Yahtzee trilogy of graphic adventures. The player takes control over secret agent Arthur Yahtzee who goes to hell in order to retrieve Hell's Cheesecake. He will visit various places, e.g. the river Styx or heaven, on his way to success.
While exploring the environments, the player interacts with the game by typing in commands into the parser. The puzzles are typically solved by collecting and using items. The writing is humorous.
