Shen Diao Xia Lv is an RPG based on the wuxia novel The Return of the Condor Heroes by the Chinese author Jin Yong. The game builds on the story's essence exploring the love between the characters Yang Guo and Xiao Long Nv. Character attributes can be improved through battles and the party can be extended with new members joining. Each party member has its own weapons and equipment. Experience is also gained by performing certain actions and tricks multiple times.
