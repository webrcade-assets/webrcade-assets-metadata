God Jul! (Merry Christmas! in Swedish) is a Christmas-themed arcade game, where the player should help a janitor to clean a staircase of small house from snow. This process is divided by three stages, all of them should be completed in 10-15 minutes. Clean staircase at each stage will end up with a score based on the completion time and congratulations from Santa Claus. The player controls the janitor on the staircase, directing him to snow and avoiding the falling icicles. Highest scores are stored into top ten table.
