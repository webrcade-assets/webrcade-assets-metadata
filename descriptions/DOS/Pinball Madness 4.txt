Billed as the "world's largest pinball collection", Pinball Madness 4 brings together 5 full-version pinball games in one box. Included are:Williams Pinball Classics ( Encore, Inc.)Pro Pinball: Fantastic Journey ( Empire Interactive)Adventure Pinball: Forgotten Island ( Digital Extremes)Platinum Pinball ( Encore, Inc.)3D Pinball Express ( Cosmi Corporation)Pinball Madness 4 is unusual even amongst other shovelware titles. For example, the included title Platinum Pinball is already a bundle of Slam Tilt Pinball, Absolute Pinball, 21st Century Ltd's Pinball Mania as well as original tables from developer Redoubt, Ltd.
