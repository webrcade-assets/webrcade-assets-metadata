Robotica is a single player, strategy / shooter.
The player controls a robot that starts off in the centre of the game area. They are surrounded by baddies and must find a way to rescue all the humans that are on the screen. Some baddies just attack the player while others will attack the humans the player is supposed to rescue.
The robot is moved around the screen by moving the mouse while the robot's gun, which fires continually, is directed by the numbers on the numeric keypad, NUM LOCK must be enabled for this game.
The game has a high score table, four difficulty levels and each difficulty has 20 game levels.
