This compilation bundles three LucasArts games, along with a couple of extras. The full games are:Indiana Jones and the Fate of AtlantisManiac Mansion: Day of the TentacleSam & Max: Hit the RoadThe box also includes demos of Full Throttle, Star Wars: Dark Forces (not present in Mac version) and three playable levels of Star Wars: Rebel Assault, along with a Star Wars Screen Entertainment and a LucasArts SuperSampler disc
