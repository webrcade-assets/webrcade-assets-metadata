This special CD-ROM edition of Jazz Jackrabbit contains the original six episodes and three new ones: "Outta Dis World", "Wild Wabbit", and "Turtle Soup".The new episodes have Jazz exploring planets with such names as Exoticus, Industrius, Deserto, Stonar, and several others. The episode features new music, new graphics, more enemies, and new bosses.A second release also includes Jazz Jackrabbit Holiday Hare 94.
