Gamebox is a compilation of seven mini-games: Bomber*

Caterpillar
Panther
Šaratica-run
Space Invaders
Tetris
Yellow Submarine

Its creators, Vladimír Chvátil and Radim Křivánek, made Gamebox while they were in university, probably in year 1994. It is a compilation of their older games with a shared high-score added.
