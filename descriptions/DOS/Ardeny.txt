Ardeny is a tactical/operational level turn- and tile-based strategy game set during the Battle of the Bulge in World War II.The player takes command of either Allied or German forces during the Ardennes Offensive in winter 1944-45. It is possible to issue move, fire support or supply orders for infantry, armor, artillery and air units. The spartan interface is entirely keyboard-driven. The game comes with basic background information about the battle and the equipment.
