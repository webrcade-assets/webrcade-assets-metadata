White Wolf Software: Series IV is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label. This CD contains the following shareware products:

A: Rise of the Triad: The HUNT Begins
B: Bio Menace
C: Race The Nags
D: The Adventures of Captain Comic
E: 3-Point Basketball
F: Cheat: a selection of game codes and cheats
G: Movie Trivia
H: Active Life: A personal planner
I: Drum Blaster: music making software from Epic Megagames
J: Alpha Man

The software is installed individually, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
