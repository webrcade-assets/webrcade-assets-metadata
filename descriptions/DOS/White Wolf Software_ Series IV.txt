White Wolf Software: Series IV is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label. This CD contains the following shareware products:A: Rise of the Triad: The HUNT BeginsB: Bio MenaceC: Race The NagsD: The Adventures of Captain ComicE: 3-Point BasketballF: Cheat: a selection of game codes and cheatsG: Movie TriviaH: Active Life: A personal plannerI: Drum Blaster: music making software from Epic MegagamesJ: Alpha ManThe software is installed individually, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
