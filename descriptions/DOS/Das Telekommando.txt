Das Telekommando is a point-and-click adventure where the player takes the role of a communication technician working for the Telekom. His job is repair technical devices like distribution boxes for example in a German town named Sabbelheim. To reach his destinations he can use a small white service van by selecting his goal on a map of Sabbelheim.It is an advergame for a German communications company. Its intent is to give an insight to the job of a communications technician working for the Telekom.
