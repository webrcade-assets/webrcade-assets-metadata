SITris is a single-player, shareware, falling block game. This game is essentially a Tetris clone with virtually identical gameplay and some additional features. In this game the player can:redefine the action keys;choose whether the next piece is displayed;choose between regular, small, large, wide or skinny blocks;start at any of the ten levels of difficulty;select from five different heights at which the blocks are released;use three different block sets or a use a combination of all of them;enable the SITris amoeba, an invisible creature that eats random blocks creating holes;enable a block creation function that causes blocks to spontaneously appear.The game has a single high score table that serves all modes of play and is entirely keyboard-controlled.
