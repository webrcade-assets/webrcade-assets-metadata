Writer Rabbit makes preparations for a Sentence Party. The game teaches users how to form sentences and reinforces good grammar and typing skills. There are six activities including "Icecream Game", three "Cake Games", "Juice Game" and "Silly Story Party". The player can choose one of three difficulty levels to play the activities. The key words for forming sentences are "How", "Who", "What", "When", "Why", "Where" and "Did What".
