Number five in the Megapak -compilation series. The collection includes ten games. As always, games from nearly all common genres are included. This compilation is a typical "cash cow" re-release of quite successful games (though, not many best-sellers are included) of a prior "gaming year".
Included games are:

Entomorph: Plague of the Darkfall
Flight Unlimited (Special Edition)
FX Fighter
Great Naval Battles: Burning Steel 1939-1942
Jagged Alliance
Pinball Fantasies Deluxe
Pool Champion
Primal Rage
Terminal Velocity
Warlords II Deluxe
