Niszczyciel is a simple first perspective shooter. The player takes on the role of an assassin and completes various orders for money. Each mission is in a different city and requires you to complete a different task. There are 14 completely different vehicles and weapons in the game, including cars, tanks, shotguns, flame throwers. The player may, apart from fighting numerous enemies and the police, smash garbage cans, fire hydrants, lamps, trees, and steal cars.
