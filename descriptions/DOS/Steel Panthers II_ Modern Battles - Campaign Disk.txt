As the name implies this add-on contains new campaigns for Steel Panthers 2: Modern Battles. You get three new campaigns, based on fictional events:Korea 1998: Play a second Korea war, taking command over some Korean forces.Battle for the Low Countries: A resurgent and aggressive Germany threatens Europe in the 1990s. British and German forces clash in Belgium.Drive into the Rhineland: The French stage an assault into the Rhineland after a renewed German aggression in Poland.The disc also contains 30 new stand-alone scenarios covering various periods of post-WW2 conflicts.
