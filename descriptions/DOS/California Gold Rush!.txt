California Gold Rush! is a re-release of Sierra’s original Gold Rush! by its designers Ken and Doug MacNeill. It is packaged in a handmade wooden box that includes:* 2 x 3.5 disks

a poster-sized map of the period showing all three routes to California
a user’s guide with detailed setup and playing instructions, the historical background on the California Gold Rush of 1849, helpful suggestions for playing each section of the game and information to help you plan a vacation to the Gold Country
a flyer for the collector’s edition.
