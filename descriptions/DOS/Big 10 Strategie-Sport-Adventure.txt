This compilation is one of two Big 10 compilations. While the counterpart, Big 10 Simulation-Action, focuses on Action and Simulation, this one here focuses on strategy, sport, and adventure. Like the game title says, there are 10 games included, which are spread over 7 CD's. The game manuals are as usual only on CD, while the printed manual has only setup instructions.
The following games are included:

3-D Table Sports
Anyone For Cards?
Grandmaster Chess
HardBall III
Jagged Alliance
Ripley's Believe It or Not!: The Riddle of Master Lu
Shannara
Terra Nova: Strike Force Centauri
The Journeyman Project 2: Buried in Time
Twisted Mini Golf
