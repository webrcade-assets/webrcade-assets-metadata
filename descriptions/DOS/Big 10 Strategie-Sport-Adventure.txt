This compilation is one of two Big 10 compilations. While the counterpart, Big 10 Simulation-Action, focuses on Action and Simulation, this one here focuses on strategy, sport, and adventure. Like the game title says, there are 10 games included, which are spread over 7 CD's. The game manuals are as usual only on CD, while the printed manual has only setup instructions.The following games are included:3-D Table SportsAnyone For Cards?Grandmaster ChessHardBall IIIJagged AllianceRipley's Believe It or Not!: The Riddle of Master LuShannaraTerra Nova: Strike Force CentauriThe Journeyman Project 2: Buried in TimeTwisted Mini Golf
