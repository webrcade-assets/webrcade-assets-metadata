Micro Adventure No. 10: Spellbound is a novel and series of type-in BASIC adventures for multiple systems.Secret agent Orion, computer whiz of ACT (Adventure Connection Team) must defeat a master of murderous magic.  Someone or something is after the nation's top athletes using voodoo or black magic.  Orion must use his computer skills to defeat this otherworldly threat.The player must use their computer to play a strategy game against a deadly cheat, intercept the enemy's secret transmissions, and stop a computerized nuclear missile countdown.Though the story, there are numerous interactive type-in programs that offer numerous outcomes. An appendix is included describing how to modify the BASIC program listings for greatest compatibility with many of the most popular home microcomputer BASICs, as well as describing what each program does and how it works.
