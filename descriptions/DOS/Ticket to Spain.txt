Ticket to Spain is an educational game designed to teach players about Spanish language and culture.The player arrives in Spain with several hundred dollars in American currency with the goal of finding their cousin and learning Spanish language and culture. The player can travels to the major cities of Spain bus, train, or rental car, and more. During travels, they must regularly eat and sleep to keep their energy up, if they don't they will collapse and have to spend the day in the hospital. At every location the player visits, they receive several multiple choice questions about the Spanish language.As the player explores, they receive hints to the location of their cousin who is also vacationing in Spain. In each city, the player can visit multiple locations, including the local cathedrals, marketplaces, hotel, hospital, and other famous locations for that city. Whenever the player finds their cousin, he briefly says hello before traveling again on his way.
