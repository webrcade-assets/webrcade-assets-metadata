Xplosiv Top 10 is a compilation of 10 games of different genres, previously released separately on the Xplosiv budget label. Most games have patches and a digital manual included. The box comprises the following games:

Combat Chess
International Cricket Captain 2000
Panzer Dragoon
Pro Pinball: The Web
SEGA Touring Car Championship (as SEGA Touring Cars)
SEGA Worldwide Soccer
Sonic 3D
Sonic R
Virtua Cop 2
Virtua Fighter 2
