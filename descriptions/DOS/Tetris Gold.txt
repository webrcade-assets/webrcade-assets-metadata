Tetris Gold is a compilation released by Spectrum HoloByte in 1993 containing all of the Tetris games and spinoffs they ever made:TetrisSuper TetrisTetris ClassicWordtrisFaces...Tris IIIWelltrisprototype version of TetrisIt includes versions for DOS (all 7), Windows (Tetris Classic and Super Tetris only) and Macintosh (all but Tetris Classic and the prototype version, color and B&W versions included). The CD also includes a 10-minute video interview with Alexey Pajitnov, the creator of Tetris.
