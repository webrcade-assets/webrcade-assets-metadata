Tetris Gold is a compilation released by Spectrum HoloByte in 1993 containing all of the Tetris games and spinoffs they ever made:

Tetris
Super Tetris
Tetris Classic
Wordtris
Faces...Tris III
Welltris
prototype version of Tetris

It includes versions for DOS (all 7), Windows (Tetris Classic and Super Tetris only) and Macintosh (all but Tetris Classic and the prototype version, color and B&W versions included). The CD also includes a 10-minute video interview with Alexey Pajitnov, the creator of Tetris.
