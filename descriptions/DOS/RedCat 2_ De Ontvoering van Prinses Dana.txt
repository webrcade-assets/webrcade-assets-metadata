This is the second Redcat game, similar to the first one. In this game, princess Dana has been kidnapped and it's up to Redcat to save her.The game plays a lot like the first Redcat game. You play as Redcat, who has to fight through seven levels to rescue the princess. During these levels, you will have to kill a lot of mice again to get to the end. This second game features some new additions not available in the first game, like some new enemies and items for Redcat to pick up.
