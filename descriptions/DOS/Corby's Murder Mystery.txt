Corby has been invited by his uncle to his countryside mansion to look after the place in his absence for the weekend. Corby asks his friends to stay with him. Not long after that Corby's friends are getting killed one by one. This fan game makes use of Sierra's AGI2 Engine, but unlike every Sierra game, there are almost no deaths for the player character. The storyline is divided into ten acts and the player needs to deduce who killed who for the best conclusion.
