Super All-Stars is a compilation of five games by Codemasters.
The games in this compilation are:* Steg The Slug

Magicland Dizzy
Captain Dynamo
CJ In The USA

The last game of the compilation was different on some of the platforms:

DOS: Seymour Goes To Hollywood
Amiga, Atari ST Robin Hood: Legend Quest
Spectrum, C-64: Turbo the Tortoise
