White Wolf Software: Series I is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label. This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: DOOM: Knee Deep in the Dead

B: Ken's Labyrinth
C: Overkill
D: Kiloblaster episode one
E: Flying Tigers
F: Cheat: a selection of game codes and cheats
G: Roboto
H: World War II Trivia
I: Heroes & Hunters: an early form of e-book about the American wild west
J: Capture the Flag v3.1

The software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory
