White Wolf Software: Series I is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label. This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: DOOM: Knee Deep in the DeadB: Ken's LabyrinthC: OverkillD: Kiloblaster episode oneE: Flying TigersF: Cheat: a selection of game codes and cheatsG: RobotoH: World War II TriviaI: Heroes & Hunters: an early form of e-book about the American wild westJ: Capture the Flag v3.1The software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory
