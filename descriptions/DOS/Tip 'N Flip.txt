Tip 'N Flip is a single-player logic game.A picture consisting of 4 different shapes or objects can be manipulated by rotating left or right, or by flipping vertically or horizontally. The player gains 10 points for each correct answer, and has a scratch pad option for testing out manipulation of the picture. The player loses 1 point for each use of the scratch pad. In Game 1, the player sees the original picture and two possible answers, and the player must choose which picture is possible. In Game 2, the player must manipulate a picture so it matches another. There are 4 different difficulty levels for each challenge.
