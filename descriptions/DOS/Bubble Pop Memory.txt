Bubble Pop Memory is a freeware Memory -style game. 32 cards are placed face down in a random order on the screen, and the goal is to locate the 16 pairs of identical cards in the least possible amount of trials. The player can click on two cards at once to reveal their value. If they are identical, they remain visible. If they are different, they are flipped down again. The player has 4 minutes to complete the game.The best scores are recorded in a high score table.
