RACE! to the finish against miniature opponents in this top-down arcade racing game. Pick from one of the twenty levels of the game and play against the computer or up to three friends via the same keyboard. Obstacles abound on the levels of RACE!, from the typical oil slicks to the non-typical tornadoes. Performing well in the races will increase the speed of your car, traction and acceleration. The better performance will also speed up the helicopter which comes to your rescue when you crash.The game also includes an easy editor for players to create their own tracks and add their own hazards to existing levels.
