Girlfriend Terri is an entry in the Girlfriend titles, a series of simulation games. Like the other girlfriends, you can start a conversation with her and ask her to perform certain actions. The girl replies using both voice and text and responds to the input of the player. She also remembers earlier conversations. The girl is shown through live action full motion video. HUD elements include the current location, time, mood, a system window, a window where text is entered and replies appear, and a status panel for syntax, semantics, response and voice.This is an adult game including erotic images and activities.
