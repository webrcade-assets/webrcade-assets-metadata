You are an explorer in the Amazon. You have heard rumors of giant ants, and have gotten a native to lead you to one of their nests.Ant Hill volume 1 is a simple game where you explore a giant ant hill through ten levels. Avoid the red ants, collect pickaxes to break walls, and grab all the ant eggs and gemstones you can find.Volume 1 was distributed as Shareware. Registration just hides the shareware screen, but there is a volume 2 with more levels.
