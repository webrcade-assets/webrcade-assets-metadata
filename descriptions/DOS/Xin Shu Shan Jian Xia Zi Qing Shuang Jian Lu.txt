Xin Shu Shan Jian Xia Zi Qing Shuang Jian Lu is an ancient Chinese-themed role-playing game and is adapted from Huanzhu Lou Zhu 's novel. The story is about Li Yingqiong of Emei, who was ordered by her teacher to pick Seven Star Grass. She rescued some villagers, and she learned that someone had harmed the world under the guise of alchemy. Li Yingqiong would like to investigate the truth - which is where the story begins.
