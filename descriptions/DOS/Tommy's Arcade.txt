Tommy's Arcade is a compilation of fifteen games from Tommy's Toys.
The games included in this compilation are * Tommy's Palace

Tommy's Blob
Tommy's Turkeys
Tommy's Egg
Tommy's Starball
Tommy's Hyperdrive
Tommy's Jammer
Tommy's Meteors
Tommy's Floppy Discus
Tommy's Gauntlet * Tommy's Flipout
Tommy's Mole
Tommy's Energy Net
Tommy's Space Goblins
Tommy's Nyet

This compilation was released as shareware. The player had full use for 28 days after which the game had to be registered.
