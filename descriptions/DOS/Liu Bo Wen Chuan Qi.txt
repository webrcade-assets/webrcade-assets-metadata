Liu Bo Wen Chuan Qi is a role-playing game and the second title in the Qimen Duanjia series developed by Soft-World. The game tells the story of Zhu Yuanzhang, who is the first king of the Ming Dynasty. Liu Bowen, a national minister, goes to South Guangdong. It is the beginning of a series of strange incidents where the people discover the ambitions of some officials.
