This special compilation was released in order to support the launch of the fourth part in the Battle Isle Series: The Andosia War.
The compilation includes all parts of the series as well as the mission disks/CDs:

Battle Isle
Battle Isle: Scenario Disk Volume One
Battle Isle '93 - The Moon of Chromos

The Great War: 1914-1918
Battle Isle 2200
Battle Isle 2: Scenery CD - Titan's Legacy

Battle Isle 2220: Shadow of the Emperor
Incubation: Time Is Running Out
Incubation: The Wilderness Missions
