The Soccer Game II is largely a feature upgraded version of the popular shareware title The Soccer Game, where the player is hired as a probationary manager of a soccer team.You manage the player roster and staff, directing them to do their jobs as well, such as the team Scout looking for new players or the team Medic (Physio) managing injuries.  Budgetary choices must be made, such as investing in a youth team, improving your stadium, and staff salaries.The goal is for your team to amass a winning record and elevate to higher divisions, to become the top-ranked team while not going bankrupt in the process.  The game has a save/restore feature so you can enjoy this detailed game around your busy life.(For clarity, this game features Soccer as understood by Americans, it is called Football in some other cultures.  The game instructions references both terms at one point.)
