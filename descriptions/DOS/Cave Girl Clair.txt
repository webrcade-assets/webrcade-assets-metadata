Cave Girl Clair is an educational adventure game.Resourceful and handy, Clair must survive the challenges of the New Stone Age. When her people migrate to their summer cave, Clair and her pet rabbit are mistakenly left behind. But she is a spunky girl and knows they will return one day.The player starts at their cave, where they must decide from two difficulty levels. At the cave, the player must decide whether they they will start their fire with flint or with a stick. The player must explore a number of screens, in which Clair must gather food, firewood, and medicinal herbs, as well as construct tools. The player defend themselves from dangerous creatures. The goal is to survive four seasons until Clair's family returns to her cave.
