Poker Squares is a 1 - 4 player strategy game for multiple systems.The player has a 5 x 5 grid, and is randomly dealt 25 cards from a deck one at a time. The player must place the cards in the square, trying to play pokers hands for maximum score. The player has a total of 10 hands, 5 read vertically and 5 read horizontally. In multiplayer games, each player is dealt the same set of cards, with the players competing to maximize score. More experienced players can be given a handicap, and players are given 5 - 60 seconds per play.
