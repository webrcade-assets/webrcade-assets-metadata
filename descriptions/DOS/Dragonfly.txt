Dragonfly is a single player, keyboard controlled game, believed to be freeware.The player controls a small green helicopter that starts in the middle of the screen. The background 'beneath' the helicopter is blue signifying water and on that water red hydrofoils zip about. The objective is to destroy the boats without being shot down.Movement is controlled either with a joystick or the numeric keypad (numbers one to nine except five). Weapons are the machine gun (fired by numeric keypad five) or bombs which are dropped by pressing the space bar.
