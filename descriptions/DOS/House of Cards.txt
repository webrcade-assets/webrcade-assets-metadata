House of Cards is a compilation of six casino-style single-player-versus-computer card games for DOS.  All games can be controlled via mouse, keyboard, or joystick, and all cards are graphically displayed on the screen.
Contains:

Bridge
Cribbage
Gin Rummy
Hearts
Pinochle
Spades

For each game the player can set a game limit, play demo mode, practice, and show rules or win log.
