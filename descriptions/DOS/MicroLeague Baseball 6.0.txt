MicroLeague Baseball 6.0 is the fifth and final edition in the MicroLeague Baseball series. You can manage your favorite Major League team, playing exhibition games or League Play to manage or simulate scheduled contests. Phillies' broadcaster Harry Kalas provides the play-by-play commentary. It includes Team Manager, Game Manager, Stat Manager and Network Play options. Players create their own leagues and teams, customize rosters, line-ups and pitching rotations. It is possible to conduct drafts and edit schedules.
