Stickybear ABC is an early "Edutainment" game for kids 3-7.  Stickybear was a children's book series from the early to mid 80s that focused on teaching children pre-school and elementary school knowledge.  Stickybear ABC follows the same vein by teaching letters with the help of the main character, Stickybear.There are three different modes to Stickybear ABC: ABC, Match and Order.  The ABC mode allows the player to hit a key on the keyboard, and then see an object that starts with that letter.  The Match mode, which challenges the player to match a letter to an object.  Lastly, the Order mode, ask the player to order letters in the alphabet.
