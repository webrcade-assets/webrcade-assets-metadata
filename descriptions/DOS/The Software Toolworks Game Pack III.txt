This compilation includes five full-version games on five 720K floppy disks:

Jack Nicklaus' Unlimited Golf and Course Design
Life & Death
Road & Track Presents Grand Prix Unlimited
F-19 Stealth Fighter
Gin King / Cribbage King

The box also contains a 204-page manual, a quick start card with instructions on running the games, an addendum card for Jack Nicklaus' Unlimited Golf and Course Design, and a keyboard control reference for F-19 Stealth Fighter.
