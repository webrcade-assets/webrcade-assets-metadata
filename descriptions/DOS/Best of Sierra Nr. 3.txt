This is the November/December 1997 issue (Volume 3) of the Best of Sierra series.This issue's title games are:3-D Ultra Pinball: Creep NightFreddy Pharkas: Frontier PharmacistIn addition to these two full version games, the CD also contains these demos: DSF Golf and Print Artist 3.Furthermore, there is a Trailer for 3-D Ultra Pinball: The Lost Continent and a Screensaver for Red Baron II included.The magazine's special features are this time:A 10 pages long detailed table description for 3-D Ultra Pinball: Creep Night, a 9 pages long detailed walkthrough for Freddy Pharkas: Frontier Pharmacist, and a 12 pages long ECTS 97 article with the following games: Red Baron II, Pro Pilot '99, 3-D Ultra Pinball: The Lost Continent, Half-Life, Outpost 2: Divided Destiny, Police Quest: SWAT 2, Lords of Magic, Diablo: Hellfire, DSF Golf, DSF Off-Road, DSF Ski, DSF Baseball Pro '98, and DSF Football Pro '98.
