The Comedy Collection in Infocom's series combines four interactive text-based adventure games on CD-ROM. The featured titles are:

Ballyhoo
Bureaucracy
Hollywood Hijinx
Nord and Bert Couldn't Make Head or Tail of It

Bonus games:

Planetfall
Zork I

The games are compatible with Microsoft Windows or can be run directly in DOS. They are also compatible with Apple Macintosh computers.
