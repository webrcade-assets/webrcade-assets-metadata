Action Stations is a Naval Surface Combat Tactical Simulation covering the years 1922-1945. The game treats thoroughly in detail smoke, starshells, flares, searchlights, radar, shore batteries, stack gasses, aircraft recce, and spotting, burning oil slicks, capsized hulks, weather, and more. Ship classes go from PT Boats to Battleships and represent the U.S., German, British, Italian, French, Japanese, Dutch, and Russian Fleets. Many "design only" classes are included. In addition to 30 packaged scenarios, the build scenario feature allows you to generate an infinite number of new situations.
