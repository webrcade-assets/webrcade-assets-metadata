In Atomia Akorny, the player works for Count Akorny of Transylvania. The ancient crown of the family was stolen and now someone has to bring it back. Because the reward is the hand of the count's daughter (and punishment for cowardice is jail), the player moves forward into the dark woods...
The player interacts with the game by typing commands into the parser. However, it is relatively simple and, by the author's admission, only knows a few verbs. Puzzles are typically solved by collecting and using items.
