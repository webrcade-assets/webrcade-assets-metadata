The Roberta Williams Anthology is a compilation of games created by Sierra game designer and co-founder Roberta Williams. It contains:the first seven King's Quest games :Roberta Williams' King's Quest I: Quest for the CrownKing's Quest II: Romancing the ThroneKing's Quest III: To Heir is HumanKing's Quest IV: The Perils of RosellaKing's Quest V: Absence Makes the Heart Go Yonder!King's Quest VI: Heir Today, Gone TomorrowRoberta Williams' King's Quest VII: The Princeless BrideHi-Res Adventure #0: Mission AsteroidHi-Res Adventure #1: Mystery HouseHi-Res Adventure #2: The Wizard and the PrincessHi-Res Adventure #6: The Dark CrystalTime ZoneThe Colonel's BequestThe Dagger of Amon RaRoberta Williams' Mixed-Up Mother Goosethe first chapter of Roberta Williams' Phantasmagoriavideo interviews with Ken and Roberta Williams, and some behind-the-scenes footage of the making of King's Quest: Mask of EternityThe Apple II titles contain an Apple II emulator for Windows that can run them.
