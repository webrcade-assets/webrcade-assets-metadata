The Wing Commander Premiere Pack was a special edition shipped in a container resembling a film canister. Inside, it contained the following:Wing Commander III: Heart of the Tiger (Four CDs) and the game manual.Making of Wing Commander III CD-ROMBehind the Scenes of Wing Commander III VHS Tape.A Wing Commander III T-Shirt.A Wing Commander III Wall Calendar for 1994.Some versions additionally contain:The novel Wing Commander: Fleet Action by William R. Forstchen.Origin Audio CD Vol 3 with game soundtracks.The Warbirds reference sheet in full-colour instead of black and white.
