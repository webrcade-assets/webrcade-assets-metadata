Leisure Suit Larry Collection Series is, as the name implies, a collection of all Leisure Suit Larry games from 1 to 6 (with the exception of Leisure Suit Larry 4: The Case of the Missing Floppies, which never came out because "Larry doesn't believe in 4-play").The collection comes on two discs, where the first contains DOS versions of the following games:Leisure Suit Larry in the Land of the Lounge LizardsLeisure Suit Larry 1: In the Land of the Lounge LizardsLeisure Suit Larry Goes Looking for Love (in Several Wrong Places)Leisure Suit Larry III: Passionate Patti in Pursuit of the Pulsating PectoralsLeisure Suit Larry 5: Passionate Patti Does a Little Undercover WorkLeisure Suit Larry 6: Shape Up or Slip OutThe second disc contains a Windows-based version of Leisure Suit Larry 6: Shape Up or Slip Out featuring a revised interface and speech.Some editions additionally include the following content:Softporn AdventureThe Laffer Utilities - Helps handle your non-productive hours more efficientlyLarry's Big Score - A pinball game featuring LarryCrazy Nick's Software Picks: Leisure Suit Larry's CasinoVideo Interviews with Al LoweThe book The Official Book of Leisure Suit LarryA hint book
