This is a Japanese adventure style game. 
The story takes place in a village in the mountains of the Balkans.Every summer, many tourists come here for sightseeing and vacation, bringing a lively atmosphere to the mountain city.Near the village is a clear and beautiful lake. It is said that many years ago a couple committed suicide by throwing themselves into the lake for their love.The villagers were moved by the unswerving love of the couple and called the lake a lake of sadness.
At the beginning of summer vacation, I went to play with  Xiao Ai.Xiao Ai said that he had made an appointment with Xiao Wei. He thought about staying in his old castle for a few days. Xiao Lan and Xiao Qi also went with him. But they were accidentally involved in a bizarre murder case....
