King's Quest: Collector's Edition is an earlier version of the King's Quest: Collection Series which contains:

King's Quest (with the VGA version)
King's Quest II: Romancing the Throne
King's Quest III: To Heir is Human
King's Quest IV: The Perils of Rosella
King's Quest V: Absence Makes the Heart Go Yonder!
King's Quest VI: Heir Today, Gone Tomorrow

It also contains a preview of:

Roberta Williams' King's Quest VII: The Princeless Bride

Additionally, it includes a bunch of small programs, like a checkers game and a trivia game.
