Champ Centipede is a variant of Atari's Centipede.
One or two players must get rid of all the insects invading a mushroom patch, including Fleas, Spiders, Scorpions and the Centipede. The game includes different difficulty levels, sound effects and a high score table. It is controlled either by the keyboard, joystick or the mouse and has single player, player v player and a two player co-operative mode
