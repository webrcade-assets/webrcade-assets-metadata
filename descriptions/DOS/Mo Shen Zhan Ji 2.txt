Mo Shen Zhan Ji 2 is a role-playing game. During the years of God, a murderer named Carosotho appears in the world of Miatis, killing many innocent people. When his legacy spread the fearing people gave him the title Dark King. The ruler of the Devil Empire feared the Dark King would carry out more massacres, so he combines the strength of the Elves, Orcs, Dragons and the people in a fight. After a fierce battle the Dark King is finally eliminated.
Eighteen years later, rumor spread that if three artifacts were collected, the power of immortality would be gained. But no one knows where the rumor originated and nobody knows what the three artifacts are. However, the Devil Emperor actively dispatches people to carry out the search. Those people are however worried that if the artifacts are found and brought together a new Dark King may rise.
