Battune Becomes an Actor is another text adventure in the Battune series.This story is set in the 1900's and the player's character is living on a farm in England and is bored. Seeing an advertisement for a life on the stage they set off to make their fortune. Along the way the player will encounter American Gangsters, sail on the Titanic, visit Hollywood and more. Battune is a friendly alien guide who accompanies the player on their journeyThe game is entirely text based and must be played in upper case at all times.
