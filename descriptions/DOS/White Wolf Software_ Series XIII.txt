White Wolf Software: Series XIII is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label.
This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: Wolfenstein 3D

B: Taxi Run
C: Crusher Castle II
D: Spider Run
E: Tommy's Tenpins
F: Battle for Atlantis
G: Ultris
H: Women of the World Trivia
I: Kosynka
J: MinerVGA

The software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
