White Wolf Software: Series XIII is one of a series of CDs published by Wiz Technology on their White Wolf label.This CD contains the following shareware products:* A: Wolfenstein 3DB: Taxi RunC: Crusher Castle IID: Spider RunE: Tommy's TenpinsF: Battle for AtlantisG: UltrisH: Women of the World TriviaI: KosynkaJ: MinerVGAThe software is installed singly, there is no 'Install All' option. Once installed the user is left at the DOS prompt in the newly created directory.
