The Mystery Collection in Infocom's series combines six interactive text-based adventure games on CD-ROM. The featured titles are:DeadlineLurking HorrorMoonmistSherlock: The Riddle of the Crown JewelsSuspectThe WitnessBonus games:PlanetfallZork ZeroThe games are compatible with Microsoft Windows or can be run directly in DOS. They are also compatible with Apple Macintosh computers.
