The Mystery Collection in Infocom's series combines six interactive text-based adventure games on CD-ROM. The featured titles are:

Deadline
Lurking Horror
Moonmist
Sherlock: The Riddle of the Crown Jewels
Suspect
The Witness

Bonus games:

Planetfall
Zork Zero

The games are compatible with Microsoft Windows or can be run directly in DOS. They are also compatible with Apple Macintosh computers.
