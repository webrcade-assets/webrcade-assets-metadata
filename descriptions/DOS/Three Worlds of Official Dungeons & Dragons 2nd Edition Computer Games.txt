Three Worlds of AD&D is a single CD compilation of three games:Ravenloft: Strahd's PossessionAl-Qadim: The Genie's CurseDark Sun: Shattered LandsAlthough related by an adherence to the 2nd edition AD&D rules, the three worlds are not related. Each game is derived from a unique Dungeons & Dragons campaign setting. The compilation comes with a full 3-in-1 manual for the games (250+ pages).The CD also has a demo version of Thunderscape.
