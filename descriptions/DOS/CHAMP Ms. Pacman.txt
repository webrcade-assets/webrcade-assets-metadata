CHAMP Ms. Pacman is a one or two player, shareware implementation of the 1981 classic.The object of CHAMP Ms. Pacman is to clear as many mazes as possible while accumulating the highest score possible by eating all the pills in each maze. The player must avoid the four ghosts, contact with them costs a life, but in the four corners of each maze are power pills which,when eaten, turn the ghosts blue and vulnerable for a short while.Sometimes, bonus fruit will enter the maze, Ms. Pacman can score bonus points by eating this before the fruit leaves the maze.The game can be controlled by keyboard or mouse and it has a high score table and optional sound.
