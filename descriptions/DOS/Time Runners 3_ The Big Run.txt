Time Runners 3: The Big Run is a game in the Time Runners series of games about an alien race called the Skunks who are trying to conquer the universe, and a man named Max who's trying to stop them. The gameplay is primarily platforming action, with some point-and-click adventure elements.
At the end of the second episode, Max had just succeeded in stealing the Time Stone, but was surrounded by Skunks and captured again. In the third episode, he needs to once again escape and get back to his motorcycle/time machine.
