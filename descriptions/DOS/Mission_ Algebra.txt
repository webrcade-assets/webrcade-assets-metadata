Mission: Algebra is a 1-player computer game designed to teach linear equations.
Your crippled sister ship is stranded, and you must find her. Determine the flight path of your sister ship by simplifying and solving linear equations.
The player must simplify linear equations and solve for y. The player can either choose to draw pictures, or have the computer choose a picture to draw by plotting these linear equations. The player receives a score for their success solving the equations. The player can choose easy or hard equations.
