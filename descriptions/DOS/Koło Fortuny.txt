Ko≈Ço Fortuny (also known as Zgadula) is a Polish video game based on the TV game show of the same name (similar to the English Wheel of Fortune). Players take turns guessing letters to form a word or a saying. It can be played with up to three players. They have the option of playing with or without computer players giving contestants the ability to go head-to-head in a game against a friend. The game can be controlled by keyboard or mouse.
