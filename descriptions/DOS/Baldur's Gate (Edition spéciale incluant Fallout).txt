Baldur's Gate: Version francaise Intégrale (Edition spéciale incluant Fallout) is a compilation of two of Interplay's role-playing games. It contains:

Baldur's Gate (five discs)
Baldur's Gate manual
Baldur's Gate quick reference card
Fallout (one disc)
Fallout manual
Infonie dial-up internet trial (one disc)
Infonie internet guide
Infonie sign-up offers & info brochure
