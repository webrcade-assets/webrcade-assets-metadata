Zone 66 is a top down shareware shooter published by Epic MegaGames. There are eight episodes in the full game: * Episode 1 - Foreign ShoresEpisode 2 - Ice WindEpisode 3 - Desert HeatEpisode 4 - War PlainsEpisode 5 - Highway FuryEpisode 6 - Plantation CrashEpisode 7 - HellEpisode 8 - Final FrontierEpisode one was released as shareware with episode three being made available on registration.The game was also published by B&N who released episodes in pairs under their 'Moon Doggie' label. The big selling point about B&N games is they were run or installed directly from the floppy disc by just typing 'GO'. This game consists of episodes three and four.
