Zone 66 is a top down shareware shooter published by Epic MegaGames. 
There are eight episodes in the full game: * Episode 1 - Foreign Shores

Episode 2 - Ice Wind
Episode 3 - Desert Heat
Episode 4 - War Plains
Episode 5 - Highway Fury
Episode 6 - Plantation Crash
Episode 7 - Hell
Episode 8 - Final Frontier

Episode one was released as shareware with episode three being made available on registration.
The game was also published by B&N who released episodes in pairs under their 'Moon Doggie' label. The big selling point about B&N games is they were run or installed directly from the floppy disc by just typing 'GO'. This game consists of episodes three and four.
