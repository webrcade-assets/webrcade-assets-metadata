Operation Sledgehammer is the first add-on for the Megafortress flight simulator. Mission Disk One: Middle East features 25 new missions in Iran and Libya, new ground targets (chemical and nuclear weapons research facilities, terrorist training camps, oil storage depots), new enemy aircraft (MiG-25, Mirage F-1), ships and SAMs (SA-10 "Grumble", SA-12A "Gladiator", RIM-66 SM-1MR, MIM-23 Hawk). New weapons are also available for the player (CBU-95 FAE, Penguin MK3).
