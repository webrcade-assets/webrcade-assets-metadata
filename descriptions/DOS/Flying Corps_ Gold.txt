Flying Corps Gold is a re-release of the 1996 WWI air combat simulator Flying Corps. Adding to the realism and six airplanes of the first, the roster now also includes the Fokker D-VII, as well as a new campaign, a mission editor, Direct3D and 3DFX support, a multiplayer mode and support for the CH Force FX joysticks.Owners of the original game could partially update their version via a patch.
