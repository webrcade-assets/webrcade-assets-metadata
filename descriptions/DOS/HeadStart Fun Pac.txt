A classic game package that includes five games:

Airplane Factory : build planes to be imported into Chuck Yeager's Advanced Flight Trainer.

Chuck Yeager's Advanced Flight Trainer : learn how to do acrobatic flight maneuvers in fourteen different advanced airplanes.

Paperboy : avoid obstacles while making sure all the papers are delivered in this simulation of the tough life of a paperboy.

Superstar Indoor Sports : play ping-pong, bowling, air hockey and darts in this indoor sports simulation.

Test Drive : get behind the wheel of five different sports cars as you weave through traffic going as fast as you can while avoiding cops.
