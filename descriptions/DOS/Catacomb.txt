Catacomb is a 1 - 4 player roleplaying game for MS-DOS.A medieval role playing game which allows 1 - 4 characters to go on adventure in subterranean caves and dungeons. Characters can be a Dwarf, Elf, or Human, male or female, or be a warrior, wizard, or thief. Each character can train specific skills or spells in town, where weapons and armor can be purchased.Contains a chessboard tactical display for combat. Six function menus support scores of player commands. Items can be stored in the town repository.
