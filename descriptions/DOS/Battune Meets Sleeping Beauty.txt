Battune Meets Sleeping Beauty is another text adventure in the Battune series.This adventure is based on the Sleeping Beauty fairy story. In it a wicked witch cast a spell over the castle putting the princess to sleep before moving in herself. The player starts the game by the north castle wall and must find a way into the castle where they collect treasure, kill monsters, kill the witch and awaken the princess.This is a text adventure, there are no graphics, and all commands must be entered in upper case.
