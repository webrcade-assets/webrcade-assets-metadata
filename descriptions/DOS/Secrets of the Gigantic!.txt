Gigantic is a single player, keyboard controlled text adventure.This game is loosely based on the sinking of RMS Titanic, an unsinkable ocean liner with insufficient lifeboats hits an iceberg and sinks leaving just seven hundred survivors from its two thousand two hundred passengers and crew.The game starts when the ship has been re-floated and brought into dock. After a long time on the sea bed it is in an awful state and it is the player's task to investigate it, retrieve treasures and avoid being killed.The complete game has thirty-six locations/rooms and a maximum score of one hundred and forty.
