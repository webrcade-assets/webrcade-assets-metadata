Fraction Factory is an educational math game for multiple systems.Contains 5 educational mini-gamesFractions and Sets - A number of objects in a set are circled, and the player must write the fractionEquivalent Fractions - The player must provide the equivalent fractionFractions of a Number - The player solves the whole number is a fraction of a larger wholeAdding Fractions - The player adds fractions with different denominators with the help of a number lineSubtracting Fractions - The same, but with subtractionIn each section, the player's goal is to get 10 answers correct, and they are rewarded with an animated sequence for their progress.
