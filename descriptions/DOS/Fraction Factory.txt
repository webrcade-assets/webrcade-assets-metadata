Fraction Factory is an educational math game for multiple systems.
Contains 5 educational mini-games

Fractions and Sets - A number of objects in a set are circled, and the player must write the fraction
Equivalent Fractions - The player must provide the equivalent fraction
Fractions of a Number - The player solves the whole number is a fraction of a larger whole
Adding Fractions - The player adds fractions with different denominators with the help of a number line
Subtracting Fractions - The same, but with subtraction

In each section, the player's goal is to get 10 answers correct, and they are rewarded with an animated sequence for their progress.
