Speedway Manager '96 is a management game of the Polish speedway league. The main task of the player is the appropriate financial management of the first league club and win the championship. Income comes only from tickets. The expenditure includes purchase and repair of motorcycles, training, salaries of players, travelling to matches. The player does not have access to any financial institution. The teams and the way they are set are in line with the realities and rules of the Polish league. During the game it is possible to turn on the animations in the form of recordings of real events.
