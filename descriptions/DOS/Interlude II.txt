Interlude II is a text-based computerized guide to helping couples spice up their love lives.Designed for 1 or 2 heterosexual players, each participant inputs their sexual preferences, including current level of arousal, their comfort with different erogenous zones, ideal sexual partner, and their ideals towards the night's lovemaking. Each encounter is referred to as an interlude, and the program contains 160 possible interludes that are chosen from the participants' current moods and desires.
