An expansion and strategy guide disc for the massive 'Gihren's Ambition' tactical game. It functions as a database and tactical aid, providing players with deep lore, unit statistics, and 'what-if' scenario guides for the Universal Century timeline of Gundam. It also includes exclusive save data and bonus missions that can be transferred to the main game. This disc is a treasure trove for Gundam fans, featuring high-quality mechanical illustrations and cinematic clips from the history of the franchise.
