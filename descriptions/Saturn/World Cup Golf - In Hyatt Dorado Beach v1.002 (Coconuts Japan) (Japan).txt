A specialized Japanese release of World Cup Golf, featuring the Hyatt Dorado Beach course in Puerto Rico. The game uses a high-resolution 3D engine and photographic backgrounds to provide a realistic and immersive golfing experience. It features a deep swing mechanic and various tournament formats including skins and match play. This version is noted for its atmospheric sound design and its focus on providing a professional-grade simulation for golf enthusiasts in Japan. It captures the beauty and technical challenge of the real-world course with impressive accuracy.
