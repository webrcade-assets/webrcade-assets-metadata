The Japanese release of the global puzzle phenomenon. Published by Sunsoft, this version features high-quality localized text and voice-overs to bring the story of the Linking Books to a Japanese audience. The game utilizes the Saturn's high-resolution capabilities to present the pre-rendered environments with impressive clarity. It remains a slow-paced, cerebral experience that focuses on environmental storytelling and immersive sound design, providing a unique challenge for adventure fans in Japan.
