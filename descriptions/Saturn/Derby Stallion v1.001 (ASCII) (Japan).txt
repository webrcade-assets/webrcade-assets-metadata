Derby Stallion is the most popular horse-breeding and racing sim in Japan. Players are responsible for breeding, training, and racing their own stable of thoroughbreds. The game is famous for its depth, requiring players to understand pedigrees and track conditions to succeed. The Saturn version features improved graphics for the racing sequences and a cleaner interface, allowing players to manage their ranch with more precision. It was a massive hit in Japan, leading to many sequels and spin-offs.
