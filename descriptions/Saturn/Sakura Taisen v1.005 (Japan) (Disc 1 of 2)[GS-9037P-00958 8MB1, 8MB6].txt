The game that started a revolution in the Japanese gaming market. Sakura Taisen is a 'Steampunk Romance Adventure' where players lead a team of young women who are stage actresses by day and mech pilots by night. Disc 1 establishes the world of the Imperial Theater and the threat of the Hive of Darkness. The game uses a unique system where your success in social interactions directly affects the stats of your units in the turn-based tactical battles. It is a colorful, high-energy title that defined the 'Saturn style' of JRPGs.
