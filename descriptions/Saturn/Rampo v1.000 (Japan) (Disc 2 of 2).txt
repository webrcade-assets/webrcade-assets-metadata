The conclusion of the Rampo investigation. As the mystery of the 'Watcher in the Attic' unfolds, players must solve complex environmental puzzles and make critical dialogue choices. The game's second disc features the narrative's most dramatic turns and provides multiple resolutions based on the player's findings. It is a unique example of the 'Sound Novel' genre expanding into a fully cinematic 3D space, offering a unique blend of historical fiction and psychological thriller.
