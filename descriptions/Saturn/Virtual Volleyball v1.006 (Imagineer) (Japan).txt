A fully 3D volleyball simulation featuring a variety of international teams and diverse court types. Virtual Volleyball emphasizes timing and positioning, requiring players to master the art of the spike, set, and block. The game features multiple camera angles and a comprehensive tournament mode. This Japanese release is prized for its fluid animations and its realistic player physics. It provides a technical and challenging representation of the sport, making it a solid choice for sports fans looking for something beyond traditional soccer and baseball titles.
