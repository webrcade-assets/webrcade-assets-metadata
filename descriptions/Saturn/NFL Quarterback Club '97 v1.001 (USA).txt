Published by Acclaim, NFL Quarterback Club '97 focuses on the precision and strategy of the quarterback position. It features impressive high-resolution graphics and a unique 'simulation' feel. The game includes a variety of modes, including a historical 'Simulation' mode where players can recreate famous moments from NFL history. The Saturn version is noted for its clean visuals and the large variety of play-calling options, catering to fans who wanted a more technical and visually sharp football experience.
