Set in a gritty, futuristic city, Crimewave is an isometric vehicular combat game where players act as bounty hunters for the city's corrupt police force. Tasked with hunting down criminals in armored cars, players navigate a 3D city filled with traffic and destructible environments. The game features a wide array of weaponry, from machine guns to homing missiles, and emphasizes collecting bounties to upgrade your vehicle. Its unique art style and fast-paced arcade action make it a standout 'cult' title for the Saturn.
