The final disc of the second Sakura Taisen epic. This disc contains the dramatic finale of the war against the Antikythera and the final romantic resolutions for Ogami and the heroines. Depending on the player's choices across all three discs, the story culminates in a personalized ending with the girl the player has built the strongest bond with. It is a massive, multi-faceted experience that solidified Sakura Taisen as a premier Sega franchise, blending romance, comedy, and tactical strategy into a unique cultural phenomenon.
