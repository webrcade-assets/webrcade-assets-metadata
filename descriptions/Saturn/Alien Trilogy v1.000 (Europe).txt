A comprehensive FPS experience that adapts the narrative of the Alien cinematic trilogy. Players navigate through 30 levels of terrifying corridors, utilizing iconic weaponry like the Pulse Rifle, Smart Gun, and Flamethrower. The game utilizes a mission-based structure where players must achieve specific objectives—such as clearing out nests or activating self-destruct sequences—before they can progress. The inclusion of the Motion Tracker adds a layer of strategic tension to every encounter.
