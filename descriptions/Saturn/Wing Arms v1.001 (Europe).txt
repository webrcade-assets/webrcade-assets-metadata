Wing Arms is an alternate-history flight shooter set after World War II, where a mysterious organization known as 'The Organization' threatens world peace. Players pilot legendary planes like the Spitfire and the Zero, but with futuristic upgrades and weapons. The game features fully 3D environments and massive bosses, including a giant flying fortress. The European version is celebrated for its smooth 30FPS performance and its high-energy arcade gameplay, offering a fun and visually impressive take on aerial combat that blends historical planes with sci-fi technology.
