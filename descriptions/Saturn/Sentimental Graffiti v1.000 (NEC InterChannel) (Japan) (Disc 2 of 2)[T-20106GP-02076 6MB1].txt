The second disc of the ambitious 'Sentimental Graffiti' project. The game follows a protagonist who travels across 12 different cities in Japan to reconnect with girls from his past. Disc 2 contains the latter half of these regional stories, featuring high-quality 2D art and thousands of lines of voiced dialogue. The game is unique for its travel-simulation elements, requiring players to manage train schedules and budgets while navigating a map of Japan. It is a nostalgic and sentimental journey that captured the hearts of Japanese fans.
