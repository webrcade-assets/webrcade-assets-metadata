Released as Lunacy in North America, Torico is a surreal and artistic cinematic adventure. Players follow Fred, an amnesiac traveler imprisoned in a misty town, as he attempts to regain his memories and find the legendary City of Moons. Disc 1 focuses on the exploration of the town and the interactions with its eccentric, often frightening inhabitants. The game features a unique 'pre-rendered FMV' aesthetic that creates a dream-like atmosphere, emphasizing story-driven dialogue and item-based puzzles.
