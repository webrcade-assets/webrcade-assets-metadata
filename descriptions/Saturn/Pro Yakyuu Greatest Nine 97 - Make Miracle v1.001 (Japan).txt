An updated version of the '97 baseball installment, 'Make Miracle' reflects the mid-season updates and rosters of the Nippon Professional Baseball league. The game improved upon the original '97 release with smoother animations and a more refined pitching interface. It captures the unique atmosphere of Japanese baseball stadiums, complete with team-specific cheers and high-energy play-by-play. It remains a definitive sports title for the system in Japan, showcasing Sega's dominance in the genre.
