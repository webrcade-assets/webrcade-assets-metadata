The second chapter of the Blue Destiny saga. Following the events of the first game, the protagonist joins the pursuit of the experimental EXAM system. This volume introduces more complex missions and several new enemy Mobile Suits to battle. The gameplay continues to emphasize the cockpit-view immersion, requiring players to manage radar and weapon heat. The Saturn version is noted for its cinematic presentation and for providing a deeper look at the 'Blue Destiny' units that became fan favorites.
