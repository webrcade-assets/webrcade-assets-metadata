An 'updated' version of the classic dating sim Doukyuusei. The 'if' version adds new scenarios and character interactions not found in the original PC release. Players navigate a town, managing their time to meet various girls and build relationships through dialogue choices. It is a highly influential title in the genre, known for its large cast of characters and the complexity of its schedule-based gameplay. The Saturn version features improved artwork and full voice acting for the female cast.
