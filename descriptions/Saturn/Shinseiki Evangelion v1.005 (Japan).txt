An interactive retelling of the Evangelion story, blending visual novel exploration with tactical combat sequences. Players take the role of Shinji Ikari, interacting with the NERV staff between Angel attacks. The combat segments involve choosing tactical commands to pilot Eva Unit 01 against the Angels in cinematic, animated battles. The game features original voice acting and high-quality graphics that closely mimic the show's art style. It remains a key piece of Evangelion media, offering fans the chance to make choices that differ from the original anime's plot.
