The Japanese release of the 1998 installment, known for its high-resolution presentation and realistic soccer simulation. The game includes a variety of modes including exhibition, world cup, and league play. It features a unique 'behind-the-player' camera during goal kicks and corners to enhance the cinematic feel. This version is highly regarded for its refined AI and the inclusion of real player names and likenesses, making it the definitive soccer title for Japanese Saturn owners during the 1998 World Cup season.
