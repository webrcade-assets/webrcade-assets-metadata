The final North American release of Sega's premier baseball franchise on the Saturn. World Series Baseball 98 features the most advanced 3D graphics of the series, including detailed player likenesses and authentic MLB stadiums. The game includes all the teams and players from the 1997 season and offers a deep management mode. It is praised for its realistic 'batting' perspective and its refined AI, offering a comprehensive and polished baseball simulation that pushed the hardware to its limits before the transition to the Dreamcast.
