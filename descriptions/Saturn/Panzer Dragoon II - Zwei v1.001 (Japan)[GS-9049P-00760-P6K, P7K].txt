The Japanese release of the highly acclaimed prequel to the original Panzer Dragoon. Zwei introduces the 'Berserk' attack and a branching level system where the player's performance dictates the dragon's evolutionary path. It features some of the most impressive technical feats on the Saturn, including water reflections and complex particle effects. This version remains a benchmark for 32-bit 3D shooters, offering an epic narrative following Lundi and his companion dragon, Lagi.
