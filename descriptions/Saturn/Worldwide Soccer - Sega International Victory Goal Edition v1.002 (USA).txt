The North American version of the first Worldwide Soccer, featuring an expanded roster of international teams. The game uses 'Virtual Stadium' technology to provide multiple 3D camera angles while maintaining high-quality 2D player sprites. It captures the global fervor of soccer with realistic stadium sound effects and high-energy commentary. This edition is noted for its accessible controls and its role in establishing the Saturn as a premier platform for soccer fans, providing a high-fidelity alternative to the more pixelated sports games of the 16-bit era.
