A mature and unsettling visual novel that explores themes of identity, trauma, and psychological mystery. Players follow a young doctor who becomes involved with a mysterious girl named Maria. Disc 1 sets up the atmospheric narrative, using a mix of traditional 2D art and highly stylized FMV sequences. The game is known for its heavy, somber tone and its focus on medical and psychological realism, offering a much darker experience than the typical dating sims found on the platform.
