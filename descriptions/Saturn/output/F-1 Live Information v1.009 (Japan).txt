The Japanese release of Sega's F1 simulation, notable for its 'Live Information' gimmick. The game features a unique windowed UI that displays real-time data and a 'broadcaster' style presentation. It includes real-world tracks like Suzuka and features a diverse roster of licensed cars. The Saturn version is designed to be played with the Arcade Racer steering wheel, offering high-precision control that was essential for navigating the technical turns of the Formula 1 circuit.
