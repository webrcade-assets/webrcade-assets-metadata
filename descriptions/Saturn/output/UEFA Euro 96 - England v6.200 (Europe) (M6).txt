The official game of the Euro 96 tournament held in England. This title uses an improved version of the Victory Goal engine, featuring all the participating national teams and official squads. It captures the specific atmosphere of the 1996 tournament, complete with realistic stadiums and the 'Three Lions' fever of the era. The gameplay offers a balance of tactical management and arcade soccer action. This multi-language release was a key sports title for European fans, providing the most accurate digital representation of the tournament available.
