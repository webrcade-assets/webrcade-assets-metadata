Shunsai is a unique board-game style title that focuses on the theme of traditional Japanese seasonal culture and cuisine. Players navigate a digital game board, participating in mini-games and trivia related to the various seasons of Japan. The game features beautiful, high-resolution photographs and traditional music, creating a relaxing and culturally rich experience. It is a rare example of a 'lifestyle' board game on the system, designed for a more mature audience interested in the aesthetics and traditions of Japanese life.
