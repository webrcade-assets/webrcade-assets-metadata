The European release of the Iron Man and X-O Manowar crossover. This version features localized text for three languages and maintains the same high-action gameplay as the US release. Players must navigate platforming stages while managing their character's energy levels for flight and special attacks. The game is a mix of traditional run-and-gun combat and environmental exploration, providing a challenging experience for fans of the source material. It captures the '90s aesthetic of the comics with its dark, metallic art style.
