Developed by ELF, this is a mature mystery adventure set in the Nonomura Hospital. Players take the role of an investigator looking into a series of strange occurrences and deaths. The game features high-quality 2D character art and a branching narrative that requires careful investigation and dialogue choices to uncover the truth. It is known for its adult themes and its dark, suspenseful atmosphere. This Saturn version includes full voice acting for the main cast, significantly enhancing the cinematic feel of the investigation.
