The 'Final Chapter' of the Super Robot Wars F saga. This is a massive tactical RPG that brings together robots and characters from dozens of iconic anime franchises, including Gundam, Evangelion, and Mazinger Z. Players manage their units on a grid-based map and engage in cinematic combat sequences that feature full voice acting and faithful recreations of famous attacks. This conclusion contains the most powerful mechs and the most difficult missions in the series, offering an epic finale for fans of mecha anime and strategic gameplay.
