A prequel to the Genesis classic Beyond Oasis (The Story of Thor), Legend of Oasis is a top-down action-adventure game featuring stunning 2D sprite work and large, colorful environments. Players control Leon, who must master the powers of six elemental spirits to stop an ancient evil. The game is a mix of Zelda-style exploration and beat 'em up combat, featuring complex puzzles and massive screen-filling bosses. The US version is highly sought after for its vibrant art style and smooth, responsive gameplay.
