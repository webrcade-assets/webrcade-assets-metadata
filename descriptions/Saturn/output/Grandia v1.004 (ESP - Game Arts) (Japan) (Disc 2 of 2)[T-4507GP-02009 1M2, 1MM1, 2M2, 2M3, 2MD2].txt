The second half of Game Arts' masterpiece JRPG. This disc follows Justin and his companions as they travel beyond the 'End of the World' to uncover the mysteries of the Angelou civilization. Grandia is famous for its 'IP' battle system, which combines turn-based strategy with real-time movement and timing. This final disc contains the most epic story moments and the resolution of the conflict against General Baal. It is widely regarded as one of the best RPGs ever made, featuring a moving soundtrack and a genuine sense of adventure that defined the era.
