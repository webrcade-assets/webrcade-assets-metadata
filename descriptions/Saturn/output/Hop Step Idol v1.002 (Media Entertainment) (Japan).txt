A unique idol-management simulation where players take on the role of a talent manager for an aspiring pop star. The goal is to guide her through various training sessions, interviews, and performances to reach the top of the charts. The game features high-quality 2D character art and voiced dialogue. It combines elements of schedule management with rhythm-style mini-games, providing a glimpse into the Japanese idol industry of the mid-90s. The Saturn version is noted for its charming presentation and multiple endings based on the idol's success.
