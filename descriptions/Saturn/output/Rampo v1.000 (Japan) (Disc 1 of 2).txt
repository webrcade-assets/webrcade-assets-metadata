Based on the films of the famous Japanese mystery writer Edogawa Rampo, this is a cinematic investigation game. Players explore a world that blends 1920s aesthetics with surreal mystery elements. Disc 1 sets the stage for a dark detective story, utilizing high-quality FMV and pre-rendered backgrounds. The game is known for its moody atmosphere and its tribute to the 'Ero-Guro' style of the source material, providing a slow-burning and mature adventure for fans of Japanese literature.
