Virtual Kyoutei 2 is a deep and specialized simulation of Japanese powerboat racing (Kyoutei). Players must master the physics of water and the unique handling of the high-speed boats to win races across several realistic Japanese circuits. The game features detailed boat customization and a comprehensive betting system that reflects the gambling culture surrounding the sport in Japan. The Saturn version is noted for its impressive water effects and its use of the console's CD-audio to provide authentic engine sounds and high-energy commentary.
