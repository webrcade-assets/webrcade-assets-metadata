The original Japanese release of Sega's dream-based action title. This version established the series' unique 'A-Life' system, where the player's interactions with 'Nightopians' can change the music and environment of the stages in real-time. The Japanese release is celebrated for its beautiful manual art and the high level of polish in its pre-rendered cutscenes. It remains a defining title for the Saturn, showcasing the hardware's unique ability to blend 2D and 3D graphics into a seamless, artistic whole.
