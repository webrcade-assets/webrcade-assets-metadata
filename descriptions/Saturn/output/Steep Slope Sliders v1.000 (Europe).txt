A high-energy snowboarding game from Victor Interactive, Steep Slope Sliders focuses on style and trick execution. Players can choose from a variety of riders and compete on several snowy mountain courses filled with jumps and rails. The game features a unique control scheme that emphasizes fluid movement and complex aerial maneuvers. The European version includes a pulse-pounding electronic soundtrack and is noted for its clean 3D graphics and fast-paced gameplay. It is one of the most polished extreme sports titles on the system, offering a great balance of racing and freestyle modes.
