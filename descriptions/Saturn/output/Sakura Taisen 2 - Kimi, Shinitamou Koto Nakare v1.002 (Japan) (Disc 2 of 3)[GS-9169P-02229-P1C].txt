The middle chapter of Sakura Taisen 2 focuses on the deepening bonds of the flower division and the escalating tactical battles against the 'Kouma.' Combat takes place in isometric 3D arenas where players command Koubu mechs. This disc features extensive character development and the series' signature blend of slice-of-life interaction and high-stakes military drama. The Saturn version is celebrated for its seamless integration of 2D character art and 3D battle environments, setting a high bar for the genre in Japan.
