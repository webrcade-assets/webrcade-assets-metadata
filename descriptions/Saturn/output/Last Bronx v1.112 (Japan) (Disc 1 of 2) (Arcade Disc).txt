The first disc of the Japanese 'Special' release of Last Bronx. This disc contains the core arcade game, featuring pixel-perfect 3D combat and the full roster of weapon-wielding street gang leaders. It utilizes the Saturn's VDP2 processor to create beautiful high-resolution backgrounds. The gameplay is fast and emphasizes 'cancelling' moves to create long, devastating combos. This disc is focused on providing the purest arcade experience possible on home hardware.
