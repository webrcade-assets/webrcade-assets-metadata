A cyberpunk JRPG set in the futuristic Amami City. As a member of the hacker group 'Spookies,' players must navigate a digital world and the real world to stop a corporate conspiracy involving demons. The game features the classic Shin Megami Tensei demon recruitment and fusion systems, but adds a high-tech twist with 'COMP' upgrades. Disc 1 introduces the neon-lit world and the first-person dungeon crawling mechanics that the series is famous for.
