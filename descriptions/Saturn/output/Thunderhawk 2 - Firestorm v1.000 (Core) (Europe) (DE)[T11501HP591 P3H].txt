The German-localized release of the high-octane helicopter combat sequel. As an elite pilot of the Firestorm battalion, players navigate 3D battlefields to eliminate terrorist threats across several global theaters. The game emphasizes mission-based objectives and features a high frame rate that provides a great sense of speed and fluidity. This version includes localized briefings and text, ensuring that German players can follow the tactical instructions for the diverse range of search-and-destroy missions.
