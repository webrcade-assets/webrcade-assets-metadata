A landmark title for the FIFA franchise, Road to World Cup 98 introduced a fully 3D engine and the ability to play through the entire qualification process for the 1998 World Cup. It features a massive roster of national teams and refined gameplay mechanics that allowed for more precise ball control and tactical depth. This European 'M7' release includes seven languages, making it a truly international title, and features the iconic 'Song 2' by Blur as its opening theme.
