The German-localized version of the official game of the 1996 Atlanta Olympics. Olympic Soccer features 32 international teams and a fully 3D polygonal engine. The gameplay is fast and arcade-oriented, emphasizing high scores and flashy plays. This version includes localized menus and commentary, providing an accessible experience for German soccer fans. It captures the global excitement of the Olympic Games, offering a variety of tournament modes and realistic 3D stadiums with dynamic lighting and weather effects.
