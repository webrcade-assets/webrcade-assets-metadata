The game that defined the Sega Saturn's early identity. Panzer Dragoon is a cinematic 3D rail shooter set in a beautiful, desolate world inspired by the art of Jean Giraud (Moebius). Players fly on the back of a blue dragon, using a 360-degree lock-on system to repel waves of biomechanical monsters and Imperial airships. This Japanese version established the series' high standard for orchestral music and world-building, utilizing a fictional language to enhance the immersive atmosphere.
