The Saturn port of the legendary PC mech sim. Players choose between the Wolf Clan and the Jade Falcon Clan, piloting a variety of customizable BattleMechs in a galactic civil war. The game features 3D polygonal combat with a focus on heat management and tactical positioning. Despite the transition from PC, the Saturn version maintains the deep customization of the 'MechLab' and features impressive 3D landscapes. It is a serious, atmospheric simulation that offers a much more technical experience than typical console shooters.
