The game that started the massive J.League management craze in Japan. Players are tasked with taking a fictional team from the lower tiers to the top of the professional league. The gameplay focuses on the long-term growth of the club, requiring players to scout young talent and manage the team's public relations. While the graphics are simpler than its sequel, the core loop of management and watching the simulated matches was incredibly compelling, leading to a huge dedicated fanbase in its home country.
