NBA Action 98 is Sega's final licensed basketball simulation for the Saturn. It features a fully 3D engine with polygonal players and authentic NBA arenas. The game includes all the teams and rosters from the 97-98 season and offers deep management options alongside the on-court action. It is highly regarded for its smooth framerate and the inclusion of signature moves for star players. The European version provides a comprehensive simulation experience for fans of the NBA, serving as a precursor to the 2K series.
