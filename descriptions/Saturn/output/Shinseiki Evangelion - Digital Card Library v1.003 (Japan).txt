A specialized fan-disc for the Neon Genesis Evangelion franchise. This title is a collection of various digital cards, mini-games, and multimedia clips based on the hit anime series. Players can collect virtual cards featuring character art and scene stills, and play games like Eva-themed blackjack or quizzes. It serves as a digital encyclopedia and a celebration of the series' impact, utilizing the Saturn's CD-ROM capacity to house high-quality audio and video clips for enthusiasts of the show.
