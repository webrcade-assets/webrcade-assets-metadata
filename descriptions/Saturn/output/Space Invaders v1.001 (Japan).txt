A faithful reproduction of Taito's legendary arcade game, updated for the 32-bit generation. This version includes the classic original gameplay as well as various 'arranged' modes that feature updated graphics, background music, and two-player cooperative play. The Saturn version utilizes the console's high-resolution modes to deliver crisp, pixel-perfect visuals. It serves as a comprehensive digital archive of the game that launched the arcade industry, providing multiple versions of the classic for both nostalgic fans and new players.
