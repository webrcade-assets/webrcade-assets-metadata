Sega's flagship American football game for the 1997 season. It features a high-resolution 3D stadium engine and utilizes 2D sprites for the players to maintain a high frame rate. The game includes all NFL teams and over 1,500 real-world players. It offers a deep 'Season' mode with full stat tracking and the ability to manage team rosters. The US version is praised for its fast gameplay and the inclusion of authentic playbooks, providing a competitive alternative to the Madden franchise.
