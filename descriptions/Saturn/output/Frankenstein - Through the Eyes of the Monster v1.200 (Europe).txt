A cinematic first-person adventure game starring Tim Curry as Dr. Frankenstein. Players take on the role of the resurrected monster, exploring the doctor's castle to uncover the truth of their creation. The game uses a point-and-click interface set against high-quality pre-rendered backgrounds and FMV sequences. The game's atmospheric design and strong acting performances make it a standout title in the FMV genre, focusing on logic puzzles and a dark, gothic narrative.
