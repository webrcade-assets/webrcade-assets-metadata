The second disc of Edge's journey across the desolate, post-apocalyptic world of the Ancients. As players explore deeper into the Empire's territory, the scale of the environments increases, showcasing the Saturn's ability to handle large, atmospheric 3D landscapes. This disc focuses on uncovering the true nature of the dragon and the secrets held by the floating ruins. The narrative takes a more somber and philosophical tone as Edge realizes the weight of his quest.
