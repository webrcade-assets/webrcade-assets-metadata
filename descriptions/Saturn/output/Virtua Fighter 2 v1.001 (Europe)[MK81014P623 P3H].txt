An alternate European revision of Virtua Fighter 2, featuring minor software updates and refined data compression. The game remains the benchmark for 3D fighters on the system, offering ten legendary fighters and a wide variety of interactive arenas. Its legacy is built on its precision and its 'easy-to-learn, impossible-to-master' philosophy. This release includes the full arcade soundtrack and multiple camera perspectives, ensuring a high-quality home version of Sega's greatest fighting achievement.
