Sonic R is a unique 3D foot-racing game where Sonic and his friends compete across five vibrant tracks. The game features an upbeat Eurobeat-style soundtrack by Richard Jacques and T.J. Davis, which has become a cult classic. Players must navigate branching paths and find hidden secrets to unlock new characters like Metal Sonic and the Tails Doll. This European version is noted for its impressive graphical effects, including real-time lighting and weather changes, making it one of the most visually distinctive titles on the system.
