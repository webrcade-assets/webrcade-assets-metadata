A futuristic FPS where players take control of Kimberley Stride, a scientist piloting a hover-ship called the 'Machine Head.' The game is set across various stages representing different infected zones of a high-tech facility. Players must navigate 3D environments, solve environmental puzzles, and engage in fast-paced combat against mutated creatures. Its high-energy soundtrack and unconventional setting make it a standout title in the Saturn's shooter library.
