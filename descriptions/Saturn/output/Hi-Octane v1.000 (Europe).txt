Developed by Bullfrog Productions, Hi-Octane is a futuristic combat racer where players pilot heavily armed hovercraft. The game focuses on high speeds and aggressive combat, with players collecting fuel and ammo power-ups while navigating 3D tracks. It features a variety of different vehicles, from light and fast to slow and heavily armored. The European version on Saturn is noted for its sense of speed and its focus on wide-open track designs that allow for intense dogfighting while racing for the finish line.
