The ambitious follow-up to Myst, Riven expands the scope of the puzzle-adventure genre with even more complex logic challenges and stunning pre-rendered environments. Players are sent by Atrus to a decaying world to rescue his wife, Catherine. Disc 1 introduces the player to the primary island and its intricate hydraulic machinery. The game is famous for its 'seamless' transition between still images and video, creating a highly immersive and tactile world for the player to decipher.
