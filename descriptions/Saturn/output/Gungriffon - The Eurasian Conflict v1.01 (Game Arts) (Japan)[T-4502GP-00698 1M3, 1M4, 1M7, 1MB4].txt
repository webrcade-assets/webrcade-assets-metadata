The original Japanese release of Game Arts' premier mech combat title. Set during a fictional global conflict in the year 2018, the game offers a gritty, military-sim feel with high-quality cinematic briefings. This version is noted for its responsive 'tank-style' controls and its use of the Saturn's VDP processors to create atmospheric smoke and explosion effects. The 'Eurasian Conflict' subtitle highlights the game's deep political narrative, which is told through mission briefings and in-game radio chatter.
