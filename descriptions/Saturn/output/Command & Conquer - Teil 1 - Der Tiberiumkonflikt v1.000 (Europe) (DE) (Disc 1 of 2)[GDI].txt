The German-localized version of the legendary RTS that defined the genre. Disc 1 follows the Global Defense Initiative (GDI) campaign as they battle the terrorist Brotherhood of Nod for control over the mysterious resource known as Tiberium. The Saturn port is notable for its excellent use of the system's high-resolution modes for the UI and the inclusion of full-motion video briefings. This edition features localized German text and voice acting, enhancing the immersion for the regional audience.
