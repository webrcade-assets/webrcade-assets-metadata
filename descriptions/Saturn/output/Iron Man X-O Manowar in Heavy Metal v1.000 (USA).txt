A side-scrolling action game based on the Marvel and Valiant comic book characters. Players can choose between Iron Man and X-O Manowar, each with their own unique weapon systems and flying abilities. The game features large sprites and pre-rendered backgrounds, tasking players with fighting through various industrial and alien environments. While the game received mixed reviews for its difficulty and controls, it remains a notable example of a comic book crossover on the Saturn, featuring high-quality cinematic cutscenes.
