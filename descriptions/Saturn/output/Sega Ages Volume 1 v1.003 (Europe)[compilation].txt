The first volume of the Sega Ages collection in Europe brings three absolute arcade legends to the Saturn: Space Harrier, OutRun, and After Burner II. These are not mere ports, but enhanced versions that utilize the Saturn's hardware to provide 'arcade-plus' experiences, including 60FPS modes and arranged soundtracks. This compilation served as a definitive showcase of Sega's arcade heritage, offering pixel-perfect gameplay for fans of the 1980s coin-op era in a single, high-value package.
