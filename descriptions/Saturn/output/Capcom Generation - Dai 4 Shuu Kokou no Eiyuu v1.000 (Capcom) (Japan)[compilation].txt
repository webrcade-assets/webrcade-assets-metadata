The fourth installment in the series, 'The Solitary Hero,' brings together Capcom's premier run-and-gun and vertical shooter titles. This volume contains Commando, Mercs, and Gun.Smoke. Each game features remastered sound and pixel-perfect graphics. Gun.Smoke, an unconventional vertical shooter set in the Wild West, is a highlight of the package, utilizing a unique three-button firing system that allows players to shoot at various angles while navigating treacherous frontier towns.
