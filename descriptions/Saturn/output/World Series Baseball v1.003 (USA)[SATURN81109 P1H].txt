The North American launch-era baseball title that set the standard for the genre on 32-bit consoles. World Series Baseball features an innovative 'behind-the-catcher' camera angle that provides a realistic view of the pitch's movement. It includes the full MLB and MLBPA licenses, allowing players to use their favorite real-world stars. This version is noted for its fast-paced gameplay and its detailed stat-tracking system. It was a key early victory for the Saturn's sports library, offering a level of depth and realism that was unmatched by competing consoles at the time.
