The conclusion to the Soul Hackers narrative. This disc expands the game's scope, leading players into the most dangerous digital dungeons and the final confrontation with the forces behind the 'Paradigm X' virtual world. The Saturn version is the original release of this fan-favorite title, featuring impressive animated cutscenes and a deep, atmospheric soundtrack. It remains a landmark title for the system's RPG library, blending occult themes with a futuristic setting.
