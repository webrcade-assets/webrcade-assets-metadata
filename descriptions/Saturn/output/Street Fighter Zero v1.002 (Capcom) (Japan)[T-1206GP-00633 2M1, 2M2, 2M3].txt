The initial entry in the Zero series in Japan. This version established the 'three-bar' super meter system and the iconic air-blocking mechanic. The Saturn port is a technical achievement, delivering an experience that is virtually indistinguishable from the arcade original. This specific revision includes several balance tweaks to character move sets. It is a vibrant and high-energy fighter that breathed new life into the franchise, featuring a memorable soundtrack and a stylish visual overhaul that set the standard for 2D fighters for years to come.
