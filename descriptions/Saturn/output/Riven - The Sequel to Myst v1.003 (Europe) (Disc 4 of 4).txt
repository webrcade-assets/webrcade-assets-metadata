The final chapter of the Riven experience. On this disc, players must confront the villainous Gehn in his final stronghold and find a way to signal Atrus to initiate the prison book sequence. The game’s multiple endings are determined by the player’s actions on this disc, specifically regarding the 'Trap Book' and the safety of Catherine. It serves as a breathtaking conclusion to one of the most visually and intellectually demanding adventure games of the 1990s, showcasing the pinnacle of pre-rendered CG technology.
