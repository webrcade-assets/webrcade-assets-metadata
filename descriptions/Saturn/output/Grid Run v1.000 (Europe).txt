Known as Grid Runner in North America, this is a fast-paced game of high-tech 'tag' set on various 3D grid-based arenas. Players must navigate the grid to collect flags while avoiding or capturing an opponent. The game features a variety of power-ups and environmental hazards that change the flow of movement. With its bright, neon-infused aesthetic and frantic multiplayer mode, Grid Run offers a unique arcade experience that blends reflex-based action with light strategic positioning.
