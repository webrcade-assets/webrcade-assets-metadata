A landmark Japanese visual novel that utilizes a unique multi-sight system. Disc 1 focuses on Kojiroh Amagi, a down-on-his-luck private investigator who takes on a job to find a stolen painting. The game allows players to switch between the two protagonists at any time, where actions in one story can affect progress in the other. It features high-quality 2D art, a mature noir-inspired storyline, and a complex web of mystery involving corporate espionage and murder.
