The Japanese sequel to Mystaria: The Realms of Lore. Riglordsaga 2 features significantly improved 3D polygonal graphics and a more refined combat engine. Players lead a new group of heroes in a tactical war across a fantasy continent, using a deep magic system and positional strategy. The game is noted for its high difficulty and the complexity of its unit-building mechanics. It remains an important title for the Saturn's RPG library, proving the system's strength in 3D strategy.
