The European release of Virtua Fighter 2 brought the pinnacle of 3D fighting to PAL consoles. Despite the conversion to 50Hz, the game maintained its legendary smooth animation and high-fidelity character models. It features a massive move list for each character and a robust training mode to help players master the complex inputs. This version is prized for its balanced gameplay and remains a competitive favorite among Saturn fans for its incredibly deep and rewarding combat mechanics.
