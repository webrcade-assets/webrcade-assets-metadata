The landmark 3D adventure that introduced the world to Lara Croft. Tomb Raider on the Saturn was the first platform release of the game, focusing on the exploration of vast, atmospheric tombs in Peru, Greece, and Egypt. The gameplay is a mix of precision platforming, environmental puzzle-solving, and combat against wild animals and ancient guardians. The European version captures the cinematic feel of the game, utilizing a fixed-camera system and a minimalist soundtrack to heighten the sense of isolation and discovery.
