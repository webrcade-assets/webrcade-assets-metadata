The European release of the second entry in Sega's baseball series. Despite baseball's lower popularity in Europe, this title was praised for its technical polish and high-resolution presentation. It features an improved 3D engine with smoother pitcher animations and more diverse hitting options. The game includes the full MLB license and offers a variety of modes including exhibition and a full season. It serves as a solid example of the Saturn's strength in high-fidelity sports simulations, providing a deep and rewarding experience for baseball fans in the region.
