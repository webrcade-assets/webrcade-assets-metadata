The first entry in Sega's NBA Action series on the Saturn. This version uses the 'Virtual Stadium' technology, blending high-quality 2D player sprites with a fully 3D court and camera system. It features real NBA teams and stars like Shaquille O'Neal and Hakeem Olajuwon. The gameplay is a balance between arcade accessibility and simulation depth, featuring real-time commentary and multiple play-calling options. It set the standard for basketball presentation on the system during its first year.
