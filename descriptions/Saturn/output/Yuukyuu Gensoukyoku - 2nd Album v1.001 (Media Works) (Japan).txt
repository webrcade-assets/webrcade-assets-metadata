A unique hybrid of visual novel and management simulation. Players manage a guild of adventurers in a vibrant fantasy city, choosing which members to send on missions and interacting with them to build relationships. The '2nd Album' features an expanded roster of characters and new story paths. The game is known for its beautiful 2D artwork and its lighthearted, character-driven narrative. This Japanese version is prized for its high volume of voiced dialogue and its complex social-sim elements, offering a relaxing and engaging experience for fans of the genre.
