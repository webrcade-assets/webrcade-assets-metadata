The first entry in Sega's 'Greatest Nine' series for the Saturn. It translates the excitement of Japanese professional baseball into a polished 32-bit simulation. The game features a unique 'behind-the-pitcher' camera angle and a robust batting system that requires precise timing. It was highly regarded at launch for its clean graphics and authentic stadium atmospheres. It includes a full league mode and allows for deep team management, setting the groundwork for what would become Sega's dominant baseball franchise in Japan.
