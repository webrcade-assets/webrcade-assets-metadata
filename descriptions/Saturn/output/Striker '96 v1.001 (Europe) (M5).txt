Striker '96 is a fast-paced 3D soccer game that emphasizes arcade accessibility and high-scoring matches. It features over 3,000 real players and supports multiple camera angles to provide a television-style presentation. The game is known for its smooth controls and its 'easy-to-learn, hard-to-master' gameplay loop. This European multi-language release includes localized text and features a variety of national teams and tournament formats, serving as a solid, high-energy representation of the sport during the 32-bit era's peak.
