The Saturn port of the game that revolutionized 3D racing. Virtua Racing features flat-shaded polygonal graphics and a high-speed frame rate that captures the pure arcade experience. Players can choose between three tracks and various camera angles, including a cockpit view. This Japanese version by Time Warner Interactive is noted for its faithful translation of the Model 1 arcade hardware, offering a nostalgic and historically significant racing experience that emphasizes precision driving and mastering the racing line on the Big Forest and Acropolis circuits.
