The 'Worldwide Edition' of Sega's Victory Goal features an expanded roster including international national teams. It utilizes a 3D stadium engine with high-quality 2D sprites, offering a fluid and visually consistent soccer experience. The game is known for its fast-paced arcade handling and its diverse range of camera options. This Japanese release served as the bridge between the initial J.League-focused titles and the more global Worldwide Soccer series, providing a high-energy soccer simulation for the international stage.
