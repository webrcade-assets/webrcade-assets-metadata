Tunnel B1 is a high-speed hovercraft shooter set in a series of subterranean industrial tunnels. Players pilot the B1 craft through 3D environments, destroying automated defenses and navigating through tight corridors at breakneck speeds. The game is a technical showcase for the Saturn, featuring impressive lighting effects and high-fidelity textures. This European release includes multi-language support and focuses on fast-paced, arcade-style reflexes, tasking players with finding and destroying a secret ultimate weapon buried deep underground.
