Known as WipEout XL in North America, this sequel is a massive improvement over the original in every way. WipEout 2097 features a more refined handling model, more aggressive weapon systems, and a legendary electronic soundtrack featuring The Chemical Brothers and Prodigy. The game is a technical masterpiece on the Saturn, featuring some of the system's best lighting and transparency effects. The European version is noted for its incredible sense of speed and its high difficulty, representing the absolute pinnacle of futuristic racing on 32-bit hardware.
