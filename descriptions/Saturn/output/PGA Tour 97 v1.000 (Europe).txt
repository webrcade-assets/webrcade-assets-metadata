PGA Tour 97 provides a realistic and atmospheric golfing experience featuring licensed pro golfers and authentic courses like Spyglass Hill. The game uses a sophisticated 3D engine that calculates ball physics based on wind, terrain, and the player's swing timing. It features real-time commentary and multiple gameplay modes including stroke play and skins games. The European release is praised for its clean interface and the inclusion of high-quality FMV flyovers for each hole, providing a professional and relaxing golf simulation.
