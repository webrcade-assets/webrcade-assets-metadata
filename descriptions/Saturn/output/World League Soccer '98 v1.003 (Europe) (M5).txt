World League Soccer '98 is a fast-paced 3D soccer game that was a late-cycle hit for the Saturn in Europe. It features a refined engine with smooth animations and a very responsive control scheme. The game includes dozens of international and club teams and offers deep tactical options for formations and player substitutions. This multi-language release is highly regarded for its balanced gameplay and its high-energy commentary. It remains a favorite for European soccer fans, providing a polished and technically sound representation of the sport during the 1998 season.
