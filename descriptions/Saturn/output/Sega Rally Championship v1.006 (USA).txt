The North American release of Sega Rally Championship. This version includes a hidden fourth track, 'Lakeside,' and the ability to unlock a secret car. The game is famous for its 'ghost car' feature, which allows players to race against their own best times to shave off every possible millisecond. The US release was a critical hit, often cited as one of the best racing games of the 32-bit generation due to its perfect difficulty curve and its addictive, time-attack focused gameplay loop.
