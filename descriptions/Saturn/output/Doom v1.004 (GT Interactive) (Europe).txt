The Saturn port of the legendary FPS, Doom. This version includes levels from both Ultimate Doom and Doom II, offering a massive amount of content. While it runs at a lower frame rate than some other versions, it is famous for its unique atmospheric lighting and a completely original ambient soundtrack that differs from the PC's MIDI music. It supports the Saturn mouse and the Link Cable for two-player deathmatch, making it a unique way to experience the grandfather of FPS games.
