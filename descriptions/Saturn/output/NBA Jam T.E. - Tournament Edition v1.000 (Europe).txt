The definitive 2D version of the arcade classic on the Saturn. Tournament Edition features updated rosters, new hidden characters, and the 'Injury' system where players' stats decrease if they are knocked down too often. The Saturn version is a high-fidelity port, featuring large, clear sprites and the full range of the announcer's famous catchphrases. With its iconic two-on-two gameplay and secret codes, it remains one of the most beloved sports titles on the platform for competitive play.
