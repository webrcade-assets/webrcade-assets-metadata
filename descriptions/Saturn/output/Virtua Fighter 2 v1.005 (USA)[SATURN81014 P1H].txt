The North American release of the 3D fighting legend. This port was a major victory for Sega of America, proving the Saturn could handle the high-end arcade graphics of the time. It includes various modes such as Arcade, Versus, and Team Battle. The US version is highly regarded for its crisp visuals and responsive controls, particularly when used with the Saturn's six-button controller. It remains an essential part of the console's library and a defining moment in the history of the fighting game genre.
