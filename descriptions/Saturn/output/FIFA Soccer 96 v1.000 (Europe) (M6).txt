The first entry of the FIFA series on the Saturn, FIFA 96 used 'Virtual Stadium' technology to provide multiple camera angles in a 3D environment while still utilizing high-quality 2D sprites for the players. It featured over 3,000 real players and real-time commentary by John Motson. The game set a high standard for sports presentation on 32-bit hardware and was one of the first titles to offer a comprehensive management mode alongside the on-pitch action.
