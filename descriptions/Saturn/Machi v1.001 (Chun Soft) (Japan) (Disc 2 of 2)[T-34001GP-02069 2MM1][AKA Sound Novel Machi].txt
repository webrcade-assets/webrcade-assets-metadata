The conclusion of the massive Machi narrative. As the stories of the eight protagonists converge, the difficulty of the 'Zapping' puzzles increases, requiring the player to have a deep understanding of the timeline and character motivations. The game is famous for its witty writing, emotional depth, and its innovative use of hypertext-style 'tips' that provide context for Japanese culture and slang. It is a masterpiece of experimental storytelling that has influenced countless Japanese adventure games since.
