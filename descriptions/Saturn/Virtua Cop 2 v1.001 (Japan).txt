The Japanese release of Virtua Cop 2. This version brought the high-speed 3D arcade action of the Model 2 hardware into the home with incredible accuracy. It features several Saturn-exclusive modes, including a training range and a detailed ranking system. The game is celebrated for its cinematic presentation and its use of the Saturn's VDP2 processor to create smooth, high-resolution backgrounds. It is a quintessential arcade experience on the console, particularly when played with the Twin Stick or Virtua Gun controllers.
