A cinematic investigation game set in the Wan Chai district of Hong Kong. Players take on the role of a detective investigating a series of crimes involving the local triads. Disc 1 introduces the atmospheric urban setting and the main cast of characters, using high-quality photography and FMV sequences to tell its story. The game focuses on gathering evidence and making critical dialogue choices to navigate the complex social and criminal hierarchy of the district. It is an early showcase of the Saturn's ability to handle high-resolution photographic backgrounds.
