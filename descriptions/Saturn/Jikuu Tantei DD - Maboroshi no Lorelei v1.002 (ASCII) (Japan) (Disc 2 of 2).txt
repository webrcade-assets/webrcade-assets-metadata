The second half of the Time Detective DD mystery. This disc contains the more difficult investigation segments as the detective moves closer to identifying the source of the 'Maboroshi no Lorelei' phenomenon. The game is highly regarded for its creative writing and atmospheric sci-fi tone. As a late-cycle Saturn title, it features some of the best-produced FMV and voice acting on the system, providing a satisfying conclusion to its multi-generational detective story.
