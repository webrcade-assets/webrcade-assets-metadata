A unique crossover that brings the Virtua Fighter characters into the world of All Japan Pro Wrestling. Players can pit legends like Mitsuharu Misawa against Akira Yuki in a 3D polygonal wrestling engine. The game features the deep 'Giant Gram' grappling system and a high-resolution presentation. This Japanese release is highly regarded for its fluid animation and its authentic wrestling atmosphere, complete with real-world moves and licensed arenas. It stands as a fascinating and technically impressive bridge between Sega's fighting and sports legacies.
