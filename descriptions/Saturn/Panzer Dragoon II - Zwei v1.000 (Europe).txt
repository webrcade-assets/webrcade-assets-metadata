The prequel to the original Panzer Dragoon, Zwei expanded the series with branching paths and a dragon evolution system. Players control Lundi and his dragon Lagi, engaging in spectacular 3D rail-shooting combat. The game is a technical masterpiece on the Saturn, featuring some of the system's best lighting and transparency effects. The European release is highly regarded for its epic story and atmospheric world-building. It refined the lock-on mechanics and introduced the 'Berserk' attack, making it one of the most polished and satisfying shooters on the console.
