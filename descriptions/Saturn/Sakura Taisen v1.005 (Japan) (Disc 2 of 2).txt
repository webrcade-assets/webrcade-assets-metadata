The concluding disc of the first Sakura Taisen. The story moves toward the final assault on the demonic forces threatening Tokyo. This disc features some of the most challenging tactical encounters in the game and the emotional climax of the character arcs. The Saturn hardware is pushed to its limits here with full-screen anime cutscenes and a sophisticated layering of 2D and 3D graphics during the battle sequences. It remains a historic title for the console, representing the perfect marriage of storytelling and strategy.
