Known as Highway 2000 in the West, Wangan Dead Heat is a high-speed illegal street racing game set on the Tokyo Shuto Expressway. Disc 1 contains the main racing game, featuring a variety of customizable Japanese sports cars and intense night-time racing against rival street gangs. The game is known for its sense of speed and its 'edgy' 90s urban aesthetic. It includes a variety of game modes including a story campaign and time-attack, providing a solid arcade-style racing experience for fans of the Japanese car scene.
