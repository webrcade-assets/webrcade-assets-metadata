Known as Lunacy in North America, Torico is a surreal, cinematic adventure game. Players control Fred, a man with no memory of his past, as he explores the mysterious 'Misty Town.' The game features high-quality pre-rendered CG environments and FMV characters, creating a dream-like and often unsettling atmosphere. Disc 1 focuses on exploring the town and interacting with its bizarre residents to uncover clues about Fred's origin and the strange 'City of Moons.'
