The concluding disc of the Apocalypse IV epic. The story moves toward the East Coast, featuring parodies of New York and other iconic locations. This disc contains the game's most dramatic plot twists and the final confrontation with the demonic forces behind the invasion. The Saturn version is prized for its excellent CD-quality soundtrack and the sheer volume of content, including hidden side-quests and diverse character interactions. It remains a standout title in the Saturn's RPG library for its unique setting and high entertainment value.
