The Japanese title for the second part of the Clockwork Knight saga, 'Gekan' (meaning 'Final Volume'). Following the cliffhanger of the first game, Sir Tongara de Pepperouchau continues his quest to rescue the Clockwork Princess from an unknown kidnapper. This installment introduces new mechanical gimmicks, four massive new toy-themed worlds, and significantly improved 3D boss encounters. The game retains the series' signature 2.5D gameplay, blending 2D platforming precision with vibrant 3D environments and characters.
