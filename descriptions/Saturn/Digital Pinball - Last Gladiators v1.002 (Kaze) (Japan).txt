Widely regarded as one of the best pinball simulations of the 32-bit era. Last Gladiators features four highly detailed tables: Gladiator, Knight of the Roses, Dragon Show, and Warlock. The game focuses on realistic ball physics and intricate table mechanics, complete with 'missions' that trigger special scoring events. The Japanese version features a rocking heavy metal soundtrack that changes based on the table, providing an authentic and high-energy arcade pinball experience.
