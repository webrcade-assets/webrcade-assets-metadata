GT 24 is an arcade-style touring car racer that emphasizes high speeds and aggressive driving. Players compete across multiple day-and-night cycles in endurance-style races. The game features a variety of licensed-style cars and multiple camera angles, including a cockpit view. It is known for its bright, arcade-style aesthetics and accessible handling model, making it an easy-to-pick-up racer for casual players looking for a quick fix of 32-bit racing action.
