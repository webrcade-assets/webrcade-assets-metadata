Jikuu Tantei DD (Time Detective DD) is a sci-fi adventure game that follows a detective who travels through time to solve complex cases. Disc 1 introduces the player to the protagonist and the 'Lorelei' mystery, which spans multiple historical eras. The game uses a mix of static 2D backgrounds, character portraits, and animated FMV sequences. Players must gather clues and interact with characters in different time periods, where actions in the past can ripple through to change the state of the future.
