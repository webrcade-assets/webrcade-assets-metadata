A boxing simulation that focuses on the arcade-style thrill of the sport. Known as Center Ring Boxing in the West, this Japanese version features various fictional fighters and a deep training mode where you can customize your boxer's attributes. The gameplay uses a 3D engine with large, detailed character models. It emphasizes timing and the 'sweet science' of boxing, requiring players to master jabs, hooks, and defensive slips to win by knockout. The high-energy Japanese commentary adds to the authentic ringside feel.
