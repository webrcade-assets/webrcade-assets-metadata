The European release of Sega’s touring car racer. This version brings the fast-paced arcade action of the GT circuit to PAL consoles. The game features multiple tracks with varying weather conditions and a deep championship mode. While noted for its high difficulty and unique handling, the Saturn version compensates with a high level of detail in the car models and environments. It supports the Arcade Racer steering wheel, which is recommended for mastering the game’s demanding physics and reaching the top of the leaderboards.
