Disc 3 delves into the history of the mansion's original owner, a 19th-century magician named Zoltan Carnovasch. Adrienne uncovers evidence of his grisly crimes and the demonic entity that possessed him. The game's atmospheric design and shocking gore effects were controversial at the time. This Saturn port is a technical achievement, managing to spread the massive amount of video data across eight CDs while maintaining respectable video quality.
