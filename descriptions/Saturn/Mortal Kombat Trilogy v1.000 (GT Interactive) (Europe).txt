The European release of the definitive Mortal Kombat experience on the Saturn. This version includes the 'Aggressor' meter, which rewards aggressive play with temporary increases in speed and damage. It is a massive celebration of the franchise's history, featuring digitized sprites from MK1, MK2, MK3, and Ultimate MK3. The Saturn version is noted for having a unique soundtrack and background layers that set it apart from other console versions, providing the most content-heavy fighter available for the system.
