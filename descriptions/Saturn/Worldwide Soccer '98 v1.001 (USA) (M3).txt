The North American release of Worldwide Soccer '98, known for its high-resolution graphics and realistic player physics. It features 48 national teams and allows for deep customization of team tactics and player attributes. The game is celebrated for its smooth 60FPS animation and its intuitive 'one-touch' passing system. This 'M3' version includes multi-language support and remains one of the most beloved sports titles on the system, often cited for its perfect balance of simulation depth and arcade-style fun, particularly in its frantic multiplayer modes.
