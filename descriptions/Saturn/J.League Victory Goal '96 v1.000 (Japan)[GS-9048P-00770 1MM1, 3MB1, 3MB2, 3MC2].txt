The 1996 update to Sega's Victory Goal series, featuring the full J.League license. This version introduced significantly improved 3D stadium graphics and more fluid player animations. The gameplay is a mix of accessible arcade controls and deeper tactical formations. It captures the fervor of Japanese soccer during the mid-90s, including authentic team chants and stadium atmosphere. This release was a key sports title for the system in Japan, proving the Saturn's ability to handle fast-paced, high-resolution 3D sports action.
