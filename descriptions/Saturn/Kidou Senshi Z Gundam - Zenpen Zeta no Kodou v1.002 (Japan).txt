An action-heavy adaptation of the first half of the Zeta Gundam anime. Players control various Mobile Suits, including the Rick Dias and the Zeta Gundam itself, in fast-paced 3D missions. The game features a mix of ground and space combat, utilizing a lock-on system similar to Virtual On. It is highly praised for its cinematic presentation, which seamlessly blends real-time gameplay with high-quality animation from the series. It captures the dark, intense tone of the Zeta timeline perfectly.
