A unique gambling-themed adventure game where players aim to become the 'Gambling King.' The gameplay involves traveling to various locations to compete in different games of chance, including horse racing, mahjong, and casino games. It features a narrative-driven campaign mode where players must manage their funds and interact with various colorful characters. The Saturn version utilizes the CD format to provide voiced dialogue and various mini-game transitions, offering a quirky take on the gambling genre common in Japanese arcades.
