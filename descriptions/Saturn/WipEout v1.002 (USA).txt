The North American release of the game that defined 'cool' in the 90s. WipEout is a futuristic anti-gravity racer where players pilot high-speed ships across seven diverse tracks. The game features a unique physics model where the ships hover and bounce realistically based on speed and terrain. The US version includes an iconic soundtrack and is praised for its stylish art direction and challenging, weapon-heavy racing. It was a key early title for the Saturn, showcasing the console's ability to handle high-speed 3D geometry and vibrant, neon-lit environments.
