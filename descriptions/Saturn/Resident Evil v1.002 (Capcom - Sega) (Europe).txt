The European release of the survival horror pioneer. As Chris Redfield or Jill Valentine, players investigate a mansion overrun by zombies and biological experiments. This Saturn port includes several exclusive features, such as a 'Battle Mode' where players fight through waves of enemies for a high score, and the inclusion of a second Tyrant during the final encounter. It is highly regarded for its atmospheric tension and its role in popularizing the horror genre on 32-bit hardware.
