The conclusion to the 'Orochi Saga,' KOF '97 allows players to choose between 'Advanced' and 'Extra' playstyles, catering to both aggressive and defensive strategies. Like its predecessor, this Saturn version utilizes the 1MB RAM cart to deliver a high-fidelity arcade experience. It features some of the most iconic character debuts in the series and a dramatic story that culminates in a battle against the god-like Orochi. The game's fluid 2D sprites and deep team-based mechanics represent the pinnacle of 90s fighting games.
