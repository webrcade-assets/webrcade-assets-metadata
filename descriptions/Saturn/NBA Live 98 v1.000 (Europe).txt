The follow-up to Live 97, this version features refined graphics and much smoother player movement. It includes the full rosters for the 1997-1998 NBA season and introduces the 'Three-Point Shootout' minigame. The AI was significantly improved to better reflect real-world team strategies. On the Saturn, the game utilized the console's processing power to handle detailed textures on the players' jerseys and a more active crowd, making it one of the most visually impressive sports games of 1997.
