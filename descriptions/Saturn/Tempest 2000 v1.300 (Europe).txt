The European release of Jeff Minter's masterpiece. This version brings the intense, sensory-overload gameplay of Tempest to PAL consoles with smooth performance. The game is famous for its 'Particle' effects and its high-energy, trance-infused audio that synchronizes with the action on screen. It offers a unique 'A.I. Droid' feature that assists the player during the most chaotic waves. For Saturn owners, this is a definitive arcade experience that showcase the system's ability to handle complex sprite-based effects and high-quality CD audio simultaneously.
