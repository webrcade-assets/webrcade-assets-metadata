The European release of the Championship Circuit Edition, optimized for PAL consoles. It brings the high-speed arcade thrills of Daytona to European Saturn owners with improved draw distance and a wider selection of tracks compared to the initial 1995 release. The game includes the 'Mirror' and 'Reverse' track options, adding significant replay value. While it lacks the original arcade music, its remixed soundtrack and fluid controls made it a favorite for European racing fans.
