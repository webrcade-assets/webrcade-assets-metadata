The first half of the 'F' saga, this title is a landmark in the long-running crossover series. It features a deep narrative that interweaves the plots of multiple anime series into a cohesive tactical campaign. Players can upgrade their favorite mechs and pilots, choosing between 'Real Robot' and 'Super Robot' routes. The Saturn version is highly regarded for its excellent sound quality and its detailed 2D battle animations. It is a must-play for mecha enthusiasts, offering hundreds of hours of tactical depth and fanservice.
