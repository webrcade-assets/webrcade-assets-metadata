A launch-era action game that retells the story of the original 1979 Gundam anime. Players pilot the RX-78-2 Gundam in a series of 3D missions that recreate iconic battles from the series. The game uses a mix of 2D sprites for some effects and full 3D models for the mechs. It features the original voice cast and music, providing a high level of fanservice. For its time, it was one of the most accurate ways for fans to experience the 'One Year War' in a 3D gaming format.
