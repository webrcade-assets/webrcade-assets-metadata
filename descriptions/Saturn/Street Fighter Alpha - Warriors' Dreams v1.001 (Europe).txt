The European release of Capcom's high-speed 2D fighter. This version maintains the smooth animation and complex tactical systems that made the Alpha series a competitive favorite. Players can choose between 'Normal' and 'Auto' modes, making it accessible to both veterans and newcomers. The Saturn's hardware excels here, providing fast loading times and a diverse range of colorful stages. It remains an essential title for fighting fans, marking the beginning of a new era for the Street Fighter franchise on 32-bit consoles.
