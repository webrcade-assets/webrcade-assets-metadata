Known as Center Ring Boxing in the US, Victory Boxing is a fully 3D boxing simulation. The game features a first-person or close-third-person perspective that brings players right into the ring. It includes a deep career mode where you can train and customize your boxer to rise through the rankings. The European release is noted for its technical focus on the 'science' of boxing, requiring players to carefully manage their stamina and time their counters to earn a knockout victory against a roster of varied opponents.
