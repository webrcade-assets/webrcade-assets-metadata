The 'Addition' disc for Wangan Dead Heat features a wealth of bonus content and specialized 'Real Arrange' modes. This disc includes exclusive music tracks, a detailed gallery of car parts and history, and additional challenge missions not found on the main disc. It also features 'dating-sim' elements where players can interact with the various race queens from the series. This version is a comprehensive 'fan-disc' that expands the world of the Wangan street racing subculture, offering a deeper look at the characters and vehicles that defined the franchise.
