Released in the West as Cyber Speedway, Gran Chaser is a high-speed hover racing game set in a distant future. The game stands out for its unique 'Syd Mead' inspired vehicle designs and its heavy focus on a story-driven campaign mode. Players must compete across various planetary circuits, using turbo boosts and drafting techniques to stay ahead of the pack. The Japanese version features an original soundtrack and character designs that lean into the space-opera theme, showcasing the Saturn's ability to render complex 3D tracks and scaling sprites simultaneously.
