Ed, Edd n Eddy: Jawbreakers is a video gamereleased on March 25, 2003, by Cartoon Network Interactive, based on the popular television cartoon Ed, Edd n Eddy for the gameBoy Advance. It was developed by Climax Group and BAM! Entertainment. The gamemirrors Ed, Edd (Double Dee), and Eddy's television quest to make money to buy jawbreakers.
