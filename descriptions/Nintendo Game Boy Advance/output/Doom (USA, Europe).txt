From the legendary gamedevelopers at id Software, comes an explosive journey into the nightmare world of DOOM™. Embark on a harrowing mission to rid of Martian Moon Base of hordes of demonic creatures. Navigate through DOOM™'s 24 maze like levels, scouring the corridors for secret areas and combat aids as you take on hell spawned demons with an explosive arsenal of chainguns, plasma rifles and rocket launchers. Use the gameBoy® Advance gameLink® Cable to team up with a friend and blast the levels together or link up to 4 players for the ultimate deathmatch.®
