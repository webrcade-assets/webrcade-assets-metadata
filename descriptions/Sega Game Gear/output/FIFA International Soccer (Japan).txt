EA Sports has teamed up with the governing body of international soccer to bring you the most realistic soccer game ever created on GameGear. Incredible goalie saves - lunge or dive after hard to reach shots in the corners! Unique "play-finder" window lets you scope the field on corner kicks and free kicks. Find the open man and hammer the ball into the goal! Drop your opponent onto the turf with a slide tackle. Be careful, slide tackles from behind can result in yellow cards!
