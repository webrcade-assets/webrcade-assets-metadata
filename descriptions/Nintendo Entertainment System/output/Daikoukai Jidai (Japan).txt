Climb aboard your flagship and set sail into the open seas! As a young Portuguese captain, you ware in search of fortune and fame. With your first mate at the helm, navigate the way to distant lands and legendary riches.

Port ho! Cast anchor in a new land and celebrate your safe journey! Later, barter with stubborn merchants for exotic spices and gems. use your profits to improve your fleet or face the dangers of the sea pirates, storms and mysteries of the deep. Win allies for Portugal and the King will reward you grandly!
