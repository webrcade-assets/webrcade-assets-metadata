Blistering pace - pixel perfect passing - superb tactical game play! KICK OFF is all action-packed soccer with a host of features: 1 or 2 players option, plus Team Mode, against the computer; choice of 4 playing surfaces and 4 different wind conditions; Cup, League, and Euro Trophy Competitions with penalty shoot out; instinctive joystick controls to Pass, Dribble, Shoot, Head and Chip the ball, or do a sliding tackle; Curling Corner Kicks, controlled Throw-Ins and Goal Kicks; Injury and Extra Time; Red and Yellow Cards; Set Piece Free Kicks and famous after touch to let you bend, swerve, and dip the ball!
