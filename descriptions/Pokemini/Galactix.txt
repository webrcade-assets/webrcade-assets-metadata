You have to fight the aliens to protect your home, your family and your satelite-TV! Fight the aliens in three battles and kill as many as you can! 
Shake your space ship to ignite a destructive bomb and blast all aliens away if you are in trouble, but be aware that you only have three of these bombs!

To fire the laser you just press the "A" control of your spaceship repeatedly. Be aware that you only have three space ships, if the aliens destroy
the whole fleet the earth is lost!

Good luck!
