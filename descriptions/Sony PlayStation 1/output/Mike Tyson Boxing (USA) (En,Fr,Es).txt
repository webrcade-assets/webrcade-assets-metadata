Get ready to nosh on some ears with MIKE TYSON BOXING. You'll spend most of your time in the Career mode, where you can manage almost every aspect of your boxer's career, right down to his diet, exercise, and sleep patterns. If you need help managing your fighter, you can always ask Mike for help, who offers advice on how to win the title. You can also customize your fighter's look, or play as Mike Tyson himself. The gameplay is similar to how Tyson fights in real life: load up on big punches and go for spectacular knockouts. Of course, you can also step into the ring with a buddy and find out who is the real king of the ring. Master the intricacies of the sweet science with MIKE TYSON BOXING.
