Wacky Races is a popular cartoon series that's being brought to life in the form of a wild and wacky racing videogame by Infogrames. This madcap racing game is ready to set the PlayStation ablaze with all of the outrageous characters from the popular Wacky Races cartoon, such as Penelope Pitstop, Dick Dastardly, his sidekick Muttley and many other characters from the show. There are numerous game modes available to the player including Arcade, Championship, Time Trial, Endurance, and Versus. In each mode, players will demolish his foes with a variety of weapons and outlandish gadgets that can be picked up on the racing track along the way.
