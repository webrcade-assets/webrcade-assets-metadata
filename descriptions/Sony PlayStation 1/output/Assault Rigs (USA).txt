In the world of ASSAULT RIGS, physical sports no longer exist. All sports now take place in the virtual world of the Internet. The most popular sport is the mayhem and destruction caused by virtual rigs, or hover tanks. Choose one of three distinctive rigs and pilot it through 40 levels of competition. Your only goal is to win and make your sponsor, ZamCam, Inc., the biggest sponsor of the World Net Championship. Losing is not an option. After dominating the computer-controlled competition, turn your attention to some real world foes with the Two-Player mode. In the world of ASSAULT RIGS, it's all mind over matter.
