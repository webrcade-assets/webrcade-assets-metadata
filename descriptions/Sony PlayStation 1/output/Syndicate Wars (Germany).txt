In the not so distant future, two corporations are battling for control of the Earth: the long established Eurocorp and the mysterious Church of the New Epoch. Choose either side for a series of 15 missions (30 in total) where your goal is to complete one of the following:
(1) Eliminate persons or buildings
(2) Persuade people of importance with your Persuadetron
(3) Escort and protect persons, vehicles or buildings
(4) Acquire important items by stealing
(5) Evacuate your agents to safety.
