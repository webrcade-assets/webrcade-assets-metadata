The ultimate BMX gaming experience is here! Maximum Remix includes the entire best-selling Dave Mirra Freestyle BMX game plus more of what you asked for--more levels, more modes, more riders--it's more BMX than you can handle with 10-Time World Champion Dave Mirra.

Dave Mirra Freestyle BMX is the only BMX game to feature Dave Mirra, 10-time World Champion and 11-time X Games medallist. Aspiring pros can play as Dave himself or 9 other superstar riders including: Ryan Nyquist, Kenan Harkin, Chad Kagy, Leigh Ramsdell, Troy McMurray, Mike Laird, Joey Garcia, Shaun Butler and Tim Mirra. Maximum Remix also includes two new special characters giving you a total of four wild characters to choose from.
