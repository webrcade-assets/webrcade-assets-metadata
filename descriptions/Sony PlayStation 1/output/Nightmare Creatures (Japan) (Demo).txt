A bloody good time in jolly old London.

In 1834 on London's blackest eve, a secret cult known as The Brotherhood of Hecate, rediscovered the key to man's unholiest fears. It was through this arcane act of terror that the evil leader of the Brotherhood, Adam Crowley, swore to overtake the world... Ever since this fateful night, not a London street, alley or town square has escaped the whispered screams of "Nightmare Creatures!"
