You eliminated the Syphon Filter virus and prevented the massacre of millions of lives. You escaped from the Agency and exposed them for what they really are. Now the time has come to finish the job once and for all, using whatever means necessary. But remember - you must face your past before you can look to the future! Enter the Syphon Filter drama once more. Play as Gabe Logan, Lian Xing and Theresa Lipan, and discover the truth about their pasts. Journey through Tokyo, Panama, Afghanistan and Costa Rica as you evade, retrieve, demolish and assassinate your way through a host of nail-biting missions.
