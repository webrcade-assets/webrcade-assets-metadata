Fast-paced, high-flying basketball action comes to the PlayStation with NBA HOOPZ. Get into a three-on-three game with your favorite NBA team you'll get a guard, forward, and center. When you are ready to play, you can challenge the computer or play with five of your friends in the Exhibition mode. If you want more of a challenge than a single game, try to claim the NBA championship in a 14-, 28-, 56-, or 82-game Season. In all of the modes, players can perform huge dunks, make wild passes, and hit some spectacular shots. Should you want to be more involved in the game, you can create a player and put yourself in the game; when you correctly answer random trivia questions that pop up, your custom player earns extra attribute points. Get ready for some serious hang time with NBA HOOPZ and show off your skills.
