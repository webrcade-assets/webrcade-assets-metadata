Players select warlords to control, and rise to dominance by defeating other warlords in quiz encounters. Each attempt at conquering a province leads to a quiz posed by the defending warlord. The themes of the quiz may have nothing to do with the historical period and range from general knowledge to Japanese anime and video games.
