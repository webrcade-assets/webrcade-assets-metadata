The second Shiawase Usagi game contains two scenarios, of which one, dubbed Sailor Z, is non-interactive: it is a parody on a Sailor Moon story with sexually violent and disturbing themes The interactive scenario of the game is called Toraware Usagi; its theme is rape as well. The nameless protagonist finds a girl in a Japanese high-school uniform, tied and blind-folded. The entire scenario consists of a long scene, during which the "hero" proceeds to rape the girl.
