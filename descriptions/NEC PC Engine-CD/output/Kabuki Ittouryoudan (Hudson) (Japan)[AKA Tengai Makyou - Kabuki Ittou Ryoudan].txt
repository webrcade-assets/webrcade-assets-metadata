Tengai Makyō: Kabuki Ittou Ryoudan (カブキ一刀涼談) is a fighting video game and the fourth game in the Tengai Makyō series. It was released on PC Engine CD on February 24, 1995 and was developed by Red Entertainment and published by Hudson Soft in Japan only. You play the roles of the legendary Fire Warriors who battle the Daimon Cult, among other evil forces.
