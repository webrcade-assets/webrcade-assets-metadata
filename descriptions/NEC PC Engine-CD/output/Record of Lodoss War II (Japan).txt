Set in the Record of Lodoss War fantasy universe, created by the Japanese writer Ryo Mizuno, this game is based on the anime Chronicles of the Heroic Knight. The story focuses on a young man named Spark, who wants to become a knight of the country of Flaim. King Kashue needs Spark to prove himself worthy of this title, and tells him to gather a band of mercenaries and prepare to stop the dark wizard Wagnard's mysterious plans.
